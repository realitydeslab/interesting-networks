# Gallery TPW (Toronto Photographers Workshop)

Artist-run centre and independent gallery in Toronto, Canada. Presents contemporary photographic art and hosts public programming including artist talks, screenings, and critical discussions. One of Canada's leading independent spaces for photography-adjacent contemporary art.

## Why Interesting
[[Christina Sharpe]] gave a foundational talk here in 2019 that became the germ of *Ordinary Notes* — illustrating how TPW functions as an intellectual gathering point that crosses between art and theory. Small but influential; the kind of artist-run space that serious thinkers prefer over big institutions.
