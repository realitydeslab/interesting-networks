One of the world's most important media art institutions. Founded 1989 in Karlsruhe, Germany. Museum, research center, and production facility spanning media art, digital culture, critical computing, and art-science intersection. Hosts permanent collection, residencies, and major international exhibitions. Under new leadership since 2023.

## Key People
- **[Alistair Hudson](https://linkedin.com/in/alistair-hudson-87641430)** — Scientific-Artistic Chairman & CEO (since April 2023). Previously Director of The Whitworth and Manchester Art Gallery (2018–2023). UK-based — bridge figure between UK and Germany.
- **Peter Weibel** — Founding director (d. 2023). Shaped ZKM as the global center for media art.
- **[Hertzlab](https://zkm.de/en/lab/hertzlab)** — Department of Artistic Research & Development. Hosts residencies in acoustics, visual media, software development.

## Topics
- Media art and digital culture
- AI and computational creativity
- Performative art and technology (current focus via Rauschenberg)
- Sound and music technology (Hertzlab, Giga-Hertz Award)
- Critical computing and society
- Art history of technology (e.g., Connection Machine conference)
