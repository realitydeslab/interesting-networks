# Nottingham Contemporary

Major UK contemporary art gallery known for interdisciplinary programming bridging art, theory, and environmental concerns. Publishes through The Contemporary Journal.

## Notable Events (relevant)
- **Caves, Dwellings & Vibration** (2023) — two-day programme; keynote by [[Kathryn Yusoff]]: "Rethinking Geologic Subjectivity in Broken Earths," moderated by Andrew Goffey. Part of exhibition *Hollow Earth: Art, Caves & The Subterranean Imaginary*.
