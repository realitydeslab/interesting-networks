# EASST Conference

European Association for the Study of Science and Technology biennial conference. The European counterpart to 4S — the flagship STS gathering in Europe. Strong ANT tradition; regularly features work on technology, AI, infrastructure, nonhuman agency, and ontological politics. Alternates years with 4S (which is US/international); every 4 years they hold a joint 4S+EASST meeting.

## Key People
- European STS scholars; ANT community; post-ANT (Mol, Law, Akrich networks)

## Topics
- Science and technology studies
- Infrastructure and platforms
- Nonhuman agency / ANT
- Feminist STS
- AI and algorithms
- Ontological politics
