# The Whitworth

University of Manchester's art gallery. Major civic art museum with strong curatorial programming in contemporary and modern art. Extensively renovated 2015.

**Raqs connection**: Hosted [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s major solo *Twilight Language* (2017). *Bending Man* (from *Coronation Park*) is in the permanent collection. Raqs collaborated with scientists and historians across Manchester's institutions in making the exhibition.
