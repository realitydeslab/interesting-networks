# STRP Festival

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via [[Kyle McDonald]] deep research.

Art and technology festival based in Eindhoven, Netherlands. Focuses on "the future of humanity through art and technology." Exhibitions, performances, workshops. Eindhoven is home to Philips and a strong design+technology culture (TU/e, Design Academy Eindhoven). STRP sits at the art/tech/design intersection with a European critical slant.

[[Kyle McDonald]] showed *Highsight* here (2015) and *Crypto Therapy* (2022).

## Why Interesting
Niche enough to be curated, big enough to reach broad audiences. Eindhoven's design culture creates an interesting context — not just media art but design futures. Worth tracking for CFPs.
