# MOCO (Movement and Computing)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Thecla Schiphorst deep research.

The premier interdisciplinary conference at the intersection of movement and computing. Co-founded in 2014 at IRCAM-Centre Pompidou, Paris. Now in its 10th+ year. Brings together researchers from arts, humanities, and sciences for work on dance, gesture, movement analysis, choreographic computing, embodied cognition, and more.

## Origins
- **2014**: First MOCO at IRCAM-Centre Pompidou, Paris. "International Workshop on Movement and Computing." [[Thecla Schiphorst]] was a co-organizer/steering committee member.
- **2015**: MOCO at ISEA 2015 in Vancouver — brought to Schiphorst's home city.
- **2024**: MOCO'24 at Utrecht University, Netherlands — 9th conference, marking 10th anniversary of initiative.

## Topics
- Movement analysis and recognition
- Dance technology and choreographic tools
- Embodied cognition and interaction
- Laban Movement Analysis in computing
- Music, sound, movement relationships
- Interactive performance systems
- Movement-based creative technologies
- Somatic approaches to interaction
