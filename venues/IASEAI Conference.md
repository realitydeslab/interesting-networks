# IASEAI Conference

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-14:** Initial deep research. Created venue note with full conference details, program committee, and CFP info.
- **2026-02-26:** IASEAI'26 concluded at UNESCO House, Paris. 1,300+ attendees, 99+ countries.

The annual conference of the [[IASEAI|International Association for Safe and Ethical AI]]. Held each February in Paris, strategically timed around major government AI summits. Combines academic paper presentations with policy talks, panels, and workshops. By invitation or paper acceptance. Features an extraordinary speaker roster — multiple Nobel laureates, Turing Award winners, UN officials, and leading AI safety researchers.
