# Ars Electronica

Major international festival for art, technology, and society. Founded 1979 in Linz. Includes Prix Ars Electronica (awards), Futurelab (R&D), and annual festival.

## Key Components
- **Festival** — annual, September, Linz
- **Prix Ars Electronica** — prestigious awards for media art
- **Futurelab** — R&D lab for art-tech
- **Ars Electronica Center** — Museum of the Future

## Key People
- Gerfried Stocker — artistic director (since 2014)
- [[Jussi Parikka]] — connected
- [[Lauren Lee McCarthy]] — exhibited/awarded

## Why Interesting
The biggest and most established art-tech festival. Prix Ars Electronica winners are always worth tracking. Residency and fellowship opportunities. Essential venue for anyone at the art-science intersection.
