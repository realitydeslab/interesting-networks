# Ars Electronica

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Deep research completed. Full venue profile with Prix categories, submission deadlines, Futurelab structure, and key network connections.

World's longest-running media art festival and competition (since 1979). The gold standard for art-technology intersection with Prix Ars Electronica awards (€10,000 + Golden Nica), annual festival, Futurelab R&D, and Center museum.
