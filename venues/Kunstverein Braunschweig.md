# Kunstverein Braunschweig

German Kunstverein (artist/collector society gallery) in Braunschweig. Long history as an important venue for contemporary art in Northern Germany.

**Raqs connection**: Hosted [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s solo exhibition *The Laughter of Tears* (2021).
