# Ljubljana Biennale of Graphic Arts

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during [[Bogna Konior]] deep research.

Biennial at the International Centre of Graphic Arts (MGLC), Ljubljana, Slovenia. Has expanded beyond traditional printmaking into digital art, video essays, and new media. [[Bogna Konior]] presented video essays and contributed catalog texts to the 34th edition (2021, "Iskra Delta" theme).

## Why Interesting
Eastern European contemporary art institution with strong digital/new media programming. Part of the Slovenian art-tech ecosystem alongside [[Maribor Computer Arts Festival]] and [ŠUM Journal](https://www.sum.si/).
