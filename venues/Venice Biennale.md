# Venice Biennale (La Biennale di Venezia)

The world's oldest and most prestigious contemporary art biennial. Founded 1895. Held every two years in Venice, Italy (odd-numbered years for art; even years for architecture). The Art Biennale consists of two main components: the International Exhibition (curated by the artistic director) and National Pavilions in the Giardini and Arsenale. Approximately 90 countries maintain national pavilions. Winning the Golden Lion is the most prestigious award in contemporary art.

## Why Interesting
The Venice Biennale remains the most symbolically significant node in the global contemporary art world. National pavilions function as cultural diplomacy; the choice of artist and curator for each pavilion is a statement about national art policy and global aspirations. [[Cuauhtémoc Medina]]'s 2009 Mexican Pavilion was a landmark use of this diplomatic/institutional framework for radical political art.
