# transmediale

Annual festival for art and digital culture in Berlin. Since 1988. More critical/theoretical than Ars Electronica.

## Format
- Conference, exhibition, film/video programme
- Performances and workshops
- Held at Haus der Kulturen der Welt

## Key People
- [[Jussi Parikka]] — frequent contributor
- [[Metahaven]] — exhibited
- Kristoffer Gansing — former artistic director

## Connections
- [[Ars Electronica]] — peer festival (transmediale is more critical)
- CTM Festival — sister event (music/sound)
- [[ZKM]] — peer institution
- [[Urbanomic]] — Robin Mackay has spoken here

## Why Interesting
The critical theory counterpart to Ars Electronica. More intimate, more politically engaged. Berlin location makes it a hub for European media art discourse. CTM Festival (music) runs alongside — good for the sonic/affect angle.
