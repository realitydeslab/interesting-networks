# Le Lieu Unique (Nantes)

National venue for contemporary creation based in a repurposed biscuit factory (the old LU biscuit factory) in Nantes, France. A Scène Nationale — one of France's premier publicly-funded contemporary arts centres. Known for adventurous, interdisciplinary programming spanning performance, music, visual art, and literature.

## Why Interesting

**Site of the world's first cultured meat performance.** In 2003, hosted *L'Art Biotech* — a landmark bio-art exhibition curated by [Jens Hauser](http://www.labo-lhybride.org/en/). [[Tissue Culture and Art Project]]'s *Disembodied Cuisine* was performed here: grown frog skeletal muscle cooked and served to gallery visitors, the living frog present throughout. This event preceded the cellular agriculture industry by a decade.

Key bio-art venue in France. Connected to [[SymbioticA]] and [[Tissue Culture and Art Project]] through the L'Art Biotech exhibition (also published a catalogue with Filigranes Editions). Jens Hauser is a key curator/theorist of biological art who organized this event; also connected to [[SymbioticA]]'s *Still, Living* (BEAP 2007).

## Key People
- **Jens Hauser** — curator of *L'Art Biotech* (2003) and *Still, Living* (BEAP 2007). Key theorist and curator in bio-art. Worth deeper research.
