# imagineNATIVE Film + Media Arts Festival

Annual festival in Toronto presenting the largest global showcase of Indigenous screen-content. Covers film, video, audio, and interactive/new media work by Indigenous artists worldwide. The **Best New Media** award is the premier recognition for Indigenous digital and interactive art.

## Relevance to Jason Edward Lewis
Lewis's Obx Lab and AbTeC productions have won Best New Media multiple times (2009, 2010, 2013) and received honorable mentions. A key venue where Indigenous new media art gets institutionally recognized.

## Connection to Amber
Not a primary venue for Amber, but useful as a discovery space for Indigenous artists working in interactive/computational media. Lewis, Skawennati, LaPensée all circulate here.
