# Aksioma – Institute for Contemporary Art

Ljubljana-based research and exhibition space specializing in contemporary art, media culture, and theory. Important node in Eastern European digital art network.

## Key Programs
- Research projects on digital culture and politics
- Exhibitions of international media artists
- Publications and theoretical development
- Artist residencies and collaborations

## Why Interesting
Significant research and exhibition space connecting Eastern and Western European digital art scenes. Strong theoretical foundation combined with contemporary practice. Consistent programming of important international artists.

## Key Projects (James Bridle connection)
- "My delight on a shining night" — James Bridle solo exhibition (2019)
- "Under the Shadow of the Drone" — James Bridle solo (2015)
- "Transnationalisms" (2018) — curated program including James Bridle
- "The Black Chamber" (2016) — surveillance-themed exhibition

## Network Position
Connected to broader European media art circuit (ZKM, transmediale, HeK Basel) while serving as important hub for Eastern European region. Bridge institution between theory and practice.
