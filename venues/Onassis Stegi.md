# Onassis Stegi (Onassis Foundation)

Athens-based cultural institution. Runs **Plásmata** exhibition series — immersive art trails blending reality and illusion. Also runs **Onassis ONX** (new realities programme) which has won awards at SXSW. Major funder/commissioner of art × tech work in the Mediterranean.

## Key People
- [[Jiabao Li]] — invited for Plásmata 3: "We've met before, haven't we?" (2025)

## Next Edition

## Why Interesting
European institution with serious funding for art-tech, outside the usual Northern European circuit. Athens location gives access to Mediterranean/Southern European art scene. Onassis ONX programme actively commissions new work.
