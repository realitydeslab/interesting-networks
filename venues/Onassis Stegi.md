## What It Is

Three overlapping entities:

**[Onassis Foundation](https://www.onassis.org/foundation/onassis-foundation/)** — Greek private nonprofit founded 1975 by Aristotle Onassis. Based in Vaduz (legal), operating in Athens and New York. Mission: culture, healthcare, education. Runs 7,500+ scholarships since 1978. Built the Onassis National Transplant Center (2024) in Athens. Governed by Anthony S. Papadimitriou (President/Secretary General).

**[Onassis Stegi](https://www.onassis.org/initiatives/onassis-stegi)** — The flagship cultural center in Athens. 18,000m², at 107-109 Syngrou Avenue, Athens 11745. Opened December 2010. Covers theater, dance, music, contemporary art, digital arts, debates. International co-productions with Almeida Theatre (London), Théâtre de la Ville (Paris), Kampnagel (Hamburg), Münchner Kammerspiele. Also runs Movement Radio (international web radio), and Onassis AiR.

**[Onassis ONX](https://www.onassis.org/initiatives/onassis-digital-innovation/onx-studio/)** ([onx.studio](https://www.onx.studio)) — "A global art & advanced technologies platform." Founded 2020 as ONX Studio in Midtown Manhattan, jointly with New Museum's [NEW INC](https://www.newinc.org/). Now expanded to two physical locations:
- **New York:** 390 Broadway, Floor 4, Tribeca, NY 10013 (new studio launched Jan 2026 with TECHNE Homecoming)
- **Athens:** Onassis Ready building, Athens (ONX Showcase in May 2026)

## Programmes

### Plásmata — Public Digital Art Series
A series of large-scale public art exhibitions in Greek cities exploring AI, digital bodies, ecology.

- **Plásmata 1 / "You and AI"** (2021) — Pedion Areos park, Athens. Outdoor group exhibition on AI in everyday life.
- **Plásmata / "Bodies, Dreams, and Data"** (2022) — Pedion Areos park, Athens. 25 works by Greek and international artists. Digital art on bodies, dreams, data.
- **Plásmata II: Ioannina** — touring edition to Ioannina. Included "Parallels" — work that later traveled to Lumen Prize and Lincoln Center, NYC.
- **Plásmata 3** (2025+) — includes [[Jiabao Li]]'s *"We've met before, haven't we?"* — bat/interspecies/AI work. Also used HoloKit hardware.

### Onassis AiR — Artistic Research & Residency

## SXSW 2025 — Key Achievement
Two Onassis ONX projects at SXSW 2025 (March 7–14, Austin, Texas):

1. **"In the Current of Being"** by **Cameron Kostopoulos** — haptic VR experience, true story of Carolyn Mercer, a survivor of electroshock conversion therapy. Uses wearable haptic vests/sleeves/gloves to physically transmit heartbeat and breath. **Won the Agog Immersive Impact Award** (XR Experience Competition, World Premiere).

2. **"EchoVision"** by **[[Jiabao Li]]** — mixed reality bat echolocation experience. **Uses custom bat-shaped headsets built on open-source HoloKit** (Amber's invention). Participants echolocate like bats; AI categorizes bat calls (mating, kissing) into compositions. Inspired by Thomas Nagel's "What Is it Like to Be a Bat?" (U.S. Premiere, XR Experience Spotlight).

→ **Direct connection to Amber:** Jiabao Li used HoloKit hardware to make a SXSW-premiered work about interspecies perception and more-than-human experience. This is Amber's invention in Amber's thematic territory.
