# Khoj Studios

Delhi-based independent arts organization and residency lab. Founded 1997. One of South Asia's most important sites for experimental contemporary art, artist residencies, and critical practice. Connected to a wider international Khoj network.

**Raqs connection**: Showed [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s *Na-Bam* (2024).
