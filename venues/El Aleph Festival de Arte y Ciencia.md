# El Aleph Festival de Arte y Ciencia

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during MUAC deep research.
- **2024:** Edition hosted "Museo digital. Inteligencias y artificios" — a major forum on AI, digital museums, and data colonialism co-organized by [[MUAC]] and Fundación Telefónica Movistar México.

Annual festival co-produced by **UNAM** and held at [[MUAC]] and other UNAM spaces (Centro Cultural Universitario, Ciudad Universitaria). Connects art, science, and technology with a Latin American critical perspective. Named after Borges' "El Aleph" — the point in space containing all other points.

The 2024 edition included: Sara Diamond (OCAD), Ulises A. Mejias (*Data Colonialism*), José-Carlos Mariátegui (LSE), Fernanda Pitta (MAC São Paulo), Tania Aedo (Cátedra Max Aub) — a genuinely international and intellectually serious lineup focused on decolonial digital practices.

## Key People
- **Tania Aedo** — Coordinator of Cátedra Max Aub, Transdisciplina en Arte y Tecnología; key organizer for art-tech programming
- [[MUAC]] staff — Julio García Murillo, Guillermo García Pérez as co-moderators

## Topics
Art + science + technology from a Latin American critical perspective. Recent focus: data colonialism, AI, digital archives, indigenous knowledge and technology, decolonial digital practices.
