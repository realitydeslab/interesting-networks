# Nome Gallery

Berlin-based contemporary art gallery with strong focus on art-technology intersection and critical engagement with digital systems.

## Key Artists
- **James Bridle** — Multiple solo presentations (2015, 2017, 2022)
- Consistent program of artists working with technology, surveillance, digital culture

## Why Interesting
Small but influential gallery that has consistently supported important work at intersection of art and technology. Regular venue for critical technology art practice. Part of Berlin's vibrant digital culture scene.

## Exhibition History
- "Dialogue: James Bridle and Jonas Staal" (2022)
- "Failing to Distinguish Between a Tractor Trailer and the Bright White Sky" — James Bridle solo (2017)
- "The Glomar Response" — James Bridle solo (2015)
- "Agency" — Curated by James Bridle (2018)
- "Evidentiary Realism" — Group show (2017-2018)

## Network Position
Connected to international art-tech circuit. Artists shown here often also appear at Serpentine, ZKM, HeK Basel, etc. Represents cutting-edge curatorial vision in smaller-scale venue.
