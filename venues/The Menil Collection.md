# The Menil Collection

Major private art museum in Houston, Texas. Founded by John and Dominique de Menil in 1987. Known for its exceptional collection of Surrealism, African, Byzantine, and contemporary art. Part of the cultural ecosystem around Rice University. Timothy Morton gave an interview/talk there in 2024 around the publication of *Hell*. The Menil has a distinctive intellectual culture that bridges contemporary art and humanistic thought.
