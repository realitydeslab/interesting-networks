# CTM Festival

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Enriched during transmediale deep research. Origins and relationship to transmediale documented.

Annual festival for adventurous music and art in Berlin (January/February). Originated inside [[transmediale]] in 1999 as "club transmediale." Now fully independent. The sound/music counterpart to transmediale's visual/theoretical focus. Together they form Berlin's critical media art ecosystem.

## History
- **1999:** Founded as "club transmediale" — nighttime electronic music programming within transmediale, by Marcus Weiser (Rechenzentrum), Lillevan Pobjoy, Timm Ringewaldt, Remaclus Schuurbiers, Jan Rohlf. First edition at Maria am Ostbahnhof.
- **2001:** Skipped a year when transmediale had funding crisis.
- **~2003:** Began operating more independently; Oliver Baurhenn joined, first public funding.
- **2012:** Formally rebranded as CTM Festival (from club transmediale).
- **Current:** 10-day event with concerts, installations, panel discussions, screenings, workshops.

**Relationship to transmediale:** Sister festival. Joint Vorspiel pre-festival programme. Joint evening events. Shared audiences and critical orientation.

## Key People
- **Jan Rohlf** — Co-founder, ongoing leadership
- **Oliver Baurhenn** — Joined 2002, co-director, shaped independent identity
