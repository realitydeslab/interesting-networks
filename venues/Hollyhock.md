# Hollyhock Leadership Centre

Retreat center and leadership institute on Cortes Island, BC, on the traditional territories of the Klahoose, Tla'amin, and Homalco Nations. Where the Salish Sea meets ancient cedars. Non-profit. Programs in ecological wisdom, progressive leadership, and personal growth.

Regular venue for [[David Abram]] (leading "Between the Human Animal and the Animate Earth" Aug 2026; Geopoetics Symposium 2022). Also hosts programs in coastal foraging, movement arts, somatic practices.
