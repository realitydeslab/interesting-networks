# Biennale de l'Image en Mouvement (BIM)

Geneva's biennial for moving image art. Presented by Centre d'Art Contemporain Genève (CAC Genève). One of the key European platforms for video art, film installation, and artists working with time-based media. International scope; tends toward politically engaged, formally experimental work.

## Key People

- [[Meriem Bennani]] — world premiere of *Party on the CAPS* (2018)

## Topics

Moving image art, video installation, documentary hybrid forms, experimental film, digital/animated work.

## Notable Editions

- **BIM 2018** — Meriem Bennani's *Party on the CAPS* world premiere

## Venue

Centre d'Art Contemporain Genève, Geneva, Switzerland. Also associated with various Geneva cultural partners.

## Why Interesting

Important European entry point for artists working at the intersection of video, installation, and digital animation. The *Party on the CAPS* premiere here — before the Whitney Biennial and New York gallery show — suggests BIM is ahead-of-the-curve for MENA-connected, digitally-fluent video art. Worth tracking for emerging video artists.
