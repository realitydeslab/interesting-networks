# The Renaissance Society

University-affiliated non-profit contemporary art space at the [University of Chicago](https://www.uchicago.edu/). Founded 1915 — one of the oldest institutions in the US dedicated to contemporary art. Known for commissioning ambitious artist projects with intellectual and theoretical depth, often collaborative research with artists. Free admission.
