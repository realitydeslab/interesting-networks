# Venice VR (Venice Immersive)

Annual immersive media section of the Venice Film Festival (La Biennale di Venezia) — now called **Venice Immersive**. The European peer to [[Sundance New Frontier]]. Founded 2017, rapidly became the second most prestigious immersive art exhibition in the world. Held on Lazzaretto Vecchio island (separate from main festival), creating an immersive island experience. Awards include Grand Jury Prize, Best Experience, Best VR Film.

## Key People
- Various curators under Biennale di Venezia's Cinema sector
- Strong connections to French immersive art ecosystem (France heavily represented)

## Topics
VR, AR, XR, interactive narrative, live performance, installation. Slightly more European/art-house in tone than Sundance. Documentary and fiction both represented.

## Why Interesting
Part of the same circuit as [[Sundance New Frontier]]. Works often premiere at Sundance then show at Venice, or vice versa. Key European showcase for the same artist community. Amber's immersive work would naturally target both.
