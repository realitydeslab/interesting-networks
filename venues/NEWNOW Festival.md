# NEWNOW Festival

German media art and new technologies festival. Held at the iconic Zeche Zollverein (historically Europe's most productive coal mine, now a UNESCO World Heritage Site and cultural center) in Essen. Commissioned and premiered [[Kimchi and Chips]]' *Another Moon* (2021) — an energy-ethics artwork powered by solar panels at a former coal mine. The site-work resonance is exemplary.

## Why Interesting
Strong industrial-cultural site; genuine commissioning capacity; focus on art-technology-society intersection. Zeche Zollverein as venue adds powerful conceptual layer to any work about energy, industry, or post-fossil futures.
