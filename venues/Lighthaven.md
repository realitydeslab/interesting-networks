# Lighthaven

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Deep research completed. Berkeley campus run by Lightcone Infrastructure since ~2023; hosts AI safety, rationalism, consciousness, and longevity conferences. MC0001 (CIMC founding conference) is scheduled here May 29–31, 2026.

## What
A 40-bedroom conference and retreat campus in Berkeley, California, run by **Lightcone Infrastructure** (a nonprofit). Capacity: ~80 overnight guests; conference space for up to 140 inside or 500 outside with 20+ session rooms. Opened ~2023. Physical home of a cluster of rationalist/EA/AI-focused intellectual communities in the Bay Area.

## Who Runs It
**Lightcone Infrastructure** — a nonprofit "dedicated to facilitating intellectual progress on humanity's most important questions." The organization also runs the LessOnline festival and supports the rationalist blogging community (LessWrong ecosystem).

## Focus & Aesthetic
High-context intellectual gatherings at the intersection of AI, philosophy of mind, consciousness research, prediction markets, progress studies, longevity science, and AI safety. Events are small-to-medium format (50–500 people), emphasizing direct intellectual exchange, debate, and collaboration. Notable events hosted:
- **LessOnline** — annual festival for internet writers (LessWrong community), 400+ attendees; Scott Alexander, Zvi Mowshowitz
- **Manifest** — prediction markets festival; Nate Silver, Robin Hanson, Dwarkesh Patel
- **Eleos ConCon** — AI consciousness and welfare conference; David Chalmers, Jeff Sebo, Rob Long
- **SPARC** — applied rationality for high schoolers
- **Progress Conference** — progress studies; Tyler Cowen, Jason Crawford
- **The Curve** — AI policy and researchers across perspectives
- **MATS** — AI safety fellowship (multi-week residential)
- **Vitalist Bay** — 8-week longevity program; Peter Diamandis, George Church
- **Inkhaven** — writing residency

## Open Calls / Residencies
No standard open calls. Events are organized by external organizers who book the space. Lightcone does run an internal writing residency (Inkhaven). To host an event, contact via lighthaven.space.
