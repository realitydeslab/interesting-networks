# HeK (House of Electronic Arts Basel)

Major Swiss institution dedicated to digital and electronic art. Regular exhibitions of leading international artists at intersection of art and technology.

## Key Programs
- Exhibitions of contemporary digital art
- Research partnerships with universities  
- Artist residencies and commissions

## Why Interesting
Central venue in European digital art circuit. Consistently shows cutting-edge work at art-technology intersection. Basel location makes it accessible hub for international digital art community.

## Key Exhibitions (Recent)
- "Earthbound – In Dialogue with Nature" (2022) — included James Bridle
- "Entangled Realities: Living with Artificial Intelligence" (2019) — major AI art exhibition

## Network Position
Connected to major international digital art circuit including ZKM, Ars Electronica, transmediale. Regular venue for established art-tech practitioners.
