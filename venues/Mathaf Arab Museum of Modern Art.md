# Mathaf: Arab Museum of Modern Art

Doha, Qatar. Major museum of Arab modern and contemporary art, part of the Qatar Museums network. Opened 2010.

**Raqs connection**: Hosted [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s solo exhibition *Still More World* (2019). *Alive, with Cerussite and Peppered Moth* (2017) is in their permanent collection.
