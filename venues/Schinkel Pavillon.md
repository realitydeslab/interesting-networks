# Schinkel Pavillon

Contemporary art space in Berlin. Housed in a neoclassical pavilion in central Berlin. Known for ambitious, technically sophisticated exhibitions bridging contemporary art and technology/politics. Curated *Proof of Work* (2018) with Simon Denny — the seminal blockchain art exhibition where [[terra0]]'s Premna Daemon debuted. Part of Berlin's serious contemporary art infrastructure.
