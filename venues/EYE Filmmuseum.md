# EYE Filmmuseum

Amsterdam's national film museum, home to the Dutch national film heritage collection. One of the most significant film archives in Europe. Hosts the annual **EYE Museum International Film Conference** — a major European gathering for film scholars, archivists, and researchers.

The 2024 conference (May 26–29) featured a session by [[Daniel Chávez Heras]], Nanne van Noord, and Mila Oiva: "Sculpting time with Computers: Identifying temporal dynamics in large audiovisual collections."

## Why Interesting
The EYE Filmmuseum sits at the intersection of film archiving, cultural heritage, and digital humanities. Its annual conference is one of the key European venues for film scholarship that takes archives and preservation seriously alongside critical/theoretical work. As AI tools become central to film archive workflows, EYE's conference is becoming an increasingly important venue for computational film studies.
