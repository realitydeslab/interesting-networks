# ICCC — International Conference on Computational Creativity

The primary academic venue for computational creativity research. Annual conference, held since 2010. Proceedings published at [computationalcreativity.net](https://computationalcreativity.net/).

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Oblique Strategies deep research.

## Vibe
The place where researchers working on creative AI, generative systems, and computational aesthetics publish. Smaller and more focused than CHI or NeurIPS — quality > quantity. Strong overlap with ALife, HCI, and music/art computing.

## Key People
- Jon McCormack (Monash SensiLab) — key figure
- Anna Jordanous — computational creativity researcher
- Tony Veale — conceptual blending, language and creativity
- Geraint Wiggins — music cognition and computational creativity

## Topics
- Computational aesthetics
- Generative systems (music, art, narrative)
- Creative AI agents
- Evaluation of creative systems
- Constraint-based creativity
