# NTT InterCommunication Center [ICC]

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research.

Art/technology center in Tokyo, run by [NTT](https://www.ntt.co.jp/). Located in the Tokyo Opera City Tower, Shinjuku. One of Japan's premier institutions for art at the intersection of technology and communication. Has a permanent collection and presents exhibitions, performances, and artist talks. Commissions significant new works.

The *Sensing Streams* collaboration between [[Daito Manabe]] and Ryuichi Sakamoto was presented here. The *Generative MV* (2023, Manabe + Kyle McDonald, tribute to Sakamoto) was also shown here. Artist talk between Manabe and Toshio Iwai (2024) held here.

Distinct from YCAM in being Tokyo-based and NTT-funded (telecommunications giant), giving it resources to commission technically ambitious work.

## Key People / Projects
- [[Daito Manabe]] — frequent exhibitor
- *Sensing Streams* (Manabe + Ryuichi Sakamoto, 2014)
- *Generative MV* (Manabe + Kyle McDonald, 2023) — tribute to Ryuichi Sakamoto
- Toshio Iwai — pioneering Japanese media artist, frequent collaborator

## Why Interesting
The NTT connection means serious technical infrastructure and funding for ambitious commissions. The Tokyo location makes it Japan's most accessible major art-tech venue. Gateway to the serious Japanese media art institutional scene.
