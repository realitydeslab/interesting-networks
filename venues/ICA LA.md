# ICA LA (Institute of Contemporary Art, Los Angeles)

Small, nimble contemporary art institute in Los Angeles. Focused on experimental, emerging, and politically engaged practice. Run by Anne Ellegood (former Hammer Museum senior curator who organized *Made in L.A.* 2012 and 2018). Often described as LA's most adventurous non-collecting contemporary art institution. Punches above its weight — tight curatorial vision, strong artist-first ethos.
