# 4S Conference

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during EASST deep research. Joint 4S+EASST 2024 was Amsterdam — record attendance.

Annual meeting of the **Society for Social Studies of Science** (4S) — the global umbrella organization for STS. North American-centered but globally attended. Every four years, holds a joint conference with [[EASST Conference]].

## Mission
The Society for Social Studies of Science (4S) was founded in 1975. Annual conferences typically held in North America or Europe. Publishes *Science, Technology, and Human Values* (STHV) with Sage.

## Next Edition
- 4S 2025 likely in North America (check [4sonline.org](https://www.4sonline.org/) for exact location/dates)
- Next joint 4S+EASST: likely 2028 or 2029

## Key Facts
- Joint 4S+EASST meetings: 1992, 1996, 2000, 2004, 2008, 2012, 2016, 2020/21, 2024 (Amsterdam) — roughly every 4 years
- Making & Doing program: speculative/practice-based work — same format as EASST
- Size: ~1500-2000+ at annual meetings; joint meetings larger
