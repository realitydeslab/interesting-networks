Hugo, World Fantasy, and British Fantasy Award-winning English-language science fiction and fantasy magazine. Founded October 2006 by Neil Clarke (Wyrm Publishing). Published monthly; 6–8 original fiction pieces per issue plus interviews and articles. Consistently at the pinnacle of the short fiction field — the most award-recognized SF magazine of the 2010s–2020s. The primary English-language venue for translated Chinese science fiction. ISSN 1937-7843.

## Mission & Editorial Model

Monthly publication (since Oct 2006). All fiction published online for free AND in print/ebook formats. Revenue model: subscription (Patreon, B&N, Weightless Books, Amazon) + ebook sales + print anthology sales.

**Editorial philosophy:** Rigor in both language and idea. SF need not be "hard" but must be thoughtful. Fantasy "can be folkloric, contemporary, surreal." Strong preference for work that functions equally in text and audio formats (every story gets a podcast). Explicitly pro-translation: "Translations are welcome and encouraged, even if the work has been previously published in another language."

**Anti-AI policy:** Will not consider any submissions translated, written, or assisted by AI tools. Attempting such submissions may result in permanent ban. Work will not be used to train AI tools.

**Rights model:** First world electronic + first print rights + non-exclusive anthology rights (annual *Clarkesworld Year* anthology). All other rights retained by author. English language only unless specifically stated.

## Submission Guidelines (How Amber/Collaborators Can Submit)

### Fiction Submissions
- **Word limit:** 1,000–22,000 words (no exceptions)
- **Pay rate:** 14¢ per word
- **Genres:** Science fiction and fantasy. No horror but dark SF/F permitted.
- **Language:** English. **Translations explicitly welcome and encouraged**, even if previously published in another language.
- **Simultaneous submissions:** NOT permitted
- **AI tools:** Strictly prohibited — will result in ban
- **System:** Online submissions tracker at [clarkesworldmagazine.com/submissions_tracker/](https://clarkesworldmagazine.com/submissions_tracker/)
- **Format:** Standard manuscript format (.RTF or .DOCX)
- **Response time:** Typically under 2 days (!)
- **Status:** Currently OPEN for fiction submissions

### Non-Fiction Submissions
- **Word limit:** Up to 2,500 words
- **Pay rate:** 10¢ per word
- **What they want:** "Thoughtful, in-depth, well-written" pieces on SF/F industry, writing process, reading experience, scientific background useful for SF
- **Explicitly NOT interested in:** Reviews, literary criticism, reprints, interviews (those are commissioned)
- **Process:** Query only (not completed articles). Subject line: `NONFICTION QUERY: [title]`. Email: nonfiction@clarkesworldmagazine.com
- **Response:** Only respond to queries they wish to follow up on
- **Status:** Currently OPEN for non-fiction queries

### Art / Cover Submissions
- **Format:** Digital/traditional 2D paintings and illustrations only. No photography, photomanipulations, or AI/AI-assisted work.
- **Pay:** $600 + 2 print copies
- **Process:** Send URL of online portfolio to art@clarkesworldmagazine.com (no image attachments)
- **Rights:** Restricted to cover use + marketing for that issue
