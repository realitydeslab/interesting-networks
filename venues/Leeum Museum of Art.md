# Leeum Museum of Art

Private art museum in Seoul, South Korea, operated by the Samsung Foundation of Culture. One of South Korea's most prestigious art institutions, with a permanent collection spanning Korean traditional art and international contemporary art. Building designed by Mario Botta, Jean Nouvel, and Rem Koolhaas.

## Why Interesting
Leeum is the flagship of Samsung's cultural patronage. Shows at Leeum are a major signal of institutional credibility in the East Asian contemporary art circuit.
