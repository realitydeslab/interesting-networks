# Institute of Network Cultures (INC)

Amsterdam-based research institute and publisher. Founded and driven by Geert Lovink. Publishes the **Theory on Demand** and **Network Notebooks** series (free PDFs). Organizes events around critical internet culture, social media, platform politics.

## Why Interesting
INC is a key node for critical media and internet theory in Europe. Published Rosa Menkman's *Glitch Moment/um* (Network Notebook #04, 2011) — her foundational text. Other Network Notebooks have covered topics like unlike us, unlike Twitter, etc.

Lovink is the intellectual center — his work on "organized networks," platform critique, and the politics of internet infrastructure is essential context for Amber's interest in protocol theory and decentralized systems.
