# Gwangju Biennale

One of Asia's oldest and most important contemporary art biennials, held in Gwangju, South Korea. Founded 1995. Runs every two years (odd years). The 15th edition (2024) was titled *Pansori: A Soundscape of the 21st Century*. Known for bringing together artists across the Global South and East Asia with European and American art scenes. Strong track record with politically engaged, experimental, and media-based art. Associated with the memory of the 1980 Gwangju Uprising — democratic resistance is embedded in the institution's DNA.

**15th Gwangju Biennale (2024):** Participating artists included [[Angela Bulloch]] (with her AR app AB_AR), and numerous international artists. Runs September–December 2024, across galleries 1–5 and surrounding Yangnim neighborhood.
