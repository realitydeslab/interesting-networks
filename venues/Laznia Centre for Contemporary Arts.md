# Laznia Centre for Contemporary Arts

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Ionat Zurr deep research.

Contemporary art centre in Gdansk, Poland. Hosted the **Crude Life: TC&A Retrospective** (2012) — first full retrospective of [[Ionat Zurr]] and [[Oron Catts]]'s Tissue Culture & Art Project, with an accompanying catalogue. An important venue in the Eastern European media/contemporary art circuit.

## Why Interesting
