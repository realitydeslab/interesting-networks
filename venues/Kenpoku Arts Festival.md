# Kenpoku Arts Festival

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Ionat Zurr deep research.

Japanese outdoor arts festival (Ibaraki Prefecture). Commissioned [[Ionat Zurr]] and [[Oron Catts]]'s **Compostcubator** (2016) — a massive compost structure using bacterial decomposition heat as an incubator for living cells. A notable instance of TC&A working in site-specific Japanese festival context.

## Why Interesting
Japanese outdoors festival circuit; TC&A presence there in 2016. Connects to the broader Japanese art-festival infrastructure (Setouchi, Echigo-Tsumari, etc.) where international artists create ambitious commissioned works.
