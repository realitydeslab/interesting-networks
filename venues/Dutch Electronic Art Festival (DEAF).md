# Dutch Electronic Art Festival (DEAF)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during V2_Lab deep research. Last edition: 2014.

Biannual festival organized by [[V2_Lab for the Unstable Media]] in Rotterdam, showcasing crossovers between art, technology and society. Evolved from the Manifestation for the Unstable Media (1987-1993). Ran from 1994 to 2014 (10 editions), consistently featuring major figures in international media art. Status unclear after 2014 — no new edition announced as of 2026.

## Themes (Selected Editions)
- 1994: Generated Nature
- 1998: The Art of the Accident
- 2000: Machine Times
- 2004: Affective Turbulence
- 2007: Interact or Die!
- 2012: The Power of Things
- 2014: The Progress Trap

## Why Interesting
One of the foundational European media art festivals. If/when revived, would be a significant venue for Amber's work. Track V2_ for announcements.
