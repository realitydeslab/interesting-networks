# Aarhus Conference (Decennial)

The decennial Aarhus conference — one of the most important critical computing venues. Held every 10 years since 1975. ACM-affiliated, archival proceedings.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **Aarhus 2025: "Computing (X) Crisis"** — the 6th decennial edition
- Themes: computing in/with/after crisis, climate, inequality, democracy, more-than-human flourishing

## Quick Facts
- **History**: 1975, 1985, 1995 (computing in working life/democracy) → 2005, 2015 (critical alternatives) → 2025 (crisis)
- **Tradition**: Scandinavian participatory design roots, critical computing, democratic values
- **Proceedings**: ACM Digital Library (archival for papers/critiques)
- **Tracks**: Papers, critiques, work-in-progress, demos, workshops, doctoral consortium
