# ISEA (International Symposium on Electronic Art)

Annual international symposium on electronic art. Travels to different cities each year. Since 1988.

## Format
- Academic papers, artist talks, exhibitions
- Workshops and performances
- Different host city each year

## Connections
- [[Ars Electronica]] — peer event
- [[transmediale]] — peer event
- SIGGRAPH — adjacent (ISEA is more critical/artistic)

## Why Interesting
Major venue for electronic art discourse. Traveling format means different cultural contexts each year. More academic than Ars Electronica, more artistic than SIGGRAPH. Good for finding new people at the art-science intersection.
