# FAccT (ACM Conference on Fairness, Accountability, and Transparency)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Kate Crawford deep research. ACM conference spun out of FATE (co-founded by Kate Crawford and collaborators at Microsoft Research).

Annual ACM conference on fairness, accountability, and transparency in sociotechnical systems. The primary academic venue for critical AI studies and algorithmic accountability research.

## Key People
- Co-founders/originators from **FATE** (Fairness, Accountability, Transparency and Ethics in AI) at Microsoft Research, including [[Kate Crawford]]
- Broad community including researchers in CS, social science, law, humanities

## Topics
- Algorithmic fairness
- Accountability in ML systems
- Transparency and explainability
- AI governance
- Social impacts of AI

The dominant academic venue for critical AI research as a discipline.
