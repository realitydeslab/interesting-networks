## HRI 2026 — Papers Relevant to Amber

### 🔥 Core Relevance (Agent Ethology / Trust / Machine Death)

1. **"Disambiguating Anthropomorphism and Anthropomimesis in Human-Robot Interaction"** — Minja Axelsson & Henry Shevlin. Conceptual: are we studying what agents *are* or what they *pretend to be*?

2. **"Robots in the Room: Democratic Agency and AI-Augmented Deliberations"** — Damith Herath, Hans Asenbaum et al. Robots in democratic processes — decentralized agency, AI governance.

3. **"'Please Don't Hate Me': Robot Anxiety Elicits Empathy and Compassion"** — Minh Duc Nguyen & Daniel J. Rea. Emotional dimension of machine death — if we empathize with anxious robots, what happens when they die?

4. **"Who's the Boss? Attributions of Control Influence Blame toward Robot Decision-Makers"** — Hanna Parikh et al. Blame attribution in autonomous agent decisions.

5. **"When Robot Overrides Us: The Impact of Autonomous Decisions on User Trust"** — Xiaoyu Chang et al. Trust when agents override humans.

6. **"Investigating Cross-Agent Trust Transfer"** — Xiaoxuan Hei et al. Does trust transfer between different agents? Multi-agent trust networks.

7. **"Beyond Justification: Command Rejections That Include Constructive Elaborations"** — Gordon Briggs & Christina Wasylyshyn. Protocol design for agent refusal — corrigibility/alignment.

8. **"Anticipating Safety and Security Risks of Embodied AI"** — Hong Wang, Katie Winkle & Ginevra Castellano. Risk framework for foundation-model-powered embodied agents.

### 🧠 Conceptually Adjacent

9. **"Making Memories Together: Transparent and Co-constructed Robot Memory"** — Elena Malnatsky et al. Shared memory — if memory is co-constructed, what dies when the robot dies?

10. **"'It Almost Wanted to Hurt Someone': The Impact of Intentional Creepiness"** — Jessica Turner et al. Horror aesthetics in robots, adjacent to Terrified Agents.

11. **"Giving Robots a Hard Time: Robot Politeness and Social Norms for Robot Bullying"** — Johannes Kraus et al. Machine dignity, care ethics.

### 🎨 Design / More-than-Human

12. **"The Nudging Machine: Expressive Touch-Based Movement for Abstract Robots"** — Nataliia Kaminskaia et al. Non-humanoid, somaesthetic robot interaction.

13. **"MenstaRay: Knitted Soft Wearable Robotic Interface"** — Mingke Wang et al. Somaesthetic design, intimate care-oriented interaction.

14. **"Learning from Human-Dog Collaboration"** — Katharina Lisa Kleiser et al. Ethology → robotics pipeline. Studying human-animal collaboration to design human-robot collaboration.

15. **"Co-Blauw: Human-Robot Co-creation for Ceramic Tile Painting"** — Chalmers Ong et al. Agent as creative collaborator.

## For Amber

HRI is a natural venue for agent ethology work — the community already studies trust, blame, empathy, and social norms with embodied agents. The gap: HRI focuses on human-robot dyads; Amber's agent ethology extends to multi-agent ecologies, non-embodied agents (LLMs, DAOs), and machine death. A position paper bridging ethological methods to HRI would be well-received.
