# Hammer Museum

Contemporary and modern art museum affiliated with UCLA, located in Westwood, Los Angeles. Free admission. Known for adventurous contemporary programming, international biennials, and a strong civic/social justice focus. Runs **"Made in L.A."** — the biennial showcasing LA-based artists (launched 2012). Important institution for understanding the LA contemporary art scene.

## "Made in L.A." Biennial
- Showcases artists based in Los Angeles; typically very good at identifying emerging + mid-career practitioners before they become globally known
- Previous editions have featured many artists who went on to international recognition
