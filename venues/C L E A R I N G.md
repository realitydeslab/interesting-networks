# C L E A R I N G

Contemporary art gallery with spaces in New York and Brussels (also Los Angeles). Founded by Olivier Antoine and Nathalie Karg. Specializes in large-scale installation, video, and sculpture — artists working at the intersection of politics, materiality, and digital culture. One of the key bridges between the New York and European contemporary art scenes.

## Key Artists

- [[Meriem Bennani]] — primary gallery
- Also represents other video/installation artists

## Why Interesting

A primary infrastructure node for artists like Bennani working in ambitious installation and video. Their Brussels location means genuine European reach, not just a transatlantic branch. Finding interesting artists here before they hit major institutions is a reliable signal.
