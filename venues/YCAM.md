# YCAM — Yamaguchi Center for Arts and Media

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created via Dumb Type deep research.

Major Japanese media art institution in Yamaguchi City, founded 2003. Commissions, produces, and archives experimental media art. Long-term home for significant Dumb Type works (*Voyage* 2004, *Voyages* installation 2004) and Ryuichi Sakamoto + Shiro Takatani collaborations (*LIFE - fluid, invisible, inaudible...* 2007, *LIFE-WELL* 2013, *water state 1* 2013). Also commissioned Fujiko Nakaya fog installations.

Parallel to [[NTT ICC]] as the key Japanese institutional infrastructure for serious media art — but more production-oriented and willing to commission large-scale immersive works.

## Why Interesting
Key venue in the Japanese media art circuit. Amber should know YCAM if planning Japan engagements or seeking institutional partnerships for embodied/computational performance work.
