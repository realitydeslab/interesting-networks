# The Shed

Major NYC cultural center (Hudson Yards) designed for artistic experimentation at the intersection of art, technology, and performance. Opened 2019. Moveable building shell (the "Shed") expands to cover outdoor plaza. Partnered with [[Acute Art]] on *The Looking Glass* (2021) — 17 AR artworks across the plaza and High Line. Chief Curator Emma Enderby; supported by major NYC philanthropy.

## Key People
- **Emma Enderby** — Chief Curator (co-curated The Looking Glass with [[Daniel Birnbaum]])
- **Alex Poots** — Artistic Director & CEO

## Topics
Multi-disciplinary art, performance, technology, digital art, commissions.

## Next Edition
Ongoing programming. Check [theshed.org](https://theshed.org/) for commissions and open calls.

## Fellowships & Programmes
[Shed Labs](https://theshed.org/programs/shed-labs) — artist development program for early career artists.

## Why Interesting
The Shed is one of the few major NYC institutions actively commissioning digital/AR/VR art as part of public programming (not just digital art in a secondary track). *The Looking Glass* partnership with Acute Art (2021) was exemplary: 17 AR works, free and outdoors, across Hudson Yards and the High Line. For Amber, The Shed is a potential venue for HoloKit-based or XR art projects.
