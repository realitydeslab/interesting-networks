# Le Fresnoy — Studio national des arts contemporains

France's National Studio of Contemporary Arts, located in Tourcoing (near Lille). A unique 2-year post-graduate programme that supports artists working at the intersection of cinema, video art, digital art, performance and installation. Provides professional equipment, production budget, mentorship from leading international artists. Strong focus on expanded cinema and new media.

## For Amber
Le Fresnoy is one of the most distinctive post-graduate environments for art-technology practice in Europe. Unlike UK or US programmes, it's specifically focused on *production* — students make ambitious work with professional support. The alumni network is excellent (Ismailova, Chia-Wei Hsu). Application deadline March 2026 is imminent. **If Amber wants to move into high-production video/installation work, this is the place.**

Worth tracking for:
- Exhibition opportunities (Panorama + touring)
- Potential residency/collaboration
- Visiting artist talks

## Why Interesting
Le Fresnoy consistently produces artists working at the exact intersection Amber cares about — where contemporary art practice meets ecological, spiritual, and political inquiry, using cinema/video/installation as medium. It operates at the cinematic scale that makes institutional ambition possible.
