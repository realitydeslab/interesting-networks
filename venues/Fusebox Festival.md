# Fusebox Festival

Austin-based annual arts festival. Collaborative with **The Contemporary Austin** museum. [[Ecocentric Future Lab]] had a major 2024 presence: ["Host: Fusebox Program with Jiabao Li"](https://thecontemporaryaustin.org/exhibitions/host-fusebox/) — included site-specific activation under Austin's Congress Avenue Bridge (1.5M bat colony), bat expert Merlin Tuttle, 500 attendees, live performance of Nocturnal Fugue, and EchoVision exhibition at The Contemporary Austin.

## Why Interesting
Austin-based festival that takes site-specific performance seriously. The Fusebox/Contemporary Austin partnership gave Jiabao Li's EchoVision project an unusual public event format: starting at real bat colony, ending at museum. Model for art × science × public activation.
