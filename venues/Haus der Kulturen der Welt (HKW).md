# Haus der Kulturen der Welt (HKW)

Berlin's premier institution for contemporary art and culture from a global, non-Western perspective. "House of World Cultures." Government-funded cultural centre operating at the intersection of art, science, and politics — with an unusually rigorous intellectual programme combining exhibitions, residencies, research projects, and publications. Hosted the landmark "Forensis" exhibition (2014) with [[Forensic Architecture]]. Run by the Federal Government Commissioner for Culture. One of the most intellectually serious art institutions in Europe.

## Recent Editions (relevant shows)
- **"Forensis: The Architecture of Public Truth"** — 2014. Major exhibition co-produced with [[Forensic Architecture]] and [[Eyal Weizman]]. First major presentation of forensic architecture as practice.
- **"Anthropocene Project"** — 2013–2014. Multi-year programme on Earth systems, human impact, non-human agency. Related intellectual territory.
- Numerous programmes on global futures, postcolonial science, and speculative politics.
