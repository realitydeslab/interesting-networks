# MUTEK

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research.

International festival of digital creativity and electronic music, founded in Montreal (2000). Now operates in multiple cities: Montreal, Mexico City (MUTEK.MX), Barcelona, Buenos Aires, Dubai, San Francisco, Tokyo (MUTEK.JP). One of the premier global venues for experimental audiovisual performance.

[[Daito Manabe]] performed *Phase Forms* at MUTEK.JP (2025). He also lectured at MUTEK.MX (2019). MUTEK sits alongside [[Sónar Barcelona]], TodaysArt (The Hague), and Dekmantel (Amsterdam) as the core European/global AV performance circuit.

## Why Interesting
The multi-city model means MUTEK is genuinely global, not just European. MUTEK.JP provides a specific access point into the Tokyo experimental performance scene. The festival consistently commissions and showcases technically ambitious audiovisual works.
