## Gallery Context — Tokyo Art Scene
Gallery Ether operates in Nishiazabu — a neighborhood known for upscale galleries and restaurants, distinct from Roppongi's major art institutions (Mori Art Museum, National Art Center, 21_21 Design Sight). It is a **commercial contemporary art gallery**, not an art-tech institution. It does **not** sit in the same ecosystem as:
- [ICC (Inter Communication Center)](https://www.ntticc.or.jp/en/) — NTT's media art institution
- [teamLab](https://www.teamlab.art/) — immersive digital art
- [Mori Art Museum](https://www.mori.art.museum/en/) — contemporary art with strong media art programming
- [Ars Electronica partner events](https://ars.electronica.art/) in Japan

Gallery Ether is closer to the small commercial gallery circuit: interesting for art market purposes, but not a tech-art venue.
