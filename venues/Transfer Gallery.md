# Transfer Gallery

"An experimental space that explores simulation and expanded practice." Founded and directed in New York. Currently building a decentralized **DATA TRUST** — an artist-owned archive and not-for-profit cooperative trust focused on data sovereignty, digital equity, and long-term care of media art.

## Why Interesting
Transfer is one of the few serious galleries in the US that treats digital and media art as primary practice, not decorative technology. Their artists: Rosa Menkman, Morehshin Allahyari, Alan Warburton, Memo Akten, Alfredo Salazar-Caro, Stephanie Dinkins, and ~20 others. The DATA TRUST project is a direct response to institutional fragility for born-digital artworks — decentralized, artist-owned infrastructure for long-term preservation.

Rosa Menkman launched her **i.R.D.** (institutions of Resolution Disputes) at Transfer in 2015. The DCT work won first prize at the Crypto Design Challenge (MOTI Breda, 2015).

The DATA TRUST model is interesting for Amber's interest in decentralized AI and agentive commoning — artists collectively owning the infrastructure for their own cultural preservation.
