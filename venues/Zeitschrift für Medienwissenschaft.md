# Zeitschrift für Medienwissenschaft (ZfM)

The flagship peer-reviewed journal for German-language media studies. Published by transcript Verlag (Bielefeld). The ZfM is the central publication venue for the German *Medienwissenschaft* tradition — including media archaeology, cultural techniques, media philosophy, and digital culture studies.

## Key Facts
- **Publisher:** [transcript Verlag](https://www.transcript-verlag.de/) (Bielefeld)

- **Languages:** Primarily German; some English accepted
- **URL:** [zeitschrift-fuer-medienwissenschaft.de](https://www.zeitschrift-fuer-medienwissenschaft.de/)
- **Acceptance:** German *Medienwissenschaft* community standard; international authors welcome
