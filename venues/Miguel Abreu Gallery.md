# Miguel Abreu Gallery

Contemporary art gallery in New York City that regularly hosts philosophical events and book launches for **[[Sequence Press]]** and the **[[Urbanomic]]** publishing network.

## Locations
- **88 Eldridge Street, NYC** — Main event space
- **36 Orchard Street, NYC** — Additional location

## Key Events & Collaborations
- **Yuk Hui**: *Post-Europe* book launch (April 2025)
- **Omnicide II**: Evening with Jason Bahbak Mohaghegh & Reza Negarestani (January 2024)
- **Alain Badiou**: "The Dialectic in Politics" lecture (November 2019)
- Regular **[[Sequence Press]]** book launches and philosophical events

## Key Communities
- **[[Sequence Press]]** — Regular partner for book events
- **[[Urbanomic]]** network — Hosts events for their publications
- NYC theory-art circuit

## Why Interesting
Rare venue that bridges contemporary art and serious philosophical discourse. Provides crucial infrastructure for the speculative philosophy network in NYC. The gallery's willingness to host complex theoretical events suggests a sophisticated art-theory audience and programming vision.
