# Critical Times: Interventions in Global Critical Theory

Journal published by Cambridge University Press / Duke University Press. Subtitled "Interventions in Global Critical Theory." One of the most intellectually serious new venues for critical theory with explicit commitment to non-Western, Global South perspectives. [[Premesh Lalu]] published "What is the university for?" here (2019).

Focus: critical theory, postcolonial and decolonial thought, continental philosophy in global contexts, political theory. Explicitly positions itself as an intervention in critical theory from multiple global sites — not just Frankfurt School tradition.

## Why Interesting
One of the few journals that is both rigorous (peer-reviewed, Cambridge/Duke quality) and explicitly committed to non-Western critical theory. For work connecting AI/technology theory with postcolonial perspectives, this could be a strong venue.
