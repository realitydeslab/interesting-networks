New York City's primary museum dedicated to contemporary art from emerging and underrepresented artists. Founded 1977 by Marcia Tucker. Located on the Bowery in Manhattan (SANAA-designed building, expanded with OMA extension opening March 2026). Institutional home of **[[Rhizome]]** (digital art platform/preservation). Hosts *The Generational* — a major triennial exhibition series. Runs **NEW INC** — the leading art+technology incubator in NYC.

## Key People
- **Lisa Phillips** — Director ([New Museum](https://www.newmuseum.org/))
- **[[Massimiliano Gioni]]** — Director of International Exhibitions; co-curated *Younger Than Jesus* (2009 Triennial)
- **[[Lauren Cornell]]** — Curator/Adjunct Curator 2007–2017; organized two Triennial editions (*Surround Audience* 2015, *Free* 2010); now at MoMA
- **[[Laura Hoptman]]** — Senior Curator; co-curated *Younger Than Jesus*
- **Rhizome** — Digital preservation org in residence; key for net art, software art, web archiving

## 🔴 MAJOR: New Humans: Memories of the Future (Opening March 21, 2026)
- **URL:** [https://www.newmuseum.org/](https://www.newmuseum.org/) (on homepage)
- **Opening:** March 21, 2026 (free admission weekend March 21–22)
- **Building:** Entire new expanded building (SANAA original + OMA 60,000 sq ft extension)
- **Scale:** 200+ international artists, writers, scientists, architects, filmmakers
- **Theme:** "Technological developments and evolving definitions of 'the human'" — tracing industrialization, mechanized warfare, automation, AI as reshaping body and mind
- **Why Amber:** Perfect alignment — posthumanism, machine behavior, AI and human redefinition. This is the exact territory of Amber's FAccT/CHI papers on agent ethology, dissociative agents, machine death.
- Includes works by: Precious Okoyomon, Daria Martin (archival), and 200+ others
- Described in Artforum as tracing "a diagonal history of 20–21C" through moments of technological rupture

## NEW INC — New Museum Incubator
- **URL:** [https://newinc.submittable.com/](https://newinc.submittable.com/)
- **What:** NYC's leading art+design+technology incubator. 1-year membership. Hybrid (NYC-based but accessible internationally for some programming).
- **Year 13 open call:** JUST CLOSED March 2, 2026 (for Sept 2026 – Aug 2027 cohort)
- **Next open call:** February 2027 for Year 14
- **Benefits:** Mentorship, workspace in New Museum, professional development, community of artists/designers/technologists
- **Commitment:** 4–8 hrs/week; hybrid; most programming Mon–Fri 10am–6pm ET
- **Tracks:** Creative practitioners + organizations at intersection of art, design, technology
- **Values:** Collectivism, experimentation, interdisciplinary collaboration, stewardship, social justice
- **Note:** International members allowed; not required to be NYC-based for all programming
