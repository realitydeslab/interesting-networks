Art and technology center founded 1997. Originally a physical NYC space, now hybrid/itinerant with programs globally and a renewed NYC physical residency (returning 2025/2026). One of the most important critical art+tech institutions in the US. Focus on artists who work with technology from a critical, socially conscious standpoint — questioning power structures, surveillance, technofeudalism, and collective futures. Flagship program: residency and fellowships.

## 2025/2026 Season: Speculating on Plurality
- **URL:** [https://eyebeam.org/program/speculating-plurality/](https://eyebeam.org/program/speculating-plurality/)
- **Announced:** October 21, 2025
- **Theme:** Artists, technologists, and writers addressing:
  - Shared realities vs. parallel realities in a hyperconnected world
  - Technofeudalism and state surveillance
  - Technology's impulse toward sameness vs. pluralistic commons
  - Digital fugitivity and climate
- **Programming includes:**
  - Artist conversations at Secret Riso Club
  - Experimental writing and storytelling
  - **Physical NYC residency** — returning for first time since pandemic onset
- **URGENT INQUIRIES program** — for artists with urgent/immediate concerns around technology and society
