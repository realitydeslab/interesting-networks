# Eyeo Festival

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via [[Kyle McDonald]] deep research.

Annual gathering in Minneapolis, MN for the creative coding and data visualization community. Brought together artists, designers, coders, and data practitioners. The "TED for creative coders." Talks, workshops, parties. **High curatorial standards** — not just any technologist. Known for bridging data visualization, generative art, physical computing, sound art.

Kyle McDonald was a speaker multiple times (*Weird Intelligence* 2016 closing keynote; *How to Give Everything Away* 2012; workshops 2011, 2012). Golan Levin ran workshops there. The festival's speaker alumni constitute a key node map of the creative coding world.

**Status:** On hiatus since approximately 2019. Whether it will return is unclear.

## Key People (speakers/organizers)
- **[[Kyle McDonald]]** — multiple talks and workshops
- **[[Golan Levin]]** — workshops
- **[[Zach Lieberman]]** — likely speaker

## Why Interesting
Even on hiatus, Eyeo's archive of talks (available on Vimeo) is one of the best records of creative coding practice 2011–2019. For Amber: understanding the creative coding community requires knowing Eyeo's alumni network.
