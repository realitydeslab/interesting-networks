# Transitio_MX

Latin America's largest and most prestigious festival of new media art and video, held in Mexico City. Biennial format. **[[Joanna Zylinska]]** was Artistic Director of **Transitio_MX05 'Biomediations'** in 2013, giving the festival an international profile rooted in biopolitics and media philosophy. Part of the Centro Multimedia (National Center for the Arts, Mexico City) ecosystem.

## Why Interesting
Transitio_MX is the premier node connecting European media art theory with Latin American new media practice. For a UK-based researcher, this is the entry point to the Latin American media art circuit. The Biomediations theme (2013) under Zylinska was particularly strong theoretically.

## Key Edition
- **Transitio_MX05 'Biomediations'** (2013) — Artistic Director: [[Joanna Zylinska]]. Theme: biopolitics, mediation, organisms and machines. Signalled the festival's engagement with posthumanism and critical media theory.
