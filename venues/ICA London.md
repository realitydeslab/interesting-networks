# ICA London (Institute of Contemporary Arts)

London's foremost institution for avant-garde and politically engaged contemporary art, film, performance, and digital practice. Founded 1946. Located on The Mall, London. Consistently programmes work at the intersection of art, politics, technology, and culture — including major shows by artists whose practice intersects with Amber's interests.
