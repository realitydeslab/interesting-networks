# Petzel Gallery

Major New York commercial gallery, founded by Friedrich Petzel. Known for rigorous conceptual and critical art. Located in two spaces in Manhattan. Simon Denny's primary New York gallery for over a decade, with major solo shows in 2011, 2013, 2016, 2021, and 2024 (Dungeon).

Roster includes artists at the intersection of critical theory and visual art practice. Known for showing work that engages with technology, politics, and culture seriously.

Denny used Petzel as the launch platform for key ideas: *Corporate Video Decisions* (2011), *All You Need Is Data* (2013), *Blockchain Future States* (2016), *Mine* (2021, including NFT Mine Offsets), *Dungeon* (2024). Also curated group show *Multi-User Dungeon (MUD)* here (2024) featuring Seth Price.

## Key Artists (connected to Denny's orbit)
- [[Simon Denny]] — multiple solo shows
- Seth Price — in MUD group show (2024)

## Why Interesting
If you're approaching the New York commercial art market from a critical-tech angle, Petzel is the right entry point. Friedrich Petzel has excellent critical instincts and the gallery has been consistent about supporting ambitious, intellectually serious work.
