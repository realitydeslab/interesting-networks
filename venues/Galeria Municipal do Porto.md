# Galeria Municipal do Porto

Municipal contemporary art gallery in Porto, Portugal. Hosted **The Shape of a Circle in the Mind of a Fish, Part 5: The Shape of a Circle in the Dream of a Fish** (26–27 November 2022) — the fifth and final Serpentine-era edition of [[Lucia Pietroiusti]]'s interspecies symposia series, co-curated with [[Filipa Ramos]]. The event included live presentations, film screenings, and performances.

## Key Events
- **Fish Part 5** (November 26–27, 2022) — co-hosted with Serpentine General Ecology
