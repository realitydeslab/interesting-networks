# Sónar Barcelona

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research.

Major international festival of advanced music and creative technology, held annually in Barcelona (June). One of the key venues in the European electronic music + art-technology performance circuit. Has an associated professional congress, **Sónar+D** (innovation in music, creativity, and technology).

[[Daito Manabe]] has performed here multiple times including premiering *Phase Forms* in 2025. Nosaj Thing × Daito Manabe toured here (2017). Sónar+D hosted Manabe as keynote speaker (2019). MUTEK, TodaysArt, and Dekmantel form the complementary circuit.

Described Nosaj Thing × Daito Manabe as "extremely captivating, technologically advanced, show of inordinate beauty."

## Why Interesting
One of the few high-profile contexts where experimental audiovisual performance (not gallery installation) is the primary art form. Artists including Daito Manabe, Arca, Ryoji Ikeda, and others bring technically serious AV works here. Essential venue for understanding computational performance art.
