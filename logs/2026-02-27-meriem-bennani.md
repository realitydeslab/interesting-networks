## Reflections

Bennani is a **perfect triple intersection**: computational creativity (3D animation as political medium) + more-than-human (animal characters as epistemological tool) + speculative design (CAPS world as political analysis). Her trajectory from Moroccan immigrant + Cooper Union + ENSAD Paris → Whitney Biennial → viral Instagram → TIFF feature film → BOSS Award is a useful model for how an artist builds genuine institutional legitimacy while maintaining experimental edge.

The most interesting thing: she treats **distribution as curatorial practice**. Instagram (2 Lizards) → Art Dubai → BIM Geneva → Whitney Biennial → TIFF → NYFF → Film Movement. Each venue is chosen for who it reaches, not just prestige. The Tunis and Essaouira shows exist because those audiences matter to her practice — not as "returning to roots" gesture but as genuine institutional commitment.
