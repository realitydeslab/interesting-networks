## Intellectual Assessment

Margolles is ⭐⭐⭐ for several reasons beyond what the stub captured:

1. **Material witness** — her practice is the most radical version of what could be called "material witness": making the bodies of the invisible present through their literal material traces. This is a methodological contribution to art theory, not just a stylistic choice.

2. **Forensic as methodology** — not forensic aesthetics (photographing crime scenes, etc.) but genuinely importing forensic protocols into art. Chain of custody, material specificity, documentation standards.

3. **Political art that doesn't aestheticize** — the central problem of political art is that it tends to aestheticize the very suffering it claims to indict. Margolles avoids this by making the suffering itself the medium. There's no aesthetic distance.

4. **Connection to Amber's mortality thread** — Amber's interest in machine death, machine consciousness, and mortality as a philosophical category is directly illuminated by someone who has spent 35 years making death materially legible.

5. **London/UK presence** — Fourth Plinth visible through late 2026. Tate collection. This is an artist whose work Amber can encounter without traveling.
