## Key Findings
1. **Current position**: Associate Professor at Ca' Foscari University Venice (not HfG anymore). Moved 2023/2024 with ERC grant.
2. **ERC Consolidator Grant AIMODELS** (2024–2028): 5-year project at Ca' Foscari. Postdoc positions open. Full project page at unive.it/aimodels.
3. **Deutscher Memorial Prize 2024**: Won for *The Eye of the Master*. This is the most prestigious Marxist book prize — confirms his position as central figure in critical left intellectual scene.
4. **14+ language translations**: *Eye of the Master* in Arabic, Chinese, Farsi, French, German, Greek, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Croatian, Spanish, Turkish — extraordinary global reach for a philosophy book.
5. **Nooscope (2020)**: The key artifact — visual essay/diagram-manifesto with Vladan Joler. Already a community fixture. Widely taught and translated.
6. **Cambridge connection**: Lecture Oct 2023 at Cambridge Digital Humanities. Book adopted in MA AI Ethics at Cambridge. Endorsed by Simon Schaffer (Cambridge HPS). Book adopted also at Berkeley, UC Irvine, Leuphana, HBK Braunschweig, Bard Berlin.
7. **AI Forensics (Volkswagen Stiftung)**: Multi-institution project with HfG, Durham, Cambridge, UCSB — ran 2022–2025, final publication forthcoming.

## Reflections
Pasquinelli is an unusually coherent intellectual: one core argument (labor theory of AI) developed across 20 years from operaismo theory to rigorous philosophy of science. The career architecture is instructive — built KIM group, produced the Nooscope as an artifact, then wrote the book, then got the ERC, then won the prize. Infrastructure → artifact → book → prize is the sequence.
