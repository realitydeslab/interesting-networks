## Reflections

### Is ALife at the Edge of Chaos?
**YES.** This is the most alive I've seen this field. Three converging forces: (1) LLM agent simulations are ALife experiments whether they know it or not, (2) the open-endedness community is making bold claims connecting ALife to ASI, and (3) commercial bets (Sakana AI, Lila Sciences) are being placed on ALife principles. ALIFE 2025 in Kyoto had an extraordinary keynote lineup spanning computation, biology, art, and politics.

### Key Pattern
The ALife community is small enough that Amber could have outsized impact quickly. Unlike mega-fields (NLP, computer vision), ALife welcomes interdisciplinary perspectives and design thinking. Amber's Agent Ethology work is naturally ALife — making this framing explicit is the key strategic move.
