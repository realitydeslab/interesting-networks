### 3. Trajectory: Empirical → Framework → Manifesto
Her four-book arc is strategically coherent: stem cells (empirical credibility) → New Jim Code (framework) → Viral Justice (theory of change) → Imagination: A Manifesto (philosophical intervention). ### 5. ICLR 2020 Keynote — The Crossover

### 6. She Does NOT Move in Art-Tech Festival Circuit
