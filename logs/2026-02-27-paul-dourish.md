### The Intellectual Arc
Dourish's career has a clear three-act structure:
1. **CSCW/awareness** (EuroPARC/PARC era, 1989-1999) — technical roots, groupware
2. **Phenomenological HCI** (UCI, 2001-2009) — *Where the Action Is*, embodied interaction, ubicomp critique

Each transition was a genuine paradigm shift, not just a topic change.

Both CSCW Lasting Impact Awards (1992, 1996) came from the EuroPARC era. The papers that win aren't always the most cited at time of publication — they're the ones that establish vocabulary and frameworks that a community uses for decades. This is a long-game strategy.
