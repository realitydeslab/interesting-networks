### The OOO Community Map
Core figures:
- **Harman** — withdrawal ontology; objects exceed all relations
- **Levi Bryant** — "onticology"; virtual/actual objects (later: machine ontology); coined the term OOO
- **Timothy Morton** — hyperobjects; ecological OOO; now also at SCI-Arc
- **Ian Bogost** — alien phenomenology; "carpentry"; media/game studies
- These four + Ray Brassier + Quentin Meillassoux + Iain Hamilton Grant = the Speculative Realism universe

### The New Metaphysics Series
Co-edited with Bruno Latour (deceased). Titles: Bryant's *Democracy of Objects*, Morton's *Realist Magic* (×2 editions), Bogost's *Alien Phenomenology*, Dolphijn/van der Tuin's *New Materialism*, Sparrow's *Plastic Bodies*, Leach's *Machine Sensation*, Debaise's *La naturaleza como acontecimiento*, Bensusan's *Being Up For Grabs*, Yoran's *The Interfact*, Roderick's *The Being of Analogy*, Shaviro's *Sin Criterios*. All open access.

### SCI-Arc Architecture Connection
**Both Harman and Timothy Morton are now at SCI-Arc** — two major OOO figures at the same architecture school. *Architecture and Objects* (Minnesota UP, 2022) is the key text. Architects who explicitly engage OOO: Mark Foster Gage, Tom Wiscombe. This is an unusually dense philosophy-architecture intersection.

### The Cairo Isolation Thesis
Harman spent 16 years (2000–2016) at the American University in Cairo — intellectually isolated from the Western mainstream. This isolation is key to understanding OOO's development: he wrote without institutional pressure to conform. The parallel with Amber's potentially non-standard institutional positions is worth noting.

## Reflections

### Why This Matters for Amber
1. **OOO is the background theoretical radiation in architecture and art-tech spaces** — you encounter it everywhere without necessarily knowing the source. Understanding it properly lets Amber position her work relative to it.

2. **The SCI-Arc model** — a philosopher as the intellectual anchor of a design school — is directly relevant to Amber's trajectory. If you want practitioners to engage with your philosophy, being at a practice school beats being at a philosophy department.

3. **The open-access strategy** (New Metaphysics series, re.press, punctum) turbocharged OOO's reach beyond academia. This is worth studying as a publishing strategy.

4. **The blog-as-infrastructure model** — Harman's 20-year blog built his audience before (and between) books. Short daily posts > long silence.

### The Debate with Brassier
The Harman-Brassier split (OOO vs. transcendental nihilism) is the central fault line in Speculative Realism. Brassier's dismissal ("pan-psychist metaphysics") vs. Harman's accessibility. This debate is productively unresolved: both are serious positions.

### The Wolfendale Challenge
Peter Wolfendale's *Object-Oriented Philosophy: The Noumenon's New Clothes* (Urbanomic, 2014) is the most rigorous internal critique. Reading it alongside Harman's *Skirmishes* response gives the best picture of OOO's actual philosophical problems.
