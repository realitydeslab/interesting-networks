## Key Findings

### Editorial Structure
TCS is run from Goldsmiths, University of London by a tight editorial team:
- Mike Featherstone (Editor-in-Chief, 40+ year tenure) — rare founding-editor persistence
- Sunil Manghani (Managing Editor, Southampton/WSA) — interestingly has Alan Turing Institute AI for Arts affiliation
- Rainer Winter (Managing Editor, Klagenfurt + honorary Beijing) — Austrian/Chinese bridge figure
- Ryan Bishop (Book Reviews) — co-leads Archaeologies of Media and Technology with Jussi Parikka at WSA

Editorial board includes: Tiziana Terranova, Achille Mbembe, Stuart Elden, Lisa Blackman, Adrian Mackenzie, David Beer, Tim Ingold — a who's who of critical theory/media studies.

Amber's work at the intersection of Chinese cosmotechnics, computational systems, and design theory is exactly the kind of "borderline" work TCS champions.
