# Speculative Realism

Stub — needs deep research.

Contemporary Continental-inspired philosophical movement defined by resistance to "correlationism" (the idea that we can only access the correlation between thinking and being, not either term independently). Named after a 2007 conference at Goldsmiths College (London). Four founding thinkers: Graham Harman (OOO), Quentin Meillassoux (speculative materialism), Ray Brassier (nihilist realism), Iain Hamilton Grant (Schellingian naturphilosophie). [[Object-Oriented Ontology]] is its most influential branch.
