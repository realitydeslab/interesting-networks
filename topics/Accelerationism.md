# Accelerationism

Philosophical framework arguing that the processes of capitalism/technology should be accelerated rather than resisted — but branches diverge wildly.

## Lineage
- **[[Nick Land]]** — originator, CCRU (Cybernetic Culture Research Unit) at Warwick in the 90s with Sadie Plant. Unconditional accelerationism (u/acc). Dark enlightenment.
- **Left accelerationism (l/acc)** — Alex Williams & Nick Srnicek (*Inventing the Future*). Reclaim technology for emancipation.
- **[[Effective Accelerationism]]** (e/acc) — Silicon Valley mutation. Pro-growth, pro-tech, anti-regulation. Marc Andreessen, Beff Jezos. Very different from Land's original but genealogically linked.

## Key Texts
- Nick Land — *Fanged Noumena*
- CCRU — *Writings 1997–2003*
- Alex Williams & Nick Srnicek — *Inventing the Future*
- [[Benjamin Bratton]] — *The Stack* (adjacent, not accelerationist per se but in dialogue)

## Connections
- [[Nick Land]] → [[Anna Greenspan]] (married) → [[NYU Shanghai IMA]] → [[Bogna Konior]]
- [[Effective Accelerationism]] → [[LessWrong]] community

## Why It Matters
Love it or hate it, accelerationism shaped how we think about technology and politics. The genealogy from Land/CCRU through Bratton to e/acc to LessWrong maps a crucial intellectual network.
