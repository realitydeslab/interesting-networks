# Active Inference

Framework from Karl Friston's free energy principle. All living systems minimize surprise by either updating their beliefs or acting on the world.

## Key Ideas
- Free energy principle — organisms minimize variational free energy
- Active inference — action and perception unified
- Markov blankets — boundaries of self
- Applies to biological organisms, AI systems, social systems

## Key People
- Karl Friston — originator
- Michael Levin — morphogenetic active inference
- Maxwell Ramstead — social active inference
- Daniel Friedman — Active Inference Institute
