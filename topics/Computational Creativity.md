# Computational Creativity

The study and simulation of creative behavior in computers. When is a machine creative? Can creativity be computed?

## Key Venues
- ICCC (International Conference on Computational Creativity)
- C&C (Creativity and Cognition)
- [[ALIFE Conference]]

## Key People
- Margaret Boden — foundational theorist (*The Creative Mind*)
- Simon Colton — computational creativity pioneer
- [[Ken Stanley]] — novelty search as creative search

## Related
- [[Open-Endedness]] — creativity as open-ended search
- [[Artificial Life]] — emergent creativity
- Generative AI — current explosion

## Why Interesting
The generative AI explosion makes this field suddenly central. But the deep questions — what is creativity? can machines be creative? — predate DALL-E by decades.
