# Sinofuturism

Speculative concept exploring Chinese technological futures. Coined by [[Steve Goodman]] (Kode9) as CCRU member, later developed by others.

## Key Works
- Lawrence Lek, *Sinofuturism (1839–2046 AD)* (2016) — video essay
- [[Yuk Hui]], *The Question Concerning Technology in China* (2016)
- [[Chen Qiufan]] — Chinese sci-fi as sinofuturist practice
- [[Cao Fei]] — artistic sinofuturism (RMB City)
- [[Bogna Konior]] & [[Benjamin Bratton]], *Machine Decision is Not Final* (2025)

## Why Interesting
China's relationship to technology cannot be understood through Western frameworks alone. Sinofuturism — whether as art, philosophy, or fiction — creates space for thinking about technology from Chinese perspectives. Directly relevant to Amber's position between China and the West.
