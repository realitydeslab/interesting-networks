# More-than-human Design

Design that decenters the human. Considers nonhuman agencies — animals, plants, machines, ecosystems, materials — as stakeholders and participants in design.

## Key Texts
- [[Ron Wakkary]], *Things We Could Design* (2021)
- [[Elisa Giaccardi]] — connected objects, more-than-human participation
- Rosen, Salovaara, Botero, Søndergaard (eds.), *More-Than-Human Design in Practice* (2024)
