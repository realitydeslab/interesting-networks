# More-than-human Design

Design that decenters the human. Things, animals, ecosystems, algorithms as stakeholders, participants, even designers.

## Why Edge of Chaos
Rapidly expanding from a niche DIS/DRS concern into a cross-disciplinary movement. Now touching ALife, ecology, policy, AI ethics, indigenous knowledge systems. The boundaries of "who designs" and "who is designed for" are being rewritten in real time.
