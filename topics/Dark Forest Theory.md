# Dark Forest Theory

Originally from Liu Cixin's *The Three-Body Problem* — civilizations hide because any visible civilization gets destroyed. Applied to the internet by [[Bogna Konior]] and Yancey Strickler.

## Key Texts
- [[Bogna Konior]], *Dark Forest Theory of the Internet* (Polity, 2025)
- Yancey Strickler, "The Dark Forest Theory of the Internet" (2019 blog post)
- Liu Cixin, *The Dark Forest* (2008)

## Why Interesting
Powerful metaphor for internet culture — people retreating to private channels, encrypted messages, small communities. Konior's book-length treatment elevates it from metaphor to theory. Connects to Amber's interest in protocol studies and dark patterns of digital sociality.
