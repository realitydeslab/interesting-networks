# Computational Creativity

The study and simulation of creative behavior in computers. When is a machine genuinely creative vs. just generative?

## Key Ideas
- Creativity as computation (Margaret Boden's taxonomy: combinational, exploratory, transformational)
- Generative art, algorithmic composition
- LLMs and creativity — are they creative?
- Co-creativity — human-AI collaboration

## Key People
- Margaret Boden — foundational theorist
- [[Ken Stanley]] — novelty search as creativity
- [[David Ha]] — creative AI systems
- Simon Colton — The Painting Fool
- [[Lauren Lee McCarthy]] — art as creative computation
