# Open-endedness

The study of systems that continually produce novelty — never settling, always generating new forms. Key problem in ALife: how does nature keep inventing?

## Key Ideas
- Why does evolution keep producing new things?
- Can we build artificial systems that are truly open-ended?
- Novelty search — searching for novelty rather than objectives ([[Ken Stanley]])
- Quality-diversity algorithms
- MAP-Elites
