# Open-Endedness

The study of systems that continuously produce novelty without converging to a fixed point. Biology is the paradigmatic open-ended system. Key question: can we build artificial systems that are genuinely open-ended?

## Key Texts
- [[Ken Stanley]] & [[Joel Lehman]], *Why Greatness Cannot Be Planned* (2015)
- Tim Taylor et al., "Open-Ended Evolution: Perspectives from the OEE Workshop" (2016)
- Banzhaf et al., "Defining and Simulating Open-Ended Novelty" (2016)

## Why Interesting
The deepest question in ALife: can we make systems that never stop being surprising? Connects to creativity, evolution, and the nature of innovation. Ken Stanley's insight that objectives can be counterproductive is paradigm-shifting.
