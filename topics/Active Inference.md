# Active Inference

Framework from Karl Friston's Free Energy Principle. Organisms minimize surprise by either updating beliefs or acting on the world. Unifies perception, action, and learning under one principle.

## Why Interesting
Potentially the most ambitious unified theory of mind, life, and agency. If it works, it explains everything from bacteria to consciousness. Active Inference Institute is building a community around applying it to AI. Edge of chaos — fast-evolving, contested, productive.
