# Effective Accelerationism (e/acc)

Silicon Valley techno-optimism movement. Pro-growth, pro-entropy, pro-building. Descended genealogically from [[Accelerationism]] but mutated significantly.

## Key People
- Guillaume Verdon (Beff Jezos) — physicist, founder
- Marc Andreessen — amplifier

## Genealogy
- [[Nick Land]] / CCRU → philosophical accelerationism
- → mutates through Silicon Valley into e/acc
- → cultivates community overlap with [[LessWrong]], rationalist movement

## Adjacent
- [[LessWrong]] — rationalist community, AI safety discourse
- EA (Effective Altruism) — in tension with e/acc
- [[Accelerationism]] — philosophical ancestor

## Why Interesting
Maps power dynamics of who's shaping AI discourse. The e/acc ↔ EA/safety tension is one of the defining fault lines in AI politics right now.
