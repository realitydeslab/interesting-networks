# Machine Behavior

Studying AI systems the way we study animals — observationally, behaviorally, ecologically. Proposed by Iyad Rahwan et al. in Nature (2019).

## Key Ideas
- Machines as agents in an ecology
- Behavioral observation over mechanistic explanation
- Machine societies, machine cultures
- AI in the wild vs. in the lab
