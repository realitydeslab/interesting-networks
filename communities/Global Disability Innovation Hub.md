# Global Disability Innovation Hub (GDI Hub)

World's first assistive technology (AT) research hub, co-located at UCL and Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park (East London). WHO Collaborating Centre for Assistive Technology — the only institution with this formal WHO designation. Research, policy, and practice on AT access, with particular focus on low/middle-income countries. Academic leadership: [[Nadia Bianchi-Berthouze]] and [[Catherine Holloway]] at [[UCL Interaction Centre]].
