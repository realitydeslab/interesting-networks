# Research Network for Philosophy and Technology

International research network founded and convened by [[Yuk Hui]] since 2014. Dedicated to philosophical and historical study of technology and technosciences. Publishes the open-access journal [*Technophany*](https://technophany.philosophyandtechnology.network/) (Radboud University Press, DOAJ-indexed).

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial note created during Yuk Hui deep research.

## Mission & Objectives
Transdisciplinary reflections on philosophy and history of technology. The institutional base for the **cosmotechnics** discourse and broader continental philosophy of technology.

## Key People
- **[[Yuk Hui]]** — founder and convenor since 2014

## Key Publications & Output
- [*Technophany — A Journal for Philosophy and Technology*](https://technophany.philosophyandtechnology.network/) — open-access, peer-reviewed. Publishes on posthuman perception, cybernetics, AI, aesthetics of technology. CFP for 2026 currently open.
