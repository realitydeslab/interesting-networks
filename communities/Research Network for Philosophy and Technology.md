# Research Network for Philosophy and Technology

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created during cosmotechnics deep research. Key network convened by Yuk Hui.

Academic research network dedicated to philosophical and historical studies of technologies and technosciences. Founded and convened by [[Yuk Hui]] since 2014.

## Mission & Objectives
Creates transdisciplinary space for philosophical reflections on technology. Facilitates original reflections and provocations on philosophy and history of technology. Aims to reshape contemporary landscape of technological thought.

## Key People
- **[[Yuk Hui]]** — convenor since 2014, central figure
- Network of continental philosophy of technology scholars worldwide
