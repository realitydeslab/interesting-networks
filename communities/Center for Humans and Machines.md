# Center for Humans & Machines (CHM)

Research center at the [Max Planck Institute for Human Development](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/en) in Berlin, founded and directed by [[Iyad Rahwan]]. The institutional home of the **Machine Behaviour** field. Conducts interdisciplinary science to understand, anticipate, and shape disruptions from AI and digital media.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial deep research.
- **2025:** Two Nature papers: "Science Fiction Science Method" and "Delegation to AI Can Increase Dishonest Behaviour."

## Mission & Objectives
Understand how AI and digitalization impact the way humans think, learn, work, play, cooperate, and govern. Three guiding concepts:
1. **[Machine Behaviour](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/chm/guiding-concepts/concept-1-machine-behavior)** — studying AI behaviourally using concepts from across behavioral sciences
2. **[Science Fiction Science](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/chm/guiding-concepts/concept-2-science-fiction-science)** — anticipating future technology impacts by combining imagination with empirical study
3. **[Machine Culture](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/chm/guiding-concepts/concept-3-machineculture)** — studying how humans and AI jointly build cultural artefacts and institutions
