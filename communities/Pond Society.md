# Pond Society (池社)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Zhang Peili deep research.

Chinese avant-garde artist collective founded in **1986** in Hangzhou by [[Zhang Peili]], **Geng Jianyi** (耿建翌), **Song Ling** (宋陵), **Wang Qiang** (王强), and **Bao Jianfei** (包剑斐). One of the earliest and most conceptually rigorous artist collectives of the **'85 New Wave** (八五新潮) movement. Known for existentialist conceptual art, public happenings, and street interventions in Hangzhou. Their cool, analytical approach distinguished them from more expressionistic '85 New Wave groups.

## Key People
- **[[Zhang Peili]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — co-founder. "Father of Chinese video art."
- **Geng Jianyi** (耿建翌, 1962–2017) — co-founder. Important conceptual artist. *The Second State* (1987).
- **Song Ling** (宋陵) — co-founder. Later emigrated to Australia.
- **Wang Qiang** (王强) — member.
- **Bao Jianfei** (包剑斐) — member.

## Why Interesting
Foundational collective in Chinese contemporary art history. Directly connected to [[Zhang Peili]] and the roots of Chinese new media art. Part of the institutional genealogy of [[China Academy of Art]] and by extension Amber's teaching context.
