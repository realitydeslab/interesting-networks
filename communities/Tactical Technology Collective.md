# Tactical Technology Collective (Tactical Tech)

Berlin-based non-profit organization working at the intersection of technology, digital rights, and civil society since 2003. Known for making complex data and surveillance issues accessible to civil society, journalists, activists, and ordinary people. Published influential resources including [*Visualising Information for Advocacy*](https://visualisingadvocacy.org/) (2014, with Maya Indira Ganesh, Stephanie Hankey, Tom Longley, Marek Tuszynski) and the [Glass Room](https://theglassroom.org/) exhibition series on digital data and privacy.

## Mission
Help people understand and use technology to create a better world. Produce resources, campaigns, exhibitions, and investigations that expose the data economy and empower civil society.

## Key Projects
- [*Visualising Information for Advocacy*](https://visualisingadvocacy.org/) (2014) — toolkit for civil society on data visualization. Co-authored with [[Maya Indira Ganesh]].
- [The Glass Room](https://theglassroom.org/) — traveling exhibition on surveillance and data
- My Shadow, Me and My Shadow tools — digital privacy resources
