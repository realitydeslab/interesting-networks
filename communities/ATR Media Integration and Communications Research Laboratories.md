# ATR Media Integration and Communications Research Laboratories

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via Christa Sommerer & Laurent Mignonneau deep research.

Advanced Telecommunications Research Institute International, Kyoto, Japan. One of Japan's premier art-technology research institutions. In the 1990s, ATR was a major funder and host of artist-scientist collaborations — [[Christa Sommerer and Laurent Mignonneau]] were Artistic Directors & Researchers here from 1995. They organized the "ART-Science-ATR" Symposium there in 1996. Part of the broader Japanese institutional ecosystem (alongside [[IAMAS — Institute of Advanced Media Arts and Sciences]] and [[NTT InterCommunication Center (ICC)]]) that made Japan the global frontier for art-technology work in the 1990s.

## Why Interesting
Key node in the history of art-technology collaboration. Japan's model of institutional support for artists working with high-end computing in the 1990s has no Western equivalent. Understanding ATR helps explain why important artists (Sommerer & Mignonneau, and others) went to Japan for years.
