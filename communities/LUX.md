# LUX

London-based organisation supporting the production, distribution, exhibition, and study of artists' moving image. One of the most important UK institutions for experimental and essay film. Represents and distributes work by [[The Otolith Group]], and many other major artists working with moving image.

## Why Interesting
Essential infrastructure for artists' moving image in the UK. If Amber is interested in the essay film tradition or any artists working in moving image in London, LUX is the institutional hub. Screening programmes, publications, artist talks.
