# Soma Design Research Community

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Kia Höök research. International network working on somaesthetic interaction design.

International research community focused on somaesthetic interaction design - methodology that foregrounds felt bodily experience in design practice.

## Mission & Objectives
Developing and practicing soma design methods that bridge Richard Shusterman's somaesthetics philosophy with interaction design. Focus on body awareness, embodied experience, and felt sense in human-computer interaction.
