# Graffiti Research Lab

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created via [[F.A.T. Lab]] deep research.

Art project founded by Evan Roth and James Powderly, operating out of [[Eyebeam]] OpenLab (NYC), precursor and sibling to [[F.A.T. Lab]]. Known for LED Throwies (magnetic LED lights for graffiti) and L.A.S.E.R. Tag (large-scale laser projections onto buildings). Open-sourced all tools for street artists and activists. Pioneered "no mess" graffiti. Active ~2005–2010.

## Key People
- **Evan Roth** — co-founder. MFA Parsons. Now in Berlin.
- **James Powderly** — co-founder. NYU ITP alum. Robotics background.

## Why Interesting
Precursor to FAT Lab's tactical media practice. Proves that open-source philosophy + street art + tech can produce high-impact public interventions. LED Throwies became globally reproduced.
