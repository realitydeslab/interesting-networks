# Deep Lab

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial stub created during Kate Crawford deep research. Feminist tech collective.

Collaborative group of cyberfeminist researchers, artists, writers, engineers, and cultural producers. Interests span privacy, surveillance, code, art, social hacking, race, capitalism, anonymity, and digital infrastructures.

## Mission & Objectives
Critical assessments of contemporary digital culture; exploiting potential for creative inquiry in the deep web. Research conducted independently and collaboratively; manifested as lecture series, publications, contemporary art, public programming, and performances.
