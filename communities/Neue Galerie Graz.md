# Neue Galerie Graz

The museum of modern and contemporary art in Graz, Austria. Part of the Universalmuseum Joanneum network (Styria's state museums). Housed in the Joanneumsviertel, Graz. Founded in its current form in the 20th century; covers Austrian and international modern art from 1900 to the present.

Peter Weibel was **Chief Curator from 1993 to 2011** — an 18-year tenure concurrent with (and later extending beyond) his Venice Biennale curation and into his ZKM years. During this period, the Neue Galerie Graz became an important venue for media art and contemporary Austrian art. Major exhibitions included: *Kontext Kunst* (1993), *Inklusion: Exklusion* (1996), *Jenseits von Kunst* (1998), *Der anagrammatische Körper* (1999/2000), *Olafur Eliasson: Surroundings Surrounded* (2000/2001), *M_ARS: Kunst und Krieg* (2003), the **Bruseum** (dedicated to Günter Brus, 2011), and a major Weibel-curated retrospective on Austrian Modernism.
