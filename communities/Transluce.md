# Transluce

Non-profit research lab building open, scalable technology for understanding frontier AI systems. Founded October 2024 by [[Jacob Steinhardt]] and Sarah Schwettmann. Name means "shine light through something to reveal its structure." Key differentiator: non-profit model prevents commercial conflicts of interest in safety research.
