# Haus der Kulturen der Welt

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during transmediale deep research. Key venue and institutional partner.

Germany's national center for the presentation and discussion of international contemporary arts, with special focus on non-European cultures and societies. Primary venue for [[transmediale]] since 2008.

## Mission & Objectives
Exhibition, theatre, dance, concerts, readings, films, academic conferences on visual art and culture. Focus on non-Western perspectives, global contemporary. One of Germany's federally funded "cultural lighthouses."

Known for Anthropocene Project (since 2013) with philosophers/scientists including Bruno Latour, Hans Joachim Schellnhuber.

## Key People
- Directors: Bernd Scherer (long-running), Ricardo Carmona (current?)
- Artists/thinkers connected: Bruno Latour, many transmediale curators
