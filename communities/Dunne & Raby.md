# Dunne & Raby

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during RCA deep research. New book *Not Here, Not Now* (2025). Both now Distinguished Visiting Professors at China Academy of Art after 10 years at Parsons/The New School (2016-2025). 

**Anthony Dunne and Fiona Raby** — design studio, educators, and co-creators of speculative design / critical design as an academic discipline. The intellectual origin point of a generation of critical designers. Work in permanent collections at MoMA, V&A, MAK Vienna. MIT Media Lab Award (2015, inaugural). Royal Designers for Industry and Life Fellows of the Royal Society (2021).

**The trajectory:** RCA (1994–2015) → Parsons/The New School (2016–2025) → China Academy of Art (2025–present). The return to the East/West axis is significant.

## Key Contributions
- Coined and institutionalized "speculative design" and "critical design"
- *Speculative Everything* (2013 MIT Press) — the canonical text that defined a field
- *Hertzian Tales* (1999/2005) — electronic products as cultural critique
- *Design Noir* (2001/2021) — dark design, electronic objects
- *Not Here, Not Now* (2025) — latest book
- Research supported by European Commission, Wellcome Trust, EPSRC, Arts Council England, Mellon Foundation, Intel, Microsoft

## Why Interesting
**The wellspring.** Understanding Dunne & Raby is understanding where critical design came from — and where it might go next with their pivot to China Academy of Art. Their DI students at RCA populate the global media art and design research world. For Amber, knowing Dunne & Raby's work is knowing her own intellectual genealogy.
