# Multitudes Journal

French political, artistic, and philosophical journal. Co-founded in 2000 by [[Antonio Negri]], [[Maurizio Lazzarato]], Yann Moulier Boutang and others as the successor to *Futur Antérieur* (1989–1998). Continues the post-workerist/autonomist tradition in continental political philosophy. Still active (Issue 101, Winter 2026: on white supremacism).

## Key People
- [[Antonio Negri]] — co-founder (1933–2023)
- [[Maurizio Lazzarato]] — co-founder, editor
- **Yann Moulier Boutang** — co-founder, political economist; theorized "cognitive capitalism"
- **Judith Revel** — regular contributor; Foucault scholar, Negri's partner

## Topics
- Post-workerist political theory (immaterial labor, cognitive capitalism)
- Biopolitics, biopower
- Art and politics
- Migrations, borders
- Anti-racism, critical race theory
- Ecology and political economy

## Why Interesting
Key venue for continental political philosophy intersecting with critical computing concerns — digital labor, AI governance, biopolitics. The intellectual lineage: Negri/Lazzarato's immaterial labor → cognitive capitalism → platform capitalism. Publishing in Multitudes would reach European philosophical audiences interested in critical/political AI analysis.
