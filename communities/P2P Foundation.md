# P2P Foundation

Global non-profit network dedicated to advocacy and research of commons-oriented peer-to-peer dynamics in society. Founded 2005 by [[Michel Bauwens]]. Maintains the largest curated wiki on commons theory, open source, peer production, and Commons Transition. The intellectual hub connecting digital commons, open hardware, post-capitalist economics, and cosmo-localism.

## Key People
- [[Michel Bauwens]] — founder, chief theorist
- David Hammerstein — co-founder, policy work
- Stacco Troncoso — GuerrillaTranslation, cosmo-localism

## Topics
Commons-based peer production, digital commons, open source, open hardware, platform cooperativism, cosmo-localism, Commons Transition.

## Events & Programmes
- Wiki: <https://wiki.p2pfoundation.net> — ongoing collaborative knowledge commons
- P2P Value project — EU-funded research on CBPP communities
- Commons Transition Plan resources: <https://commonstransition.org/>

## Why Interesting
Best community entry point for Amber's [[Agentive Commoning]] concept. Bauwens actively curates new commons theory and would likely champion AI agents as next phase of peer production. P2P Foundation sits at intersection of digital commons, post-capitalism, and political economy — all directly relevant.
