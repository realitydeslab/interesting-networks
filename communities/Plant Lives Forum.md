# Plant Lives Forum

Online research forum convened by [[Sarah Nuttall]] and [[Isabel Hofmeyr]] at [[WISER]], University of the Witwatersrand. Third in a trilogy of 6-month forums (after Heated Conversations 2023, Breathing In 2024). Currently active (2025–).

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created via Sarah Nuttall deep research.

## Mission & Objectives
Argues for a shift from abstract concern with "plant life" to consider **material plant lives**: the conversations that plant worlds enable, envisioning a postcolonial plantarium that encompasses:
- Plantations and colonial plant economies
- Pre-colonial pharmacopoeias
- Philosophy of plant worlds
- Phytopoetics (both visual and textual)
- Plant sensing and intelligence

## Why Interesting
The Plant Lives forum is one of the few scholarly spaces treating plants as serious theoretical objects *from a Global South perspective*, including attention to pre-colonial plant knowledge that disrupts Western assumptions about plant intelligence and pharmacology. The postcolonial plantarium concept — encompassing plantations (colonial extraction), pre-colonial pharmacopoeias (indigenous knowledge), and phytopoetics (aesthetic engagement) — is a multi-register framework for plant-human relations that has direct resonance with more-than-human computing.
