# Future Observatory

Design-meets-policy. Run by the Design Museum (London) + UKRI/AHRC. £40m programme. Uses museum as public forum for design research.

## Key People
- Justin McGuirk (Director)
- Rebecca Lewin (Curatorial)
- Liz Thornhill (Research)
- 100+ funded research projects across UK universities

## Topics
Green transition, circular economy, low-carbon construction, alternative materials, systemic design, speculative futures, design for policy

## Why Interesting
Bridges design research ↔ public engagement ↔ industry. Journal (fojournal.org) and exhibitions are where ideas get tested publicly. Not a traditional conference — a living research programme with events.

## Connections
- UK design research university network
