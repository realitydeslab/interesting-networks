# Open Humanities Press

An international open-access publisher in the humanities, founded in 2006 with a strong emphasis on **radical open access** — free, CC-licensed, genuinely free-to-read and free-to-republish scholarly work. Not diamond/platinum OA as a compliance checkbox, but as a political commitment to decommercialising scholarly knowledge.

Co-founded by **[[Gary Hall]]** (Coventry University) and others. **[[Joanna Zylinska]]** is editor of the MEDIA : ART : WRITE : NOW series, published through OHP.

## Key Series
- **MEDIA : ART : WRITE : NOW** (ed. Joanna Zylinska) — short, urgent essays on technology-based arts: AI/computational arts, gaming, digital/post-digital, photography, amateur media. Titles include *AI Art* (Zylinska), *A Stubborn Fury* (Gary Hall), *Fungi Media* (Piotr Bockowski), *Geological Filmmaking* (Sasha Litvintseva), *Glitch Poetics* (Nathan Allen Jones), *Remixing Persona* (Mark Amerika + Laura Kim)
- **New Metaphysics** — speculative realism and related continental philosophy
- **Liquid Books** — experimental hybrid print/digital forms

## Radical Open Access Collective
OHP is a founding member of the **Radical Open Access Collective** — a network of scholar-led open-access publishers advocating for non-commercial academic publishing.

OHP is a publisher that specifically produces short, urgent humanities texts at the intersection of art, technology, and critical theory. The MEDIA:ART:WRITE:NOW series is essentially a curated feed of interesting ideas in the space Amber cares about. Worth following as a discovery channel.
