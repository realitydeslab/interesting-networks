# Whitney Independent Study Program (ISP)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created via American Artist deep research.

The most prestigious artist-theorist training program in the US art world, hosted at the Whitney Museum of American Art. Founded 1968. Runs three tracks: artists, critics/curators, and art historians. The seminar is grounded in critical theory (psychoanalysis, Marxism, feminism, postcolonialism). Alumni disproportionately shape US curatorial and artistic discourse. Known for producing artists who are simultaneously rigorous theorists. 

Alumni include: American Artist, many leading critical artists. The ISP is the node connecting US critical art to the European critical theory tradition.

## Why Interesting
If Amber wants to understand the lineage of US critical art practice and theory, ISP is the institution. Understanding who comes through ISP tracks the sharpest critical practitioners. American Artist's time there shaped their Black Studies theoretical framework.
