# IRI (Institut de recherche et d'innovation)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created during Bernard Stiegler deep research. Founded by Stiegler in 2006 at Centre Pompidou.

Research institute founded by [[Bernard Stiegler]] in 2006 at Centre Pompidou to anticipate, accompany, and analyze cultural, scientific and economic transformations induced by digital technologies.

## Mission & Objectives
Develop new critical contributive devices and research the epistemological, cultural and political stakes of digital technology. Focus on "organology of knowledge" and the figure of the amateur.

## Key People
- [[Bernard Stiegler]] — founder and director until 2020
- Current leadership through Scientific and Industrial College since Stiegler's death

## Key Research Areas
- Parentality, Care and Digital Technology
- Urban Contribution Design  
- Contribution Economy
- Knowledge and Technologies

## Key Publications & Output
- Digital Studies network (established 2012)
- Territoire Apprenant Contributif (Plaine Commune project, 2016)
- ECO local contribution currency project
