# STS-Africa Network

A continental platform for advancing Science and Technology Studies in Africa. Co-initiated in 2011 by [[Richard Rottenburg]] and others. Bridges African scholarly communities with global STS, with increasing focus on AI and digital technology governance in African contexts.

## Key People
- [[Richard Rottenburg]] — co-founder; WISER, University of the Witwatersrand

## Why Interesting
African STS is a natural intellectual home for critical AI governance work that takes non-Western contexts seriously. As ML/AI systems are deployed across African governance contexts, the STS-Africa network is where the critical vocabulary is being built.
