# Sharjah Art Foundation

Major cultural institution in Sharjah, UAE. Director: **Hoor Al Qasimi**. Runs the **Sharjah Biennial** (one of the world's most important international art biennials), the **March Meeting** (annual convening), residencies, and an extensive collection. Strongly oriented toward the Global South and postcolonial perspectives since Sharjah Biennial 11 (2013), when it pivoted toward Afro-Asian solidarity and Third World liberation. Has sustained relationships with [[The Otolith Group]] (co-curated Xenogenesis with Annie Fletcher). The March Meeting (annual) titled 2022: "The Afterlives of the Postcolonial."

## Why Interesting
One of the most politically committed major art institutions in the world. Genuinely Global South-oriented, not just Western art with Global South subject matter. Strong connections to postcolonial theory and practice communities. Sharjah Biennial is a major platform.
