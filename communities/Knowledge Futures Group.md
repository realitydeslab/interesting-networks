# Knowledge Futures Group

Nonprofit building open-source infrastructure for scholarly publishing and knowledge sharing. Makers of **PubPub** (open scholarly publishing platform used by MIT Press and many academic publishers). Works on open data curation, metadata standards, and knowledge graph infrastructure.

## Key People
- [[Agnes Cameron]] — registry coordinator, developing tools for open data curation

## Why Interesting
KFG is infrastructure work — the less glamorous but necessary layer beneath open access publishing. Agnes Cameron's role here reveals her commitment to open knowledge infrastructure alongside her more visible artistic practice. PubPub is used by MIT Press, Berkman Klein, and many journals.
