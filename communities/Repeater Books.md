# Repeater Books

Independent publishing imprint launched 2014 by former Zero Books team. Founded by Mark Fisher, Tariq Goddard, Etan Ilfeld, Alex Niven, Tamar Shlaim, and Matteo Mandarini as part of Watkins Media. Continues Zero Books' mission of accessible critical theory with stronger editorial control.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Mark Fisher deep research. Active publisher of contemporary critical theory.

## Mission & Objectives

Continues Zero Books' mission of publishing accessible critical theory that maintains intellectual rigor. Focus on cultural criticism, political theory, music criticism, and experimental forms.

Launched after disagreements with Zero Books parent company over editorial direction.
