# BPA// Berlin Program for Artists

Artist-led fellowship programme for emerging Berlin-based artists, founded 2016 by Angela Bulloch, [[Simon Denny]], and Willem de Rooij. Centered around mutual studio visits between participants and mentors. Free of charge, two-year cycle. Partner of [[KW Institute for Contemporary Art]] (annual BPA// exhibition). Alumni include [[Paul Kolling]] (2021–22). Applies: must be Berlin-based and have completed masters-level education or equivalent.
