# Breathing In: Air and Atmospheres Forum

Online research forum convened by [[Sarah Nuttall]] and [[Isabel Hofmeyr]] at [[WISER]] in 2024. Second in a trilogy of 6-month forums (after Heated Conversations 2023, before Plant Lives 2025). Explored air as a political and epistemic object. Now reaching publication as book/special issue.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created via Sarah Nuttall deep research.

## Mission & Objectives
Explored the growing non-transparency of air and the importance of making air explicative. Connected to Achille Mbembe's "Universal Right to Breathe" (Critical Inquiry, 2021) written during COVID lockdowns. Atmospheric politics, breath, and postcolonial ecology.

## Topics
- Air as political object
- Breath and atmospheric rights
- Non-transparency of air (pollution, opacity)
- Postcolonial atmospherics
- Making air "explicative" — methodological claims
