# Rhizome

Digital culture organization affiliated with the New Museum, NYC. Net art, digital preservation, born-digital art. Founded 1996.

## Programmes
- **Rhizome World** — annual event/conference
- **Net Art Anthology** — preserving internet art history
- **Webrecorder** — web archiving tools
- Commissions for digital artists

## Key People
- [[Wendi Yan]] — speaker at Rhizome World
- Michael Connor — artistic director
- Dragan Espenschied — preservation

## Connections
- [[NEW INC]] — both at New Museum
- [[Eyebeam]] — NYC art-tech ecosystem

## Why Interesting
One of the oldest and most respected digital art orgs. The preservation work is itself interesting — what does it mean to keep internet art alive? Adjacent to Amber's interests via digital culture × archives × art.
