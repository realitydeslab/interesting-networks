# Institute of Network Cultures (INC)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Tactical Media deep research.

Amsterdam-based research center and publisher focused on critical internet culture. Founded and directed by Geert Lovink at Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. Publishes free open-access readers (Theory on Demand series), organizes conferences (MoneyLab, Unlike Us), and maintains archives including the [Tactical Media Files](http://www.tacticalmediafiles.net/). Current institutional home of the tactical media legacy.

## Why Interesting
Most active institutional heir of tactical media. Produces high-quality theoretical writing on platform power, digital labor, internet critique. Publishes work that could be cited in Amber's critical tech framing.
