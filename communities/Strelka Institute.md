# Strelka Institute

Architecture and design institute in Moscow. Closed in 2022 following Russia's invasion of Ukraine. Enormously influential through two programmes directed by [[Benjamin Bratton]].

## Key Programmes
- **The New Normal** (2016–2020) — speculative design research programme
- **The Terraforming** (2020–2022) — planetary-scale design

## Notable Alumni/Participants
- [[Lukáš Likavčan]] — The Terraforming
- [[Nicolay Boyadjiev]] — now at Antikythera
- Many alumni went on to [[Antikythera]]
- Liam Young — speculative architecture
- Keller Easterling — faculty

## Connections
- [[Benjamin Bratton]] — programme director
- [[Antikythera]] — spiritual successor (many Strelka people moved to Antikythera)
- [[Liam Young]] — participated

## Why Interesting
Strelka's New Normal and Terraforming were arguably the most ambitious speculative design education programmes ever run. Many of the most interesting people in planetary computation came through these programmes. Antikythera is essentially Strelka's continuation.
