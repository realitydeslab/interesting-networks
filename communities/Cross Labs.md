# Cross Labs

Kyoto-based research lab focused on artificial life, intelligence, and complex systems. Subsidiary of Cross Compass Ltd. Organized ALIFE 2025 in Kyoto.

## Key People
- [[Olaf Witkowski]] — Director
- [[Alyssa Adams]] — Deputy Director
- [[Hector Zenil]] — External member (algorithmic information dynamics)
- Nicholas Gutternberg — Senior researcher (origins of life, artificial chemistry)
- Martin Biehl — Senior researcher (Bayesian agents, active inference)
- Q. Tyrell Davis — Senior researcher (abstract life-like systems)
- Nathanael Aubert-Kato — External member (molecular robotics)

## Research Areas
- **Machine Intelligence** — ML, evolutionary computation, NLP
- **Human Intelligence** — consciousness, phenomenology, cognitive science
- **Brain Intelligence** — neuroinformatics, brain decoding, computational neuroscience
- **Life Intelligence** — ALife, open-ended evolution, biological networks, agent-based modeling
- **Complex Intelligence** — complex systems, novelty search, information theory
- **Social Intelligence** — collective intelligence, swarm AI, multi-agent simulation

## Events & Programmes
- Organized **ALIFE 2025** in Kyoto (Oct 6-10, 2025)
- Co-founded **Artificial Life Japan** (2021)
- Regular research collaborations and visiting positions
