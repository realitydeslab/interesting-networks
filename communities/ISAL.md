# International Society for Artificial Life (ISAL)

The learned society behind the ALIFE conference and the *Artificial Life* journal (MIT Press). The institutional home of the ALife community.

## Key People
- [[Olaf Witkowski]] — Vice President
- Chrystopher L. Nehaniv — Board (elected 2025)
- Stefano Nichele — Board (elected 2025)
- Susan Stepney — former president
- [[Emily Dolson]] — active member

## Topics
Artificial life, open-ended evolution, self-organization, emergence, digital evolution, evolutionary computation, origin of life, embodied cognition.

## Events & Programmes
- **ALIFE Conference** — annual, the flagship event: <https://alife.org/>
- **ISAL Awards** — annual awards for research, community, and early career. Nominations open Feb 2026.
- **Artificial Life journal** (MIT Press) — the flagship journal: <https://direct.mit.edu/artl>
- **ALife Encyclopedia** — community-written educational resource: <https://alife.org/encyclopedia/>
- **ALife Discord** — community chat: <https://discord.gg/alife>

## Recent Award Winners (2025)
- Imran Khan — ALife 2025 Award
- Clifford Bohm — Best Reviewer
- Earnest Kota Carr — Rising Star
- Piotr Walas — Community Spirit

## Why Interesting
The gateway to the ALife community. Membership gives access to the journal, conference discounts, voting rights, and the community network. For Amber entering the field, ISAL membership is the first concrete step.
