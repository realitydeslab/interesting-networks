# Foreign Objects

Research and design studio founded by [[Agnes Cameron]] and [[Gary Zhexi Zhang]]. Based between New York and London. Works at the intersection of speculative technology, political ecology, and critical simulation. Won the **Mozilla Creative Media Award 2019** for *Bot or Not* ("Truth or Dare with a bot or person").

## Key Projects
- **Bot or Not** (2019) — Mozilla Creative Media Award. Asks whether you're talking to a bot or a person. Early critical exploration of epistemic uncertainty in human-machine interaction.
- **Permaculture Network** (2019/2020) — agent-based ecological simulation of Sakiya land, Palestine. Schloss Solitude / ZKM web residency.
- **The First 10,000 Years** (2020/2021) — catastrophe insurance marketplace simulation; part of fud (Arts Catalyst, Bloc Projects, Medialab Prado).

## Key People
- [[Agnes Cameron]] — co-founder; artist, technologist, researcher
- [[Gary Zhexi Zhang]] — co-founder; artist, writer, researcher

## Why Interesting
Foreign Objects is the institutional form of the Cameron-Zhang collaboration. Their work uses speculative simulation as a political tool — modelling Palestinian land, catastrophic futures, epistemic uncertainty. The studio model (vs. solo practice) means they take on commissions and fellowships as a unit, giving them reach beyond individual artists.
