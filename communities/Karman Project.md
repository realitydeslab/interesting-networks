# The Karman Project

Fellowship and dialogue programme fostering trust, cooperation, and independent dialogue between global leaders in science, technology, business, arts, politics, and space. Named after the Kármán line (the boundary between Earth's atmosphere and outer space).

## Key People
- [[Xin Liu]] — Karman Fellow 2022

## Topics
- Space exploration and governance
- Art × science × space policy
- Interdisciplinary leadership and cooperation

## Events & Programmes
- Annual Karman Fellowship cohort
- Dialogue sessions between global leaders
