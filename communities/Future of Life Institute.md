# Future of Life Institute (FLI)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created during CSER deep research.

Non-profit organisation focused on steering transformative technology toward benefiting life. Co-founded by Max Tegmark, Jaan Tallinn, Meia Chita-Tegmark, Anthony Aguirre, and Victoria Krakovna. Based in Cambridge, MA and Boston, MA.

Key activities: the 2015 Puerto Rico AI Open Letter (signed by Hawking, Musk, hundreds of researchers); FLI International Safety Grants Competition ($2M+ to AI safety research, funded CSER's AGI paradigms project); policy work on lethal autonomous weapons; the 2023 open letter calling for a pause on giant AI experiments.

Sister organisation to CSER and partner on multiple projects. Co-funded CSER's *Paradigms of Artificial General Intelligence and Their Associated Risks* project.

## Key People
- Max Tegmark — Co-founder, MIT physicist
- Jaan Tallinn — Co-founder (also CSER co-founder, Skype co-founder)
- Victoria Krakovna — DeepMind researcher, co-founder

## Why Interesting
Shares intellectual DNA with CSER; Jaan Tallinn bridges both organisations. Key funder of early AI safety research ecosystem. ERA Fellowship community likely overlaps significantly.
