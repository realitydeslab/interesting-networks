# Centre for Attention Studies (King's College London)

Interdisciplinary research centre at King's College London directed by **[[Joanna Zylinska]]**. Tagline: "Forging new responses to the foundational digital age crisis: distraction." Works at the intersection of cognitive science, media philosophy, and digital culture to understand how attention operates, fails, and is commodified in the attention economy.

## Mission
Understanding attention as both a cognitive phenomenon and a political economy. Connects philosophy of mind, media studies, and digital culture. Especially focused on XR (extended reality) and its effects on perception.

## Key Events (from KCL calendar)
- "Attention and Perception in XR" hybrid symposium (June 2024)
- "Attending to Technology in a Time of Radical Change" symposium (June 2024)
- Attention Reading Group — regular series
- "The Ethics of Immersion: Beyond 'Tech for Good'" (Feb 2026)
