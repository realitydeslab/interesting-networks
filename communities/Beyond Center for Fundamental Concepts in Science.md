# Beyond Center for Fundamental Concepts in Science

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created note during Sara Walker deep research.

Arizona State University center devoted to confronting the big questions of science and philosophy. Tackles subjects from cosmology and astrobiology to time travel, Mars colonization, and the relationship between mathematics and nature.

## Mission & Objectives
Create new ideas that push research boundaries "beyond" traditional categories. Conduct interdisciplinary research on foundational questions in science while exploring their philosophical ramifications. Present science as a key component of culture significant to all humanity.

## Key People

- **[[Sara Imari Walker]]** — deputy director, leads emergence lab

## Key Contributions
Research projects addressing cosmology, astrobiology, complexity, time travel, multiple universes, Mars colonization.
