# Data & Society Research Institute

Independent nonprofit research organization based in New York City. One of the most important hubs for critical data studies, algorithmic accountability, and surveillance capitalism scholarship.

## Key People
- [[Kate Crawford]] — co-founder; *Atlas of AI*; surveillance + infrastructure + labor
- danah boyd — founder; youth, social media, privacy
- Meredith Whittaker — former affiliate; see [[Meredith Whittaker]]
- Alex Hanna — see [[Alex Hanna]]

## Topics
- Algorithmic accountability and governance
- Surveillance capitalism and data extraction
- Platform labor and content moderation
- AI bias and fairness
- Data governance and alternatives

## Events & Programmes
- Annual conference and public events: <https://datasociety.net/events/>
- Research Affiliate Program: <https://datasociety.net/programs/fellowship/>
- Reports and working papers: <https://datasociety.net/research/>

## Why Interesting
The primary intellectual hub bridging surveillance capitalism theory with empirical research and policy. Many of the most important critical AI scholars are affiliated. For Amber, an Oxford-based researcher, engaging with Data & Society's reports and attending their events would connect her to the US side of the critical computing infrastructure.
