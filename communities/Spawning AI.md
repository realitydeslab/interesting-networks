# Spawning AI

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial community note created during Holly Herndon deep research.

AI consent and ethics platform. Created practical infrastructure for artists to control how their work is used in AI training.

## Mission & Objectives
Advancing artist consent and data rights in AI development. Provides tools for artists to check if their work was used in AI training datasets and opt out of future training.

## Key People
- **[[Holly Herndon]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Co-founder, artist, AI-human collaboration pioneer
- **[[Mat Dryhurst]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Co-founder, writer, technologist

## Key Publications & Output
- **[Have I Been Trained?](https://haveibeentrained.com/)** — Database allowing artists to search if their work appears in popular AI training datasets
- Tools and advocacy for AI data consent

## Key Contributions
Practical infrastructure for AI ethics rather than just theoretical frameworks. Gave artists actual tools to understand and control AI training data use.
