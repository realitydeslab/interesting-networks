# Aarhus University Research on the Anthropocene (AURA)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial discovery through Anna Tsing research. Created stub note based on website investigation.

Major European research center for Anthropocene studies. Funded by the Danish National Research Foundation from 2013-2018. Applied trans-disciplinary approaches combining anthropology, biology, and philosophy to understand life and futures in Anthropocene landscapes.

## Mission & Objectives
"To open up a novel and truly trans-disciplinary field of research into the Anthropocene which is needed to understand the kinds of lives that are made and the futures that are possible in the ruined, re-wilded, and unintended landscapes of the Anthropocene."
