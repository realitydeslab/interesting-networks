# Erasmus Institute for Philosophy and Technology

New research institute at [Erasmus University Rotterdam](https://www.eur.nl/), housed within the Erasmus School of Philosophy. Founded in 2025, coinciding with [[Yuk Hui]]'s appointment as Chair of Human Conditions. The institutional home of the [[Research Network for Philosophy and Technology]] within the European university system.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created. Institute founded 2025. First event: Idealism Summer Seminar (Jul 2, 2025).

## Mission
To develop rigorous philosophical research on technology, from continental and non-Western perspectives. Close connection to cosmotechnics research agenda. Bridge between European philosophy tradition and global philosophy of technology.

## Key People
- **[[Yuk Hui]]** — Chair of Human Conditions, Erasmus School of Philosophy; Director
- **Milan Stürmer** — Research associate/co-organizer
