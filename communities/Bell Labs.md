# Bell Labs

Historic industrial research laboratory. Site of major technological breakthroughs. Now runs artist residency programs exploring new forms of art and technology.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created note during Sougwen Chung research. Historic research lab with artist residency program.

Legendary industrial research laboratory originally part of AT&T/Bell System. Site of invention of transistor, laser, Unix, C programming language, and numerous Nobel Prize-winning discoveries. Now continuing tradition with artist residencies.

## Mission & Objectives
Fundamental research and development. Historic mission to advance telecommunications and computing. Current artist residency program explores "new forms of drawing in virtual reality, with biometrics, machine learning, and robotics."
