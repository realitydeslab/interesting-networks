# Diasporas Now

Live art collective founded by **Rieko Whitfield** (Japanese-American artist/composer), **Paola Estrella** (Mexican multimedia artist, London), and **Lulu Wang** (Chinese interdisciplinary artist, London). Currently in residence at [[ICA London]] as part of the Speaking Futures × Diasporas Now programme (2025-26).

Platform for "expanded performance by the global majority." Explores collaborative practice, art+technology intersections, sound as political practice, and the body as language. Runs music label (self-released through Diasporas Now).

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during ICA London deep research. Identified as key collective in ICA's current programming.

## Why Interesting
Paola Estrella's practice explicitly addresses "impact of new technologies on public and private spheres" and uses speculative fiction — direct overlap with Amber's speculative design + technology work. Lulu Wang works at intersection of digital subcultures and new technologies. Both are London-based. The collective models cross-cultural, cross-disciplinary collaboration — a structural model Amber might find inspiring.
