# LOST Research Group

Law, Organisation, Science and Technology (LOST) — research group founded in 2006 by [[Richard Rottenburg]] at Martin Luther University Halle-Wittenberg. Central node in European STS, focused on how juridical, organizational, technological and scientific models translate in the Global South. Generated key work on traveling models, biomedicine in Africa, and Sudan studies.

## Key People
- [[Richard Rottenburg]] — founder and director
- [[Sandra Calkins]] — core collaborator; evidence, infrastructure, Sudan studies
- [[Andrea Behrends]] — conflict management models

## Why Interesting
LOST is the institutional home of the "traveling models" framework — the STS vocabulary most applicable to how ML systems move across cultural contexts. A precursor to AI governance studies.
