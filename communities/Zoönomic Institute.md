# Zoönomic Institute

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-02:** Stub created during Mediamatic deep research.

Dutch foundation developing the **Zoöp** organizational model — a governance framework that formally integrates non-human interests into organizational decision-making. "Zoöp" = zoe (Greek: life) + coöp (cooperation). Member organizations appoint a "Speaker for the Living" who advocates for the ecosystem's interests in organizational decisions. Mediamatic adopted this model in 2025.

## Why Interesting
The Zoöp model is more-than-human governance as institutional practice — not as art or speculation but as actual organizational structure. This is exactly the kind of thing Amber's work in more-than-human design and agentive commoning is circling around. A "Speaker for the Living" is functionally an AI-agent-like proxy for non-human interests in a human institution.
