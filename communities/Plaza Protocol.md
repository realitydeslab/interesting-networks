# Plaza Protocol

Ljubljana-based arts organization. Published the *Temporal Secessionism Sourcebook* (2019), which included Paul Seidler and Max Hampshire's essay "Distributed temporal mutations — Consensus-systems as novel temporal regimes." Hosted Timezone #4 (terra0 digital-only work, 2021). Connected to the Slovenian media arts scene (Aksioma, MGLC/Ljubljana Biennale of Graphic Arts).

## Why Interesting
One of the Eastern European nodes in the critical blockchain/tech-culture circuit. The Temporal Secessionism Sourcebook is a rare serious publication on blockchain temporality. Connected to the Ljubljana Biennale (MGLC) and Aksioma, making it part of a small but serious European media art geography.
