# ŠUM Journal

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during [[Bogna Konior]] deep research.

Slovenian journal for contemporary art criticism and theory, published by Društvo Galerija Boks. Publishes long-form theoretical essays at the intersection of art, technology, and philosophy. Notable for publishing [[Bogna Konior]]'s major essays: "Angelsexual: Chatbot Celibacy and Other Erotic Suspensions" (#22) and "Determination from the Outside: Stigmata, Teledildonics and Remote Cybersex" (#12).

## Why Interesting
Small but serious theoretical journal. Part of the Slovenian art-theory ecosystem (with [[Ljubljana Biennale]], [[Maribor Computer Arts Festival]]). Publishes the kind of long-form, rigorous, speculative writing that doesn't fit in mainstream academic journals. Potential publishing venue for Amber.
