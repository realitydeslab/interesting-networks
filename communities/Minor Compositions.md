# Minor Compositions

Anarchist/radical theory imprint based in the UK. Publishes political philosophy, critical theory, and activist writing at the intersection of autonomist Marxism, post-anarchism, and radical practice. Books are made available as **free PDF downloads** alongside paid print editions. Distributed by AK Press (US/Canada) and Autonomedia.

Part of the broader ecosystem around **[[punctum books]]**, Autonomedia, and the anti-copyright left publishing network.

## Notable Publications (selected)
- Brian Massumi, *Toward a Theory of Fascism for Anti-fascist Life: A Process Vocabulary* (2025) — free download available; companion to Duke UP's *The Personality of Power*
- Works by Nick Dyer-Witheford, Stevphen Shukaitis, Silvia Federici, and other autonomist/feminist theory figures.

## Why Interesting

Minor Compositions occupies an interesting niche: it publishes the same intellectual tier as major academic presses but makes everything free. The fact that [[Brian Massumi]] published *Toward a Theory of Fascism* here simultaneously with Duke UP signals deliberate dual-strategy: reach the academy AND reach the activist community that can't/won't pay $30 for a pamphlet. The name itself echoes Deleuze & Guattari's "minor literature" — becoming-minor as a political strategy.
