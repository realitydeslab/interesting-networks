# Media Art 21

Bilingual (Chinese-English) online editorial platform co-organized by **Institute of Sci-Tech Arts, Central Academy of Fine Arts (CAFA, Beijing)** and **ZKM | Center for Art and Media Karlsruhe**. Covers global media art since 2000. Three key themes: Ecologies, Post-human, The Commons.

Also runs a **Lecture Series** (2025–) organized with [[Chronus Art Center]], Peking University, Fudan Art Institute, and HKUST Guangzhou. Lectures archived at [ma21.org/en/lectures](https://ma21.org/en/lectures).

## Why Note-Worthy
ZKM + CAFA partnership is the most significant China–Germany media art institutional bridge. Lecture archives are free and excellent — primary resource for understanding what the global media art field considers important.
