# Electronic Disturbance Theater (EDT)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created during Critical Art Ensemble deep research.

Hacktivist art collective founded by Ricardo Dominguez (former CAE member) after leaving [[Critical Art Ensemble]] in 1993. Pioneered "virtual sit-ins" using FloodNet software against the Mexican government (in support of Zapatistas). EDT is a key node in the tactical media → hacktivism lineage.

Ricardo Dominguez is now professor at UC San Diego Visual Arts.

## Why Interesting
Direct spinoff from CAE. EDT operationalized electronic civil disobedience into actual tools (FloodNet). Bridge between tactical media theory and hacktivist practice. Relevant to Amber's interest in critical computing and networked resistance.
