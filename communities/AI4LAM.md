# AI4LAM

International community of practice focused on **Artificial Intelligence for Libraries, Archives, and Museums** (GLAM sector). Organises the annual **Fantastic Futures** conference — in 2025 held at the **British Library, London** (Dec 3–5), themed "AI Everywhere, All at Once."

The community brings together practitioners and researchers working on AI/ML in cultural heritage institutions. Topics: ethical AI implementation, sustainability at scale, collection digitisation, multimodal access, cybersecurity/data governance, human-centred design.

Conference recordings available on YouTube; 2025 repository archived at doi.org/10.23636/54k0-ny43.

## Why Interesting
Growing pressure point where AI capabilities meet cultural heritage institutions — the GLAM sector is conservative by nature, and AI4LAM is where the tension gets worked through. [[Daniel Chávez Heras]]'s ISSA project (BFI-funded AI for UK screen archives) is squarely in this space.
