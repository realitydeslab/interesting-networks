# Arctic Circle Artist & Scientist Residency

Expedition-based residency on a sailing vessel in the Arctic. Artists + scientists + architects + educators together in extreme environment. Founded 2007.

## Structure
- ~4 week expeditions
- ~$5,800 participant fee (partial fellowships available)
- International exhibit schedule follows expeditions
- Open to all disciplines

## Key People
- [[Wendi Yan]] — alumna

## Why Interesting
Self-selecting group: people who choose to do art-science work on a boat in the Arctic are a specific kind of interesting. The extreme environment produces unusual work. Worth tracking alumni.
