# AI & Culture Research Center (AIC)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Discovered through [[Bogna Konior]] research. Created stub note.

NYU Shanghai's center investigating philosophical and cultural implications of artificial intelligence. Closely affiliated with [[Interactive Media Arts]] department. Makes use of Shanghai location at premier Sino-American university to research cutting edge of emerging planetary media.

## Mission & Objectives
Investigates AI as it suffuses and transforms the contemporary world. Research draws on philosophy (analytic and continental traditions), media theory, history of science and computation, cultural and artistic practice, empirical research and production.
