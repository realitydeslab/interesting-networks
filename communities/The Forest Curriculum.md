# The Forest Curriculum

Artistic and research platform co-founded and co-directed by [[Pujita Guha]] and collaborators (2018-2023). Focus on Indigenous lifeworlds, forest ecologies, and upland Asia. Developed curatorial and educational programs connecting artists, scholars, and communities across India, SE Asia, and SW China.

Now concluded (2023). Succeeded by [Hosting Lands](https://hostinglands.com/) (decentralized land and ecological curatorial project, Denmark, 2023-2025), also co-directed by Pujita Guha.

## Why Interesting
Node in global indigenous curatorial networks. Framing of forests as living, technical, non-extractive lifeworlds — more-than-human approach to ecology and culture. Adjacent to Amber's interests.
