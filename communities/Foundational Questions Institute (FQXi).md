# Foundational Questions Institute (FQXi)

A private foundation that funds high-risk, high-reward foundational research in physics and cosmology — especially questions that lie beyond the remit of conventional institutional funding. Over $27 million in Zenith Grants (formerly Large Grants) awarded since founding.

**Mission:** "FQxI catalyzes research and outreach primarily on questions at the foundations of physics and cosmology. FQxI supports high-risk, high-reward science—in particular new frontiers and innovative ideas integral to a deep understanding of physical reality that lie beyond the remit of conventional institutional support and funding."

## Key People
- **[[Paul Davies]]** — Connected. Member of the FQXi network.
- **[[Sara Imari Walker]]** — Connected. Funded by FQXi for origins of life / information theory work.
- Various foundational physics researchers worldwide.

## Topics
Foundations of quantum mechanics, cosmology, consciousness and physics, quantum biology, information theory, time, emergence, nature of physical laws.

## Grant Programs
- **Zenith Grants** (formerly Large Grants) — Major research grants to theorists and experimenters. Competitive international applications. Topics rotate: recent cycles include "Consciousness in the Physical World."
- **Essay Contest** — Annual essay competition on foundational topics. Current: "How Quantum is Life?" (in partnership with Paradox Science Institute).

## Resources
- **QSpace** — Free online content: videos, articles, games, podcast episodes at edge of physics. <https://qspace.fqxi.org/>
