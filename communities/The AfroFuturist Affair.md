# The AfroFuturist Affair

Platform and community for writers of colour working in speculative fiction and related genres. Founded in 2011 by [[Rasheedah Phillips]] in Philadelphia. Functions as both an online platform and a community space, and also as the publisher of [[Black Quantum Futurism]] books through its imprint **House of Future Sciences Books**.

## Key Functions
- **Web platform** — curating writing by BIPOC speculative fiction authors
- **Community events** — readings, workshops, gatherings
- **Publisher (House of Future Sciences Books)** — published *Black Quantum Futurism: Theory & Practice Vol. 1* (2015), *Space-Time Collapse Vol. I* (2016)
- **AfroFuturist circuit** — connected to Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture, John Jennings / Reynaldo Anderson's *Cosmic Underground* anthology, and broader speculative Blackness community

## Background
Founded 2011, 3 years before the formal founding of the [[Black Quantum Futurism]] collective (2014). The Affair established Rasheedah's community-builder/organiser credibility before the art collective came into existence — giving BQF immediate community context and a publisher.
