# Esalen Institute

Residential retreat center and counterculture intellectual hub in Big Sur, California. Founded 1962 by Michael Murphy and Dick Price. The human potential movement's physical and intellectual center from the 1960s onward. Hosted [[Gregory Bateson]], Alan Watts, Abraham Maslow, Fritz Perls, Stanislav Grof, Carlos Castaneda, and hundreds of others. Still operating, running workshops and residencies. Where systems thinking, humanistic psychology, and embodied/somatic practice first mixed.

## Why Interesting
Esalen is where California counterculture + systems thinking + embodied practice first synthesized. This synthesis is a precursor to much contemporary more-than-human and somatic design work. Bateson's presence here in the 1970s is part of this history.
