# Leuphana Institute for Advanced Studies in Culture and Society (LIAS)

The [Leuphana Institute for Advanced Studies in Culture and Society (LIAS)](https://www.leuphana.de/en/institutes/lias.html) at [Leuphana University Lüneburg](https://www.leuphana.de/en/). Directed by Erich Hörl (since 2022). Runs a visiting fellows programme for established international researchers in humanities, social sciences, and cultural studies.

## Background

LIAS follows the model of international research institutes like the IAS Princeton, ZiF Bielefeld, or Wissenschaftskolleg Berlin — bringing international researchers together for extended periods to work on shared themes. The connection with Erich Hörl means that LIAS themes are likely oriented around media culture, philosophy of technology, general ecology, and disruption theory.

The institute hosts visiting fellows from across disciplines and from international institutions, including Global South institutions (the UWC partnership suggests South Africa-Germany exchange).
