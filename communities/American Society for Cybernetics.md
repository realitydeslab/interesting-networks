# American Society for Cybernetics (ASC)

The primary professional organization keeping second-order cybernetics and the BCL/Macy tradition alive. Founded 1964 — predates the [[Biological Computer Laboratory]] formal closure. [[Heinz von Foerster]] was a central figure; [[Ranulph Glanville]] was president 2009–2014 and reformatted the conferences in a cybernetic/conversational style (following Margaret Mead's 1967 challenge). Publishes *Cybernetics & Human Knowing* (journal) and awards the **Norbert Wiener Gold Medal** — the highest honor in cybernetics.
