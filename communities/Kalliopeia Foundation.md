# Kalliopeia Foundation

A small, philanthropic operating foundation based at the edge of Point Reyes National Seashore in Northern California, funding the intersection of **ecology, culture, and spirituality**. Best known as the founding funder of [[Emergence Magazine]] and the creator of the [Global Oneness Project](https://www.globalonenessproject.org/). Grants by invitation only. Quietly powerful — this is the philanthropic substrate sustaining the ecological humanities world.

## Mission & Objectives

Kalliopeia supports grantmaking, education, and storytelling that "uphold sacred relationships with the living Earth." Their three pillars:

- **Ecology** — rooted in *oikos* (house), upholding interconnected relationships of the living world
- **Culture** — from *colere* (to cultivate), supporting cultures that tend the places they are part of
- **Spirituality** — from *spirare* (breath), the human capacity to be in relationship to something greater than ourselves

Their guiding principles include: recognizing life's oneness, honoring the sacredness of land, respecting women's leadership and feminine principles, supporting nonhierarchical communities, and seeding a future based on kinship with the Earth. Explicitly grounded in Coast Miwok unceded territory with a land acknowledgment.

They explicitly use **"more-than-human world"** language on their site — placing themselves within that intellectual tradition alongside Wakkary, van Dooren, etc.

## Key Programmes & Outputs

### Emergence Magazine
The flagship output. Founded by Kalliopeia, [[Emergence Magazine]] is a quarterly print + digital publication at the intersection of ecology, culture, and spirituality. Peabody Award–nominated journalism, essays, and storytelling. The most aesthetically refined ecological humanities platform in the Anglophone world.
- URL: <https://emergencemagazine.org>

### Global Oneness Project
Founded 2006 as a Kalliopeia initiative. Free archive of films, photography, essays, and lesson plans for educators on global social, cultural, and environmental issues. Reaches students worldwide.
- URL: <https://www.globalonenessproject.org>

### Grantmaking
Grants by **invitation only**. US-based focus. Prioritizes land-based projects at the ecology × culture × spirituality intersection. General operating or project support. Contact: grants@kalliopeia.org

## Key People (Grantees)

Kalliopeia funds individuals and organizations rather than running fellowships. Notable grantees:

- **[Dr. Suzanne Simard](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/dr-suzanne-simard/)** — Forest ecologist, UBC. Pioneer of plant communication and intelligence. Author of *Finding the Mother Tree*. Trees as interdependent, relational, caring beings. Core more-than-human science.
- **[Joanna Macy (1929–2025)](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/joanna-macy-in-memoriam/)** — Scholar of Buddhism, systems thinking, deep ecology. Creator of *The Work That Reconnects* — frameworks helping people move from isolation to participation in the web of life. Kalliopeia supported her in her final decade.
- **[Center for Humans and Nature](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/center-for-humans-and-nature/)** — Ideas hub bringing philosophers, ecologists, artists, political scientists, anthropologists together. Digital + print press. Based Libertyville, IL.
- **[The On Being Project](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/the-on-being-project/)** — Krista Tippett's project; meaning, religion, ethics, and human flourishing
- **[The Forum on Religion and Ecology](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/the-forum-on-religion-and-ecology/)** — Yale-based; religion × ecology scholarship
- **[St. Ethelburga's Centre for Reconciliation & Peace](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/stethelburgas/)** — **London** (Bishopsgate). Climate × peace at the intersection of faith. Spiritual ecology programs. Lifelines project: hedgerow/tree planting with diverse communities. ⭐ Note: this is a **London institution** relevant to Amber.
- **[Earth Law Center](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/earth-law-center/)** — Rights of nature legal advocacy
- **[Soul Fire Farm](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/soul-fire-farm/)** — Afro-Indigenous farming and food sovereignty
- **[Cultural Conservancy](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/cultural-conservancy/)** — Indigenous cultural/land sovereignty
- **[Weaving Earth](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/weaving-earth/)** — Nature-based education for young people
- **[Kindle Project](https://kalliopeia.org/grantee-partner/kindle-project/)** — Creative culture change funding

## Events & Programmes

Kalliopeia does **not** run open fellowships or residencies accessible to outsiders. Their model:
- Invite-only grantmaking
- Operating their own media platforms (Emergence, GOP)
- Hosting gatherings and field research (not public-facing)
- Supporting London-based St. Ethelburga's for ecological/spiritual programming in the UK

**For Amber specifically:** No open programs to apply to directly. Engagement is through Emergence Magazine (as writer/contributor) or through connected institutions like St. Ethelburga's (London).
