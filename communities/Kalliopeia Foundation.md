# Kalliopeia Foundation

Philanthropic foundation supporting grantmaking, education, and storytelling at the intersection of ecology, culture, and spirituality. Best known as the founding funder of [[Emergence Magazine]]. Also funds ecological humanities programmes broadly.

## Why Interesting
The funder behind Emergence Magazine. Understanding Kalliopeia helps understand the philanthropic ecosystem around ecological humanities. They fund at the ecology × culture × spirituality intersection — a rare and specific niche.
