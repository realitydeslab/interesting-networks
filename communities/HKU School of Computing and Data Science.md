# HKU School of Computing and Data Science (CDS)

Established July 1, 2024. Brand new school at the University of Hong Kong merging Department of Computer Science + Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science + new Department of AI and Data Science. ~60 faculty, ~800 postgrad students, 15 academic programmes.

**Shanghai Campus:** MOU signed July 2024 with Shanghai Municipal Government. Teaching and Research Site in **Zhangjiang Hi-Tech Park, Pudong**. Building completed December 2025 — just opening. Launching **Tech Immersion Fellowships** for undergrad and taught postgrad.
