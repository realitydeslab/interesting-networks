# Outfest

**Los Angeles LGBTQ+ film festival**, founded 1982. One of the oldest and largest LGBTQ film festivals in the US.

[[Shari Frilot]] served as Co-Director of Programming from 1998 to 2001. She founded **"Platinum Oasis"** during her tenure — Outfest's first cinematic performance installation section, which introduced immersive and performance works to the festival for the first time.
