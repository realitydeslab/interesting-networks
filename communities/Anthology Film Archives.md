# Anthology Film Archives

NYC's premier non-profit dedicated to the preservation, study, and exhibition of film as an art form. Founded 1970 by Jonas Mekas and others. The canonical venue for American avant-garde and experimental cinema. Recurring venue for [[MIX NYC]] (1987, 1992–1994, 2005, 2019); also hosted early queer experimental programming. Archive of rare 16mm prints used in recent MIX "Museum of Mix" events.
