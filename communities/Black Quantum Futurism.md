# Black Quantum Futurism

Interdisciplinary creative practice between **Camae Ayewa** and **Rasheedah Phillips**. Explores intersections of futurism, creative media, DIY aesthetics, and activism in marginalized communities through an "alternative temporal lens." Work focuses on recovery, collection, and preservation of communal memories, histories, and futures. Produces experimental music, exhibitions, writing, films, performances, and community events. Known for the **Community Futures Lab**. 2021 Knight Arts + Tech Fellows.

## Why Interesting
Temporal theory applied to Afrofuturism and community memory work. The "alternative temporal lens" concept is philosophically interesting — it challenges linear time narratives from a diasporic perspective. Peer of [[Stephanie Dinkins]] in the Knight Fellows network.
