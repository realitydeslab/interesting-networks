# HUIS Utrecht (Posthuman Studies)

Humanities Centre for Interdisciplinary Studies at Utrecht University, directed by [[Rosi Braidotti]]. The institutional home of posthuman studies in Europe. Organizes summer schools (After Summer School series), international conferences, and visiting scholars programme. Published major posthumanism journals and books.
