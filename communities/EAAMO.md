# EAAMO — Equity and Access in Algorithms, Mechanisms, and Optimization

Annual conference at the intersection of algorithm design, mechanism design, and social equity. Closely affiliated with [[FAccT]] community. Co-founded by [[Rediet Abebe]] (UC Berkeley). Smaller and more technical than FAccT; strong on economics + CS theory applied to equity problems.

**Why relevant:** FAccT-adjacent; overlapping community. Good secondary venue for work that has technical components + equity framing.
