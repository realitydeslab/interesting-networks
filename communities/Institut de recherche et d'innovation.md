# Institut de recherche et d'innovation (IRI)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during [[Bernard Stiegler]] research. Founded 2006 at Centre Pompidou by Stiegler, continues his research program.

Research institute founded in 2006 at Centre Pompidou by [[Bernard Stiegler]]. Focuses on cultural, scientific, and economic mutations induced by digital technologies. Continues Stiegler's research program posthumously.

## Mission & Objectives
Anticipate, accompany, and analyze mutations in cultural, scientific and economic activities induced by digital technologies. Develop new critical contributive devices.

## Key People
- **[[Bernard Stiegler]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — founder (2006), director until death (2020)
- **Collège Scientifique et Industriel** — current leadership structure

## Key Publications & Output
Research on parentality, urban contribution design, contributive economy, knowledge and technologies.
