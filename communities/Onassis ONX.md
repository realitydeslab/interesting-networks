# Onassis ONX

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via NEW INC deep research. ONX is Extended Realities track partner for NEW INC.

The Onassis Foundation's global platform for art and advanced technologies. Co-founded by [[NEW INC]] and Onassis Foundation USA in 2020. Studios in NYC (390 Broadway, Floor 4) and Athens. Supports immersive, spatial computing, gaming, AI art.

## Background
Co-founded 2020 as joint initiative between NEW INC and Onassis Foundation. Since spun out as independent platform but maintains NEW INC Extended Realities track partnership. Extended Realities track members have priority access to ONX.

## Mission
"A catalyst for digital cultures, supporting genre-defying new media and immersive projects." Covers AI, immersive, spatial computing, gaming, and other emergent time-based media.

## What They Provide
- Technical, curatorial, and professional services
- Capacity-building programs
- Incubation and applied research initiatives
- Acceleration services
- Seed funding
- Exhibition opportunities
- International network (Athens + NYC + European cultural networks)

## Programs
- **NEW INC Extended Realities track partnership** — Priority access for NEW INC members
- **Members & Fellows program** — Own residency/fellowship track
- **Exhibitions** — e.g., TECHNE HOMECOMING (current), Group Hug (game installations by Theo Triantafyllidis, Danielle Brathwaite-Shirley, Tale of Tales)
- **Tribeca Immersive** — partnership with Tribeca Festival (2025 edition)

## Key People
- Leadership from Onassis Foundation USA

## Connected Institutions
- [[NEW INC]] — co-founder, Extended Realities track partner
- Onassis Foundation (parent)
- Onassis Stegi, Athens (sister institution)
- Tribeca Festival (Immersive partner)

## Why Interesting
One of the most institutionally serious XR/immersive platforms globally. Greek/European conceptual tradition meets NYC art-tech infrastructure. The ONX + NEW INC partnership is an interesting model of how to create structured support for XR art with both critical and production dimensions.
