# Ars Industrialis

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during [[Bernard Stiegler]] research. Founded 2005, transitioned to [[Association Épokhè]] after Stiegler's death (2020).

Political and cultural group founded in 2005 by [[Bernard Stiegler]]. Manifesto called for "industrial politics of spirit." Active until Stiegler's death in 2020, then transitioned to [[Association Épokhè]].

## Mission & Objectives
"Industrial politics of spirit" — developing alternatives to industrial destruction of cultural and social bonds. Critique of "disadjustment" in contemporary technological society.

## Key People
- **[[Bernard Stiegler]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — founder and primary theorist
- **Marc Crépon** — co-founder, philosopher
- **George Collins** — co-founder
- **Catherine Perret** — co-founder, art theorist
- **Caroline Stiegler** — co-founder, Stiegler's wife

## Key Publications & Output
- Founding manifesto (2005)
- Updated manifesto (2010)
- "Réenchanter le monde" (2006) — collective book on spiritual value vs industrial populism

## Events & Programmes
- **Entretiens du nouveau monde industriel** — annual conferences
- Regular public meetings and philosophical seminars
- Research on contributive economy and cultural politics
