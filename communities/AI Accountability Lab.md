# AI Accountability Lab (AIAL)

Research group at Trinity College Dublin founded and led by [[Abeba Birhane]] (launched November 2024). Studies AI technologies and their downstream societal impact with the aim of fostering a greater ecology of AI accountability. Focus: justice-oriented auditing, algorithmic bias, AI governance and policy.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created during Abeba Birhane deep research.
- **2025-06-25:** EAISF grant awarded (~€199,978) for justice-oriented AI auditing framework.
- **2024-11-28:** Official launch at Trinity College Dublin.

## Mission
Ensure the AI ecology — from research and product development to regulation — centres public interest, particularly the most marginalised. Covers both large-scale systemic audits and specific model/dataset evaluations.
