# IMMA (Irish Museum of Modern Art)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during The Otolith Group deep research.

Irish Museum of Modern Art, Dublin. Royal Hospital Kilmainham. Director: **Annie Fletcher** (former director of Van Abbemuseum, Eindhoven; curated [[The Otolith Group]] *Xenogenesis* touring exhibition). Runs exhibitions, residencies, and collection programme. Final venue of the major [[The Otolith Group]] *Xenogenesis* exhibition (2023). Strong programming focus on postcolonial and socially engaged contemporary art.

## Why Interesting
Under Annie Fletcher's directorship, IMMA is oriented toward politically committed, research-based contemporary art. Fletcher co-curated *Xenogenesis* — one of the major survey exhibitions of the last decade. Potential route for Amber to encounter international contemporary art in Dublin context.
