# Inflexions: A Journal for Research-Creation

**Open-access, multimedia peer journal** produced by the [[SenseLab]] at Concordia University. Published 11 issues + 1 pop-up (2008–2019) before ceasing. The canonical journal of **research-creation methodology** in Canada and arguably the world. The founding issue ("How is Research-Creation?", 2008) remains the key text defining this methodology.

## Why Important
The first issue's question ("How is Research-Creation?") generated the ongoing institutional and methodological debate in Canadian arts academia about what research-creation is. Inflexions answered it by *being* research-creation: multimedia, non-text-only, process-driven, open access. After ceasing, the 3Ecologies/Immediations book series at [[punctum books]] became the successor publication vehicle.
