# Sarai

Critical media lab at the Centre for the Study of Developing Societies (CSDS), Delhi. Founded 2000 by **[[Raqs Media Collective]]** (Jeebesh Bagchi, Monica Narula, Shuddhabrata Sengupta) and CSDS faculty **Ravi Vasudevan** and **Ravi Sundaram**. One of the most important sites of critical media theory from the Global South in the 2000s.

Focus areas: media urbanism, Cybermohalla practice, intellectual property critique, free software, public domain, language and city, media archaeology, infrastructure, data and law.

Published the foundational **[[Sarai Reader]]** series (9 volumes, 2001–2013) as free/open-access PDFs — one of the earliest experiments in open-access critical theory from Asia. Also publishes BioScope (blind peer-reviewed film/media studies journal) and the urban classic *Trickster City*.

Current projects: media archaeology, infrastructure, data and law, social media histories, climate change.
