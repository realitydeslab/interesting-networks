# China Academy of Art

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Cao Fei deep research. Critical institution in Chinese contemporary art, formerly Zhejiang Academy of Fine Arts.

Premier art institution in China. Home to the New Media Department led by Zhang Peili, considered the father of Chinese video art. Critical node in the development of Chinese digital and experimental art practices.

## Mission & Objectives
Leading art education institution in China, pioneering new media and experimental art practices.

## Key People
- **[[Zhang Peili]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Dean of New Media Department, father of Chinese video art, made first Chinese video artwork "30x30" (1988)
- **[[Cao Fei]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Graduate, studied under Zhang Peili, now internationally prominent digital artist

## Key Contributions
- **Foundational Chinese video art** — Zhang Peili's 30x30 (1988) was first Chinese video art piece
- **New Media Department** — Training ground for generation of Chinese digital artists
- **Bridge between traditional and digital** — Institution spanning traditional Chinese art and contemporary experimental practices

## Events & Programmes
- New Media Department programmes
- Graduate and undergraduate programs in experimental art
