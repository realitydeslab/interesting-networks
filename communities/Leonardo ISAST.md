# Leonardo/ISAST

International Society for the Arts, Sciences, and Technology — founded 1968 by artist Frank Malina. Publisher of *Leonardo* (MIT Press), the leading peer-reviewed journal at the intersection of art, science, and technology. Also publishes *Leonardo Music Journal* and *Leonardo Electronic Almanac (LEA)*. Organizes LASER (Leonardo Art Science Evening Rendezvous) talks globally.
