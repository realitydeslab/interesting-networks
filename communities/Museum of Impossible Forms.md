# Museum of Impossible Forms

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Stub created. Discovered as curatorial collaborator in Helsinki Biennial 2023.

Cultural centre located in Kontula, East Helsinki. Queer-feminist, anticolonial, non-fascist arts platform. "A heterogeneous platform to engage with experimental, marginal, and migrant forms of expression, and as a laboratory for experiences, critical thought, and radical imagination." Run collectively, working for "dignity, diversity, equity, allyship and solidarity" and "a plurality of knowledges and practices in the arts."

Was selected by Joasia Krysa as one of five curatorial collaborators for Helsinki Biennial 2023 — recognized as an important node in the Helsinki critical arts ecosystem.

## Why Interesting
Interesting as a community hub in the Helsinki art scene. Connected to Helsinki Biennial. Potential entry point if Amber has work in the space of feminist/posthuman/more-than-human that would resonate with their programming.
