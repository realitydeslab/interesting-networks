# Delfina Foundation

London-based international residency and research programme. One of the UK's most prestigious artist residency programmes. Hosts themed seasons of international artists in residence in London.

Offers an **Art, Science and Technology Fellowship** (sponsored by Gaia Art Foundation) — notable as this is what Anna Ridler received in 2020.
