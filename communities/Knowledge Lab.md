# Knowledge Lab

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial note created during [[James Evans]] deep research.

Interdisciplinary research lab at the University of Chicago directed by [[James Evans]]. Studies how collectives — human, machine, and hybrid — think, know, and create. Bridges social science, data science, AI, and complexity science. Backed by a $20M NSF APTO grant.

## Mission & Objectives
"Brings together scholars from the social sciences, data science, artificial intelligence, and other fields to foster innovation and push the boundaries of research." Builds partnerships between universities, industry, and government. Uses advanced computational tools to study knowledge processes at scale.

## Key Initiatives
- **Computational Creativity** — can algorithms be truly creative?
- **AI Societies** — emergent creativity of AI collectives
- **Digital Minds** — understanding how AI systems think
- **Complementary AI** — AI that augments rather than replaces
- **Global Innovation Observatory** — AI + big data for tracking worldwide S&T
