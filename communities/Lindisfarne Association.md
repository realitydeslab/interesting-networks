# Lindisfarne Association

Influential 1970s–90s think tank and intentional community founded by cultural historian William Irwin Thompson. Described as a "learning community" focusing on the transformation of culture. Brought together ecologists, mystics, scientists, artists, and philosophers including Gregory Bateson, Francisco Varela, James Lovelock, Stewart Brand, and others. The meeting point of systems theory, ecology, and spirituality before those combinations became mainstream.

## Why Interesting
The Lindisfarne context explains how Varela encountered Gregory Bateson's cybernetics milieu AND how Evan Thompson came to work with him. It's the informal network that seeded contemplative-scientific synthesis. A model of how a small intentional gathering can shape intellectual history.
