# Wihanble S'a Lab (Bard College)

**Wihanble S'a Lab** (also Wihanble S'a Center for Indigenous AI) is the Indigenous AI research center at Bard College, directed by [[Kite (Suzanne Kite)]]. 

**NEH-designated Humanities Research Center on AI** — formally recognized by the National Endowment for the Humanities as a research center focused on AI from Indigenous perspectives.

**Pod for the Abundant Intelligences Research Program** — connected to the broader Abundant Intelligences network of AI research centers.

The center investigates contemporary Lakota ontologies through research-creation, computational media, and performance. It positions Indigenous AI on the forefront of innovative research integrating Indigenous Knowledge systems with artificial intelligence.

*Wihanble* = "dream" in Lakota; *S'a* = a modifier suggesting vividness/redness. The name evokes the Lakota dream knowledge traditions central to Kite's practice.
