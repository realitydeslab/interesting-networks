# Economic Space Agency (ECSA)

Post-blockchain cooperative developing programmable economic spaces — tools for communities to design their own non-capitalist micro-economies. Partners with [[SenseLab]] / [[3 Ecologies Institute]] on the "3E Process Seed Bank" project (2016–2019), which aimed to build a DAO for creative alter-economies using affective intensities (not just tokens) as units of value.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during 3 Ecologies Institute deep research.

## Key Ideas
- **Programmable economic spaces** — beyond simple crypto transactions: customizable spaces where communities design their own governance and value systems
- **Post-blockchain architecture** — peer-to-peer, rhizomatic, lower-cost than traditional blockchain
- **"Commons" and "undercommons"** (after Fred Moten/Stefano Harney) as the organizational foundation
- Smart contracts → "Self-Organizing Propositions (SOPs)" (the term 3e preferred)

## Collaboration with 3E
- Joint events: "Adventure Capital Study Retreat" (Oka, July 2016), "Open Source the Economy" hackathon (Oakland, Nov 2016), "Distributing the Insensible" (Montreal, Dec 2016)
