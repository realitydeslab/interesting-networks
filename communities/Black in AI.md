# Black in AI

Community of Black researchers working in artificial intelligence. Founded 2017 by [[Timnit Gebru]] and [[Rediet Abebe]] after Gebru counted only 5 Black men and herself (the sole Black woman) among 8,500 NeurIPS attendees. Aims to increase presence, visibility, and well-being of Black professionals and leaders in AI.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Timnit Gebru deep research.

## Mission & Objectives
Increase the presence and inclusion of Black researchers in AI. Organizes workshops at major AI conferences, mentorship programs, and community events.

## Key People
- [[Timnit Gebru]] — co-founder
- [[Rediet Abebe]] — co-founder

## Why Interesting
The community that made Gebru's post-Google support network possible. Connected to [[FAccT]], NeurIPS, ICML workshops. Important node in the critical AI ecosystem. Origin story is a perfect example of how structural observation → collective action.
