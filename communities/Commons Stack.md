# Commons Stack

Design and engineering group creating systems to fund and govern public goods using token engineering. Pioneered the **Augmented Bonding Curve (ABC)** — a primary issuance AMM for sustainable community funding — and **Conviction Voting** for continuous preference signaling. Close collaborator with [[BlockScience]] (Michael Zargham is quoted validator). Piloted with Token Engineering Commons (TEC) in 2021: $TEC held value relative to ETH while comparable small-cap DAOs collapsed; common pool received $300K+ in tributes.

## Key People
- Michael Zargham ([[BlockScience]] founder) — key validator and co-designer

## Key Tools
- **Augmented Bonding Curve** — primary issuance AMM with vesting, tributes, and trust scores
- **Conviction Voting** — continuous governance without one-shot votes

## Why Interesting
Mechanism design applied to public goods governance. The closest thing to engineering Elinor Ostrom's commons principles into working tokenomic systems.
