# Forensis

Berlin-based sister agency to [[Forensic Architecture]], founded 2021 by former FA researchers. Applies forensic architecture methodologies to European and historical cases, with particular focus on colonial histories and European far-right violence.

## Key Projects
- **Namibia colonial genocide** (with Nama and Ovaherero civil society groups) — reconstructing sites of the German colonial genocide (1904–1908), including multiple burial grounds and the Shark Island concentration camp. Using oral history from descendants as testimony.

## Key People
- Former [[Forensic Architecture]] researchers who spun off

## Why Interesting
Represents the maturation and geographic expansion of the FA model — independent units applying the same methodology in new contexts. The Namibia work in particular pushes the "situated testimony" methodology into historical time: testimony transmitted across generations through oral tradition.
