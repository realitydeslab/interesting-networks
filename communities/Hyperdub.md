# Hyperdub

Electronic music record label founded by Steve Goodman (Kode9) in 2004. Crucial bridge between CCRU theory-fiction and contemporary electronic music culture. Known for dubstep, UK funky, and experimental electronic music with theoretical sophistication.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Mark Fisher deep research. Important connection between theory and electronic music.

## Mission & Objectives

Experimental electronic music label combining cutting-edge sound with theoretical sophistication. Applies CCRU-influenced concepts to music production and curation. 

Founded by Steve Goodman (Kode9), who was CCRU member and befriended Mark Fisher at University of Warwick.
