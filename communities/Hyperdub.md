# Hyperdub

Record label founded by [[Steve Goodman]] (Kode9) in 2004. Originally a webzine in 2000.

## Key Artists
- Burial, DJ Rashad, Zomby, Fatima Al Qadiri, Laurel Halo

## Connections
- [[CCRU]] — Kode9's philosophical background
- [[Steve Goodman]] — founder
- UK bass/dubstep scene → theory crossover

## Why Interesting
Cultural institution that bridges CCRU theory and experimental music. Label artists often engage with themes of posthumanism, hauntology, and speculative futures through sound.
