# Weizenbaum Institut

Berlin-based interdisciplinary internet and digitalization research institute. Named after Joseph Weizenbaum, creator of ELIZA (1966) and author of *Computer Power and Human Reason* (1976) — one of the earliest critical perspectives on AI. Funded by the Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Climate Action (Germany). Multi-university consortium (TU Berlin, FU Berlin, HU Berlin, Universität der Künste, ESMT).

Research focus areas: digital technologies and society; digital markets and public platforms; organization of knowledge; digital infrastructures and democracy.
