# CREATE — Steckler Center for Responsible, Ethical, and Accessible Technology (UCI)

Center at UC Irvine's Donald Bren School of ICS, established 2020 through a gift from Vincent and Amanda Steckler. Directed by [[Paul Dourish]]. Promotes research and education into creating "technological futures that produce positive change in the world," focused on principles of equity, accountability, and care.

Home to scholars spanning informatics, CS, STS, law, anthropology, media studies, sociology, political science, economics. Research asks: what barriers exist to equitable technological participation? What principles should guide platform design? How should infrastructures be reconfigured to center justice and care?

## Key People

## Events & Programmes

## Why Interesting
CREATE is a model for what a "responsible AI" research center looks like when it's grounded in STS and critical theory rather than just ethics principles or technical safety. Dourish brings 30 years of critical computing scholarship to the institutional framing. The seed fund mechanism is interesting — worth monitoring for open calls.
