# Future Histories Studio

Research lab and studio founded by [[Stephanie Dinkins]] at [Stony Brook University](https://www.stonybrook.edu/). Investigates equitable AI ecosystems through community collaboration, speculative design, oral history, and storytelling. The academic home of the N'TOO project and Dinkins' broader community-centered AI research.

## Why Interesting
Model for what a university-based lab looks like when it combines community engagement + speculative design + actual AI building. Not a critique lab — a making lab. Could be a model for Amber's research group at Oxford.
