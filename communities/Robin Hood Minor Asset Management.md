# Robin Hood Minor Asset Management

A decentralized hedge fund and artistic/financial experiment (2012–2016) founded by [[Akseli Virtanen]], Tere Vadén, and collaborators in Finland. Used algorithmic mimicry of Wall Street (the "Parasite" algorithm) to extract value from financial markets and redistribute it to cultural and political projects — essentially open-sourcing finance as an art/activist practice. The precursor to [[Economic Space Agency (ECSA)]].

## Key Facts
- **Founded:** January 2012, Finland
- **Famous moment:** Presented at Kassel Documenta(13), September 2012 — "famously annoyed everyone"
- **Kone Foundation grant:** $100k in September 2013
- **Dissolved into ECSA:** ~2015–2016 as team moved to California

## Why Interesting
The conceptual lineage: Robin Hood → ECSA is one of the most interesting arcs in alter-economy thinking. It moves from "parasitically extracting from capitalism" to "building new infrastructure outside capitalism" — a deepening of the political project.
