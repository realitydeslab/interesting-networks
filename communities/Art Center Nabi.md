# Art Center Nabi

Korea's first and most prominent digital/media art institution. Founded 2000 in Seoul. Hosted ISEA 2025 as general organizer. Director: **Soh Yeong Roh**.

## Vibe
Operating around three C's: **Critique, Creation, Community**. 25 years old in 2025. Critical observer of contemporary society + supporter of new media art production + community builder for artists, scientists, engineers.

## Key Activities
- Incubation and production of new digital artworks
- Exhibitions and commissions
- Education programs (NABI SCHOOL)
- Archive exhibition "Beyond the Wall" — 25 years of Nabi history (shown ISEA 2025)
- General host of ISEA 2025 (30th international symposium)

## Key People
- **Soh Yeong Roh** — Director. General Chair ISEA 2025. Central figure in Korean media art.
- Art Center Nabi Curatorial Team — Soyeong Ghim, Yeonjin Kim, Jinyoung Yang (curators)
