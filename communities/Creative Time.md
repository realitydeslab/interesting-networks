# Creative Time

New York-based public art organization that commissions ambitious public and socially engaged art projects. Known for major commissions with artists across the globe.

Relevant to interesting-networks: **commissioned *Cosmologyscape*** (2024) by [[Kite (Suzanne Kite)]] and [[Alisha B Wormsley]] — a participatory public sculpture project in Downtown Brooklyn using dreams as data for ML-generated mosaic benches.

Creative Time holds annual **Open Calls** (e.g., 2022-23 Open Call selected Kite/Wormsley). Projects involve public space, digital and participatory elements, and community engagement.
