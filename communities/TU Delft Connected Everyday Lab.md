Research lab at TU Delft Industrial Design Engineering focusing on IoT, connected technologies, and everyday computing experiences. Part of the broader design research community at one of Europe's leading design engineering schools.

## Mission & Objectives
Explores how connected technologies integrate into everyday life, with particular attention to more-than-human perspectives and the agency of IoT devices and systems.
