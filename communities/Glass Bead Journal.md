# Glass Bead Journal

Online theory journal at the intersection of art, philosophy, and computation. Based in Paris. Produces long-form academic essays with a strong interest in speculative philosophy, feminist theory, and the politics of abstraction. Notable for commissioning unusual hybrid work between art/design and rigorous philosophy.
