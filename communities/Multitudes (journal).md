# Multitudes (journal)

**Multitudes** is a French political philosophy and social theory journal published in Paris since 2000. Founded as a continuation of the *Futur Antérieur* (1990–1998) journal. Editorial board includes [[Antonio Negri]], [[Maurizio Lazzarato]], and many post-operaismo and French radical theorists. Still active and publishing.

The journal sits at the intersection of **post-operaismo, Spinozist political theory, ecology, feminism, digital commons, and biopolitics**. It is the primary publication venue for the Franco-Italian radical left that coalesced around the Negri/Hardt project. Published quarterly in French; occasional English translations available online.
