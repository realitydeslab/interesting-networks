# Steve Jobs Archive (SJA) Fellowship

One-year nonresidential programme for young creators (21-26) at intersection of technology and liberal arts. Created by Laurene Powell Jobs. Nomination-based — very selective. Inaugural cohort 2023.

## Structure
- Stipend + individualized mentor + peer community
- Regular virtual and in-person events
- Nomination-based (can't self-apply directly — nominated by institutions and individuals)
- US residents only, ages 21-26

## Key People
- [[Wendi Yan]] — 2023 inaugural fellow
- Jessica Shand (MIT Media Lab) — 2023 fellow
- Tom Mahota (ceramics × tech) — 2023 fellow

## Why Interesting
Very small, very selective. The annual cohort reveals who the tech-philanthropy world considers promising at the art × tech intersection. Worth tracking each year's fellows.
