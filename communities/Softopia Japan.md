# Softopia Japan

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via IAMAS deep research.

Gifu Prefecture's technology park complex in Ogaki. IAMAS relocated here in 2014. Context for understanding IAMAS's institutional position: it's simultaneously an art-technology graduate school AND an anchor institution for regional technology-industry development. The Softopia Japan ecosystem includes other technology companies and research labs. The geographic intentionality (not Tokyo) connects to Gifu's strategy of becoming a technology center through unusual cultural-industrial partnerships.

## Why Interesting
Context for IAMAS. The art-industry integration model embedded in Softopia Japan is interesting as a policy experiment — can a serious media arts graduate school also function as regional economic infrastructure? After 30 years, the answer seems to be: yes, with caveats.
