# Ignota Books

London-based experimental publisher co-founded by [[Ben Vickers]] and Sarah Shin. Publishes at the intersection of occult practice, ecology, philosophy, and experimental art. Not typical art publishing — closer to Urbanomic in intellectual ambition but with a distinctly esoteric, ecology-conscious, feminist register.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Serpentine Galleries deep research.

## Mission & Vibe
"Building a library of books that can help us navigate an uncertain future" — draws on sacred/esoteric traditions, ecology science (Lynn Margulis), experimental music (Pauline Oliveros), feminist philosophy, and contemporary art. Annual *Ignota Diary* (occult planner). Hosts events at venues like St James's Church Piccadilly and Camden Arts Centre.
