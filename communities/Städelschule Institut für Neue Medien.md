# Städelschule Institut für Neue Medien

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via Christa Sommerer & Laurent Mignonneau deep research.

Postgraduate program at the [Städelschule (Städel School of Fine Arts)](https://www.staedelschule.de/), Frankfurt am Main, Germany. The Institut für Neue Medien was run by [[Peter Weibel]] in the early 1990s. Under Weibel it became the most important training ground for European artists working at the intersection of critical theory and new media. [[Christa Sommerer and Laurent Mignonneau]] both completed postgraduate studies here 1991–93 — this is where they met and began working together. Other artists from the Weibel era include much of the European media art canon.

## Why Interesting
Origin point for a generation of European media artists. The combination of critical theory (Weibel was one of the most rigorous theorists in the field) + technical access + Frankfurt's intellectual culture created a distinctive strand of practice. Understanding the Städelschule pipeline helps explain why Weibel's later work at ZKM had such theoretical depth — he brought his students with him.
