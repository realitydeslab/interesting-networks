# Biofilia – Base for Biological Arts

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Ionat Zurr deep research.

Biological art lab established by [[Oron Catts]] and [[Ionat Zurr]] at the School of Art, Design and Architecture, [Aalto University](https://www.aalto.fi/), Helsinki (2012–2013). First biological art lab in Finland / the Nordic region. Modelled on [[SymbioticA]] at UWA Perth. Both Catts and Zurr were Visiting Professors during this period.

## Why Interesting
Proof that the SymbioticA model (artists embedded in biology/science infrastructure at a university) can be exported and established elsewhere. Shows TC&A's institution-building skills extend beyond Perth. Nordic biological art scene.

## Key People
- [[Ionat Zurr]] — co-founder, Visiting Professor (2012–2013)
- [[Oron Catts]] — co-founder, Visiting Professor (2012–2013)
