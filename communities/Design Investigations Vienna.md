# Design Investigations — dieAngewandte Vienna

Speculative and critical design department at the [University of Applied Arts Vienna (dieAngewandte)](https://www.dieangewandte.at/en/). Led by **[[Anab Jain]]** (of [[Superflux]]) since 2016. One of the premier European academic programmes for speculative design.

"For the curious and the critically engaged. For people who want to face the complexities and uncertainties of our world head-on. For the responsible radicals who want to challenge and change the world around them."

Student projects explore speculative monuments, post-capitalist futures, ecological restoration, and more.
