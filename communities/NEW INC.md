# NEW INC

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note. Discovered through **[[Lauren Lee McCarthy]]** research as Y12 member and **[[Kyle McDonald]]** connection.

New Museum's art and technology incubator. Year-long membership program providing studio space, mentorship, and community for artists working at intersection of art and technology. Strong alumni network includes leading practitioners in critical art-tech.

## Background
Founded 2014 as New Museum's initiative to support art and technology innovation. Located in NYC, provides platform for experimental projects that might not find support in traditional art or tech contexts.

## Program Structure
- **Year-long membership tracks** — Different focuses each year
- **Studio space** — Physical workspace in NYC
- **Mentorship** — Industry and art world advisors  
- **Demo festivals** — Public presentations of work
- **Alumni network** — Ongoing community
