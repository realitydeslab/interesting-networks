# Irreversible Entanglements

American free jazz quintet formed in 2015 at a Musicians Against Brutality event following the police shooting of Akai Gurley. Members: **Camae Ayewa** ([[Moor Mother]], vocals), **Tcheser Holmes** (drums), **Aquiles Navarro** (trumpet), **Keir Neuringer** (saxophone), **Luke Stewart** (bass). Labels: International Anthem, Don Giovanni, Impulse! Records.

## Why Interesting
The sonic practice of [[Black Quantum Futurism]]: liberation-oriented free jazz that treats improvisation as temporal disruption. Performed at documenta fifteen (Kassel, 2022). Also the live vehicle for the BQF collective's time-and-community framework. The name references quantum entanglement — connections that persist across time and space.
