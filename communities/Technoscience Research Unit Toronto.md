# Technoscience Research Unit, Toronto

Research unit at University of Toronto (Faculty of Information + Women & Gender Studies Institute). Directed by M. Murphy (Michelle Murphy). Since 2007, hub for critical/creative research on social justice and technoscience. Focuses on data justice, environmental justice, feminist/decolonial STS. Major sponsor of [[Catalyst Feminism Theory Technoscience]] 2017-19.

## Key People
- **M. Murphy** (Michelle Murphy) — director; works on chemical exposures, feminist data infrastructure, settler colonialism + health

## Why Interesting
Data justice + feminist STS institutional node at U of Toronto. Michelle Murphy is a key figure in feminist technoscience and data activism. Relevant to Amber's critical computing and data justice threads.
