# Cambridge Centre for Science and Policy (CSaP)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-12:** Initial deep research. Full profile created.
- CSaP's Policy Fellowship programme has connected 670 Policy Fellows with 2,600+ researchers through 15,000+ 1:1 meetings since 2009.
- 160+ workshops addressing policy challenges.
- UK Government Chief Scientific Adviser Dame Angela McLean endorses CSaP.

CSaP creates opportunities for policy professionals and academics to learn from each other. Based at Cambridge, it pioneered approaches to bring science and policy together through fellowships, policy workshops, and professional development. Its strength is the **network** — a curated ecosystem of policymakers and researchers who trust each other.

---

## Mission & Objectives

CSaP's mission is to improve public policy making through more effective use of evidence and expertise. It:
- Forges relationships based on mutual understanding and trust between academics and policymakers
- Builds networks of people with shared values of intellectual curiosity and public service
- Curates open and exploratory conversations to address public policy priorities

---

## Programmes

### Policy Fellowships
- Senior policymakers spend time at Cambridge meeting academic experts in tailored 1:1 conversations
- 670 fellows to date
- 2,600 researchers involved
- 15,000+ individual meetings

### Policy Workshops
- Convene academics and policymakers around specific policy challenges
- 160+ workshops since 2009

### Professional Development
- Supports early- and mid-career scientists transitioning into science-policy advisory roles
