# Infrastructure Studies

Interdisciplinary field examining the material conditions, social relations, and cultural significance of technological infrastructure systems.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created during Iris Long deep research. Identified as important field connecting technology materiality with cultural critique.

## Domain Summary
Field that examines technological infrastructure not as transparent background but as cultural, political, and material systems worthy of critical investigation. Includes studies of internet cables, data centers, power grids, transportation networks, and other systems that enable technological society while often remaining invisible.
