# MUAC-UNAM (Museo Universitario Arte Contemporáneo)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-01:** Stub created during deep research on [[IIE-UNAM]].

Mexico's most important contemporary art museum, housed on the UNAM campus (Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México). Opened 2008. [[Cuauhtémoc Medina]] is chief curator and also runs [[IIE-UNAM]]. MUAC is the exhibition arm; IIE is the research arm — they function as one integrated system.

## Key People
- **[[Cuauhtémoc Medina]]** — chief curator (and simultaneously heads IIE-UNAM research institute)
- Sol Henaro — curator, documentation/archive
- Pilar García — curator
- Julio García Murillo — curator; also lecturer at Centro. Diseño, Cine, Televisión
- Jaime González Solís — curator
- Getsemaní Guevara — curator

## Key Contributions
- **Centro de Documentación Arkheia** — archive of Mexican avant-garde movements, artist collectives, ephemeral practices
- **MUAC Cuadernos** — short-form publications co-produced with IIE. Free PDFs. Includes [*Museo digital: Futuros y posibilidades*](https://muac.unam.mx/assets/docs/cuaderno_muac-museo_digital.pdf) (2019)

## Recent Events
- **"Los Grupos" exhibition (summer 2025)** — major retrospective curated by Amieva/Montero/Medina on Mexican 1970s-80s artist collectives

## Why Interesting
MUAC is Mexico City's institutional center for politically engaged contemporary art. The IIE-MUAC dual structure means academic theory directly feeds exhibition practice.
