# SWR Experimentalstudio Freiburg

Experimental electronic music studio run by SWR (Südwestrundfunk, public broadcaster). One of Europe's leading centers for electroacoustic music production. Based in Freiburg, Germany (~30 min by train from Karlsruhe).

Joint partner with [[Hertzlab ZKM]] in the **Giga-Hertz Award** — production award winners can choose their residency between ZKM Hertzlab (Karlsruhe) or SWR Experimentalstudio (Freiburg). This makes the SWR studio a parallel entry point to the Karlsruhe media art ecosystem for sound artists.

## Why Interesting
European electroacoustic music production tradition, co-hosting one of the field's significant prizes. Less well-known than ZKM internationally — but may be easier entry point for sound/electronic music focused artists. Close geographic partnership with ZKM.
