# Rachel Carson Center for Environment and Society

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during AURA deep research. Munich-based European hub for environmental humanities; connected to AURA through Ursula Münster (Guest Professor at AURA).

International, interdisciplinary center for environmental humanities at Ludwig Maximilian University Munich. Named after Rachel Carson (*Silent Spring*, 1962). One of Europe's leading hubs for environmental humanities, with regular fellowship programme for international scholars.

## Key People
- **Ursula Münster** — Professor at RCC; was Guest Professor at AURA. Environmental humanities, South Asian political ecology.
- **Christof Mauch** — Director. Environmental history.
