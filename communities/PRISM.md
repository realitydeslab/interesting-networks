# PRISM — Partnership for Research Into Sentient Machines

## ⚡ Recent Updates

A UK-based non-profit preparing society for a future with conscious, or seemingly conscious, AI. Founded and led by **[[Will Millership]]**, PRISM sits at the critical junction of AI consciousness research, policy, and public engagement. They produce the **"Exploring Machine Consciousness" podcast**, run regular **Conscious AI Meetups**, publish the **Digital Minds Newsletter** (with [[Bradford Saad]] and [[Lucius Caviola]]), and co-partner the **[[Cambridge Digital Minds]] Fellowship** — the very fellowship Amber is applying to.

## Mission & Objectives

### Three Pillars
1. **Foster global collaboration on AI consciousness** — Build a coalition of academic, industry, and government partners to ensure coordinated, responsible exploration of machine consciousness
2. **Promote safe and aligned AI development** — Advocate for AI technologies that prioritise human safety, ethical alignment, and societal values
3. **Spark public dialogue about AI consciousness** — Raise awareness about risks and opportunities of conscious (or perceived conscious) AI for policymakers, industry leaders, and the public

### Why Now
PRISM's framing (from their website): Rapid AI advances have raised critical questions about machine sentience. While most agree current AIs are unlikely to be conscious ([Butlin et al., 2023](https://arxiv.org/abs/2308.08708)), many prominent neuroscientists suggest brain features responsible for consciousness could be reproduced in AI ([Butlin & Lappas, 2025](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.07290)). Society is largely unprepared for the ethical, legal, and societal implications.

### Principles for Responsible Research
PRISM has adopted principles from [Butlin & Lappas (2025)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.07290) — "Principles for Responsible AI Consciousness Research":
1. **Objectives** — Prioritise understanding/assessing AI consciousness to prevent mistreatment and understand benefits/risks
2. **Development** — Pursue conscious AI development only if it contributes to the above and minimises suffering risk
3. **Phased approach** — Gradual progression with strict safety protocols and external expert consultation
4. **Knowledge sharing** — Transparent protocols balanced against preventing misuse
5. **Communication** — No overconfident/misleading statements; acknowledge uncertainties

Open letter for endorsement hosted on [Conscium's website](https://conscium.com/open-letter-guiding-research-into-machine-consciousness/#form).
