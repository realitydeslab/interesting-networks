# IAMAS — Institute of Advanced Media Arts and Sciences

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research.

Japan's premier graduate school at the intersection of media arts and sciences. Located in Ogaki, Gifu Prefecture (not Tokyo — the geographic intentionality signals seriousness). Founded 1996. Equivalent to: ITP at NYU (US), Media Lab MIT (US), ZKM (Germany) as an educational institution. Two departments: Media Arts and Sciences.

**The IAMAS → Rhizomatiks pipeline is significant.** [[Daito Manabe]] and [[Motoi Ishibashi]] both studied at IAMAS — their meeting there led directly to co-founding Rhizomatiks in 2006. IAMAS consistently produces Japan's most significant media artists and technologists.

## Key Alumni
- [[Daito Manabe]] — co-founder of Rhizomatiks
- [[Motoi Ishibashi]] — CTO of Rhizomatiks; met Manabe here
- Many other significant Japanese media artists

## Why Interesting
IAMAS is Japan's answer to ITP/NYU — a rigorous, technically advanced, artistically serious graduate program. Its Gifu location (not Tokyo) creates a self-selecting community. The IAMAS → Rhizomatiks model shows how a graduate institution can create a creative technology ecosystem. Worth tracking as a residency or collaboration site, and as a source of important Japanese art-tech practitioners.
