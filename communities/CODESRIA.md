# CODESRIA — Council for the Development of Social Science Research in Africa

Pan-African social science research network, based in Dakar, Senegal. Founded 1973. The oldest and most important continent-wide African social science institution.

[[Achille Mbembe]] served as Executive Secretary 1996–2000 — a period that shaped his engagement with pan-African intellectual networks.

## Mission
Promote rigorous, independent social science research in Africa. Runs research networks, institutes, fellowships, and publications. Publishes major pan-African journals.

## Key Programmes
- Annual Gender Institute
- Meaning-Making Research Initiatives (MRI) — 2026 call open
- AFRIAK research grants
- The CODESRIA Bookshop
