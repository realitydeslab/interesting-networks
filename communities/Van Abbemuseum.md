# Van Abbemuseum

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during The Otolith Group deep research.

Major contemporary art museum in Eindhoven, Netherlands. Originating venue for [[The Otolith Group]]'s *Xenogenesis* exhibition (May 2019), which subsequently toured 7 venues across 4 continents. Known for politically engaged and research-based programming. Important European hub for postcolonial contemporary art. Annie Fletcher was director here before moving to IMMA Dublin.

## Why Interesting
An important node in the European contemporary art ecosystem that supported the major international survey of The Otolith Group's work. Relevant for understanding the institutional network around research-based, politically engaged moving image work.
