# Sequence Press

Independent publishing house based in New York City. Co-publishing partner with **[[Urbanomic]]** for major works in speculative philosophy and theory.

## Description
Philosophy and theory publisher that collaborates extensively with Robin Mackay's Urbanomic on major philosophical works. Hosts events at Miguel Abreu Gallery in NYC.

## Key Publications
- Co-published *Fanged Noumena* by Nick Land with Urbanomic
- Co-published *Intelligence and Spirit* (2018) and *Abducting the Outside* (2023) by Reza Negarestani with Urbanomic
- *Torture Concrete: Jean-Luc Moulène and the Protocol of Abstraction* by Reza Negarestani (2014)

## Key People
- **[[Robin Mackay]]** — Co-publishing partner through Urbanomic
- **[[Reza Negarestani]]** — Major published author
- **[[Nick Land]]** — Published collected writings

## Key Venues
- **[[Miguel Abreu Gallery]]** — Regular venue for book launches and events

## Key Events
- Yuk Hui, *Post-Europe* launch (April 2025)
- Omnicide II event with Jason Bahbak Mohaghegh & Reza Negarestani (January 2024)
- TransLatin Diagonals conversation (June 2023)
- Alain Badiou lecture (November 2019)

## Why Interesting
NYC arm of the Urbanomic publishing network. Provides crucial US distribution and event infrastructure for speculative philosophy. The Miguel Abreu Gallery partnership creates a unique theory-art venue circuit in NYC.
