# Sequence Press

NYC-based publisher closely associated with [[Miguel Abreu Gallery]] (listed in gallery navigation). The American arm of the [[Urbanomic]] publishing ecosystem. Co-publishes books with Urbanomic for the US market and organizes events at the gallery. Their launches have featured [[Yuk Hui]] (*Post-Europe*, April 2025), [[Reza Negarestani]] (*Omnicide II*, January 2024), Quentin Meillassoux (*The Number and the Siren*, 2012), Alain Badiou, and others.

## Why Interesting
The US publishing node for speculative philosophy, accelerationism, and cosmotechnics. Events held at Miguel Abreu Gallery function as the NYC salon for this intellectual community.
