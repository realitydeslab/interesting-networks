# ZKM | Hertzlab

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created during ZKM deep research. Main research/production arm of ZKM. Key residency/fellowship entry point.

---

The research and production platform of [[ZKM Center for Art and Media]]. Established 2017, merging the former Institute for Visual Media and Institute for Music and Acoustics. Named for Heinrich Hertz, who discovered electromagnetic waves at Karlsruhe Technical University in 1886.

A transdisciplinary lab operating at interface of media arts, science, and society. Studios, ateliers, hackspaces. Regular "Open Hertzlab" public events.

## Six Research Themes
1. Human-machine relations (AI, chatbots, automation)
2. Ecology / environment (climate, natural systems)
3. Future narratives (non-European/non-US storytelling)
4. Embodied interaction (body, movement, senses)
5. Ethics of technology
6. Media archaeology and digital preservation

## Events & Programmes
- **Guest Artist Programme** — 600+ artists since 1989; technical+financial support; proposal-based
- **[Giga-Hertz Award](https://zkm.de/en/projects/giga-hertz-award)** — annual; Giga-Hertz Production Awards (2× €5k + residency) — open call for electronic/electroacoustic music
- **Joint ZKM/IRCAM Residency** — with [IRCAM Paris](https://www.ircam.fr/); project-based; acoustics + visual media
- **Open Hertzlab** — monthly public sessions (e.g., "MARS! Mental Health", Feb 2026)

## Why Interesting
The working lab inside ZKM where residencies actually happen. More accessible than the institutional front door. Open Hertzlab events are free entry points. The guest artist programme has hosted 600+ artists — very open to international applications. Amber's computational creativity + posthuman + embodied work is a direct fit for the six themes.
