# Zero Books

Independent publishing imprint (2009-present) specializing in accessible critical theory and cultural criticism. Co-founded by Mark Fisher and Tariq Goddard to combat anti-intellectualism in contemporary culture. Part of John Hunt Publishing/Collective Ink.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Mark Fisher deep research. Important independent theory publisher.

## Mission & Objectives

Founded to publish critical thinking and philosophy accessible to general readers while maintaining intellectual rigor. Goal to "reinvent the left" through publications that "make readers uncomfortable" using critical theory.

Mission to combat trend of anti-intellectualism in contemporary culture.

**Founders:**
- **[[Mark Fisher]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Co-founded 2009, left 2014 to start Repeater Books

## Key Publications & Output

**Breakthrough Books:**
- **Capitalist Realism: Is There No Alternative?** (Mark Fisher, 2009) — Generation-defining analysis of neoliberal ideology
- **Kill All Normies** (Angela Nagle, 2017) — Analysis of online culture wars
- **In the Dust of This Planet** (Eugene Thacker) — Horror philosophy
- **Ghosts of My Life** (Mark Fisher, 2014) — Cultural hauntology

**Series:**
- **Neglected or Misunderstood** (2017-) — Covering left-wing theorists like Shulamith Firestone, Theodor Adorno

## Why Interesting

Zero Books demonstrated that there was significant appetite for rigorous cultural criticism that spoke to contemporary conditions. Their success with *Capitalist Realism* and other titles showed how independent publishing could achieve major cultural influence.

Relevant to understanding how alternative intellectual infrastructure gets built and sustained.
