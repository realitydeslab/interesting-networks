# Center for Humanities Research (CHR), University of the Western Cape

The [Center for Humanities Research (CHR)](https://www.uwc.ac.za/) at the [University of the Western Cape (UWC)](https://www.uwc.ac.za/), Cape Town, South Africa. Directed by Premesh Lalu. One of Africa's leading humanities research centres, working on postcolonial/decolonial theory, archives, memory, and the politics of knowledge in the Global South. Since November 2024, Erich Hörl holds an Extraordinary Professorship here — creating a distinctive German media philosophy–South African postcolonial theory axis.

## ⚡ Recent Updates

- **2024:** Hörl and Premesh Lalu (CHR Director) co-authored "Unseating Mastery: The University and the Promise of the New" (*Theory, Culture & Society*, 2024).

## Background

UWC was founded during apartheid as a university for "Coloured" people (in the apartheid classification system) and has historically been a site of resistance and critical thought. The CHR carries this critical heritage forward as a research institute focused on postcolonial and decolonial theory, archival practices, and the university in the Global South.

The CHR has strong international connections and hosts visiting researchers. The Hörl appointment creates a new channel between European media/technology philosophy and African decolonial humanities.
