# Verso Books

The world's largest independent radical publisher. London-based (with a New York office), founded in 1970 as the publishing arm of *New Left Review*. The dominant press for Marxist and critical theory in the English-speaking world. Authors include Perry Anderson, Fredric Jameson, David Harvey, Angela Davis, Slavoj Žižek, Donna Haraway, [[McKenzie Wark]], and hundreds of others. The imprint where serious left intellectual work gets published and legitimized.

## Why Interesting
Verso is the key institution for understanding who counts in the English-language radical tradition. The list of who does and doesn't publish with them is itself a map of the intellectual left. For anyone in critical theory or political economy adjacent fields, understanding Verso's commissioning patterns is understanding what's legitimate in the tradition. Also: the Verso blog is one of the best free sources of serious radical intellectual content.
