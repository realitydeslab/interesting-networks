# Berggruen Institute

Global think tank founded by Nicolas Berggruen, focused on the great transformations reshaping politics, economics, and society. Awards the annual [Berggruen Prize for Philosophy and Culture](https://www.berggruen.org/prize/) ($1M). Runs research centres in LA, Beijing, Venice, Athens, and online.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial note created during Yuk Hui deep research.

## Mission & Objectives
"Ideas to shape the future." Bridges governance, philosophy, technology, and culture at global scale. Significant focus on non-Western thought, planetary governance, and technology futures.

## Key Publications & Output
- **Berggruen Prize for Philosophy and Culture** — annual $1M prize. Past winners include Martha Nussbaum, Paul Ricoeur, Ruth Bader Ginsburg equivalent in philosophy. Being on the jury (chairing it) = extraordinary influence.
- **Noema Magazine** — <https://www.noemamag.com/> — Berggruen's ideas magazine.
- **Berggruen Press** — publishes *Proxima Kosmos* and other volumes.
