Housed within the Leverhulme Centre for the Future of Intelligence at the Institute for Technology and Humanity, University of Cambridge. Research on AI consciousness, AI welfare, moral status of artificial systems.

## Digital Minds Fellowship
- **What:** Fully funded 7-day residential programme at Jesus College, Cambridge
- **When:** August 3–9, 2026
- **Cohort:** 15 participants
- **Deadline:** ⚠️ **March 27, 2026**
- **Apply:** [outpaced.substack.com](https://outpaced.substack.com/p/apply-for-the-digital-minds-fellowship) / [EA Forum](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/NRdWZKqGyNpsXRQfJ/apply-for-the-digital-minds-fellowship-aug-3-9-cambridge)
- **Partners:** Rethink Priorities, PRISM
- **Covers:** accommodation, meals, travel, all programme costs

### Programme Structure
1. **Philosophical & Technical Foundations** (2 days) — led by Derek Shiller (Rethink Priorities). LLM architectures, consciousness frameworks, moral status.
2. **Societal Implications & Strategy** (2 days) — led by Lucius Caviola. Scenario planning, policy, public communication.
3. **Project Scoping & Career Planning** (1 day)
4. **Digital Minds Strategy Workshop** (Aug 8-9) — fellows invited alongside leading experts

### Key People
