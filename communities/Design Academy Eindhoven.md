# Design Academy Eindhoven

One of Europe's most prestigious and intellectually rigorous design schools, located in Eindhoven, Netherlands. Known for a conceptual, critical, and socially engaged approach to design that goes well beyond commercial practice. The MA programmes (Information Design, Geo—Design, Context and Conceptual Design, Social Design) consistently produce graduates who become leading figures in speculative and critical design.
