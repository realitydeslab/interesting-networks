# IDFA DocLab

The interactive and immersive non-fiction programme of the International Documentary Film Festival Amsterdam (IDFA). Presents cutting-edge interactive, VR, AR, and installation-based documentary work. Strong curatorial vision — focuses on "phenomenal friction," experiential storytelling, and new forms of non-fiction.

## Key People
- [[Jiabao Li]] — premiered Squeeker: The Mouse Coach (2023)

## Topics
- Interactive documentary
- Immersive non-fiction (VR/AR/XR)
- Art × technology installations
- Experiential storytelling

## Events & Programmes
- Annual programme within IDFA (November, Amsterdam)
- Exhibition at Flemish Cultural Center de Brakke Grond

## Why Interesting
Bridge between documentary/cinema world and interactive art/tech world. Interesting venue for work that treats installations as cinema — a different audience than the usual art-tech circuit. Worth tracking for anyone whose work has a narrative/documentary dimension.
