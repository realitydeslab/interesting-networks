# Pearl Lam Galleries

Contemporary art gallery with spaces in Hong Kong and Shanghai, founded by Pearl Lam. Focuses on bridging Eastern and Western contemporary art — one of the most significant platforms for East-West artistic dialogue. Represents A.A. Murakami, among other international and Chinese artists.

## Why Interesting
Key node in the pan-Asian contemporary art circuit. Pearl Lam herself is a major cultural entrepreneur in the Hong Kong/Shanghai art world. Gallery connects to M+ museum ecosystem.
