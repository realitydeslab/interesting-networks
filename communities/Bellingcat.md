# Bellingcat

Open-source investigative journalism collective founded in 2014 by Eliot Higgins. Pioneered the use of open-source intelligence (OSINT) — satellite imagery, social media analysis, geolocation, video verification — to investigate war crimes, disinformation, and state violence. Parallel practice to [[Forensic Architecture]] but from a journalism rather than art/architecture perspective. Key collaborator with FA on multiple investigations (e.g., El Junquito raid, Venezuela, 2018).
