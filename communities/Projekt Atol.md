# Projekt Atol

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Discovered as lead institution of [[DATAS Residency]]. Created stub.

Slovenian media arts organisation based in Ljubljana. Lead partner of the DATAS – The Data and the Sovereign residency project (EU Creative Europe). Has a fab-lab with electronics, woodworking, and metalworking tools; AV and rendering infrastructure. Connected to [osmo/za](https://osmo.si/) artist-run space in Ljubljana. Runs artist residencies focused on AI, network infrastructures, and critical computing.
