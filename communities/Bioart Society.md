# Bioart Society

Helsinki-based association working at the intersection of art, science and society. Founded May 2008, 150+ members internationally. Runs Ars Bioarctica (art & science residency at Kilpisjärvi Biological Station, sub-arctic Finland, in partnership with University of Helsinki) and Tokyo Art & Science Research Residency (with BioClub Tokyo and Finnish Cultural Institute in Japan). Active programming in 2025-2026: decolonial bioart, AI + synthetic biology film series, bioart conservation.
