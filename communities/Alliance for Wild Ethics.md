# Alliance for Wild Ethics (AWE)

"A consortium of individuals and organizations working to ease the spreading devastation of the animate earth through a rapid transformation of culture." Founded by [[David Abram]] (creative director), [[Stephan Harding]], Per Espen Stoknes, and Per Ingvar Haukeland. Based at davidabram.org (wildethics.org redirects there).

Employs the arts — often in tandem with the natural sciences — to provoke deeply felt shifts in the human experience of nature. Gives priority to oral culture and face-to-face storytelling as an ecological imperative: holds that oral culture (inherently local, place-based) is the necessary soil in which literate and digital culture must be rooted to remain sustainable.
