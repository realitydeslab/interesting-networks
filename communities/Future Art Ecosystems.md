# Future Art Ecosystems (FAE)

Annual strategic briefing series produced by [[Serpentine Galleries]] R&D Platform, embedded in Serpentine Arts Technologies. Policy-shaping documents for practitioners and organisations working at art × advanced technology. Published annually since 2020. Has a growing community of practitioners, a Telegram group, and monthly community calls.

## Mission
Building 21st-century cultural infrastructure to support art and advanced technologies for the public good. Four key areas: Advanced Production Capabilities, Protocols for Organisational Interoperability, New Ownership + Distribution Models, New Systems of Measurement.
