# Unknown Fields Division

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Liam Young deep research.

Nomadic research studio co-founded by [[Liam Young]] and [[Kate Davies]] (c. 2011). Travels on expeditions to the ends of the earth to document emerging trends and the hidden geographies of global supply chains.

## Key Expeditions
- Rare earth mineral refining lakes, Inner Mongolia — produced the [Radioactive Ming Vases](https://www.dezeen.com/2015/04/28/radioactive-ming-vases-toxic-smartphone-waste-mud-unknown-fields-division-kevin-callaghan/) (now in V&A collection)
- Container ship routes across the Pacific
- Automated mining operations
- Various "location shoots" to supply chain endpoints worldwide

## Why Interesting
Unique research methodology: physical expeditions to the invisible infrastructure of global capitalism, used as raw material for speculative art and architecture. The expedition-as-research model is powerful and replicable. Originally ran as a unit at the [Architectural Association (AA)](https://www.aaschool.ac.uk/) in London.
