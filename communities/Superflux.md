# Superflux

London-based speculative design studio founded by **Anab Jain** and **Jon Ardern**. Currently the most influential active SD studio globally. Known for immersive, provocative speculative scenarios around climate, AI governance, and more-than-human futures.

**Iconic work:**
- *Mitigation of Shock* (2017) — a fully designed apartment showing how life might look in a climate-changed city. Shown internationally.
- Work for Wellcome Collection, V&A Museum, and government commissions on AI ethics and futures.
