# Alan Turing Institute

UK's national institute for data science and AI. Based in London (British Library). Hosts the Ethics and Responsible Innovation Research group led by David Leslie — the most relevant track for Amber. Collaborates broadly with UK universities.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created. Discovered via AIAL launch event (David Leslie as speaker).

## Key People
- **David Leslie** — Director of Ethics and Responsible Innovation Research; Professor of Ethics, Technology, and Society at Queen Mary University of London. Former lecturer at Princeton, Yale, Harvard. Authored UK government guidance: *Understanding Artificial Intelligence Ethics and Safety* (2019), *Explaining Decisions Made with AI* (2020). Specialist Advisor, Council of Europe's AI Committee; member of UNESCO's High-Level Expert Group on AI Ethics. London-based.

## Why Interesting
David Leslie's work is directly policy-relevant for AI accountability. The Turing Institute is the institutional home of serious UK AI ethics. London-based → physically accessible to Amber.
