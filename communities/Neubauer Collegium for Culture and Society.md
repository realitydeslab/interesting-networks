# Neubauer Collegium for Culture and Society

Interdisciplinary research institute at the University of Chicago. Supports collaborative projects at the intersection of humanities, social sciences, and the arts. Curator [[Dieter Roelstraete]] works here.

**Raqs connection**: Hosted [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s solo exhibition *Cavalcade* (2025) and the accompanying artist talk with [[Dipesh Chakravarty]], [[Prathama Banerjee]], and Dieter Roelstraete (May 8, 2025).
