# Solarpunk Magazine

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Solarpunk deep research.

A 501(c)3 nonprofit organization and primary publication hub for the solarpunk movement. Publishes fiction, poetry, essays, and art six times per year (second Tuesday of January, March, May, July, September, November). Mission: foster dialogue and inspire action on climate change solutions through solarpunk creative works. Based in Oregon (Kalapuya Ilihi territory). Pays professional rates for contributors.
