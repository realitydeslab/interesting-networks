# New Design Congress

Research organization led by [[Cade Diehm]] investigating design as a political and security domain. Produces rigorous reports on weaponized design, platform harms, and infrastructure vulnerability. Operates outside the mainstream UX/design industry — closer to security research and political theory.

## Key People
- [[Cade Diehm]] — co-founder/director

## Topics
- Weaponized design (designed systems as tools of harm)
- Infrastructure and security vulnerabilities
- Platform critique
- Digital rights

## Why Interesting
One of the few organizations treating design as a site of genuine political struggle — not "ethical design" checklists, but structural critique of how designed systems concentrate power or enable harm. Relevant to Amber's critical computing and more-than-human interests.
