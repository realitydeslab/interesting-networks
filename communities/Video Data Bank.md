# Video Data Bank (VDB)

Founded at SAIC in 1976. The leading US resource for video art and media art — 6,000+ titles by 600+ video artists, all available for research and study. Connected to the history of performance art documentation, feminist video, and early media art. Housed at [[School of the Art Institute of Chicago]].

## Why Interesting
The canonical archive for video art — early Paik, Joan Jonas, feminist video, early net art documentation. For any research touching on media art history or embodied performance, VDB is the primary resource. Also a model institution for how art schools can build research infrastructure.
