# KYOTO Design Lab

Research and design residency programme at **Kyoto Institute of Technology (KIT)**, Japan. Runs a **Design Researcher in Residence** programme hosting international and Japanese researchers for speculative and critical design projects. [[Ai Hasegawa]] was in residence 2020–21, producing her *Techno-thanatology* and *Mt. Fuji Eruption Tea Ceremony* works. Bridges traditional Japanese craft/culture (tea ceremony, Noh) with critical design practice.
