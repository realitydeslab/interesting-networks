# InterfaceCulture Lab

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via Christa Sommerer & Laurent Mignonneau deep research.

Research and teaching lab at the [University of Art and Design Linz (Kunstuniversität Linz)](https://www.ufg.ac.at/), Austria. Founded/directed by [[Christa Sommerer and Laurent Mignonneau]] since 2004. The institutional home of artificial life art and interactive installation research in Europe. Master's program in Interface Cultures trains artists working at the intersection of computation, biology, and interactivity. Geographically embedded in the [[Ars Electronica]] ecosystem — the festival happens literally in their city, making students direct participants in global media art infrastructure.

## Why Interesting
The lab that carries on the Sommerer & Mignonneau tradition. Their students are the next generation of ALife + interactive artists. Linz = Ars Electronica = the global media art circuit. Worth knowing for residency, visiting artist, or PhD opportunities.
