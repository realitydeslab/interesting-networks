# Feral Atlas

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Nils Bubandt deep research.

Major digital humanities project hosted at Stanford University Press. A collective research platform bringing together 100+ contributors — anthropologists, biologists, historians, artists — documenting "feral" landscapes of the Anthropocene. The concept: landscapes/ecosystems that escape human intention and go "feral." Exhibited at Istanbul Biennial (2019) and Sharjah Architecture Triennial (2019).

## Mission & Objectives
Document the "feral ecologies" of the Anthropocene — the unintended consequences of human infrastructure that take on lives of their own. Each "poster" maps a specific feral dynamic: mud volcanos, jellyfish blooms, invasive species, antibiotic resistance, etc.
