# terra0

Berlin-based art collective working on blockchain-based autonomous non-human entities. Their central project: giving forests legal personhood and governance via Ethereum DAOs. Most ambitious work: **Autonomous Forest** — first German woodland managed by a DAO, presented by [[LAS Art Foundation]] (from Nov 2025).

## Key Projects
- **[Autonomous Forest](https://autonomousforest.org/)** (2025–) — Commissioned by LAS Art Foundation. A German woodland managed via Verein (German non-profit) + Ethereum DAO. NFTs priced 0.08-0.1 ETH grant voting rights in the forest's governance. Physical land parcels mapped via custom geohash grid to blockchain tokens. Custom smart contract enables NFT-staking for membership and voting.

## Why Interesting ⭐⭐⭐
terra0 is perhaps the closest existing art practice to Amber's interest in autonomous agents, non-human agency, ALife-as-governance, and decentralized systems. A forest that owns itself and votes is a real-world implementation of the questions Amber asks theoretically about machine agency, autonomous agents, and more-than-human governance. The Ethereum + Verein legal structure is an elegant solution to the "giving non-humans legal standing" problem. Highly relevant to:
- Machine behavior (what does it mean for a forest to "decide"?)
- ALife (self-sustaining ecosystem with governance)
- Decentralized AI / sovereign agents
- More-than-human design
