# Copenhagen Contemporary

Contemporary arts centre in Copenhagen, Denmark. Current partner for **Arts at CERN's Collide programme** (2023–2025 cycle). Selected for its focus on ambitious contemporary and digital art. Collide Copenhagen places the selected artist for one month at CERN (Geneva) and one month at Copenhagen Contemporary. 

Artists hosted under the Collide Copenhagen partnership:
- 2023: Joan Heemskerk (JODI)
- 2024: Alice Bucknell
- 2025: Martyna Marciniak
