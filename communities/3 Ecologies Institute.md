# 3 Ecologies Institute (3e)

**Extra-university research and learning environment** founded by [[Erin Manning]] as the successor to the [[SenseLab]] / Immediations project. A non-profit in Québec. Operates on a **processual gift economy** — not tuition-based, not grant-gated in the conventional sense.

The "3 ecologies" (from Félix Guattari's *Three Ecologies*) are:
1. **Social ecology** — how we live together, organize collectively
2. **Environmental ecology** — how we inhabit and are inhabited by land, more-than-human worlds
3. **Conceptual/mental ecology** — how we think, how ideas form, what forms of knowledge we cultivate

These three are **transversal** — you can't address one without addressing the others. 3e attempts to create a form of collective life that operates at all three levels simultaneously.

## What 3e Does

- **Processual gift economy**: participants contribute time, skill, and knowledge without the transactional logic of fees/payment. Resources circulate as gifts.
- **Land-based research**: maintains a land-based project north of Montréal where "living and learning is experimented" — something between a farm, a residency, and a philosophical laboratory.
- **800+ participants worldwide**: local groups active in Montréal, Australia, Brazil, Europe, and other cities.
- **24+ university and art-org partners**: institutional affiliations but fundamentally post-institutional in its self-understanding.
- **Research-creation events**: collective workshops, gatherings, and publishing as shared practice.
- **Immediations / 3Ecologies book series** at [[punctum books]] — the publishing arm.

## Why Interesting

3e is an attempt to build a **post-capitalist research infrastructure**. Unlike most university research centers (which depend on grant capture and institutional legitimacy) or art institutions (which depend on markets and philanthropy), 3e is trying to sustain collective intellectual practice through gift economy and land-based community.

It is also a model of how research-creation scales beyond a single university lab. The SenseLab was bound to Concordia; 3e is explicitly extra-institutional and global.

For anyone thinking about **alternative research infrastructures**, community-based knowledge production, or non-market models for intellectual life, 3e is one of the few attempts at genuine implementation in the humanities.
