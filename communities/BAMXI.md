# BAMΞ — Bamberg Mathematical Consciousness Science Initiative

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-15:** Initial deep research. Full profile created. AI Consciousness Sprint (Apr–Jun 2026) is a direct hit for Amber. Computational Neurophenomenology Cabin Workshop happening next week (Mar 22–29, 2026).

The world's first **dedicated institutional home for Mathematical Consciousness Science**. BAMΞ (pronounced "bam-zy") is an international initiative at the [University of Bamberg](https://www.uni-bamberg.de/en/), Germany, founded to serve as a permanent hub for the mathematical study of consciousness — where neuroscience, philosophy, physics, computer science, and mathematics converge on the "hard problem." It runs on a **sprint model**: two-month focused research sprints on specific open questions, each combining workshops, invited talks, discussion sessions, and residencies. Deeply embedded in the [AMCS](https://amcs-community.org/) (Association for Mathematical Consciousness Science) ecosystem, and directly connected to the [[Models of Consciousness]] conference series.

## Mission & Objectives

**Mission:** To spearhead the development of mathematically rigorous methods to support and accelerate research in Consciousness Science. ([Mission page](https://www.uni-bamberg.de/en/bamxi/mission/))

**Core Objectives:**
1. **Institutional hub** — Provide what Mathematical Consciousness Science has been missing: a dedicated home where researchers across career stages collaborate on core mathematical questions
2. **Sprint-based research** — Organized around focused 2-month sprints on specific open questions
3. **Career-spanning inclusivity** — Connects leading experts AND early-career researchers
4. **Rigorous + open-minded** — Advocates mathematical rigor combined with scientific integrity and non-dogmatic inquiry
5. **Co-organize major conferences** — Including the [[Models of Consciousness]] series

**Research Scope:**
- Models and theories of consciousness
- Measurements of consciousness
- Representations of consciousness
- Consciousness of Artificial Intelligence
- Medical and societal applications
