# Participatory Design Conference (PDC)

Biennial conference on participatory design — the approach to computing and design that centers user/worker democracy. Directly descended from the Scandinavian participatory design tradition born at the [[Aarhus Conference]]. First edition: PDC 1990, program chair was Lucy Suchman.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Aarhus Conference deep research.

## Vibe
The biennial sibling to the decennial Aarhus Conference. More accessible (every 2 years), more practice-focused. The community that actually works with communities, not just theorizes about them.

## Why Interesting
Direct community continuity with Aarhus tradition. If Amber wants to engage with the participatory design community before Aarhus 2035, PDC is the training ground.

## Key People
- [Lucy Suchman](https://www.lancaster.ac.uk/sociology/people/lucy-suchman) — program chair at PDC 1990 (inaugural)
- Pelle Ehn — founding figure
- Morten Kyng, Susanne Bødker — continuous presence
