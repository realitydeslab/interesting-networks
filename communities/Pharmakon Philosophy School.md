# Pharmakon Philosophy School

Philosophy school founded by [[Bernard Stiegler]] on 18 September 2010 in [Épineuil-le-Fleuriel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89pineuil-le-Fleuriel), a small town in the Cher department of France. Ran public courses for local community, doctoral seminars by videoconference, and summer academies (Académies d'été) bringing together activists, researchers, artists, writers, and local inhabitants. Engaged in research, critique, and analysis in line with Stiegler's pharmacological approach. Now connected to [[Association Épokhè]].

## Why Interesting
Model of philosophical institution-building outside the metropolis. Interesting for its combination of local community engagement with international intellectual networks (via videoconference seminars). Demonstrates Stiegler's theory-through-institution approach.
