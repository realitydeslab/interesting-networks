# Superblue

Experiential art company co-founded by [Pace Gallery](https://www.pacegallery.com/) and others. Operates permanent experiential art spaces, starting in Miami (1101 NW 23 St). Commissions, produces, and presents large-scale immersive art experiences. Roster includes A.A. Murakami, James Turrell, teamLab, Es Devlin.

Model: not a traditional gallery — a hybrid between a museum and a theme park, but with serious art world credentials through the Pace connection. Focuses on artists whose work *requires* large physical infrastructure.

## Why Interesting
Superblue is creating a new category: institutional-quality immersive art at commercial scale. The fact that A.A. Murakami is on their roster (alongside Turrell and teamLab) is a significant signal of their position in the art world.
