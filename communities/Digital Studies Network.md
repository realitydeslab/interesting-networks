# Digital Studies Network

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during [[IRI (Institut de recherche et d'innovation)]] deep research. Needs investigation into current activities and membership.

International research network established in 2012 by [[IRI (Institut de recherche et d'innovation)]] to create a global scholarly platform around questions of digital epistemology, metadata, and the political, cultural, and epistemological stakes of digital technologies. Connects institutions globally under the Stieglerian pharmacological framework.

## Mission & Objectives
Develop international scholarly community around "digital studies" as a discipline — analyzing digital technologies not just technically but epistemologically, culturally, and politically. Focus on:
- Digital epistemology: how digital technologies transform knowledge and knowing
- Metadata studies: how data organization shapes what can be thought
- Digital humanities: humanities scholarship in and about digital environments

## Key People
- **[[Bernard Stiegler]]** — founding inspiration through IRI
