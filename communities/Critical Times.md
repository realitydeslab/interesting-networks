# Critical Times

*Critical Times: Interventions in Global Critical Theory* — journal published by Duke University Press, launched 2018. Explicitly positions itself as a venue for critical theory from non-Western contexts and the Global South. Aims to be a counterpart to *Critical Inquiry* (Chicago) and *Theory, Culture & Society* but oriented toward postcolonial and southern intellectual traditions.

[[Premesh Lalu]] contributed a key piece — "What Is the University For?" — to Volume 2, Issue 2 (2019), one of the journal's inaugural substantial issues.

## Why Interesting for Amber
This is the venue to watch for critical theory coming from outside the Euro-American mainstream. If Amber wants to publish work that engages with postcolonial theory, decolonial design, or Global South perspectives on AI/computing, this is worth targeting.
