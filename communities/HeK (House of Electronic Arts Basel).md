# HeK (House of Electronic Arts Basel)

Basel-based institution dedicated to digital culture and electronic art. One of Europe's most important media art venues — alongside [[ZKM Center for Art and Media]], [[transmediale]], and [[Ars Electronica]]. Located in Basel's Art district (near Art Basel). Annual residency program, exhibitions, talks, and research.

## Key Connections
- [[Chronus Art Center]] — co-presented *unREAL: The Algorithmic Present* and *There Is No Perfect Spell* (online collaboration)
- [[ZKM Center for Art and Media]] — peer institution, network node
- [[transmediale]] — circuit partner

## Why Note-Worthy
Geneva/Basel location is a major European media art hub. HeK has a residency and publishes extensively. Partner in CAC's international circuit. For Amber: a Swiss media art residency to consider, near the Art Basel ecosystem.
