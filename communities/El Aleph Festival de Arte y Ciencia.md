# El Aleph Festival de Arte y Ciencia

Annual art-and-science festival in Mexico City, organized by [[Cátedra Max Aub]], Transdisciplina en Arte y Tecnología (UNAM). The primary public-facing output of the Cátedra, typically held in May across UNAM cultural venues.

**Named after Borges's "El Aleph"** — the story of a point in space that contains all other points simultaneously, from which one can see everything in the universe. The name frames the festival's ambition perfectly: a single gathering point where all disciplines can be held together.

## Format
Multi-day festival (typically ~10 days) with performances, installations, talks, workshops, and screenings. Brings together artists, scientists, critical theorists, and technologists. International guest program alongside Mexican practitioners.
