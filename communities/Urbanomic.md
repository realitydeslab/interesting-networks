# Urbanomic

Independent press and platform founded by Robin Mackay. Based in Falmouth, UK. Publishes philosophy, art theory, and speculative thought.

## Key Publications
- **Collapse** journal — philosophy and speculative thought
- [[Reza Negarestani]] — *Cyclonopedia* (2008)
- [[Bogna Konior]] & [[Benjamin Bratton]] — *Machine Decision is Not Final* (co-published with MIT Press)
- Sequence Press — imprint
- Works by [[Ray Brassier]], Robin Mackay, [[Nick Land]]
