# Urbanomic

UK-based publisher and arts organization (Falmouth, Cornwall). The central node of the speculative philosophy / accelerationism / xenofeminism publishing ecosystem. Founded and directed by [[Robin Mackay]]. Co-publishes with [[Sequence Press]] (NYC). Publishes the *Collapse* journal and a key mono series including [[Yuk Hui]], [[Reza Negarestani]], Amy Ireland, Laura Tripaldi, Nick Land, and many others.

## Key Publications
- *Collapse: Journal of Philosophical Research and Development* (8 volumes, 2006–present)
- *#Accelerate* anthology (2014, ed. Robin Mackay & Armen Avanessian)
- *The Question Concerning Technology in China* — [[Yuk Hui]] (2016)
- *Parallel Minds* — [[Laura Tripaldi]] (2022)
- *Cute Accelerationism* — [[Amy Ireland]] & Maya B. Kronic (2022)
- *Machine Decision Is Not Final*
- *Omnicide II* — Jason Bahbak Mohaghegh (2024)
- *Chronosis* — Negarestani, Tilford, Mackay

## Key People
- [[Robin Mackay]] — director, founder, editor, translator

## Events
- Hosts events globally and in partnership with [[Miguel Abreu Gallery]] (NYC)
- Residencies and commissions ([urbanomic.com/events](https://www.urbanomic.com/wordpress/event/))

## Why Interesting
The intellectual home of the speculative realism → accelerationism → xenofeminism → cosmotechnics theoretical lineage. Publishes the theory that feeds into posthumanism, more-than-human design, and machine behavior research. [[Yuk Hui]] connection directly links to Amber's school.
