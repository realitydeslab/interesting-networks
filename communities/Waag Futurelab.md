# Waag Futurelab

Amsterdam civic technology organization working at the intersection of art, science, and technology. Originally Waag Society, now Waag Futurelab. Historic venue for bioart and DIYbio (Theatrum Anatomicum — where Adam Zaretsky ran the first VASTAL public lab in 2009). Runs open wetlab (Wetlab Amsterdam), biohacking projects, open-source hardware, civic tech. Values: public interest technology, openness.
