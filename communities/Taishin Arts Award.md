# Taishin Arts Award 台新藝術獎

Taiwan's most prestigious and comprehensive arts award. Covers performing arts, visual arts, and interdisciplinary work. Annual award that serves as the primary signal of recognition from Taiwan's art establishment.

## Why Interesting
[[Chia-Wei Hsu]] won the **Annual Grand Prize at the 15th Taishin Arts Award (2017)** — this validated him within Taiwan before the EYE Prize (2024) validated him in Europe. Pattern: Taiwan-first validation → international circuit. Amber's lab is at China Academy of Art (adjacent) and has Taiwan/Chinese cultural connections — Taishin is a key node to understand.
