# Futur Antérieur (journal)

French political theory journal published 1990–1998. Direct predecessor to [[Multitudes (journal)]]. Founded and edited by [[Antonio Negri]] et al. from their Paris exile. Key venue for Italian autonomist/post-operaist theory to enter French intellectual discourse in the early 1990s. Ceased publication 1998; reconstituted as Multitudes in 2000. Historical importance as the entry point of workerist theory into France.

## Why Interesting
Historical node — understanding Multitudes requires knowing Futur Antérieur as its intellectual origin. The shift from Futur Antérieur to Multitudes in 2000 corresponded to the publication of *Empire* and the broadening of the project beyond strict Marxist political economy to include ecology, feminism, art, and biopolitics.
