# openFrameworks

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via [[Kyle McDonald]] deep research.

Open-source C++ toolkit for creative coding. The primary tool for a generation of artist-programmers working with computer vision, computer graphics, physical computing, audio, and interactive installation. Co-founded by [Zach Lieberman](https://zach.lieberman.in/), Theo Watson, Arturo Castro, and others. **[[Kyle McDonald]] served as community manager** — central to building the global community around it.

## Mission & Objectives
Provide an open-source toolkit that acts as a "glue" between various commonly used libraries and frameworks. Make it as easy as possible for non-programmers to experiment with creative uses of code. Radically open: everything is shared.

## Key People
- **[[Zach Lieberman]]** — co-founder
- **Theo Watson** — co-founder
- **Arturo Castro** — core developer (Face Substitution with Kyle McDonald)
- **[[Kyle McDonald]]** — former community manager; major addon contributor (ofxFaceTracker, ofxCv)

## Why Interesting
openFrameworks shaped an entire generation of art-tech practitioners. Its community model (conferences, workshops, open code sharing) prefigures how AI art communities are trying to organize today. The culture of radical sharing and tool-building that Kyle McDonald embodied here is a template for how to build influence through community infrastructure.
