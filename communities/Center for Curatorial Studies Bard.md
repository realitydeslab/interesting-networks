# Center for Curatorial Studies (CCS), Bard College

Located in Annandale-on-Hudson, New York. One of the most prestigious graduate programs in curatorial studies globally. Combines MA degree with an on-site museum (the Hessel Museum of Art, with the Marieluise Hessel Collection of contemporary art). Founded 1990. Known for rigorous theoretical grounding alongside practical curatorial training. Graduates include many of the most prominent curators of their generation working internationally.
