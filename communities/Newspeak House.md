# Newspeak House

Independent residential college in London, founded 2015 by Edward Saperia. Studies, nurtures, and inspires emerging communities of practice across civil society and the public sector in the UK. Runs an annual **Introduction to Political Technology** fellowship. Hosts thousands of events: meetups, hackathons, workshops, conferences.

## Fellowship
- **Introduction to Political Technology** — annual residential fellowship. 2025-26 cohort: experience across political organising, AI governance, civic tech, journalism, campaigning, service design, data science. Participants come from across Europe, Middle East, North America.

## Notable Events
- **Arms Industry Wikithon** — monthly sessions (co-organized by [[Agnes Cameron]]); expanding Wikipedia coverage of global arms industry entities
- **Campaign Lab Hack Nights** — bi-weekly; progressive data science and political tech
- **Code Club** — bi-weekly; open coding sessions
- **Political Technology Awards** — annual; judged by fellowship cohort
- **AI Signals** — monthly applied AI community events
- **Ration Club** — weekly community dinner, open to all

## Connection to Interesting-Networks
[[Agnes Cameron]] runs the Arms Industry Wikithon here. Newspeak House is a key node in London's political technology scene, connecting activist technology, journalism tools, and civic data work.
