# Electronic Literature Organization (ELO)

Organization supporting the creation, preservation, and study of literature made for digital media. Maintains the **Electronic Literature Collection** (three volumes), runs annual conferences, and gives awards including the **Robert Coover Award** (named for the hypertext fiction pioneer).

## Connection to Jason Edward Lewis
Lewis's P.o.E.M.M. series (touch-based poetry for mobile devices) has been included in ELO Collections vol. 2 and 3. He won the **inaugural Robert Coover Award for Best Work of Electronic Literature** (2014) for "The Summer The Rattlesnakes Came."

## Why It Matters
ELO represents the literary/poetic thread of computational creativity — distinct from but overlapping with media art and HCI. Lewis is one of the few figures to operate equally in ELO world (computational poetry) and the Indigenous futures/AI design world, which makes him unusual.
