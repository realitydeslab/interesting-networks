# Policy Lab UK

The UK Government's central policy design and futures unit, embedded in the Cabinet Office. Commissions speculative design and design research for policymaking — unusual for a government department. Key link between speculative design practice and actual UK governance.

Commissioned [[Superflux]] and [[Anab Jain]] to develop the **Ecological Intelligence Agency** (2023) — a speculative proposal for AI systems that advocate for ecological health in policymaking, giving voice to rivers. The forum engaged 80+ policymakers, academics, NGOs, and regulators.

Key individuals:
- **Neha Sharma** — Policy Lab; commissioned Ecological Intelligence Agency
- **Sanjan Sabherwal** — Policy Lab
- **Phil Tovey** — Defra Futures (sister unit focused on environment/nature)

Policy Lab regularly works with artists, designers, and speculative practitioners to explore future policy scenarios. They're one of the few government units globally doing this at scale. Worth tracking for potential Amber collaborations (government × speculative design × AI × ecology).
