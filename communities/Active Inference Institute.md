# Active Inference Institute

Open-science institute dedicated to the Active Inference framework (Karl Friston's Free Energy Principle).

## Key People
- Karl Friston — originator of the framework (UCL)
- Daniel Friedman — institute organizer
- Maxwell Ramstead — active inference + culture

## Activities
- Quarterly roundtables
- Open-source research
- Textbook groups
- Newsletter and ecosystem documentation

## Why Interesting
Active inference is possibly the most ambitious unified theory of mind and agency. The institute is building an open community around it. Fast-evolving, contested, productive — exactly the "edge of chaos" that's interesting.
