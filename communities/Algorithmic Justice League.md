# Algorithmic Justice League (AJL)

Organization founded by [[Joy Buolamwini]] in 2016 that combines art and research to expose and combat AI bias and algorithmic harms. Mission: raise public awareness of AI impacts, equip advocates, build voice for impacted communities, galvanize researchers/policymakers/industry. Famous for the Gender Shades research, which exposed racial and gender bias in facial recognition systems sold by IBM, Microsoft, and Face++.

## Mission & Objectives
"We mitigate the harms and biases of AI by promoting 4 core principles": Affirmative Consent, Meaningful Transparency, Continuous Oversight and Accountability, Actionable Critique.

## Why Interesting
The AJL is where AI ethics became a civil rights movement. Their art-research synthesis model — making technical bias findings into gallery exhibitions and spoken word — is a template for how technical research can move into culture.
