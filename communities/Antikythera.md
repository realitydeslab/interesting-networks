# Antikythera

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Deep research complete. Full institutional mapping, network analysis, and engagement pathways identified.

**The most ambitious institutional home for speculative computation.** R&D institute for Planetary Computation and the Evolution of Intelligence, founded by Benjamin Bratton and housed within the Berggruen Institute (2022). Spiritual successor to Strelka Institute programmes *The New Normal* and *The Terraforming*. Extraordinary network density — the 2023-2024 affiliate lists alone comprise a comprehensive map of the most interesting people in tech-philosophy.

## Mission & Objectives
Antikythera investigates planetary computation as both infrastructure and epistemic framework, researching how computation reshapes intelligence, governance, and planetary systems. Named after the first known computer—the Antikythera mechanism—it positions itself as developing "computational technologies that not only provide immense feats of calculation, but also ones that reveal and accelerate planetary intelligence."

**Core Research Areas (2025):**
1. **Agentworld** — societies of billions of AI agents and synthetic social relations
2. **Convergent Artificialization** — cross-cultural perspectives on artificial life/intelligence  
3. **Hemispherical Stacks** — multipolar geopolitics of computational infrastructure
4. **Planetary Sapience** — evolution of Earth-scale intelligence through computation
