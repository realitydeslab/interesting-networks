# SAVVY Contemporary

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during HKW deep research. Key adjacent space to HKW in Berlin.

Berlin-based independent art space and discursive platform founded by [[Bonaventure Soh Bejeng Ndikung]] (now HKW Director). Situates itself "on the threshold between concepts of the West and non-West" — exhibitions, performances, discourse, and events. Located in Berlin-Wedding. More accessible entry point than HKW for artists seeking Berlin-circuit connections.

## Why Interesting
Ndikung's original project space. Non-Western, decolonial, experimental. Smaller and more approachable than HKW. Overlaps with Amber's interests in non-Western epistemologies and more-than-human ethics.
