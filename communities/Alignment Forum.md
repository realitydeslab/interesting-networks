# Alignment Forum

The technical, curated spinoff of LessWrong focused purely on AI alignment research. Launched 2018 alongside LessWrong 2.0 relaunch. Higher signal-to-noise than LW proper — less community gossip, more actual research. Key venue where Anthropic, DeepMind, ARC, and MIRI researchers post working ideas before (or instead of) formal publication.

## Why Interesting
More relevant to Amber than LessWrong proper. The empirical/interpretability work (Neel Nanda, etc.) is posted here. If Amber wants to monitor the AI safety research ecosystem without wading through LW culture, the Alignment Forum is the filtered version.
