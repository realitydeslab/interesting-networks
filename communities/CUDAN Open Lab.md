# CUDAN Open Lab

**Cultural Data Analytics Open Lab** at Tallinn University, Estonia. Established as part of the **CUDAN ERA Chair project** funded through Horizon 2020 (Grant No. 810961, €2.5M + €200k from Estonian Research Council). Main funded period: June 2020 – August 2024. Continues as Tallinn University Research Fund–supported lab (2024–2027).

Led by ERA Chair holder **Prof. Dr. Maximilian Schich** (computational art historian). The project brought together 6 postdoctoral Senior Research Fellows and 6 predoctoral Junior Research Fellows in a "multidisciplinary science lab" — the intentional mixing of art history, cultural media studies, computational science, and information design.

Produced **69 publications** involving **103 co-authors** during its main funded phase.

## Core Mission
Novel analytical approach — "Cultural Data Analytics" — combining:
- Machine learning for image/AV analysis over historical timescales
- Linguistic topic modelling + bipartite network science for texts
- Temporal multilayer network analysis for cultural knowledge graphs
- "Embed-everything" approach for multimedia/unstructured data

## Why Interesting
One of the most serious European computational humanities research environments — competitive fellowships, genuine cross-disciplinary work, published real outputs. The Estonian base makes it interesting as a non-UK/non-US node in global digital humanities. Hosted [[Daniel Chávez Heras]] as a postdoctoral Senior Research Fellow during the main funded period.
