# AI Cognition Initiative

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via Derek Shiller deep research.

Research group focused on philosophical and technical questions raised by advanced AI systems. Co-founded by [[Derek Shiller]] at [[Rethink Priorities]]. Interdisciplinary team spanning philosophy, ML, consciousness research, statistics, and economics.

## Research Programs

- Digital Consciousness (probability of AI consciousness)
