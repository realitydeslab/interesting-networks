# nettime

The founding email list of net art and critical internet theory, established 1995 in Venice by Geert Lovink and Pit Schultz. Still active as a low-volume list. The original venue where the net.art community formed discourse — Vuk Ćosić, Heath Bunting, Olia Lialina, Alexei Shulgin all circulated here alongside critical theorists.
