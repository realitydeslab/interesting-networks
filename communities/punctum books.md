# punctum books

Indie, scholar-led open access publisher founded in 2011. One of the key members of the **[[Radical Open Access Collective]]**. Known for publishing at the edges of academia — weird theory, speculative humanities, experimental scholarship, minor traditions.

Hosts the **3Ecologies imprint**, which is the new home for the [[Open Humanities Press]] **Immediations** series (previously OHP; moved here by SenseLab / [[Erin Manning]] et al).

Sister press in spirit to [[Open Humanities Press]], [[Mattering Press]], and meson press. All refuse APCs and commercial logic.

Their tagline reflects their ethos: *"punctum books is an open-access and print-on-demand independent scholarly press dedicated to radically creative modes of intellectual inquiry and writing across a whimsical para-humanities assemblage."*
