# Center for AI Safety (CAIS)

AI safety research and policy organization. Founded and directed by Dan Hendrycks (former PhD student of [[Jacob Steinhardt]]). Known for the "AI Safety Statement" signed by leading AI researchers, and the MMLU/MATH benchmarks that Hendrycks co-created with Steinhardt during his PhD.
