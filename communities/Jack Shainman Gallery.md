# Jack Shainman Gallery

New York gallery (Chelsea + The School in Kinderhook NY). Founded by Jack Shainman and Claude Simard. Known for committed, long-term representation of artists of color including [[Kerry James Marshall]], Nick Cave, Nari Ward, Barkley L. Hendricks. The School upstate is a converted school building showing large-scale work. Model of gallerist-as-long-form-partner.
