# e-flux

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial note creation. Identified as major platform through [[Hito Steyerl]] research.

One of the most influential platforms for contemporary art theory, criticism, and discourse. Founded as announcement service, evolved into essential publication for art theory and cultural criticism.

## Mission & Objectives

Founded 1998 as digital announcement platform for art exhibitions and events. Evolved into major publisher of art theory through e-flux Journal (launched 2008). Publishes critical essays on contemporary art, technology, politics, and culture. Also produces exhibitions, projects, and publications.

## Key People

- [[Anton Vidokle]] — co-founder and director
- [[Julieta Aranda]] — co-founder  
- Editorial board includes major art theorists and critics internationally

## Key Publications & Output

- **e-flux Journal** — monthly publication of critical essays on art, politics, technology
- **e-flux announcements** — exhibition and event announcements globally
- **e-flux books** — book publishing program
- **e-flux projects** — curatorial and artistic projects

## Key Contributions

- Transformed art announcement platform into major theory publication
- Created essential platform for discourse around art and technology
- Published foundational texts like [[Hito Steyerl]]'s "In Defense of the Poor Image"
- Bridged art practice and critical theory

## Events & Programmes

- Regular lectures and discussions
- Exhibition projects internationally  
- Book launches and presentations
