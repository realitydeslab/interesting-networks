# Emergence Magazine

Literary magazine focused on ecology, culture, and spirituality. An initiative of [Kalliopeia Foundation](https://www.kalliopeia.org/). Ad-free and subscription-free (foundation supported). Publishes long-form essays, poetry, fiction, films, and audio. Key venue for ecological humanities writing.

Key contributors include [[Robin Wall Kimmerer]], Robert Macfarlane, and others at the intersection of ecology and culture. Published Kimmerer's "The Serviceberry" (2022), which became her 2024 book.

## Why Interesting
The leading literary venue for ecological humanities — where science meets culture meets Indigenous knowledge. Potential publication venue for Amber's more-than-human design writing. Takes pitches from writers.

Also: the Kalliopeia Foundation that funds it is a philanthropic vehicle worth understanding — funds at the ecology × culture × spirituality intersection.
