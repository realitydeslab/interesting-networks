# Le Fresnoy – Studio national des arts contemporains

France's elite post-graduate art+technology laboratory, in Tourcoing near Lille. One of the most selective schools in the world for artists working at the intersection of technology and contemporary art. Directed by [Alain Fleischer](https://www.lefresnoy.net/en).

## Why Interesting

Produced **two consecutive EYE Art & Film Prize winners**: [[Saodat Ismailova]] (2022) and [[Chia-Wei Hsu]] (2024). This is not a coincidence — Le Fresnoy specifically cultivates artists who bring non-Western epistemologies into technical media practice. Both Ismailova (Central Asia) and Hsu (Taiwan) trained there and then built international careers rooted in their specific geographic/cultural territories.

Le Fresnoy alumni are disproportionately represented in European media art institutions. The school offers a 2-year post-graduate programme with intensive technical resources + visiting artist programme.

## Notable Alumni
- [[Chia-Wei Hsu]] (Taiwan) — EYE Prize 2024
