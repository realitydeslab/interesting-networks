# DAIR (Distributed AI Research Institute)

Independent research institute founded by [[Timnit Gebru]] in December 2021, after being fired from Google for co-authoring the Stochastic Parrots paper. Operates across 7+ time zones, 18+ countries, 20+ languages. Community-funded (not industry), explicitly rejecting both corporate and traditional academic constraints. Represents the clearest model globally for **independent critical AI research**.

## Mission & Objectives
"We cut through the AI hype. We ground our research in community. We imagine alternative futures."

DAIR's three pillars: (1) expose real harms of AI systems, (2) center lived experience of affected communities, (3) imagine genuinely different technological futures. The organization explicitly rejects academic burnout and supports researchers having full lives outside work.
