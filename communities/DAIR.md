# DAIR (Distributed AI Research Institute)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during AI Now Institute deep research. Partner organization to AI Now; shares critical AI accountability mission.

Research collective founded by Timnit Gebru focused on AI accountability research outside of Big Tech. Partner organization to [[AI Now Institute]]. Cited in AI Now 2025 Landscape Report. Represents the academic/research wing of the independent critical AI ecosystem.

## Mission & Objectives
Timnit Gebru founded DAIR after being fired by Google in 2020 (for co-authoring a paper on large language model risks). DAIR explicitly positions itself as research conducted without being beholden to corporate funders — mirroring AI Now's no-tech-industry-funding stance.

## Key People
- **Timnit Gebru** — Founder. Ethiopian-born AI researcher. Former Google AI Ethics co-lead. TIME100. Co-author of "Stochastic Parrots" paper (2021) that preceded her firing. MacArthur Fellow.

## Key Communities
- **[[AI Now Institute]]** — Partner organization
- **[[FAccT]]** — Academic conference community

## Why Interesting
DAIR and AI Now are the two poles of the independent critical AI ecosystem. Both refuse tech industry funding. DAIR is more research/academic; AI Now is more policy/advocacy. Together they represent a viable model for independent AI accountability work.
