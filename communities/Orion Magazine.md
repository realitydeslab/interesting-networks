# Orion Magazine

America's leading literary magazine for nature and environmental writing. Nonprofit. Founded 1982. Publishes essays, poetry, fiction. Contributors include: [[Robin Wall Kimmerer]], [[Jenny Odell]], Barry Lopez, Robert Macfarlane, Terry Tempest Williams.

Published key Kimmerer works:
- "Speaking of Nature" (grammar of animacy / ki pronoun)
- "Council of the Pecans" (John Burroughs Essay Award winner)
- "Gift Thinking" (dialogue with Jenny Odell, 2025)

Also published works by [[Jenny Odell]]. Appears to be a key forum for more-than-human / ecological humanities dialogue between writers.

## Why Interesting
The prestige literary venue for ecological thinking. Small enough to have a clear editorial vision; big enough to matter. If Amber writes for the ecological humanities audience, Orion is the right venue. They also publish dialogues between thinkers — a format worth proposing.
