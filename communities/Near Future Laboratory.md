# Near Future Laboratory

Design fiction studio founded by [Julian Bleecker](https://nearfuturelaboratory.com/about/) in 2004. Pioneered design fiction as a grounded practice for corporate strategy and research. Bleecker authored *The Manual of Design Fiction* (2023) and created the canonical design fiction artifact: the "IKEA Catalog from the Future" (2014 — a full near-future IKEA catalogue as a speculation tool).

Near Future Laboratory industrialized design fiction for clients including major corporations, helping them make strategic decisions through speculative artifacts. Offers the SuperSeminar learning platform.

Based in Los Angeles. Julian Bleecker holds BSEE, MSEng, PhD; was Professor at USC School of Cinematic Arts.
