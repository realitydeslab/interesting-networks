# ITS Rio (Instituto de Tecnologia e Sociedade)

[Institute for Technology and Society, Rio de Janeiro](https://itsrio.org/en/). Brazilian research organization working on technology governance, digital rights, and technology policy from a Global South perspective. Co-hosts the **Dialogues on Philosophy and Technology** series alongside [[Erasmus Institute for Philosophy and Technology]], [[Research Network for Philosophy and Technology]], and [[Hanart Forum]].

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created. Co-host of IPT Dialogues 2025–26 series.

## Why Interesting
ITS Rio's presence in the Dialogues series signals a deliberate triangulation: Europe (Erasmus), Hong Kong (Hanart Forum), South America (ITS Rio). This isn't tokenism — it's Yuk Hui's cosmotechnics principle in institutional form: technology philosophy must be grounded in multiple civilizational contexts. ITS Rio brings Latin American digital rights tradition to the conversation.
