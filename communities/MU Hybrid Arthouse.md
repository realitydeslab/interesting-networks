# MU Hybrid Arthouse

Platform for art, technology, and design in Eindhoven, Netherlands. One of Europe's most important bioart/hybrid-art venues. Operates as exhibition space, residency host, and co-producer of ambitious art-science projects. Partner and host of the **BAD Award (Biological Art & Design Award)** — €25,000 international prize.

## Why Interesting (⭐⭐)
- Hosts the [BAD Award](https://badaward.nl/) — €25,000 for bioart + scientific collaboration. This is a meaningful funding/community entry point.
- Programs at the biodesign/hybrid art/feminist tech intersection consistently — matches Amber's areas.
- Located in Eindhoven (design capital), adjacent to Design Academy Eindhoven community.
- European counterpart to Ars Electronica / ZKM in programming ambition but with specific bioart focus.
- Has co-produced major works: Ani Liu, Monika Fleischmann and others.

## Key Events/Programs

- **Reproduction Otherwise** (2022) — bioart + reproductive politics; Ani Liu, others
- **Bio Art & Design Exhibition 2020: 10 Years BAD Award**
