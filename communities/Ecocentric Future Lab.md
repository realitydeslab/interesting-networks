# Ecocentric Future Lab

University research lab directed by [[Jiabao Li]] at UT Austin. Explores multi-species, eco-centric worldview through art × science × design. Students collaborate with actual species (spiders, cats, dogs, grackles), develop animal-computer interaction frameworks, innovate biomaterials, repurpose e-waste, design enrichment for captive animals (Costa Rica), explore voxel-based multi-material 3D printing inspired by nature, and harness AI for animal behavior/communication research.

## Why Interesting
A university lab that actually does what "more-than-human design" theorists talk about — working with real animals, real biology, real science. The lab's output is both academically rigorous (CHI, SIGGRAPH, IEEE VR papers) and artistically ambitious (Ars Electronica, Venice Biennale, MoMA). Model for how to institutionalize radical art-science practice within academia.
