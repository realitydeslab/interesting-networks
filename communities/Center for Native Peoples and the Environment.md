# Center for Native Peoples and the Environment (CNPE)

Founded by [[Robin Wall Kimmerer]] at SUNY-ESF in Syracuse, NY. Bridges Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK) with Western environmental science. Programs include:
- Native American student recruitment, retention, and mentorship
- Pre-college outreach
- Research generating strategies for Indigenous participation in environmental science
- TEK integration into ecology curricula

Mission: Native Americans are the most underrepresented group in American science ("barely a presence" per NSF). Cross-cultural competence is essential to environmental problem-solving. TEK is increasingly valued by academics, agency scientists, and policy makers as a source of ecosystem management models.

Website: https://www.esf.edu/cnpe/

## Why Interesting
The institutional embodiment of Kimmerer's intellectual project. Where TEK and Western ecology meet in practice. Potential venue for talks/engagements around more-than-human design + Indigenous epistemology intersection.
