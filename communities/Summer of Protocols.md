Ethereum Foundation-funded research programme (2023–2024) on protocol design as infrastructure. Connected [[Infrastructure Studies]] with web3/blockchain communities. Published research essays, hosted seminars. Relevant for understanding how technical protocols embed social values — directly aligned with both infrastructure studies and Amber's protocol/agent work.
