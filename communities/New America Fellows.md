# New America Fellows

Fellowship program at [New America](https://www.newamerica.org/), a US policy think tank focused on technology and social change. National Fellows include technologists, journalists, artists, and policy makers working at the intersection of technology and democracy. Annual cohort, nonresidential. Nonny de la Peña was a 2018 National Fellow.

## Why Interesting
Bridges journalism, technology, and policy in a US liberal institutional context. Good network for people whose tech work has policy implications. Notable fellows include tech journalists, civil society researchers, VR pioneers.
