# DeTao Masters Academy (DTMA)

Shanghai-based educational institution bringing international "Masters" to China to teach advanced design, art, and technology. **[[Roy Ascott]]** is **DeTao Master of Technoetic Arts** (appointed 2012) and runs the **DeTao Technoetic Arts Studio** (from 2014) at the associated Shanghai Institute of Visual Arts (SIVA). The Planetary Collegium's DeTao-Node Shanghai is housed here. Key bridge between the Western art-tech-consciousness research tradition and China's art-tech education infrastructure.

Other Masters include designers, architects, and artists from across Europe and Asia. The "Master" model imports intellectual capital from established Western practitioners to train Chinese students and practitioners.
