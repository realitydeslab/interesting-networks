# Project Hieroglyph

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Solarpunk deep research.

An initiative at Arizona State University's Center for Science and the Imagination (CSI) connecting SF writers with scientists, engineers, and technologists to produce optimistic, technically grounded speculative fiction. Named after Neal Stephenson's argument that science fiction has become too dystopian and needs "hieroglyphs" — iconic, generative future images. Published *Hieroglyph: Stories and Visions for a Better Future* (2014, Morrow). Home of Adam Flynn's "Notes toward a Manifesto" that crystallized solarpunk.

## Key People
- Neal Stephenson (founding inspiration)
- Adam Flynn (solarpunk manifesto)
- Ed Finn (founding director of CSI)

## Why Interesting
Origin venue for solarpunk's first major theoretical statement. CSI at ASU runs fellowships for speculative fiction + science collaboration.
