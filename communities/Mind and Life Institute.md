# Mind and Life Institute

Founded 1987 by Francisco Varela and R. Adam Engle to promote dialogue between scientists and the Dalai Lama on the relationship between modern science and Buddhism. Now a major nexus for contemplative science research — funding work at the intersection of neuroscience, philosophy of mind, and contemplative practice.
