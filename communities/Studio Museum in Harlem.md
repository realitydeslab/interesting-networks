# Studio Museum in Harlem

New York museum dedicated to artists of African descent. Founded 1968, Harlem. Key institution for Black American contemporary art — collection, exhibitions, artist-in-residence programs. Holds works by [[Kerry James Marshall]], [[Arthur Jafa]], and many of the most important Black American artists. The InHarlem program embeds artists in the community. New building designed by Adjaye Associates (David Adjaye).
