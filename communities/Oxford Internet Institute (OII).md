# Oxford Internet Institute (OII)

The Internet research department of the University of Oxford. Multidisciplinary social science research centre studying the societal impacts of the internet, AI, and emerging technologies. Founded 2001. Based in Balliol College annexe, Oxford.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Sandra Wachter deep research.

## Mission
Studying the internet and AI's impact on society through law, philosophy, social science, and computer science. Unusual in that it genuinely bridges these disciplines rather than just claiming to.

## Key Research Programmes
- **[Governance of Emerging Technologies (GET)](https://www.oii.ox.ac.uk/research/projects/governance-of-emerging-technologies/)** — led by Wachter, Mittelstadt, Russell. AI law, ethics, accountability.
- **[Human Centred Computing](https://www.oii.ox.ac.uk/research/themes/human-centred-computing/)** — Amber's home programme via Van Kleek.
