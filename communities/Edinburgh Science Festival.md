# Edinburgh Science Festival

UK's largest public science festival, based in Edinburgh. Founded by Simon Gage (longtime director). Affiliated **Edinburgh Science Foundation** led by Hassun El-Zafar (Executive Director). Both Gage and El-Zafar participated in [[El Aleph Festival de Arte y Ciencia]] 2025 — suggesting an active international festival-to-festival network connecting Edinburgh and Mexico City.

El-Zafar also performs/writes: *There Is No Planet B* (dramatized reading mixing science, narrative, and climate critique) was performed at El Aleph 2025.

## Why Interesting
Entry point to UK/European science festival circuit. The Edinburgh Science Foundation has a creative/critical programming arm that bridges science with art and social critique — unusual for a science festival.

## Key People
- **Simon Gage** — Science communicator, longtime Edinburgh Science Festival organizer
- **Hassun El-Zafar** — Executive Director, Edinburgh Science Foundation; also a writer/performer
