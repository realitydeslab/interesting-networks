# Bidoun

Arts and culture magazine/foundation dedicated to contemporary art in the Middle East and global diaspora. "Bidoun" means "without" in Arabic — referencing stateless populations in the Gulf. Published in New York since 2004. [[Sophia Al-Maria]] is a contributing editor. Published the foundational Gulf Futurism text "Mahma Kan Athaman" (Issue 20, 2010).

## Key People
- [[Sophia Al-Maria]] — contributing editor
- Babak Radboy — designer/publisher associated with Bidoun

## Topics
Contemporary Middle Eastern art and culture; Gulf modernity; postcolonial theory; diasporic cultural production; art criticism.

## Why Interesting
Primary critical/publishing infrastructure for Gulf Futurism. If Amber ever writes an essay on Gulf Futurism or non-Western techno-imaginaries, *Bidoun* is a primary target publication.
