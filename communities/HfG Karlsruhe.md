# HfG Karlsruhe (Hochschule für Gestaltung / Karlsruhe University of Arts and Design)

Karlsruhe University of Arts and Design. Shares the building (Hallenbau A) with [[ZKM Center for Art and Media Karlsruhe]]. Joint library with ZKM. Partners closely with [[Hertzlab ZKM]] — Margit Rosen (ZKM head of collections) teaches MediaArtHistories here.

Strong art-design-technology intersection. Key partner for understanding the Karlsruhe media art ecosystem. Founded 1992 as the practical design counterpart to ZKM's research orientation.

## Why Interesting
Co-housed with ZKM — makes Karlsruhe a dual institution destination. HfG + ZKM together cover design practice, media art production, theory, and archive. HfG offers guest lecturer positions, visiting artist residencies, and close integration with ZKM's Hertzlab. Support association Fördergesellschaft ZKM/HfG e.V. co-sponsors the Giga-Hertz PopExperimental Advancement Award.
