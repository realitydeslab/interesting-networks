# Earthsuits R&D Platform

An independent R&D platform co-curated by [[Lukáš Likavčan]] and [[Stephanie Sherman]], hosted at UAL Central Saint Martins, London. The platform takes its name from the concept of "earthsuit" — understanding Earth itself as the environmental suit that makes life possible. Investigates new modes of inhabiting the planet, with attention to how humans might live differently within planetary systems rather than simply extracting from them.

Investigating new modes of human inhabitation of the Earth, with speculative design and philosophical research approaches.
