# Eyebeam

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created via American Artist deep research.

NYC's leading art + technology residency and exhibition space. Founded 1997. Known for supporting artists who critique technology from inside it. Alumni include American Artist, Wendi Yan, and many leading critical tech artists. Residency programs include both studio space and commissioned projects. Strong curatorial commitment to technology criticism, not technology celebration.

## Why Interesting
Premier art+tech residency in the US with a critical, not celebratory, orientation. If Amber wants a US-based residency, Eyebeam is top of list. Also: the alumni network maps the critical computing art world.
