# MIX NYC

**The world's longest-running LGBTQIA+ experimental film festival.** Founded 1987 by author Sarah Schulman and filmmaker Jim Hubbard during the AIDS crisis. Annual MIX FEST + year-round programming in New York. The formative institution for queer experimental film in the US.

[[Shari Frilot]] was Director of MIX NYC from 1992 to 1996. During her tenure she co-founded **MIX Brasil** and **MIX México**, the first gay Latin American subsidiary film festivals.
