# Everyday Design Studio

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Ron Wakkary research. Key design research lab at SFU.

Design research studio at Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts and Technology. Founded and directed by [[Ron Wakkary]]. Specializes in research through design, more-than-human approaches, and tangible computing.

## Mission & Objectives
Investigates the changing nature of interaction design in response to everyday design practices and multispecies worlds. Uses reflective and generative design research to create new computational things and study their interactions in the world.

## Key People
- [[Ron Wakkary]] — founder and director
- Graduate students and postdocs working on more-than-human design
- Collaborative culture with friendly, engaging atmosphere

## Key Publications & Output
Research published at CHI, DIS, ToCHI, and other top HCI venues. Focus on more-than-human design, tangible computing, and design research methodology.
