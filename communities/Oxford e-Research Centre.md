# Oxford e-Research Centre

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-12:** Initial deep research. Full profile created.

The Oxford e-Research Centre (OeRC) is part of the Department of Engineering Science at the University of Oxford. Its expertise spans data science domains and technologies, delivering methodological, technical, social, and policy advancements across disciplines: physical sciences, engineering, arts, humanities, social sciences, energy, environment, biological and medical sciences, information and library sciences.

---

## Mission & Objectives

OeRC delivers novel research in computational and data-driven methods across all disciplines. Key activities:
- Data science across physical sciences, engineering, arts, humanities, social sciences
- Advising governments on strategic data-driven decisions
- Working with policymakers on open and reproducible science agendas
- Consulting for industry and publishers
- Collaborating with libraries and research communities worldwide
- Running courses, training modules, and events
- Hosting and supervising under- and postgraduates
