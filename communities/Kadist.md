# Kadist

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created via American Artist deep research.

International art foundation with offices in San Francisco and Paris. Collects works by artists from underrepresented regions and commissions new projects. Known for thoughtful, politically engaged collection — strong in critical technology art, climate, post-colonial perspectives. Commissioned American Artist's online *Shaper of God* research machine (2021). Runs public programs, residencies, and commissions.

## Why Interesting
One of the key commissioning bodies for critical computing art. The "Ways of Reading" commission series that produced American Artist's work is a model for experimental digital projects. Amber should know Kadist as a potential commissioner.
