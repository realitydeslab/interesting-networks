# Hanart Forum

Hong Kong-based discursive platform for contemporary thought and artistic practices. Founded 2022. Situated in a restored 1920s Edwardian-colonial row house in Happy Valley, Hong Kong. Close institutional partner of the [[Research Network for Philosophy and Technology]] — co-organizes the *Cybernetics for the 21st Century* lecture series and the *Dialogues on Philosophy and Technology 2025–26* series.

## Mission
A discursive platform for contemporary thought at the intersection of philosophy and art. Also a site for diverse artistic practices, with programming linking Chinese and global intellectual traditions.
