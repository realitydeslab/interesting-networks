# SCI-Arc (Southern California Institute of Architecture)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Updated during Graham Harman deep research — added Harman and Timothy Morton as key faculty; both are central OOO figures.
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Liam Young deep research.

Experimental architecture school in Los Angeles. Known for pushing architecture beyond buildings into film, games, VR, and speculative design.

## Mission & Objectives
Independent, accredited architecture school founded in 1972. Emphasizes experimental and speculative approaches. Notable for the [MS Fiction and Entertainment](https://www.sciarc.edu/academics/postgraduate/fiction-and-entertainment) programme run by [[Liam Young]], which trains architects to work in worldbuilding, film, games, and VR.

## Key People
- **[[Liam Young]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — MS Fiction and Entertainment Coordinator
- **[[Alexey Marfin]]** — Programme faculty, Fiction and Entertainment
- **[[Wendi Yan]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — MS Fiction and Entertainment alumna
- **[[Graham Harman]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Distinguished Professor of Philosophy and Liberal Arts Coordinator (since 2016); OOO founder
- **[[Timothy Morton]]** ⭐⭐ — Faculty, Synthetic Landscapes postgraduate programme; OOO ecologist, *Hyperobjects*

## Why Interesting
SCI-Arc's Fiction and Entertainment programme is the premier pipeline from speculative architecture to the entertainment industry. Deep ties to Hollywood (Framestore, Disney Imagineering, Netflix, Naughty Dog). Alumni network of speculative filmmakers/game designers. Model for how architecture education can be reimagined.

Additionally: SCI-Arc is the institutional home of **[[Graham Harman]]** (OOO philosopher, Distinguished Professor) and **[[Timothy Morton]]** (OOO ecologist, hyperobjects theorist). Two major OOO figures at the same architecture school makes SCI-Arc a central node for philosophy-architecture intersection. *Architecture and Objects* (Harman, 2022) is the key text.
