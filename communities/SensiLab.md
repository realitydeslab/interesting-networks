# SensiLab

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Discovered through [[Jon McCormack]] research. Stub created.

Trans-disciplinary research space at Monash University dedicated to experimental research in creative technologies. Founded and directed by [[Jon McCormack]].

## Mission & Objectives
Explores innovative creative applications and undiscovered opportunities of technology. Works across disciplines, approaches and materials with curiosity and rigour. Values process as well as outcome, experiential and sensory approaches. Creates space for new connections through diversity, collaboration, and openness to the unexpected.

## Key People
- **[[Jon McCormack]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Founder and Director. Professor jointly appointed in Faculty of Art Design & Architecture and Faculty of Information Technology.
- **[[Alon Ilsar]]** — Gestural audiovisual instruments, human-AI musical collaboration
- **[[Teresa Llano Rodriguez]]** — Computational creativity, human-AI collaboration
- Research staff in creative technologies, interaction design, AI systems

## Key Output
- Regular public SensiLab forums covering creative technology topics (live-streamed online)
- Research projects spanning computational creativity, human-machine interfaces, evolutionary art
- Collaborations with industry (Blundstone intelligent footwear project)
- PhD and research training programs
