# CARADT (Centre of Applied Research for Art, Design and Technology)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during V2_Lab deep research.

Research centre at Avans University of Applied Sciences (Breda/Tilburg, Netherlands). Focus on situated art, design and technology — specifically how technological developments influence relationships between people and their environments. Home to Michel van Dartel's research group (Situated Art, Design and Technology). Applied research orientation bridging art, design, and technology sectors.

## Why Interesting
Michel van Dartel (formerly V2_) now leads his research group here. The centre represents the applied-research wing of Dutch art-technology discourse. Relevant as a site where media art knowledge is being institutionalized in the university applied sciences sector.
