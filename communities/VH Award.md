# VH Award

Hyundai Motor Group's media art award. Asia's leading media art prize. Focuses on audiovisual artworks.

## Structure
- Open call for submissions
- Finalists exhibited at Vision Hall (Seoul), HEK Basel, Hyundai Motorstudio Beijing, [[Ars Electronica]]
- Grand Prix winner gets major exhibition tour

## Key People
- [[Wendi Yan]] — Grand Prix, 6th VH Award
- 6th finalists: Lena Bui (Vietnam), Huda × Mungomery (Indonesia/Australia), Tianyi Sun + Fiel Guhit (China/Philippines)

## Connections
- [[Ars Electronica]] — exhibition partner
- Hyundai Motor Group — also sponsors [LACMA Art + Technology Lab](https://www.lacma.org/), [Tate](https://www.tate.org.uk/)
- Korean/Asian media art network

## Why Interesting
Gateway to Asian art-tech scene. The finalists are often from underrepresented regions (Vietnam, Philippines, Indonesia). Worth tracking each year's cohort.
