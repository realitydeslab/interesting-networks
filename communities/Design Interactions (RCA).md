# Design Interactions (RCA)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Anthony Dunne deep research.

MA programme at the [[Royal College of Art]], London, run by [[Anthony Dunne]] (Professor & Head) and [[Fiona Raby]] (Reader) from 2005 to 2015. The programme that defined a generation of critical/speculative designers. In [[Paola Antonelli]]'s words, their tenure "changed the course of design."

The programme was suspended in 2015 after Dunne & Raby's departure triggered a mass exodus of teaching staff, and was eventually reformulated as other programmes (Information Experience Design, etc.).

## Why Interesting
The institutional origin point of speculative/critical design as a taught discipline. Understanding who went through DI is understanding the genealogy of the global speculative design network. Needs full alumni research.
