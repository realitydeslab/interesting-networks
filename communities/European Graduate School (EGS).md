# European Graduate School (EGS)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Graham Harman deep research.

Unusual private graduate school holding sessions in **Saas-Fee, Switzerland** (summer) and **Valletta, Malta**. EGS is the world's most unusual concentration of continental philosophers, critical theorists, filmmakers, and artists assembled in intensive short residencies. Not a conventional degree-granting institution in the traditional sense — but highly regarded as a site of intellectual encounter. Full name: **Division of Philosophy, Art, and Critical Thought** (PACT). Students receive MA and PhD degrees but the experience is about the intensive seminar encounter more than the credential.

## Mission & Objectives
Intensive graduate education in philosophy, media arts, and critical thought. Brings major intellectual figures together with international students for week-long seminars. The EGS experience is primarily about direct encounter with major thinkers — not about conventional course sequences or PhD thesis work in the standard sense.

## Why Interesting
EGS is the only institution in the world where you could study with Judith Butler, Slavoj Žižek, Graham Harman, Reza Negarestani, and Alain Badiou in the same summer. The intensive format creates real intellectual encounter rather than just curriculum transmission. The lack of conventional academic prestige makes it accessible to unconventional students. The YouTube archive is a free resource for continental philosophy education.
