# WPI-IRCN — International Research Center for Neurointelligence

World Premier International Research Center (WPI) at the University of Tokyo. Interdisciplinary neuroscience hub bridging brain science, AI, robotics, and human development. Key figures include:

- **Yukie Nagai** (Project Professor) — autism, social cognition, infant development, robots that learn like children
- **Tatsuya Daikoku** (past affiliation) — statistical learning, music, body mapping
- Connects to the ISI Lab (Kuniyoshi) ecosystem at UTokyo

## Why Interesting
The WPI-IRCN embeds AI and robotics researchers alongside neuroscientists — an unusual configuration that enables computationally rigorous models of neurodevelopmental and social processes. Yukie Nagai's work on autism and mutual imitation is adjacent to more-than-human and agent ethology themes.
