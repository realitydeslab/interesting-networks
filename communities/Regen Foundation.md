# Regen Foundation

Organization developing "ecological institutions" and "animist organizations" that give rivers, forests, and biomes agency to participate directly in governance protocols.

## Mission & Objectives
Building "coherence and infrastructure for organizations and communities to harness resources and compute without being consumed by them." Creating governance systems where non-human entities (rivers, forests, wetlands) can hold funding, make decisions, and participate in protocols.

## Key People
- [[Kei Kreutler]] — Board member
- Team building "ecological institutions" across four continents

## Key Programs
- Prototyping three new Ecological Institutions (Aotearoa, East Africa, Americas) by mid-2026:
  - A river holding communal flood-resilience funding
  - A wetland serving as fund and governance body  
  - A forest stewarding its own data and IP

## Publications & Output
- **Ecological Institutions: Law, Economics, and Technology in a More-Than-Human World** — Compendium of work on animist organizations
