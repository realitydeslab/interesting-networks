# SETI Institute Artists In Residence (AIR) Program

International art-science residency connecting contemporary artists with SETI Institute researchers. Curatorial direction emphasizes projects considering the evolution of intelligence, the beginnings of life, and anthropocentrism critique. Disciplines: visual arts, literature, music, film, dance, theatre.

## Key People
- [[Xin Liu]] — artist-in-residence
- Various SETI scientists (astrobiology, SETI research, planetary exploration)

## Topics
- Extraterrestrial intelligence, astrobiology
- Space exploration and the human body
- Cosmic scale and non-anthropocentric thinking
- Art × data science (AI-assisted SETI signal analysis)

## Events & Programmes
- SETI x AI residency with Ars Electronica (partnership)
- Making Contact programme
- AIR exhibitions calendar (conventions, conferences, exhibition circuit)

## Why Interesting
One of the few institutional homes where an artist can have genuine scientific collaborations around SETI/astrobiology, not just themed artwork. The SETI Institute has real research infrastructure (Allen Telescope Array, COSMIC on VLA, etc.). Artists-in-residence can engage with actual signal data. Very rare intersection of scientific rigor and artistic exploration of non-anthropocentric intelligence.
