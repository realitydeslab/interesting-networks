# Stimuleringsfonds Creatieve Industrie (Dutch Creative Industries Fund)

The Dutch Creative Industries Fund NL. Public fund supporting design, digital culture, and architecture research in the Netherlands. Funds individual makers, research projects, and international collaborations in Dutch creative industries.

## Why Interesting
This is the Dutch equivalent of Arts Council England for design/digital/architecture. [[Kristina Andersen]] is a longstanding advisor, meaning she has significant gatekeeping power over Dutch design research funding. Understanding this institution helps map the Dutch design research ecosystem and potential funding paths for collaborative work with Dutch researchers.

## Key People
- [[Kristina Andersen]] — longstanding advisor

## Programs
- Individual grants for designers and makers
- International collaboration grants
- Research + design residency funding
