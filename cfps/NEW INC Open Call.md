## What

NEW INC is the [New Museum's](https://www.newmuseum.org/) art, technology, and design incubator — the first museum-led incubator in the world. Now entering its 13th year, it supports 120+ creative entrepreneurs annually through a yearlong professional development program. 730+ alumni to date, $28.9M raised by members collectively.

**Year 13 program:** September 2026 – August 2027 (12 months)

**Five tracks:**

1. **Art & Code** *(partnership with [Rhizome](https://rhizome.org/))* — Computational techniques for artmaking, digital preservation, born-digital art. For artists, creative technologists, interaction designers, game designers, sound artists. "Redefining cultural and digital landscapes through artist-led research."

2. **Creative Science** — Scientific inquiry for storytelling and creative ventures. Speculative design, hybrid ecologies, biotechnology, robotics, renewable materials, immersive media. For artists and researchers using science to reveal the world. "Employing modes of scientific inquiry to advance creativity."

3. **Extended Realities** *(partnership with [Onassis ONX](https://www.onx.studio/))* — XR, VR, MR, augmented reality, immersive sound, generative systems, motion capture. Priority access to ONX's XR production studio in lower Manhattan. "Expanding the artistic potential for technology to blur the physical and digital worlds."

4. **Launchpad** — Ready-to-market products: functional art objects, packaged goods, games, educational toolkits, cooperatives. For founders taking concept to market.

5. **Social Architecture** — Design and architecture for community, public space, ecological systems. For architects, designers, urban planners, land artists.

**Cost:** $75/person/month + $150 one-time set-up fee (subsidized by institutional support). Not a stipend — members pay to participate.

**Benefits:**
- Bookable co-working space at New Museum (235 Bowery, lower Manhattan)
- 130+ mentors annually + dedicated mentor matchmaking
- Media Lab (3D capture/processing, machine learning, projection mapping, audio/video, motion capture)
- Fabrication Lab (laser cutters, 3D printers, large format printer, woodworking)
- Equipment borrowing: VR headsets, cameras, projectors, audio, event lighting
- Professional development: business modeling, fundraising, legal/IP, strategic roadmapping
- DEMO festival presentation (annual public showcase, May/June)
- New Museum network access
