## What

**EASST 2026** — the biennial conference of the European Association for Science and Technology Studies.

- **Dates:** September 8-11, 2026
- **Location:** AGH University of Krakow, Poland
- **Theme:** "More Than Now"
- **Format:** In-person only. Plenaries streamed.

### Theme Description
"More Than Now" — inspired by the "more-than-human" concept. STS has always been about "things could be otherwise." The conference explores how STS can contribute to futures thinking, imagining alternatives, and navigating crises.

Six streams:
1. **Transitions** — Anthropocene → Symbiocene, climate, infrastructure, data
2. **Speculating** — designing resilient futures, futures literacy
3. **Collaborating** — interdisciplinary STS with natural sciences and engineering
4. **Frontiers** — *gen AI explicitly mentioned*; space exploration; emerging risks and opportunities
5. **Next natures** — non-humans as actors, allies, assets, constraints
6. **Resilience** — fragile sociotechnical assemblages, compounding crises
