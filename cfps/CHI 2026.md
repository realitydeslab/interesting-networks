## Review Criteria (CHI)
CHI uses the ACM SIGCHI review system:
1. **Contribution** — What does this add to HCI knowledge?
2. **Novelty** — Is this genuinely new?
3. **Significance** — Does it matter to the field?
4. **Rigor** — Are claims supported? Are methods sound?
5. **Clarity** — Well-written and comprehensible?
6. **Fit** — Does it belong at CHI?

CHI increasingly values: qualitative methods, design research, critical and speculative approaches, non-Western perspectives, more-than-human frameworks.
