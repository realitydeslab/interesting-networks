## What

**[Akademie Schloss Solitude](https://www.akademie-solitude.de/)** is a prestigious Stuttgart-based residency program awarding 50–60 international fellowships every two years to artists, researchers, and cultural practitioners. Located in a baroque palace outside Stuttgart, it combines on-site studio residencies with international networking and interdisciplinary exchange.

The fellowship is **open-ended in terms of results** — no deliverables required. This is about creating space for concentrated work.

### Fellowship Tracks
- **Visual** — Fine Arts & Film/Video
- **Aural & Physical** — Music/Sound & Performing Arts
- **Spatial** — Architecture & Design
- **Textual** — Literature & Language
- **Digital** — New Media Art & Digital Art ← **AMBER'S TRACK**
  > *"Artistic practice engaging with digital, algorithmic and networked technologies, gaming, (live) coding, hacking, open-source culture, digital communities, and post-digital aesthetics."*
- **Scientific** — Humanities & Applied Sciences ← **ALSO RELEVANT**

### Eligibility
- International applicants welcome
- Min. 2 years, max. 10 years post-education professional experience (guideline, not strict)
- PhD candidates eligible (enrolled students typically not, unless PhD)
- Collectives of up to 2 people may apply
- English or German proficiency required

## Web Residencies (Separate Program)

Solitude also runs **[Web Residencies](https://www.akademie-solitude.de/en/fellowship/special-fellowships/web-residencies/)** — online residencies for artists experimenting with digital space. Launched in 2016, several calls per year, 4–8 weeks, €1,200 stipend. Curated around specific themes.

These are shorter, lower-stakes, and could be a stepping stone. Worth monitoring for calls.
