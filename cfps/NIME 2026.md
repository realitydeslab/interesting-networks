## What

**NIME 2026** is the 26th edition of the International Conference on New Interfaces for Musical Expression. Theme: **"Communities"** — what NIME can contribute to and learn from other musical and technical communities; participatory design; geographic and cultural specificity; ethnographic and co-design work.

## Why Relevant

### alt.nime = alt.chi resurrected

NIME 2026 introduced **alt.nime** in direct response to alt.chi being discontinued at CHI 2025:

> "NIME 2026 mourns the discontinuation of alt.chi. alt.chi was 'a forum for controversial, risk-taking, and boundary-pushing research' at the CHI conference. NIME 2026 is inspired to pick up and carry forward the tradition."

alt.nime submissions:
- "fight with core musical assumptions, explode technological paradoxes, **deconstruct research myths and narratives**"
- "apply **unorthodox methods** that expand NIME boundaries and quandaries"
- "engage with controversial socio-techno-musical issues through **politically activist or interventionist perspectives**"
- "question what a NIME research contribution is"

**Amber's work is alt.nime.** Machine death, terrified agents, parasitic agents — these are exactly the kind of contributions that alt.chi valued and that alt.nime now invites.

### London proximity

NIME 2026 is at Here East (Olympic Park, Stratford) and Rich Mix (Shoreditch). Both are easily reachable from Oxford via Paddington → Elizabeth line → Stratford. This is one of the few major conferences that comes to Amber's literal backyard.

### Community overlap

NIME regulars who also appear at CHI/DIS/HCI:
- Rebecca Fiebrink (ML for creative practitioners — Goldsmiths)
- Thor Magnusson (philosophical takes on instruments, Sussex)
- Anna Xambó (feminist + critical NIME, Newcastle)

### Theme "Communities" maps to Amber's approach

- Ethnographic/sociological study of new interface use ← agent ethology is ethnography of AI agents
- Co-design and participatory approaches ← Amber's design methodology
- Work with artists/communities outside the academic bubble ← Amber's China Academy of Art teaching
