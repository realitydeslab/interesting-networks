## What
Annual prize for electronic/electroacoustic music and sound art, running since 2007. Run jointly by **ZKM | Hertzlab** (Karlsruhe) and **SWR Experimentalstudio Freiburg**.

Total prize pool: **€25,000**. Three prizes:
- **Giga-Hertz Grand Prize** — €10,000 for lifetime achievement (jury-nominated only, **no open call**)
- **2× Giga-Hertz Production Awards** — €5,000 each — **OPEN CALL** — for sound artists/composers of electronic, electroacoustic, or mixed music
- **Giga-Hertz PopExperimental Advancement Award** — €5,000 — for experimental electronic pop/club music (since 2022, in cooperation with Popakademie Baden-Württemberg)

**Winners receive a production residency** at ZKM Hertzlab (Karlsruhe) or SWR Experimentalstudio (Freiburg) to realize a new work. New works premiere at the following year's ceremony.

## Submission Requirements (Production Awards)

Two-step application:

1. **Reference work:**
   - Must be ≤5 years old
   - Must not exceed **20 minutes**
   - Must use clearly identifiable **electronic and technical means**
   - Must be presentable without great technical effort (jury shortlisting requirement)

2. **Project exposé:**
   - Description of a **new work still to be realized**
   - Winners realize the new work during residency at ZKM Hertzlab or SWR Experimentalstudio
   - New works premiere as world premieres at the following year's Giga-Hertz Award ceremony
