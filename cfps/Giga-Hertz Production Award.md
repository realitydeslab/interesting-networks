# CFP: Giga-Hertz Production Award (ZKM | Hertzlab)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created during ZKM research. Annual award. Check ZKM open-calls page for current deadline.

## What
Annual prize for electronic/electroacoustic music and sound art. Run jointly by **ZKM | Hertzlab** and **SWR Experimentalstudio Freiburg**.

Three prizes:
- **Giga-Hertz Grand Prize** — €10,000 for lifetime achievement (jury-nominated, no open call)
- **2× Giga-Hertz Production Awards** — €5,000 each — **OPEN CALL** — for sound artists/composers
- **Giga-Hertz PopExperimental Advancement Award** — €5,000 — for experimental electronic pop/club music (since 2022)

**Winners get a production residency** at ZKM Hertzlab (Karlsruhe) or SWR Experimentalstudio (Freiburg) to realize new work. New works premiere at the award ceremony the following year.

## How to Apply (Production Awards)
1. Submit reference work: ≤5 years old, ≤20 minutes, clearly uses electronic/technical means
2. Submit project exposé for a new work to be realized
3. Work must be presentable without great technical difficulty for jury shortlisting

## Deadline
Check annually at <https://zkm.de/en/open-calls>. Typically early-to-mid year for following year premiere.

## Why Relevant
If Amber makes sound/electronic music work, this is a direct path to €5k + studio residency in Karlsruhe or Freiburg. Even without winning, applying puts Amber on ZKM's radar. PopExperimental track since 2022 broadens scope beyond classical electroacoustic.
