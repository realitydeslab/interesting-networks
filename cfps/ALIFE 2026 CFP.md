- Assigned oral or poster presentation

### Summaries (💡 Low-bar if Amber has published work)
- **Up to 2 pages**, NOT including references
- Must summarize a **previously published, peer-reviewed** work (NOT preprints — those should be full papers)
- NOT included in proceedings (publicly available but no DOI)
- Peer-reviewed for quality and relevance
- Assigned oral or poster (full papers get oral priority)

### Late-Breaking Abstracts (🏃 Last resort, work-in-progress)
- **Up to 2 pages**, NOT including references
- New ideas and work in progress explicitly OK
- NOT included in proceedings
- Poster presentation only
- **Deadline: July 20, 2026** — much more time

### Journal Fast-Track (⭐ Premium route)
- Papers accepted in *Artificial Life* journal (MIT Press) → **automatically accepted as oral presentations** at ALIFE 2026
