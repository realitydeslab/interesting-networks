## Conference Theme: ELYAH

"Just as ancient navigators turned to the stars, today's artists and technologists interpret the signals of an algorithmic cosmos, charting the digital unknown with creativity as their compass."

Sub-themes relevant to Amber:
- **Data and Identity** — how data constructs/erases identity
- **Place and Technology** — site-specific works, urban/desert contexts
- **Digital Calligraphy** — traditional knowledge × computational practice (non-Western tech)
- **Algorithmic Nature** — emergent systems, ecological thinking
