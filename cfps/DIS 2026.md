## What

ACM Designing Interactive Systems 2026, Singapore, June 13–17, hosted by NUS Division of Industrial Design.

**Theme: "Beyond Interaction"** — provocatively questioning whether "interactivity" itself reinforces a dyadic, anthropocentric model of human-system relations. Calls for an ecosystemic perspective that attends to more-than-human actors and sociocultural, political, economic, planetary forces.

Key questions the conference is asking:
- Does "interactivity" reinforce a dyadic human-system model that limits our understanding?
- How do designed things interact with broader sociocultural, political, and planetary forces?
- What lies *beyond* interaction that should be visible in design processes?
- How should we redefine what is "interactive" in interactive systems?

This is essentially Amber's research agenda in conference form.
