## What

RTD (Research Through Design) is a biennial conference for practice-based design research with a distinctive, non-conventional format:
- **Curated exhibition** of design research artifacts (the artifact IS the research)
- **"Rooms of Interest"** — themed round-table discussions, not dark-auditorium presentations
- No traditional lecture-format paper sessions
- Two-stage peer review with ~15% overall acceptance (based on RTD 2015 data)
- All areas of design accepted: interaction, product, fashion, jewellery, service, digital, speculative, critical

**Historical submission types (RTD 2015–2019):**
- **Room submissions** — practice-based artifact with documentation (annotated portfolio format)
- **Provocations** — invited plenary talks to spark debate
- Accepts work from "all areas of design" — product, fashion, graphic, communication, jewellery, industrial, interaction, service, digital, and adjacent disciplines

**Proceedings:** Past editions hosted on [figshare](https://figshare.com/) (open access). RTD 2019 proceedings: [figshare collection](https://figshare.com/articles/conference_contribution/RTD_2019_Proceedings/7855664)

## Why Relevant to Amber

RTD is arguably the conference most philosophically aligned with Amber's practice-based, artifact-first research approach. Key alignment:

1. **More-than-human design** — RTD 2019 had a dedicated "More-than-Human Partnerships" Room of Interest, featuring work on sympoietic artifacts and multispecies design. Amber's work here is directly citable.

2. **Artifact-as-research** format — HoloKit, machine behavior probes, somaesthetic AR systems are exactly the kind of artifacts RTD is built for. The format doesn't demand Amber reduce the work to text-only claims.

3. **Speculative design** — Strong tradition at RTD (Coulton et al. on "future mundane," Benque on speculative diagrams). Amber's speculative work on machine death, insured agents, AI mortality fits.

4. **Somaesthetics** — Kristina Höök's somaesthetic work appeared at RTD 2019. Amber's embodied AR aligns.

5. **Community** — Ron Wakkary (key RTD figure) is already on Amber's radar. RTD 2019 proceedings featured Steve Benford (Nottingham) — another Amber-adjacent figure.
