## Theme: TechnoPower • Technoscientific Futures

The 2026 theme is sharply political: tech power concentration, AI billionaires, assetization of knowledge, ecological justice, Indigenous knowledge, citizen science, collective wellbeing. The organizing committee explicitly calls for STS to intervene in public, policy, and political debate about technopower.

**Submission types:**
- **Paper Abstracts** (to open or closed panels) — up to 250 words
- **Closed Panels** (min. 3 papers, max. 6 interventions per 100-min session)
- **Making & Doing** (4 types: interactive exhibitions, workshops, non-interactive displays, films) — key track for Amber!
- **Roundtables** (min. 1 organizer + 2 presenters, conversational format)
- **Meetups** (informal, up to 200 words)
- **SLSA Streams** (co-hosted with SLSA 2026; 4 streams: Technology, Ecologies, Play, Arts & Literature; same April 30 deadline)
