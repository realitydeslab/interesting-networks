FAccT's focus areas are a direct match to Amber's work:
- **Machine behavior** — AI agents as sociotechnical systems with behavioral profiles
- **Critical computing** — questioning what it means for AI systems to be accountable
- **Human-AI interaction** — how people understand, anthropomorphize, and delegate to agents
- **Philosophical/STS approaches** — qualitative, speculative, and critical methods welcomed

FAccT explicitly welcomes "philosophical, sociological, and theoretical research" — rare for major computing venues. Amber's blend of ALife + STS + design fits perfectly.
