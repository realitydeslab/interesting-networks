## Theme

**"By the Mango Belt & Tamarind Road"**

The festival explored tropical epistemologies, archipelagic logics, and Global South aesthetics as alternative frameworks for thinking about digital culture. The curatorial approach used *compassing, protocoling, and metaphoring* as navigational methods — a compass-based methodology rather than a single theme title.

Curatorial quote: *"Ukitaka kwenda haraka, nenda peke yako. Ukitaka kwenda mbali, nenda pamoja na wenzako"* (Ubuntu proverb) — "If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together."

Programme strands: **High Tide** (collective, large-scale events) + **Low Tide** (intimate, durational installations)
