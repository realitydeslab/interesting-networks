## What

Le Fresnoy – Studio national des arts contemporains (Tourcoing, France) accepts applications for its unique 2-year post-graduate programme in contemporary arts. The programme accepts students from **all artistic backgrounds** (visual art, cinema, photography, video, architecture, music, performance, etc.) and **all nationalities**.

**What students get:**
- €8,400 production budget per project (2 projects over 2 years)
- Access to professional equipment: film stages, recording/mixing studios, video editing suites (16mm and 35mm), darkroom, photography studio, carpentry/metalworking/digital workshops
- Mentorship by world-leading visiting artist-professors (rotating annually — no permanent faculty)
- Exhibition of work at the annual **Panorama** show (seen at Centre Pompidou, Palais de Tokyo, TBA21, etc.)
- Free tuition for all nationalities (760€/year attendance fee only)
- International network: works travel to biennials, festivals, major cultural institutions globally
- **Optional doctorate in artistic creation** pathway with Université de Lille or UQAM (Montréal) — for enrolled students only

**Year 1:** Film, photography, video, sound creation — experimentation with traditional media in a transdisciplinary logic.

**Year 2:** Digital creation — cutting-edge technologies and contemporary forms of expression.

- **Background résumé** (10 lines): how your background led you to apply
- **Motivation letter / note of intention** (3 pages PDF): motivations and how artistic practice could develop at Le Fresnoy — *"judged on quality of writing and relevance of arguments"*
- **Projects envisaged for the 2 years at Le Fresnoy** (1 page per project): type of work, field, transdisciplinary mode (film, video, photography, sound, interactive/programmed installations, live arts, etc.)
- **Artists and works that have inspired you** in these fields (20 lines)

### Artistic portfolio — Visual material:
- **MP4 file, max 8 minutes** — work(s) representative of your practice. Can be video extracts, photo slideshow, sound excerpts, installation documentation, etc. Each extract must be preceded by a label with nature and context. If collaborative, specify your role.
- *"The importance of these documents cannot be overstated — they are the only materials examined at BOTH stages of the selection process."*

### Jury criteria:
- Originality, reach, and quality of personal artistic work
- Experience and mastery of skills (especially technical)
- Relation to multi- or trans-disciplinarity
- General culture (training, spoken/written skills)
- Effective interest towards audiovisual and digital idioms (from traditional to digital creation)
- Presentation of portfolio

### Jury composition:
- Representatives of different artistic disciplines taught at Le Fresnoy
- Director of Le Fresnoy
- 30-minute interview

## Key Observations: What Le Fresnoy Is Actually About

1. **Cinema is the spine.** Despite "contemporary arts" branding, the visitor professorship list since 1997 is dominated by filmmakers (Béla Tarr, Claire Denis, Chantal Akerman, Tsaï Ming-Liang, Wang Bing, Miguel Gomes). The "digital creation" in Year 2 is real but secondary to expanded cinema.

2. **Very European/French institutional prestige.** The value proposition is access to French cultural institutions — Centre Pompidou, Palais de Tokyo, Foundation Hermès, IRCAM. Less useful for Amber's US-UK-Chinese network.

3. **The visiting professor model is the intellectual heart.** The quality of any given year depends entirely on that year's 6 professors. 2025-26 is interesting (Donatien Aubert, Gwenola Wagon) but 2024-25 was better for Amber (Vinciane Despret, Hicham Berrada).

4. **Production budget.** €8,400/project for 2 years is real but modest by art world standards. Le Fresnoy's value is the institutional prestige and equipment, not the budget.

5. **French required.** Amber would need solid French. This is a real barrier.

6. **No AI/computation faculty in 2025-26.** The programme does not currently emphasize AI/machine learning as a medium, unlike Ars Electronica residencies or ZHdK. Justine Emard (AI+robotics) was visiting 2021-22 and 2023-24 but not continuously.
