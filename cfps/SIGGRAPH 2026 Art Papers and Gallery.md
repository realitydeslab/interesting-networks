## What

**ACM SIGGRAPH 2026** — 53rd annual conference. The world's premier venue for computer graphics + interactive techniques + digital art.

**Two tracks most relevant for Amber:**

### Art Papers
- Peer-reviewed academic papers on creative technology research
- Published in **[PACMCGIT](https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmcgit)** (Proceedings of the ACM on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) — a standing ACM journal, high prestige
- Theme 2026: **"The Creative Complexities of Translation: Practices, Artifacts, and Stories"**
  - Creative practices that translate across time, culture, community, domains, media
  - Diverse knowledge systems, hybridity, historical and speculative framings
  - Artists/technologists/designers "translating" elements of the past, remediating the present, imagining the future
- Format: Long papers (~8-15 pages) and Short papers — both fully peer-reviewed
- Organizer: **Vernelle A. A. Noel** (Art Papers Chair)
- Submission portal: [Art Paper — Long](https://ssl.linklings.net/conferences/siggraph/?page=Submit&id=ArtPapersLongSubmission&site=siggraph2026) | [Art Paper — Short](https://ssl.linklings.net/conferences/siggraph/?page=Submit&id=ArtPapersShortSubmission&site=siggraph2026)

### Art Gallery
- **Juried exhibition** of interactive artworks, installations, immersive pieces
- Theme 2026: **"In-Betweens"**
  - Artworks that foreground *relations* — technical, conceptual, temporal, spatial — across systems, materials, and publics
  - "large-scale questions and humanitarian concerns"
  - "foundational components such as algorithmic methods and computing hardware"
  - "increasingly interconnected world"
  - Speculate on "our place within the layered infrastructures of contemporary technology"
- Must be interactive and experience-able in-person at LA Convention Center
- Best in Show award; exhibited alongside "global awardees and field-defining legends"
- Organizer: **Everardo Reyes** (Art Gallery Chair)
