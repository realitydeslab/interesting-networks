## What This Is

This is a **call for workshop proposals** — Amber would be *organising* a workshop, not just submitting a paper. One-day or half-day workshop format. Proposals: 4 pages (2-column IMWUT format) + appendix CFP.

**Accepted workshops can solicit their own papers** for inclusion in ACM Digital Library adjunct proceedings.

1. **Agent Ethology in Pervasive Contexts** — studying AI agent behavior in ubiquitous computing environments; how do agents behave when embedded in everyday life?

3. **AI Welfare in Embedded Systems** — machine wellbeing as design criterion for IoT/pervasive agents
4. **Machine Behavior in the Wild** — empirical methods for studying AI agent behavior in deployment
