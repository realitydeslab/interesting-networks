# CFP: Computational Neurophenomenology Cabin Workshop 2027

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-15:** Created as future-tracking note. 2026 edition happening next week (Mar 22–29). Estimated 2027 deadline ~Dec 2026, watch from Oct 2026.

## What

A week-long intensive workshop in a mountain cabin in **Dorfgastein, Austrian Alps**, bringing together ~18 early-career researchers (senior PhD, postdocs, early faculty) for deep, focused discussions on computational and mathematical approaches to consciousness, phenomenology, and the mind-body problem.

**Format:**
- One week in a remote mountain cabin (no internet, bunk beds, shared cooking)
- 40–45 discussion sessions in small groups
- Kick-off talks (30 min) to seed each discussion
- Parallel small-group sessions on specific open questions
- No traditional conference presentations — discussion-first
- €300 workshop fee (housing + food included), financial support available

**Topics (based on 2026 edition):**
- Mathematical phenomenology
- Computational phenomenology
- Neurophenomenology and radical neurophenomenology (Varela's tradition)
- Predictive processing
- Dynamical systems approaches to consciousness
- Gestalt principles and perceptual organization
- Qualia-structure formalisms
- Temporality, selfhood, agency
- Valence, unity, intentionality

**Organizers (2026):** [[Robert Chis-Ciure]] and [[Ishan Singhal]] (both Sussex Centre for Consciousness Science postdocs, lab members of [[Anil K. Seth]])

**Funding (2026):** ERC Grant 101019254 "CONSCIOUS" ([[Anil K. Seth]]) + Marie Skłodowska-Curie Grant 101108884
