## Why Relevant

**Primary reason: Joel Lehman.** This is Amber's ERA fellowship advisor. Having Joel as workshop co-organizer creates a natural connection point.

**The workshop covers:**
- LLMs as algorithm designers (FunSearch, AlphaEvolve, LLaMEA frameworks)
- EC providing evolutionary search pressure for LLM-generated solutions
- Open-ended algorithm discovery
- "Self-improving AI design ecosystems"

**Connection to Amber's work:**
- **Open-endedness** — a core theme here, directly touching Amber's ALife interests

**Honest assessment of fit:** This workshop is more technical/optimization-focused than Amber's typical work. The audience is primarily EC researchers interested in algorithm design, not STS/design researchers. However:
- A paper framing LLM-EC hybrids through an **ethological or behavioral lens** could be distinctive
- Joel Lehman's presence means a familiar community
- Could be a good opportunity for Amber to deepen her ERA fellowship relationship
