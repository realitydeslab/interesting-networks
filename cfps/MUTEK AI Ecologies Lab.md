# CFP: MUTEK AI Ecologies Lab (Annual Residency Call)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Created note from deep research on MUTEK. 2025 call closed Feb 14, 2025. Watch for 2026 call ~January 2026.

## What
An **interdisciplinary residency** for 6 selected participants developing proposals for **AI tools that work towards a sustainable digital arts sector**. Funded programme with virtual and in-person components, culminating in a showcase at MUTEK Forum (Montréal).

**Scope:**
- Question the environmental impacts of AI in the cultural sector
- Prototype climate-sustainable AI tools for cultural organizations and artistic practice
- Develop best practices for sustainable adoption of AI
- Bridge artistic, curatorial, and technical disciplines

**Programme structure (2025 model):**
- **January–February:** Call for projects open
- **March:** Notification
- **April:** Virtual lab (10 hours, seminars + 1:1 coaching)
- **June (1 week, Montréal):** In-person lab at Concordia University + Society for Arts and Technology (SAT) — hackathon-style sprint developing prototypes
- **August:** Showcase at MUTEK Forum (Montréal)
- **September:** Evaluation and feedback

**Partners:** MUTEK + SAT + Applied AI Institute + Milieux Institute + Abundant Intelligences
