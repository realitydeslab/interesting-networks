## Why Relevant

The "Post-AGI Science and Society" framing is important territory for Amber even though this specific workshop's deadline has passed:

### Intellectual resonance
- **"Post-AGI"** is Amber's exact research landscape — what happens to machines, to agents, to societies after AGI becomes a serious possibility?
- Amber's **machine death / thanatology** work is fundamentally post-AGI thinking: only when machines might be AGI do we need to think about their death, care, and moral status
- **Cosmotechnics** (Yuk Hui) is a framework for post-AGI technology philosophy — how different civilizations will process AGI differently

- Practice-based methodology (art + design + ALife)
