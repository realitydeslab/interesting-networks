# Paul Kolling

Berlin-based artist and co-founder of [[terra0]]. Solo practice examines the social and spatial politics of infrastructure — mediated perception, the visual politics of seeing and classifying. Works across film, video, installation, and sculpture. MFA student of Simon Denny (key figure connecting Kolling to the blockchain art + New Zealand contemporary art scenes). Won ars viva prize 2023 (Germany's most prestigious prize for emerging artists). Also studied at China Academy of Art Hangzhou.

## Background
- BPA// Berlin Program for Artists (2021–2022) — prestigious Berlin-based artists' programme
- MFA with [[Simon Denny]] at HFBK Hamburg — key relationship
- Studied at China Academy of Art Hangzhou with 姚大钧 Yao Dajuin (2019) — sound/media art connections to China
