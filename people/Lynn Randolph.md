# Lynn Randolph

Feminist artist. Important art-theory collaboration with Donna Haraway (1990-1996) for *Modest_Witness* project.

## Key Works
- Collaborative images for Donna Haraway's *Modest_Witness@Second_Millennium.FemaleMan©Meets_OncoMouse™* (1997)
- Series of paintings exploring technoscience themes

## Key Relationships
- [[Donna Haraway]] — major collaborative partnership (1990-1996)

## Why Interesting
Important model of art-theory collaboration. Her partnership with Haraway demonstrates how visual art can be integral to theoretical work, not just illustration. Worth studying for collaborative methodologies between artists and theorists.
