# W. Ross Ashby (1903–1972)

British psychiatrist and pioneer of cybernetics. Author of *Design for a Brain* (1952) and *An Introduction to Cybernetics* (1956) — the clearest introductory text on cybernetics ever written. Creator of the **Homeostat** (an electromechanical device that demonstrated adaptive self-regulation). Member of the **Ratio Club** (original UK cybernetics group). Regular professor at [[Heinz von Foerster]]'s BCL from 1961.

## Why Interesting
His **Law of Requisite Variety** — "only variety can absorb variety" — is one of the most fundamental results in systems theory with direct implications for AI design. The Homeostat was a proto-ALife machine.
