# Tananarive Due

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created via American Artist deep research. Essay contributor to *Shaper of God* monograph (Pioneer Works Press, 2025).

Award-winning Afrofuturist author and UCLA professor of Afrofuturism and Black Horror. One of the leading figures in Afrofuturist fiction and theory. Collaborates with American Artist on *Shaper of God* project. Teaches at UCLA, where she developed one of the first accredited Afrofuturism courses. Author of *The Between*, *Ghost Summer*, *Blood Colony*, and many other works. 

## Why Interesting
Afrofuturism as method for understanding present technological conditions. Overlap with Amber's speculative design and experiential futures interests. The Afrofuturism-meets-critical-tech framework that American Artist uses is partly Due's lineage.
