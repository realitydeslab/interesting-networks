# Lauren Klein

Professor of Data & Decision Sciences and English at Emory University; Director of the [Digital Humanities Lab](https://dhlab.quantitative.emory.edu/) and the [Atlanta Interdisciplinary AI Network](http://aiai.network/). Co-author with Catherine D'Ignazio of *Data Feminism* (MIT Press, 2020). Her work bridges data science, machine learning, digital humanities, and American literary/cultural history with a focus on gender and race.
