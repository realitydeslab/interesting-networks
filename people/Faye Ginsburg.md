# Faye Ginsburg

Anthropologist at NYU; pioneer of media anthropology, especially indigenous media. Co-founded the field with *Media Worlds* (2002, with [[Brian Larkin]] and [[Lila Abu-Lughod]]). Known for work on Aboriginal Australian media.

## Domains
- Indigenous media and self-representation
- Media anthropology
- Disability and media

## Key Works
- *Media Worlds: Anthropology on New Terrain* (California UP, 2002) — co-edited with Larkin and Abu-Lughod; landmark text establishing media anthropology

## Why Interesting
Pioneer who established media anthropology as a recognized field; her focus on indigenous/non-Western media production is a precursor to questions about non-Western AI and technology development.
