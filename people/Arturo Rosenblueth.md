# Arturo Rosenblueth (1900–1970)

Mexican physiologist and co-founder of cybernetics. Wiener's closest intellectual collaborator — co-authored "Behavior, Purpose and Teleology" (1943, the founding cybernetics paper) and *Cybernetics* (1948). Wiener spent extended periods at Rosenblueth's Mexico City lab. Together they developed the first mathematical model of cardiac reentry (1946). Exemplar of the mathematician-embedded-in-biology-lab model.
