# Maximilian Schich

Professor of Cultural Data Analytics, ERA Chair holder at CUDAN Open Lab, Tallinn University. Multidisciplinary scientist + art historian. PhD LMU Munich; postdoc at BarabásiLab (Boston) and Dirk Helbing group (Zurich). Previously at UT Dallas (founding member, Edith O'Donnell Institute of Art History).

## Why Interesting
2014 *Science* paper ["A Network Framework of Cultural History"](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.1247768) and viral Nature video "Charting Culture" — showed that birth/death patterns of 150K cultural figures trace systematic gravity flows between cities across centuries. Pioneered network analysis in art research. Now leading [[CUDAN Open Lab]] — the most ambitious attempt to create a proper science lab for humanities in Europe.
