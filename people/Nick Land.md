# Nick Land

British philosopher. The driving force behind the [[CCRU]] after [[Sadie Plant]]'s departure. Originator of accelerationist philosophy as a systematic framework. His small, limited-circulation canon of texts had an enormous subterranean influence on philosophy, music (Kode9 studied with him), art (Jake Chapman), and theory-fiction (Reza Negarestani). Later turned to Dark Enlightenment/NRx positions — now disowned by most colleagues. Lives in Shanghai.

## Background
- Lecturer in Continental Philosophy at University of Warwick. Had distinctive pedagogical style: iron gaze, soft compelling voice, startling intellectual confidence. Kadwo Eshun: "When you were in Nick's presence, thinking mattered. It took on a mortal quality — it became enlivened, libidinised, intensified."
- Creative and personal partner of [[Sadie Plant]] before and during early CCRU.
- Co-founded [[CCRU]] with Plant in 1995. Became driving force after Plant left in 1997.
- Amphetamine use and antisocial behavior led to effective forced resignation ~1998. CCRU continued from Leamington Spa flat until Land's breakdown and disappearance in early 2000s.
- Re-emerged online. Married [[Anna Greenspan]]. Moved to Shanghai. Began publishing far-right material under own name and pseudonyms.
- Lives in Shanghai; connected to Chinese tech-philosophy scene.

## Key Works/Papers
- *[The Thirst for Annihilation: Georges Bataille and Virulent Nihilism](https://www.routledge.com/The-Thirst-for-Annihilation-Georges-Bataille-and-Virulent-Nihilism/Land/p/book/9780415060271)* (Routledge, 1992) — Only conventionally published book. Experimental mix of prose-poem, spiritual autobiography, and Bataille explication.
- ["Meltdown"](http://www.ccru.net/swarm1/1_melt.htm) (1994, Abstract Culture) — Canonical accelerationist text. "Things are breaking down. The future is accelerating. Capitalism is the process and the product of its own unfolding."
- "Machinic Desire" — Fisher: "I read it, then immediately re-read it two or three times. Theory wasn't being 'applied' here; it was being plugged in."
- *[Fanged Noumena: Collected Writings 1987-2007](https://www.urbanomic.com/book/fanged-noumena/)* ([[Urbanomic]], 2011) — Retrospective collected writings. Publication credited with re-launching accelerationism. Secondhand copies reached £180.
- Ongoing blog/online writing on Dark Enlightenment, NRx, anti-democracy

## Why Interesting
Genealogically essential — the CCRU lineage runs through almost everything in speculative tech-philosophy. The lineage Land → accelerationism → Bratton → Antikythera is direct. His Shanghai location and Anna Greenspan's AI work connects to the Chinese tech-philosophy scene. Controversial later politics make him complicated, but the intellectual influence is undeniable. Kodwo Eshun's framing is right: whether or not he's "the most important British philosopher of the last 20 years" is debatable, but his subterranean influence is enormous.
