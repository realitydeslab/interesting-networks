# Meiro Koizumi 小泉明郎

Japanese artist working with confrontational video. Breaks down taboos within Japanese society: collective memory, Japanese attitudes to the past, media culture, kamikaze/samurai myths, American veterans of Iraq and Afghanistan. Peer of [[Chia-Wei Hsu]] in EYE Filmmuseum group show *A Tale of Hidden Histories* (2019).

Key works: *Defect in Vision* (2011), *Portrait of a Young Samurai* (2009), *Battlelands* (2018).
