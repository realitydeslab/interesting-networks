# Lorenz Engell

German media philosopher and film theorist. Professor for Media Philosophy and Media History at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar. Co-founded the [[IKKM Weimar]] with [[Bernhard Siegert]] in 2008. Co-directed it until 2020. Co-edited the *Zeitschrift für Medien- und Kulturforschung* (ZfMK) and the *Archiv für Mediengeschichte*. Key figure in German media philosophy, with particular focus on film and television as media of time, attention, and the real.
