# Julian Bleecker

Co-founder of [Near Future Laboratory](https://nearfuturelaboratory.com/), the design fiction and futures studio/podcast. Principal theorist of "design fiction" as distinct from speculative design — his 2009 paper "Design Fiction: A Short Essay on Design, Science, Fact and Fiction" is widely cited. Engineer, designer, writer. Collaborated with Bruce Sterling and Nicolas Nova. Runs the Near Future Laboratory podcast, which has featured Anthony Dunne (2022). LACMA, conferences on design futures. Previous: Nokia Research, Intel Research.

## Why Interesting
Parallel to [[Anthony Dunne]] in the speculative/critical design ecosystem but coming from Silicon Valley engineering culture rather than fine art/design academia. His "design fiction" frame is more secular and tech-adjacent than D&R's critical design. Worth understanding the Bleecker vs. D&R framing difference — they're related but distinct approaches to the same territory.
