# Stavros Didakis

Associate Arts Professor of Interactive Media Arts at [[NYU Shanghai IMA]]. Academic, researcher, and artist. Speculates on technological futures through architectural augmentation and inhabitant personalization. Research: sensorial interfaces, responsive ecologies, computational frameworks as extensions of human environments.

## Key Works
- "XENOFORMS: Computational Autonomy & Hybrid Architectures" — DIS'23 (ACM)
- "Ubiquitous Colonies: Computational Parasites, Sentiment, & the Architectural Space" — IEEE ICRA, Sentimental Machines 2021
- "Beyond Digital Towards Biological" — ISEA 2018 (with Laura Beloff)
- "Spatializing Invisible Matter; Atoms and Sensor Technologies" — *[Leonardo](https://direct.mit.edu/leon)* (MIT Press)
- "Objects of Affect: The Domestication of Ubiquity" — *Technoetic Arts*
