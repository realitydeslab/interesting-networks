# Luke Hespanhol

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Lian Loke deep research.

Associate Professor at the University of Sydney Design Lab. Researches urban interaction design, playful placemaking, and contemplative/body-aware HCI. Close colleague of Lian Loke — co-authored the TOCHI contemplative framework paper (Mah, Loke, Hespanhol, 2021), the Buddhist-inspired interactive artwork (TEI 2020), and multiple urban computing papers with Louis Chew. His work bridges outdoor/urban spaces with embodied and contemplative interaction.

## Key Works
- [**Towards a Contemplative Research Framework for Training Self-Observation in HCI**](https://doi.org/10.1145/3471932) (ACM *TOCHI*, 2021) — with Kristina Mah, Lian Loke
- [**To Play and To Be Played: Exploring the Design of Urban Machines for Playful Placemaking**](https://doi.org/10.3389/fcomp.2021.635949) (*Frontiers in Computer Science*, 2021) — with Louis Chew, Lian Loke
