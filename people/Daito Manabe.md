# Daito Manabe

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note. Discovered through [[Kyle McDonald]] research as primary Tokyo collaborator.

Artist, programmer, DJ, composer. Co-founder of [Rhizomatiks](https://rhizomatiks.com/) — Tokyo's leading creative technology collective. Works at the intersection of data, body, technology, and performance. Collaborates with musicians, dancers, scientists, engineers. Multiple collaborations with [[Kyle McDonald]]: *Transformirror* (2023), *Generative MV* (2023), *discrete figures* (2018), *The Janus Machine* (2011), *Transcranial*. Key bridge between the Euro-American art-tech world and the Japanese tech-art scene.

## Key Works
- **[Rhizomatiks](https://rhizomatiks.com/)** — co-founder of Japan's premier creative tech collective
- *discrete figures* (2018) — with Kyle McDonald; touring dance performance using custom dance dataset + ML
- *Transformirror* (2023) — with Kyle McDonald; AI mirror generating all content in real time
- *Generative MV* (2023) — with Kyle McDonald; at NTT ICC, Tribute to Ryuichi Sakamoto
- Collaborations with Björk, Perfume (the band), and major Japanese artists

## Why Interesting
Rhizomatiks is arguably the world's most technically sophisticated art-tech collective. Daito Manabe is its conceptual driver. The Tokyo scene he anchors is distinct from European media art and US creative tech — more embedded in pop culture and performance. Bridge to Japanese contemporary art-tech essential for anyone working in this space.
