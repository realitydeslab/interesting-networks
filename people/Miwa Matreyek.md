# Miwa Matreyek

Los Angeles-based artist, performer, and filmmaker who creates live performances combining animation, shadow puppetry, projection, and her own body. Known for her signature form: performer as silhouette/shadow inside a world of projected animation, creating poetic digital-physical tableaux. Shown at [[Sundance New Frontier]] 2020 with "Infinitely Yours" (climate grief performance).

## Domains
- Live performance + animation + projection
- Shadow puppetry and embodied digital art
- Climate and ecological grief
- Body as interface in performance

## Key Works
- "Infinitely Yours" (2020) — 25-minute live stage performance incorporating dance, shadow puppetry, animation, live music; her own garbage integrated as materials; climate processing and personal responsibility
- "Myth and Infrastructure" (2012) — TED-shown performance
- "This World Made Itself" — animation/projection work

## Why Interesting
Matreyek bridges animation + live performance + ecology in a way that's rare — her work is technically sophisticated but deeply personal. The body as shadow/silhouette inside a digital world is a genuine formal innovation in embodied performance. Climate grief as artistic subject rather than didactic message.
