# Dorothy Roberts

Legal scholar and sociologist at University of Pennsylvania. Foundational figure in race, gender, and technology critique. Author of *Killing the Black Body: Race, Reproduction, and the Meaning of Liberty* (1997) and *Torn Apart: How the Child Welfare System Destroys Black Families* (2022). Appeared with [[Ruha Benjamin]] on Haymarket's "Policing without the Police" discussion (2020). Her work on reproductive justice and surveillance connects directly to Benjamin's New Jim Code framework.
