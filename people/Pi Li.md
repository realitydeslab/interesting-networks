# Pi Li

Lead Curator, Visual Art at M+ Museum, Hong Kong. Former curator at Serpentine Gallery, London. Deep expertise in Chinese contemporary art — particularly conceptual and installation practices. One of the key curatorial figures in the global Chinese art ecosystem with both Western institutional credibility and deep China knowledge. Acquisition lead for visual art at M+, including Chinese contemporary works.
