# Pia Fricker

Professor and Vice Head of Architecture at **Aalto University**, Finland. Holds the Professorship of Computational Design in Landscape Architecture and Urbanism. Academic lead of **CCA Aalto** (part of CCA Center for Climate Adaptation network, initiated by Pratt Institute).

Research: immersive/data-driven design, mixed reality, climate adaptation, more-than-human design. PhD + MAS in Computer Aided Architectural Design from ETH Zurich.

Work exhibited at Venice Biennale, National Design Centre Singapore, Museum of Modern Art Finland, Helsinki Design Week.

## Why Interesting

Gave a lecture at [[KYOTO Design Lab]] (Oct 2025) titled **"Terraforming Interfaces: Designing With More-Than-Human Worlds"** — treats terraforming as speculative interface design where cities meet living systems. Concepts: *Temporal Scaffolds*, *Migratory Boundaries*, *Algorithmic Seedbeds*. Directly relevant to Amber's more-than-human design research.
