## Background
PhD in Design from UC San Diego (Bratton's department). Strategy Director at Antikythera. Previously co-led City Design Studio at Royal College of Art with Julian Siravo of Autonomy (future of work think tank, London). Authored winning bid for 2024 San Diego-Tijuana World Design Capital.

Writing book: *Auto: Fordian Parables of Planetary Platform Automation* — speculative history of planetary platform automation exploring Fordian logics shaping automation and automobility today.

Co-founded Elsewhere (2003), a living museum in Greensboro NC that played role in downtown revitalization. Previously co-curated Kulturpark (2011-2013), research interventions into abandoned Soviet-built amusement park in East Berlin.
