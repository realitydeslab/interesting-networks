# Arjuna Neuman

Filmmaker, artist, and thinker. Decade-long filmmaking collaboration with [[Denise Ferreira da Silva]] producing a film trilogy (and ongoing fourth film) exploring the classical four elements as philosophical and political terrain.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created. Found through Denise Ferreira da Silva deep research.

## Domains
- Experimental and essay film
- Elemental philosophy (water, earth, fire, air as ontological categories)
- Decolonial aesthetics and filmmaking
- Ecology and extraction

## Key Works
- [*Serpent Rain*](https://www.arjunaneuman.com/serpent-rain) (2016, with Denise Ferreira da Silva) — 30 min. Element: Water. Asks: "What would become of the human being if expressed through the elements?" Major exhibition film.
- [*4 Waters: Deep Implicancy*](https://www.arjunaneuman.com/4-waters) (2018/2019, with Denise Ferreira da Silva) — Element: Water (continued). Shown at Gallery TPW Toronto.
- [*Soot Breath // Corpus Infinitum*](https://www.arjunaneuman.com/soot-breath) (2020, with Denise Ferreira da Silva) — Element: Earth. Filmed in Death Valley. About texture not movement. Shown at MACBA Barcelona, Belkin Art Gallery UBC.
- *Air* (in progress, with Denise Ferreira da Silva) — Element: Air. Filmed in Atacama lithium mines, Chile. Addresses extraction and energy.

## Key Communities
- [[Denise Ferreira da Silva]] — primary long-term collaborator (10+ years)
- [[Sensing Salon]] ecosystem

## Why Interesting
The da Silva-Neuman collaboration is one of the most sustained examples of philosophical filmmaking in contemporary art. Not illustrative cinema but genuine research through film. Each film generates new philosophical positions through the process of making it. Worth deeper research for Amber — the *how* of making theory-art at this level.
