# Jon McCormack

Professor, artist, and pioneer of evolutionary and generative art systems. Works at the nexus of art, technology and society with experimental practice driven by computing, artificial life, and computational creativity. Founder and Director of SensiLab at Monash University. His work explores "electronic after natures" - alternate forms of artificial life that might replace biological nature due to human progress.

## Key Works
- **[Eden](https://jonmccormack.info/project/eden)** — Virtual ecosystem installation where AI creatures evolve behaviors through survival in 2D world. Creatures learn, adapt, make sound, hibernate seasonally. Survival strategies emerge naturally through evolution.
- **[Turbulence](https://jonmccormack.info/project/turbulence)** (1994) — Career-defining work exhibited 40+ times globally. Acquired by Australian Centre for Moving Image (2004). Conceptual foundation: "life as process, species destroying irreplaceable nature"
- **[Nodal](https://nodalmusic.com)** — Generative music software commercialized 2009, used by thousands of musicians worldwide. Visual network-based composition tool for real-time generative music
- **[Fifty Sisters](https://jonmccormack.info/project/fifty-sisters)** — Generative art series
- **[The Unknowable](https://jonmccormack.info/project/the-unknowable)** — Video installation
- **[Morphogenesis Series](https://jonmccormack.info/project/morphogenesis-series)** — Evolved virtual flora
- **[Evolutionary Form](https://jonmccormack.info/project/evolutionary-form)** — 3D printed evolved forms
- **[The World We Made](https://jonmccormack.info/project/the-world-we-made)** — AI-generated images
- **[Holon](https://jonmccormack.info/project/holon)** — Public art installation
