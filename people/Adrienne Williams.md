# Adrienne Williams

Researcher at DAIR Institute. Studies exploited data labor, Amazon driver surveillance, and "zombie trainers" — marginalized people conscripted into AI training without pay.

## Domains
- AI data labor exploitation
- Gig worker and refugee "zombie trainers"
- Amazon driver surveillance (Driven Down report)
- Race and AI

## Key Works
- Contributor to **[Driven Down](https://www.dair-institute.org/projects/driven-down/)** (2024) — Amazon surveillance/wage theft report.
- **[Data Work and its Layers of (In)visibility](https://just-tech.ssrc.org/articles/data-work-and-its-layers-of-invisibility/)** (with Milagros Miceli)
- **["What Senator Cory Booker gets wrong about Black people and AI"](https://visiblemagazine.com/what-senator-cory-booker-gets-wrong-about-black-people-and-ai/)** (Visible Magazine)
- **["Zombie Trainers and a New Era of Forced Labor"](https://www.newsweek.com/zombie-trainers-new-era-forced-labor-opinion-1896624)** (Newsweek)

## Key Communities
- **[[DAIR]]** — Core researcher

## Why Interesting
Williams coined (or popularized) the term "zombie trainers" for refugees and gig workers conscripted into AI training. Powerful concept that makes visible an otherwise invisible labor category.
