# Michael Salu

Interdisciplinary artist, author, editor, critic, and creative director. Works at the intersection of African studies, postcolonial thought, and computational society — exploring how emerging technologies intersect with culture, society, and human experience. Strong focus on non-Western cultural heritage in AI systems and virtual architectures.

## Background
- Founder of [House of Thought](https://houseofthought.io/) — creative research practice on emerging tech × culture × society
- Founder of [Vestibule](https://vestibuleagency.cargo.site/) — interdisciplinary consultancy for creative direction, strategic development, digital experiences, cultural initiatives
- Creator of [The Red Earth Project](https://theredearthproject.org/) (2019–ongoing) — artistic/interdisciplinary study on precarity of non-Western cultural heritage, knowledge systems, and practices in Western data systems, virtual architectures, and AI
- Creator of [D.A.O.C. Film](https://daocfilm.cargo.site/) — film made with Unity game engine responding to imperial archives
- Author of *Red Earth* (book)
