# Ranulph Glanville (1946–2014)

British cybernetician and designer. President of the **American Society for Cybernetics** (ASC). Extended [[Heinz von Foerster]]'s second-order cybernetics into design theory and architectural practice. Editor of *Cybernetics & Human Knowing*. Key bridge between cybernetics and design research — argued that **design is the basis of research** (not the other way around).
