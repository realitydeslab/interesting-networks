# María Puig de la Bellacasa

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Karen Barad deep research.

Feminist STS scholar and philosopher of technoscience. Professor at UCSC's History of Consciousness Department — direct institutional colleague of [[Karen Barad]] and [[Donna Haraway]]. Author of *Matters of Care: Speculative Ethics in More Than Human Worlds* (Minnesota UP, 2017). Her "matters of care" framework brings feminist ethics of care into STS and more-than-human thinking. Based in the same UCSC cluster as Barad/Haraway/Tsing — one of the most important nodes for feminist science studies.
