# Edward Shanken

Art historian, primary academic champion and archivist of [[Roy Ascott]]'s work. His comprehensive introduction to *Telematic Embrace: Visionary Theories of Art, Technology, and Consciousness* (UC Press, 2003) is the definitive secondary source on Ascott and the field of art-technology-consciousness. Based at UC Santa Cruz. Key essay: "Technology and Intuition: A Love Story? Roy Ascott's Telematic Embrace" — deep critical analysis of Ascott's utopian telematic project through Bergson, Duchamp, Fourier, Teilhard de Chardin. Also edited and contributed to broader media art history.
