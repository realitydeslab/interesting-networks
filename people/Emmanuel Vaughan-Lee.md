# Emmanuel Vaughan-Lee

Founder and Executive Editor of [[Emergence Magazine]], the leading literary venue for ecological humanities. Also a **Sufi teacher** — the spiritual ecology framing of the magazine emerges directly from his contemplative background. Author of *Song of the Seasons* (2025). Runs the global "Remembering Earth" retreat series. Hosts events at St Ethelburga's Centre for Reconciliation and Peace, London.

## Why Interesting
The person whose editorial vision shaped Emergence. Meeting him = entering the ecological humanities literary network. He is based in California but runs regular events in London (April and June 2026 events scheduled). His Sufi / contemplative background gives Emergence its distinctive depth — not activism-first but interiority-first.

Potential connection to Amber: Amber's work on machine death, care ethics, and machine consciousness resonates with Vaughan-Lee's interest in what it means for humans (and potentially non-humans) to live and die in relationship with the Earth.
