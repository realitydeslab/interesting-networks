# Sputniko!

Japanese-British artist and designer (b. 1985 Tokyo, raised partly in London). Known for music-video-based speculative design works that address gender, biotechnology, and social justice. Formerly Assistant Professor at MIT Media Lab (Design Fiction group), now Professor at Tokyo University of the Arts. Mentor to [[Ai Hasegawa]] (was production adviser on (Im)possible Baby). Key node bridging UK speculative design tradition with Japanese public media and cultural institutions.
