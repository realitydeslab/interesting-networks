# Mami Kataoka

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Stub created. Announced as Head Curator of Helsinki Biennial 2027 (June 6 – September 19, 2027). Her framing: "environment and cosmology," "transcultural dialogue." Director of Mori Art Museum, Tokyo.

Director of [Mori Art Museum](https://www.mori.art.museum/en/) in Tokyo. Major international biennial curator: Co-Artistic Director of the 9th Gwangju Biennale (2012), Artistic Director of the 21st Biennale of Sydney (2018), Artistic Director of the Aichi Triennale 2022. Her curatorial focus: "social themes, intercultural dialogue, and the role of art in exploring global structures and the **equilibrium of fundamental cosmic elements**." Previously at Hayward Gallery London (2007–2009).

## Why Interesting
