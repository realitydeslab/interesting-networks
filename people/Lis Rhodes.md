# Lis Rhodes

Artist, filmmaker, author. Foundational figure in British experimental film through the **London Film-Makers' Co-operative (LFMC)**. Founding member of **Circles**, a distribution network for women artists working in film, video, and performance (now [Cinenova](https://www.cinenova.org/)). Writing collected in ***Telling Invents Told*** (Visible Press, 2019). Works span 1971–2022 and are distributed by LUX.

## Why Interesting
Rhodes occupies a foundational position in the feminist experimental film tradition in the UK. Her 1975 work *Light Music* (optical sound film using drawn patterns) and *Light Reading* (1978) are canonical. She was at the LFMC at its most politically charged moment — the 1970s–80s intersection of feminist politics, materialist film theory, and anti-capitalist art practice. *Disquiet* (2022) shows her still active 50 years in.
