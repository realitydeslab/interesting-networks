# Kira O'Reilly

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during SymbioticA deep research.

British performance artist and bioart pioneer. Works at the intersection of body performance, wet biological materials, ecology, and more-than-human ontologies. Major figure in the UK bio-art scene. Former [[SymbioticA]] resident — her time in Perth was transformative for her practice.

**Practice:** Often works with actual biological materials (her own blood, living organisms, tissue cultures) in durational performances. Explores vulnerability, intimacy, and the boundaries between self and other species. Has worked with ants, plants, living cultures.

**2022:** Kira O'Reilly initiated the global petition that saved SymbioticA from defunding — gathering 11,000+ signatures. Called SymbioticA "the place that made me who I am."

Lectures and teaches internationally. Connected to the UK bio-art, performance art, and feminist science studies communities.

## Why Interesting
Feminist bio-art practice at its most embodied. Her work with living material in performance is distinct from TC&A's laboratory approach — more durational, intimate, body-centered. Important voice at the intersection of feminist theory and biological arts. Her advocacy in 2022 shows her centrality to the SymbioticA community.
