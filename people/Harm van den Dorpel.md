# Harm van den Dorpel

Dutch artist and programmer based in Berlin. Pioneer of generative/computational art on blockchain. Co-founded [left.gallery](https://left.gallery/) — a platform for digital art distribution. Known for the *Nested Exchange* series (self-modifying generative works on blockchain). Showed in Simon Denny's *Proof of Work* (Schinkel Pavillon, 2018).

## Why Interesting
One of the few artists working rigorously at the intersection of computation, aesthetics, and distributed systems — not just making art about blockchain but using blockchain's state machine as a creative medium. Relevant to Amber's decentralized AI interests and to the question of computational creativity as a technical practice.
