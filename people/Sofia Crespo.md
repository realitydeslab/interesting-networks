# Sofia Crespo

Artist working at the intersection of AI and natural biology. Known for using neural networks to generate imagery of marine life, organisms, and biological forms — creating hallucinatory images that blend real and imagined nature. Key collaborator with Anna Ridler (shared Digital Art Prize, Arab Bank Switzerland, 2025). Part of the European AI art scene around HEK Basel, Ars Electronica, etc.

## Why Interesting
- More-than-human approach: biology and nature as AI training material
- Neural network aesthetics applied to organic forms (antithesis of clean tech imagery)
- Co-winner of Arab Bank Switzerland Digital Art Prize with Anna Ridler (2025)
