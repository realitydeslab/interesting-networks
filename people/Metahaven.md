# Metahaven

Artist collective (Vinca Kruk & Daniel van der Velden), founded 2007. Heads the Geo—Design MA at DAE. Practice spans filmmaking, graphic design, books, and textiles. Known for design work that examines sovereignty, propaganda, tech platforms, and now: AI and poetry. Intellectually rigorous; anti-"designer as national brand."

## Background
- Emerged from Dutch Design scene (late 2000s) but deliberately positioned against nation-state branding of design ("Dutch Design")
- Practice evolved: graphic design → filmmaking → theoretical writing → AI/poetry
- **Heads of Geo—Design MA**, DAE (took over from Formafantasma, who now run Contextual Design)
- New book on **the future of the beholder's share in art** — Verso Books, Spring 2025
- Current film: ***The Feeling Sonnets (Transitional Object)*** — about poetry and AI; based on poetry collection by Eugene Ostashevsky

## Practice
- Make films, write books, graphic design work (often speculative/hypothetical commissions)
- Interest in **art-science adjacency** — where sensory experience meets physics/complexity
- Moving image as research tool — central to Geo-Design curriculum they designed
- "Multihyphenate" practice: design is always already between disciplines

## Why Interesting
They bring a rare combination: serious design theory + filmmaking craft + genuine interest in AI/poetry. *The Feeling Sonnets* (AI + poetry film) is adjacent to Amber's interest in machine creativity. Their Verso book on "the beholder's share" connects to questions about AI + aesthetics + perception. The Geo-Design programme they run is DAE's most explicitly planetary/ecological.
