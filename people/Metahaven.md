# Metahaven

Dutch design/art duo (Daniel van der Velden and Vinca Kruk). Research-based design studio working at intersection of politics, aesthetics, and technology.

## Key Works
- *Uncorporate Identity* (2010) — design and geopolitics
- *Can Jokes Bring Down Governments?* (2013) — memes and politics
- *The Information Skies* (2014) — film
- Film work: *Possessed*, *Hometown*, *Eurasia*

## Connections
- [[Antikythera]] — affiliate researchers (2023, 2024)
- [[Benjamin Bratton]] — in dialogue
- e-flux — major platform for their writing
- Serpentine Galleries — exhibited
- Venice Biennale — exhibited

## Why Interesting
Design practice that is genuinely political and speculative — not just aesthetically but structurally. Their work on internet sovereignty, WikiLeaks aesthetics, and post-internet design is unique. Bridge between graphic design, film, and political theory.
