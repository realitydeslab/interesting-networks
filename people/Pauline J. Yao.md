# Pauline J. Yao

Lead Curator, Moving Image at M+ Museum, Hong Kong. Expert in Asian and Chinese video/media/moving image art. Featured in inaugural exhibition tours discussing Gutai, Japanese post-war experimentation, and technology in art practice. Responsible for the most sophisticated institutional moving image programme in Asia. Acquisition lead for video art and digital media at M+. **The most important contact at M+ for anyone working in computational/digital media art with an Asian cultural dimension.** Active in the M+ Shanshui and moving image exhibitions. Key figure in the field.
