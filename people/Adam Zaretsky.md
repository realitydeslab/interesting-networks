# Adam Zaretsky

Bioartist and educator. Co-founder of the VivoArts School for Transgenics Aesthetics Ltd. (with SymbioticA). Works at intersection of genetic manipulation, performance art, and animal studies. Published "Animal Enrichment and The VivoArts School for Transgenics Aesthetics Ltd." in *Inflexions* No. 7 (Animating Biophilosophy, 2013). Associated with [[SymbioticA]] and the broader bioart community.
