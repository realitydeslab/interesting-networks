# Francesca Tarocco

Scholar of Buddhism, material culture, media, and technology in China. Spoke at [[AI & Culture Research Center]] (AIC), NYU Shanghai, November 6, 2025 on: *Buddhist technocultures; Buddhism and technology as intertwined cosmotechnics; new imaginations of the self, the real, and the digital world.*

The framing of "Buddhism and technology as intertwined cosmotechnics" is a direct invocation of [[Yuk Hui]]'s cosmotechnics framework applied to Buddhist contexts — the idea that Buddhist philosophical traditions carry their own implicit technology-cosmology relationship that is distinct from Western-Greek techne.
