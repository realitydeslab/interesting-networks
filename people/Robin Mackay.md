# Robin Mackay

## ⚡ Recent Updates

- **2026-03-04:** Stub created during Miguel Abreu Gallery deep research.

Director and founder of [[Urbanomic]] (Falmouth, Cornwall). Editor of *Collapse: Journal of Philosophical Research and Development* (8 volumes, 2006–2014; Vol. IX forthcoming). Co-editor of *#Accelerate: The Accelerationist Reader* (2014, with [[Armen Avanessian]]). Translator of [[Alain Badiou]], [[Quentin Meillassoux]], and others into English. Former CCRU participant (1990s, University of Warwick). Co-editor of the original ***collapse* zine (1995–1996, with Robert O'Toole).

Mackay is the key editorial gatekeeper of the speculative realism → accelerationism → xenofeminism → cosmotechnics publishing ecosystem. His taste and editorial vision have shaped what contemporary continental philosophy looks like in the Anglophone world for nearly two decades.

## Why Interesting
The person who made the Urbanomic intellectual ecosystem happen. His editorial choices over 20+ years shaped what the philosophy-art interface looks like in the Anglophone world. Collapse II *created* speculative realism as a published movement. *#Accelerate* defined accelerationism's genealogy. Publishing [[Yuk Hui]] brought cosmotechnics to the Anglophone world. *Machine Decision Is Not Final* addresses AI through the exact cross-cultural lens that Amber's research inhabits.

Understanding Mackay's taste is understanding what the speculative philosophy ecosystem values. He is the gatekeeper to the most important theory publishing operation for Amber's intellectual context.
