# Robin Mackay

Director and founder of [[Urbanomic]] (Falmouth, Cornwall). Editor of *Collapse: Journal of Philosophical Research and Development*, co-editor of *#Accelerate: The Accelerationist Reader* (2014). Translator of Alain Badiou, Quentin Meillassoux, and others into English. Key enabler of the speculative realism → accelerationism → xenofeminism → cosmotechnics publishing ecosystem.

## Domains
- Publishing / editorial strategy in philosophy × art
- Speculative realism, accelerationism, xenofeminism
- Translation (French philosophy → English)
- Arts organization, events, residencies

## Key Works
- [*#Accelerate: The Accelerationist Reader*](https://www.urbanomic.com/product/accelerate/) (ed. with Armen Avanessian, 2014) — definitive accelerationism anthology
- *Collapse* journal (8 volumes, 2006–present) — cross-disciplinary philosophy journal
- [*Chronosis*](https://www.urbanomic.com/product/chronosis/) (with Reza Negarestani & Keith Tilford) — philosophy-comics

## Why Interesting
The person who made the Urbanomic intellectual ecosystem happen. His editorial choices over 15+ years shaped what the philosophy-art interface looks like in the Anglophone world. Understanding his taste is understanding what that ecosystem values.
