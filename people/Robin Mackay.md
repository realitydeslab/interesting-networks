# Robin Mackay

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Speculative Realism deep research.

Director of [Urbanomic](https://www.urbanomic.com/), the UK-based publisher/arts organization that served as the institutional infrastructure for Speculative Realism. Editor of *Collapse: Journal of Philosophical Research and Development*. Published the early texts that made SR possible — Harman's "Vicarious Causation," Brassier's work, Negarestani's *Cyclonopedia*. Also connected to accelerationism (Nick Land, CCRU legacy) and contemporary continental philosophy-science interface. Curator as well as publisher — organizes events and workshops at the intersection of philosophy, science, and art.

## Why Interesting
The infrastructural figure behind SR — he didn't write the philosophy but created the platform. Understanding Urbanomic is understanding how SR found its audience. Urbanomic now publishes broadly: Yuk Hui (*Post-Europe*), accelerationism, philosophy of science, continental theory. Based in UK — proximity factor for Amber. The model of a small, rigorous publisher operating at the philosophy-art-science interface is directly relevant to how Amber thinks about knowledge infrastructure.
