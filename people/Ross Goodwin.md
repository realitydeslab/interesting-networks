# Ross Goodwin

Writer and AMI artist-in-residence (2016–17). Known for *[1 the Road](https://www.amazon.com/1-Road-Ross-Goodwin/dp/1912668084)* — a novel written by driving across America with an LSTM neural network mounted in a car, generating prose from GPS coordinates, time, and surroundings. Former speechwriter. Pioneer of neural narrative — using ML not to generate generic text but to create situated, location-aware writing machines.

## Why Interesting
- One of the earliest artists to treat LSTM/RNN as a *writing instrument* rather than curiosity
- *1 the Road* methodology (sensors → context → text) is genuinely inventive
- AMI residency produced foundational AI writing work

## Key Works
- *[1 the Road](https://www.amazon.com/1-Road-Ross-Goodwin/dp/1912668084)* (2019, Jean Boîte Éditions) — AI-generated novel from road trip
- LSTM poetry systems (AMI residency, 2016–17)
