# Lola Olufemi

Black feminist writer, organiser, and researcher based in the UK. Works on cultural production, abolition, and revolutionary possibility. Author of *Experiments in Imagining Otherwise* (Hajar Press) and *Feminism Interrupted: Disrupting Power* (Pluto Press). Collaborated with [[Agnes Cameron]] on *This Is A Temporal Landscape*, an archival website supporting her PhD research on revolutionary cultural production.
