# Sarah Nuttall

Professor of Literary and Cultural Studies at [WISER](https://wiser.wits.ac.za/), University of the Witwatersrand. Directed the Institute 2013–2023. Spouse and intellectual partner of [[Achille Mbembe]]. Together they co-edited *Johannesburg: The Elusive Metropolis* (Duke UP, 2008) — the major treatment of Johannesburg as a postcolonial urban site.

Has published 15 books, many collaborative. Recent work: *Your History With Me: The Films of Penny Siopis* (Duke UP), *Reading for Water: Materiality and Method* (Routledge). Working on a book on pluviality (rain worlds) in southern African literature. Since 2023, convenes major online research forum series with [[Isabel Hofmeyr]]: Heated Conversations, Breathing In: Air and Atmospheres, Plant Lives.
