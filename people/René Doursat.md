# René Doursat

Founder of **Morphogenetic Engineering** as an explicit research program. Edited the founding Springer volume (2012) with Hiroki Sayama and Olivier Michel. Research on "embryomorphic engineering," "self-architecturing systems," and programmable morphogenesis. Previously at Complex Systems Institute Paris (ISC-PIF) / Ecole Polytechnique; later moved to Stevens Institute of Technology.

## Domains
- Morphogenetic Engineering (field founder)
- Self-organized complex systems
- Computational development / embryogenesis models
- Artificial life
- Neural field theory

## Key Works
- [*Morphogenetic Engineering: Toward Programmable Complex Systems*](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-642-33902-8) (ed. w/ Sayama & Michel, Springer 2012) — founding text of ME
- "Organically grown architectures" (Doursat, 2008, *Organic Computing*, Springer) — coined "embryomorphic engineering"
- "The evo-devo of artificial bodies and minds" — ME framework paper
