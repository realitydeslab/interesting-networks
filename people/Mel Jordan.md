# Mel Jordan

Artist, writer, and activist based in the UK. Member of **Partisan Social Club** (with Andrew Hewitt), a collaborative art-activism practice focused on class, commons, and cultural politics. Close collaborator of [[Gary Hall]] through the **Centre for Postdigital Cultures** at Coventry University.

## Why Interesting
Partisan Social Club's "culture-led recommoning" practice is a specific methodology: using cultural events and artifacts to reclaim and build commons in working-class and post-industrial communities. This is a rare practice-based approach to commoning that operates through aesthetics rather than policy. Relevant to Amber if interested in participatory/democratic design and the politics of culture.
