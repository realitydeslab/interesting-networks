# Nicolas Nova

Design researcher, co-founder of Near Future Laboratory, author. Works at the intersection of design anthropology, speculative design, and everyday technology culture.

## Domains
- Design anthropology (studying how people use technology)
- Design fiction and speculative futures
- Near-future artifacts and mundane technology
- More-than-human perspectives

## Background
Co-founded Near Future Laboratory with Julian Bleecker. Professor at HEAD (Haute École d'Art et de Design) in Geneva. Author of *A Bestiary of the Anthropocene* (2021) — a speculative taxonomy of hybrid entities in the Anthropocene. Collaborator on early design fiction workshops and artifacts.

## Key Works
- [*A Bestiary of the Anthropocene*](https://www.headpublishing.ch/Bestiaire/) (2021) — hybrid entities, speculative natural history. Exactly in Amber's more-than-human zone.
- Near Future Laboratory collaborations with Bleecker (2006–)
