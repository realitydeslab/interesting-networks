# Anne Alombert

French philosopher of technology and digital culture. Lecturer at [Université Paris 8](https://www.univ-paris8.fr/), member of the Laboratoire d'Études et de Recherches sur les Logiques Contemporaines de la Philosophie (LLCP). Key figure in extending [[Bernard Stiegler]]'s pharmacological theory of technology into contemporary digital media, computation, and ecological thought. Active collaborator with the [[Research Network for Philosophy and Technology]].

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created. Identified as upcoming Dialogues speaker (Dec 8, 2025) and key Stiegler scholar.

## Background
Student and close collaborator of Bernard Stiegler at the Institut de Recherche et d'Innovation (IRI), Centre Pompidou. After Stiegler's death (2020), has continued and extended his work on psychotechnics, libidinal economy, and digital platforms. Co-organizer of the *Reconsidering the Biosphere and the Technosphere in the Entropocene* seminar series (2022–23) with Chiara Giaccardi.
