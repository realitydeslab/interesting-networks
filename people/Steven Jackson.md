# Steven J. Jackson

Professor of Information Science and STS at Cornell. Key contemporary figure in [[Infrastructure Studies]]. Famous for "Rethinking Repair" (2014) — reframes infrastructure from maintenance-first rather than innovation-first. Runs Computing On Earth Lab: experimental collaboration bringing together social scientists, humanists, artists, and engineers to rethink material and planetary foundations of computing. Active at CSCW, CHI, 4S.
