# Joanna Zylinska

Artist, writer, curator, and Professor of Media Philosophy + Critical Digital Practice at **King's College London**. Member of **Creative AI Lab** (King's + Serpentine Galleries). Advocate of "radical open-access." One of the most rigorous thinkers at the intersection of AI, photography, creativity, and posthumanism.

## Key Works / Books
- *The Perception Machine: Our Photographic Future Between the Eye and AI* (MIT Press, 2023)
- *AI Art: Machine Visions and Warped Dreams* (Open Humanities Press, 2020, open access)
- *Nonhuman Photography* (MIT Press, 2017)
- Editor, MEDIA:ART:WRITE:NOW book series (Open Humanities Press)
