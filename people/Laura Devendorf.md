# Laura Devendorf

Associate Professor at University of Colorado Boulder's ATLAS Institute. Works on computational textiles, e-textiles, weaving, and living/regenerative materials in HCI. Strong feminist/more-than-human design perspective. Collaborates with [[TU Delft Connected Everyday Lab]] researchers (co-author on Regenerative Material Ecologies in HCI, 2025, with [[Iohanna Nicenboim]] and Lining Yao).

## Domains
- Computational and electronic textiles
- Weaving as design research
- Living/regenerative materials in HCI
- Feminist HCI, speculative design
- Craft and making as knowledge

## Key Works
- [Regenerative Material Ecologies in HCI](https://research.tudelft.nl/en/publications/c14d47d3-fc65-4cfe-b72c-dca176b8ba2c) (2025) — large coalition paper with Nicenboim, Yao, Karana and others
- Research on weaving and generative textiles at ATLAS Institute

## Key Communities
- [[TU Delft Connected Everyday Lab]] — regenerative materials collaboration
- TEI / CHI / DIS community
- ATLAS Institute at CU Boulder

## Why Interesting
Her work on computational/living textiles bridges craft, materials, and HCI in a feminist more-than-human direction. Part of the growing "regenerative materials" coalition that the TU Delft CEL is building.
