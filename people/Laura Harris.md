# Laura Harris

Poet, scholar, and professor of English at **Pomona College**. Partner of [[Fred Moten]]. Co-founder with Moten of **Three Count Pour**, a small poetry press. Author of *[Experiments in Exile: C.L.R. James, Hélio Oiticica, and the Aesthetic Sociality of Blackness](https://fordhampress.com/9780823274505/experiments-in-exile/)* (Fordham University Press, 2016).

## Key Work
- *Experiments in Exile: C.L.R. James, Hélio Oiticica, and the Aesthetic Sociality of Blackness* (Fordham UP, 2016) — on the aesthetics of exile, blackness, and radical social life in C.L.R. James (Trinidad/UK/US cricket theorist and Marxist) and Hélio Oiticica (Brazilian neo-concretist artist). Combines Black Atlantic theory with performance theory and aesthetic philosophy.

## Three Count Pour
Co-founded with Moten as a small poetry press — part of the intimate infrastructure of their shared intellectual life.

## Why Interesting (for Amber)
Her framing of C.L.R. James and Oiticica together — cricket + communism + neo-concretism + exile — is a model for building unexpected theoretical frameworks across geographic and disciplinary boundaries. The *Experiments in Exile* title also resonates with Amber's interest in how knowledge gets made in the margins of institutions.
