# Sadie Plant

British philosopher, cultural theorist. Co-founded the [[CCRU]] with [[Nick Land]] at University of Warwick.

## Key Works
- *The Most Radical Gesture* (1992) — on Situationist International
- *Zeros + Ones* (1997) — cyberfeminism, Ada Lovelace, women and technology
- *Writing on Drugs* (1999)

## Domains
- Cyberfeminism, technology and gender, Situationist International
- Pioneer of cyberfeminist thought in the 1990s

## Connections
- [[Nick Land]] — co-founded CCRU
- [[CCRU]] — founding member
- [[Mark Fisher]] — CCRU colleague
- University of Birmingham Centre for Contemporary Cultural Studies → University of Warwick

## Why Interesting
Origin point of CCRU. Zeros + Ones remains a foundational cyberfeminist text. Left academia in 1997 — rare trajectory.
