# Semiconductor

Artist duo **Ruth Jarman** and **Joe Gerhardt**, Brighton UK. 25+ years exploring human experience at the intersection of science and technology. Their works confront the ephemerality of the natural world by contextualising scientific data — from particle physics to geophysics — into sculpture, moving image, and drawings.

**2015 Collide Award** (Arts at CERN × FACT Liverpool). Spent extended time at CERN understanding particle detectors, data streams, and fundamental physics. Returned to CERN multiple times for commissions following the residency.

Known for meticulous research — they spend months in science labs worldwide. Each work uses a different technological approach. Work shown internationally, including Tate, FACT Liverpool, Serpentine.
