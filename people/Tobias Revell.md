# Tobias Revell

British designer, educator, and writer. [[Design Interactions (RCA)]] alumnus. Teaches at **London College of Communication (LCC)**, UAL. Runs **Strange Telemetry** — his independent speculative design and research practice focusing on **network infrastructure, borders, surveillance, and the politics of technology**. A bridge between DI's speculative design heritage and critical internet culture.

## Why Interesting
London-based, accessible DI alumnus who actively engages with design-politics discourse. He writes frequently — less "object maker," more "design theorist and critic." His work on network infrastructure and border technologies is relevant to Amber's interests in critical computing and political dimensions of technology. He's the most intellectually accessible DI alumnus in London — makes a good point of entry to the wider DI community.

## Key Works / Projects
- Various speculative design projects on network borders, surveillance, and infrastructure (through Strange Telemetry)
- Regular writing and criticism in design press
