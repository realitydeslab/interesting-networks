# Kelly Li

Assistant Curator, Moving Image at M+ Museum, Hong Kong. Curated *Manifesto of Spring* — the co-production between M+, ZKM Karlsruhe, and National Asian Culture Center (ACC) Gwangju — an exhibition on nature/technology/Capitalocene themes. Emerging voice in M+'s media/tech/ecological art programming. The direct circuit link between M+ and ZKM. Active writer for M+ Magazine on contemporary digital/ecological art practice.
