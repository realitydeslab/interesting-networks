# Cade Diehm

Co-founder of [New Design Congress](https://newdesigncongress.org/) — a research organization investigating the political dimensions of design, technology security, and infrastructure. Focuses on "weaponized design" — how designed systems are used as tools of oppression, surveillance, and systemic harm. Guest on [[Interdependence Podcast]] (2021) — episode titled "Weaponized Design, Security Vulnerability and Caution Over the Decentralized Web."

## Domains
- Weaponized design
- Technology security as design problem
- Infrastructure critique
- Digital rights and platform harms

## Why Interesting
Design as a site of political struggle — the design of protocols, interfaces, and systems as ethical/political acts. Direct overlap with Amber's critical computing and more-than-human interests. New Design Congress produces rigorous research at this intersection that's hard to find elsewhere.
