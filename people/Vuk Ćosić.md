# Vuk Ćosić

Pioneer of net.art who accidentally coined the term from a garbled ASCII email (1995); known for ASCII art translations of film history; co-founder of nettime, Syndicate, and Ljubljana Digital Media Lab.

## Key Works
- *ASCII History of Moving Images* (1999) — cinema translated into ASCII characters
- *Deep ASCII* — Deep Throat in ASCII
- *History of Art for the Blind* — art history in ASCII pictograms
- *File Extinguisher* — web service that deletes files (critique of internet permanence)
