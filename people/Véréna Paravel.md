# Véréna Paravel

Filmmaker and anthropologist. Half of the [Sensory Ethnography Lab (SEL)](https://sel.fas.harvard.edu/) duo at Harvard (with Lucien Castaing-Taylor). Co-director of *Leviathan* (2012) — immersive documentary on industrial fishing from a non-human perspective, using GoPros attached to fish, nets, ship hull. *De Humani Corporis Fabrica* (2022) — filmed inside human bodies during surgery, premiered Cannes 2022. Exhibited at Le Fresnoy Panorama 26 (2024).

## Why Interesting
SEL's methodology — using cameras and sensory technologies to produce radically non-anthropocentric perspectives — is directly relevant to Amber's more-than-human and agent ethology interests. *Leviathan* is a landmark work for anyone thinking about non-human agency and machine vision. Harvard SEL = key community node.
