# Jenna Sutela

Finnish artist working with biological and computational systems — slime molds, bacteria, neural networks, speech synthesis. Her practice explores non-human intelligence and communication through performance, video, and biotechnological experiments. Exhibited widely (Tate Modern, Guggenheim Bilbao, Serpentine, Haus der Kulturen der Welt, Onassis Stegi). Presented at the PLANTSEX event at [[Serpentine Galleries]] (April 2019, curated by [[Lucia Pietroiusti]]).
