# Niloufar Salehi

Assistant Professor at UC Berkeley School of Information. Worker-centered HCI researcher. Co-designed "We Are Dynamo" — a platform for collective action among Amazon Mechanical Turk workers — with [[Lilly Irani]], M. Six Silberman, and [[Michael Bernstein]]. Published "Responsible Research with Crowds" in Communications of the ACM calling for minimum wage for crowdworkers.
