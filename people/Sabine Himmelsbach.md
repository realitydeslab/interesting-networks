## Career
- Studied art history, Munich
- Galleries in Munich and Vienna (1993–1996)
- Project manager, Steirischer Herbst Festival, Graz (1996–1999)
- Exhibition Director, [[ZKM Center for Art and Media]], Karlsruhe (1999–2005)
- Artistic Director, Edith-Russ-House for Media Art, Oldenburg (2005–2011)
- Director, [[HeK (House of Electronic Arts Basel)]] (2012–present)
