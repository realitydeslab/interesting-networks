# Becky Chambers

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Solarpunk deep research.

SF author known for her "Monk & Robot" solarpunk novellas — *A Psalm for the Wild-Built* (2021) and *A Prayer for the Crown-Shy* (2022), published by Tor Books. Also known for the Wayfarers series (diverse species, queer futures, care ethics). Her work exemplifies cozy/hopeful SF — futures of abundance and nonhuman relations rather than conflict. Hugo Award nominee.

## Why Interesting
Her Monk & Robot series directly explores what happens when robots voluntarily leave human society, gain consciousness, and return — themes directly relevant to machine behavior, nonhuman agency, and what a genuinely more-than-human future looks like. The robots in her fiction develop their own ethology. High relevance to Amber's machine behavior and agent ethology work.
