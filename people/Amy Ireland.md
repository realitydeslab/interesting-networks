# Amy Ireland

Artist, theorist, poet. One half of the Amy Ireland & Maya B. Kronic collaborative duo. Core figure in the xenofeminist / accelerationist theory sphere. Connected to [[Laboria Cuboniks]] / XF manifesto network. Published by [[Urbanomic]] (Falmouth) and participates in events at [[Miguel Abreu Gallery]]. Participated in *TransLatin Diagonals* event (Casa São Roque, Porto, June 2023) alongside Gabriel Catren, Maya B. Kronic, Thomas Murphy.

## Key Works
- [*Cute Accelerationism*](https://www.urbanomic.com/product/cute-accelerationism/) (with Maya B. Kronic, Urbanomic, 2022) — xenofeminist aesthetics and accelerationism

## Why Interesting for Amber
- Xenofeminism's account of technology as potentially emancipatory (not just oppressive) — rigorous theoretical counterpoint to tech-critical approaches
- Accelerationism × feminist theory: relevant to Amber's posthumanism and more-than-human work
- Connected to the speculative philosophy network Amber should know
