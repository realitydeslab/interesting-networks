# Toshio Iwai 岩井俊雄

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research. (Artist talk with Daito Manabe at NTT ICC, 2024.)

Pioneering Japanese media artist and interactive artist. Best known for *Electroplankton* (Nintendo DS, 2005) — one of the most recognized intersections of art and commercial gaming — and the *Tenori-on* (electronic musical instrument, developed with Yamaha, 2007). A foundational figure in Japanese media art, preceding [[Daito Manabe]] by a generation. Did an artist talk together with Manabe at [[NTT ICC]] (2024).

## Why Interesting
Toshio Iwai is a predecessor figure whose work (interactive visuals, generative music, the music-and-image instrument) anticipated much of what Rhizomatiks later developed. Understanding his work illuminates the Japanese media art lineage that Manabe's practice builds on. His *Tenori-on* (commercial instrument as art object) parallels Manabe's sonicPlanet project.
