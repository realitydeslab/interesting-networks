# Alistair Hudson

Scientific-Artistic Chairman of ZKM | Karlsruhe since April 2023. Curator and museum director. Pioneer of the "useful museum" concept — art institutions as centres of social transformation. UK background (Manchester Art Gallery, The Whitworth, Grizedale Arts, MIMA). Co-heads [Asociación de Arte Útil](https://www.arte-util.org/) with Tania Bruguera. His flagship at ZKM: *Fellow Travellers* (2024–2026) — art as tool for social change.

## Why Interesting
Bridges participatory art, social transformation, and media art institutionally. Came from UK scene (Turner Prize jury, Venice Biennale selection committee) — useful UK-Germany bridge. His "useful museum" concept is adjacent to Amber's participatory/critical-computing interests. Key person at ZKM for exhibition proposals and residency discussions.
