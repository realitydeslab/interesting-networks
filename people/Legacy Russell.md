# Legacy Russell

Writer, curator, artist. Author of *Glitch Feminism: A Manifesto* (2020, Verso Books). Executive Director and Chief Curator of The Kitchen (NYC). Previously Associate Curator of Exhibitions at The Studio Museum in Harlem. Her work explores gender, performance, and digital identity through the lens of glitch — error as liberation.
