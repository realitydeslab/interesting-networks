# Suzanne Treister

British artist; pioneer in digital/net art from the early 1990s. Initially recognised as a painter. Work engages with emerging technologies, eccentric narratives, covert power structures, alternative belief systems, and the futures of humanity. Media span: video, internet, interactive tech, photography, drawing, watercolour.

**2018 Collide Award** (Arts at CERN × FACT Liverpool). Continued CERN engagement in 2022. Also received Digital Commission from Serpentine Galleries (2019). Featured in *In the Spaces Between* (2025, dpr-barcelona).

Projects span several years; characteristic reinterpretations of taxonomies and histories examining covert forces. An ongoing focus: the relationship between technology, society, and occult/alternative belief systems.
