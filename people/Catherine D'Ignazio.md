# Catherine D'Ignazio

Associate Professor of Urban Science and Planning at MIT; Director of the [Data + Feminism Lab](https://dataplusfeminism.mit.edu/). Co-author with Lauren Klein of *Data Feminism* (MIT Press, 2020) — the canonical text for feminist approaches to data science. Her work spans data literacy, civic technology, feminist hackathons, and participatory data activism.
