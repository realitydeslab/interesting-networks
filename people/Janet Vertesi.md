# Janet Vertesi

Associate Professor of Sociology at Princeton University. STS scholar studying how organizational structures and team practices shape technology use, particularly at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory. Co-authored "Social Justice and Design: Power and Oppression in Collaborative Systems" with [[Lilly Irani]] (CSCW 2017, 86 citations). Also co-editor of *Your Computer is on Fire* (MIT Press) which includes Irani's "Justice for Data Janitors."
