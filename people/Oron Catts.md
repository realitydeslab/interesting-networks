# Oron Catts

## ⚡ Recent Updates

- **2026-03-02:** Stub created during SymbioticA deep research. Key finding: Catts is **Professor at Large in Contestable Design at RCA London** — physically accessible to Amber. Pioneer of bioart / semi-living art. Needs full deep research.

---

Artist, researcher, curator. Co-founder (2000) of [SymbioticA](https://www.symbiotica.uwa.edu.au/) at UWA — the world's first bioart research lab — and co-founder (1996) of the [Tissue Culture & Art Project](https://tcaproject.net/) with [[Ionat Zurr]].

## Domains

- Bioart / biological arts
- Tissue culture as artistic medium ("semi-living" organisms)
- Ethics of life manipulation and engineered life
- Contestable design — speculative, critical design practice
- More-than-human relations; posthuman ethics
- Lab-grown food, textiles, organisms

## Key Works

See [tcaproject.net](https://tcaproject.net/) for full list:
- [*Victimless Leather*](https://tcaproject.net/portfolio/victimless-leather/) (2004) — stitch-less jacket grown from cell cultures
- [*Pig Wings*](https://tcaproject.net/portfolio/pigs-wings/) (2000–01) — pig bone grown in wing shapes; with Ionat Zurr and Guy Ben-Ary
- [*Disembodied Cuisine*](https://tcaproject.net/portfolio/disembodied-cuisine/) (2003) — lab-grown frog muscle food, eaten by guests
- [*Sunlight, Soil & Shit (De)Cycle*](https://sunlightsoilshit.systems/) (2022) — sustainable food / AgTech critique
