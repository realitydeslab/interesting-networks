# Lundahl & Seitl

Swedish artist duo (Martina Lundahl + Christer Seitl). Known for immersive, guided durational performances that combine choreography, sound, blindfolds, and mixed reality. Work operates at the intersection of proprioception, perception science, and live performance.

## Key Works
- *River Biographies* (2023, [[Chronus Art Center]] + Huangpu River, Shanghai) — immersive piece; also staged as a site-specific performance on the Huangpu River via CAC × Wuzhen Blue Moon collaboration
- *Symphony of a Missing Room* — their best-known touring work; blindfolded guided experience in museum collections
- *Rotating in a Room of Images* — durational immersive

## Why Note-Worthy
Their work engages embodied interaction and proprioception in ways that are genuinely artistically rigorous, not just "immersive experience." The Huangpu River staging shows they adapt their practice to place.
