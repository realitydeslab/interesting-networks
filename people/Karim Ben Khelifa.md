# Karim Ben Khelifa

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-01:** Stub created during deep research of [[IDFA DocLab]].

War photographer and VR artist. Creator of [*The Enemy*](https://www.theenemyishere.org/) — a pioneering VR work where viewers stand face-to-face with soldiers on opposing sides of conflicts (Israel/Palestine, El Salvador, DRC). One of the defining works of VR journalism and conflict documentary. Featured at [[IDFA DocLab]], Sundance New Frontier, and widely. Raises questions about empathy, representation, and the ethics of immersive conflict media.

## Why Interesting
Strong ethical inquiry + advanced medium. *The Enemy* is a landmark in how VR can create genuine phenomenological encounters with otherness. Connects to Amber's interests in more-than-human design and machine-mediated encounter.
