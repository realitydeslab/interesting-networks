# Lauren Lee McCarthy

Performance artist and programmer. Creator of [p5.js](https://p5js.org/) — the JavaScript library that has democratized creative coding for millions. Artist practice explores surveillance, intimacy, and automation. Known for *LAUREN* (2017–ongoing) — a performance where she acts as a human version of Amazon Alexa, living in strangers' homes and responding to their commands. 2021 United States Artist Fellow.

## Why Interesting
p5.js is infrastructure — it's how a generation of artists (and Amber's students) learned to code creatively. The *LAUREN* piece is one of the most incisive AI critiques in art: by performing the AI's role herself, she makes visible what automation conceals. Peer of [[Stephanie Dinkins]] in the Knight/USA Fellows community.
