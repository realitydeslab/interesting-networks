# Boris Groys

Russian-German philosopher and art theorist. One of the most prolific and influential contributors to [[e-flux]] journal. Major thinker on Soviet avant-garde, Russian Cosmism, art and politics, the museum as institution, and the philosophical status of the new. NYU Global Distinguished Professor.

## Background
Born 1947 in East Berlin, raised in Leningrad (St. Petersburg). Studied philosophy and semiotics at Leningrad State University. Left USSR in 1981, went to Germany. Professor at Karlsruhe, Cologne. Now at NYU. Has written extensively on the Russian avant-garde, Stalinism and art, the biopolitics of contemporary art, and Russian Cosmism.
