# N. Katherine Hayles

Literary critic and scholar. Pioneer of posthumanism and digital humanities.

## Key Works
- *How We Became Posthuman* (1999) — foundational posthumanism text
- *Writing Machines* (2002)
- *Unthought: The Power of the Cognitive Nonconscious* (2017)
- *Postprint: Books and Becoming Computational* (2021)

## Why Interesting
How We Became Posthuman is foundational for understanding the relationship between humans, information, and machines. Her work on cognitive nonconscious connects directly to machine behavior. Essential genealogy for Amber's interests.
