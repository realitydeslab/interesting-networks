# Hicham Berrada

Moroccan-French artist born 1986 in Casablanca. Works in the north of France. Makes work where science and poetry meet: activates natural processes (chemistry, magnetism, temperature, light) the way a painter uses pigments. "My brushes and pigments would be parameters such as temperature, magnetism or light."

## Why Interesting

Science-as-poetics practice — directly relevant to Amber's more-than-human and art-science interests. Uses experimental method aesthetically rather than instrumentally. Strong institutional circulation: Centre Pompidou, Palais de Tokyo, Punta della Dogana (Pinault), ZKM, Moderna Museet. Visiting artist-professor at Le Fresnoy 2024-25.
