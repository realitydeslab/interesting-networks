# Shu Lea Cheang (鄭淑麗)

Taiwanese-American artist and filmmaker. Pioneer of internet-based art and new media. Works on race, gender, technology, and institutional power.

## Key Works
- *Brandon* (1998–1999) — Guggenheim web art commission on transgender identity
- *I.K.U.* (2000) — cyberpunk porn film
- *Fluidø* (2017)
- *3x3x6* (2019) — Taiwan Pavilion at Venice Biennale

## Connections
- Venice Biennale — represented Taiwan (2019)
- [[Ars Electronica]] — connected
- [[Eyebeam]] — NYC art-tech scene
- Pioneer of net.art — historical importance

## Why Interesting
One of the earliest artists to work seriously with the internet as medium (1990s). Brandon is a landmark. Her work bridges Asian identity, queer politics, and technology in radical ways. Represented Taiwan at Venice — institutional recognition. Historical bridge from net.art to current art-tech scene.
