## Research Focus (Synthetic Society Lab's three pillars)
1. **Privacy-enhancing technologies** — facilitating data access while protecting sensitive data; critique of synthetic data that distorts rare communities
2. **Algorithmic auditing** — investigating algorithms in online platforms (pricing, dating apps, social media feeds)
3. **Human-machine interaction evaluation** — how technology impacts society through lens of human-algorithm interaction
