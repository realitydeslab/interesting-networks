# Christo Buschek

Programmer and data journalist. Pulitzer Prize winner (part of the New York Times team that won the **Pulitzer Prize for Public Service** for documenting Uyghur detention in Xinjiang). Also won **Best Data Journalism** award from the Society of Publishers in Asia. Member of the [[Knowing Machines Project]] at USC Annenberg / NYU Engelberg Center.
