# Philipp Koralus

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-12:** Created stub. Key connection — HAI Lab Director, Joel Lehman's PI at Oxford.

McCord Professor of Philosophy and AI at the University of Oxford. Founding Director of the **HAI Lab** (Laboratory for Human-Centered AI) within the Institute for Ethics in AI, based at the Schwarzman Centre for the Humanities. Fellow by Special Election at St. Catherine's College.

- **Book:** *Reason and Inquiry* (Oxford University Press, 2023)
