# Sam Kriegman

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created. Discovered via ISAL deep research.
- **2023:** ISAL Distinguished Early-Career Investigator Award.

Co-creator of Xenobots (2020) — the first "living robots" made from reprogrammed frog cells. Work sits at the boundary of soft robotics, synthetic biology, and artificial life. Collaborator with [[Josh Bongard]] and Michael Levin.

## Why Interesting
Xenobots represent a genuine breakthrough: biological robots that are neither traditional machines nor traditional organisms. They can self-replicate. Kriegman's work is the bleeding edge of "wet" ALife and raises profound questions about what machines, life, and design mean. Highly relevant to Amber's more-than-human interests.
