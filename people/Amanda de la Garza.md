# Amanda de la Garza

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during MUAC deep research.
- **2024:** Moved from Director of MUAC to Director of the Dirección General de Artes Visuales (DGAV) — the parent organization overseeing all UNAM visual arts institutions.
- **2024:** Presented at "Museo digital. Inteligencias y artificios" forum at MUAC (organized with Fundación Telefónica).
- **2025–26:** Co-conceptualized *Marta Palau: Mis caminos son terrestres* exhibition with Museu Tàpies (Barcelona) alongside curator Imma Prieto.

Second director of **[[MUAC]]** (after founding director Graciela de la Torre). Transitioned to Director of **DGAV (Dirección General de Artes Visuales)** at UNAM in 2024, the organizational body that oversees MUAC and other UNAM visual arts institutions. This makes her the highest-ranking person in UNAM's visual arts hierarchy and still deeply connected to MUAC.

Was part of the "Museo digital" 2024 forum, connecting MUAC to conversations about digital museums, AI, and inclusive knowledge. Has continued international co-production work (Museu Tàpies, Barcelona).
