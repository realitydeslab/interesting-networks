# Mark Fisher

British writer, cultural theorist, music critic. Known as k-punk (his blog). One of the most influential cultural theorists of the 2000s-2010s. CCRU member.

## Key Works
- *Capitalist Realism: Is There No Alternative?* (2009) — published by Zero Books
- k-punk blog (2003–2015) — enormously influential
- *Ghosts of My Life* (2014) — hauntology
- *The Weird and the Eerie* (2016)

## Key Concepts
- **Capitalist realism** — impossibility of imagining alternatives to capitalism
- **Hauntology** — cultural fixation on lost futures
- **Acid communism** — unfinished project combining psychedelia and leftist politics

## Why Interesting
Central node in the CCRU → accelerationism → cultural theory genealogy. k-punk blog was a prototype for theory-blogging. Capitalist realism became a generation-defining concept. His concept of hauntology connects to speculative futures work.
