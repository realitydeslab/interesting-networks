# Kenric Allado-McDowell

Co-founder of Google's Artists + Machine Intelligence (AMI) program. Writer and musician best known as co-author of *[Pharmako-AI](https://ignota.org/products/pharmako-ai)* (2021, Ignota Books) — a book written in dialogue with GPT-3, one of the first serious literary experiments with AI as co-author. Also co-edited *Atlas of Anomalous AI* (2021) and co-founded the @artwithMI Twitter initiative. Writes about AI, language, consciousness, and speculative thought.

## Why Interesting
- *Pharmako-AI* is the closest existing thing to Amber's vision of AI as genuine intellectual collaborator rather than tool
- AMI program co-founder — central node in AI art history
- The speculative/literary wing of AI art, as opposed to the purely visual/generative
