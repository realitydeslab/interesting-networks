# Alan Warburton

British artist and filmmaker whose video essays interrogate the nature of the computational image. His *RGB FAQ* (2020) asks "What is the computational image?" — examining how CGI, VFX pipelines, and synthetic imaging work, and what they say about vision, reality, and representation.

## Why Interesting
His practice runs directly parallel to Rosa Menkman's: both use the analysis of image infrastructure as their primary artistic and theoretical material. Where Menkman focuses on compression and resolution standards, Warburton focuses on CGI pipelines and the aesthetics of the synthetic image. His *RGB FAQ* was featured in Menkman's *Im/Possible Images Reader* and exhibition at Lothringer 13 Halle.

Connects to Amber's interest in machine-readable images, AI aesthetics, and computational representation.
