# Mercedes Bunz

Reader in Digital Humanities at King's College London. Co-lead (with Joanna Zylinska) of the **[[Creative AI Lab]]** — a research partnership between KCL's Department of Digital Humanities and Serpentine Galleries. Works on algorithmic culture, knowledge machines, and the political dimensions of AI and digital media.

Brings a continental European media theory background (influenced by German Medientheorie) to questions of how computational systems produce knowledge and transform creative practice.

## Key Works
- *The Silent Revolution: How Digitalization Transforms Knowledge, Work, Journalism and Politics Without Making Too Much Noise* (Palgrave Macmillan, 2014)
- Numerous articles on AI, knowledge machines, digital culture
