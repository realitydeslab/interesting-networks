# Lucien Castaing-Taylor

Filmmaker, anthropologist, founder of [Harvard's Sensory Ethnography Lab (SEL)](https://sel.fas.harvard.edu/). Bridges art, anthropology, and aesthetics through immersive cinema that challenges documentary convention. Major works: *Sweetgrass* (2009), *Leviathan* (2012, with Véréna Paravel), *Caniba* (2017), *De Humani Corporis Fabrica* (2022).

## Why Interesting
SEL's approach — embodied, multisensory, ethically attentive filmmaking — intersects with more-than-human design, somaesthetic research, and machine behavior. SEL films challenge the separation of human/animal/machine perception. Directly relevant to Amber's interests in embodied aesthetics.

Teaches at [[European Graduate School (EGS)]] (2026: "Sensory Ethnography Today and Tomorrow: Art, Anthropology, & Aesthetics").
