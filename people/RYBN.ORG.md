# RYBN.ORG

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Discovered via [[DATAS Residency]] (2025 expert mentor). Created stub.

Paris/Marseille-based artist collective (founded 1999). Leads "extra-disciplinary" investigations into algorithmic capitalism: high-frequency trading, financial algorithms, flash crashes, offshore finance, digital labour, AI. One of the most rigorous art collectives working on algorithmic governmentality.

## Key Works
- *Antidatamining* (2005–2015) — experimental algorithmic trading platform exploiting "artist legendary vision" for counter-intuitive investment strategies
- *Flash Crash* (2011) — artwork/investigation into the 2010 stock market flash crash
- *Data Ghosts* (2014) — cybernetic eschatology
- *The Algorithmic Trading Freak Show* — cabinet of algorithmic curiosities

## Why Interesting
Critical art meets financial systems analysis. RYBN brings rigour and wit to the same algorithmic-governance questions Amber's work touches. The long-running (25+ year) commitment to a single problematic (capital + algorithm) is exemplary.
