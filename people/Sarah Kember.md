# Sarah Kember

Professor of New Technologies of Communication at Goldsmiths, University of London. Media theorist and feminist scholar working at the intersection of life sciences, technology, and culture. Co-author with Joanna Zylinska of ***Life after New Media: Mediation as a Vital Process*** (MIT Press, 2012) — a key text arguing that mediation is not a process that happens *to* life but is constitutive of life itself.

Also director of **Goldsmiths Press** and an advocate of open-access and experimental scholarly publishing.

## Key Works
- *Life after New Media: Mediation as a Vital Process* (MIT Press, 2012) — co-authored with [[Joanna Zylinska]]
- *iMedia: The Gendering of Objects, Environments and Smart Materials* (Palgrave, 2016)
- *Virtual Anxiety: Photography, New Technologies and Subjectivity* (Manchester UP, 1998)
