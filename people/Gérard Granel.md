# Gérard Granel

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during [[Bernard Stiegler]] research. Stiegler's correspondence teacher during prison years (1978-1983).

French philosopher (1930-2000) who introduced [[Bernard Stiegler]] to philosophy through correspondence courses during Stiegler's incarceration (1978-1983). Professor at Université de Toulouse-Le Mirail, specialist in Heidegger and phenomenology.

## Background
Philosophy professor at Université de Toulouse-Le Mirail. Taught Stiegler through correspondence while Stiegler was imprisoned for armed robbery. This correspondence education was crucial to Stiegler's philosophical formation and transformation.
