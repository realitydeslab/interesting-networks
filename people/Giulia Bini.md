# Giulia Bini

Current **Head of Arts at CERN** (Curator and Head). Took over from Mónica Bello as programme director. Based at CERN's campus in Meyrin, Geneva. Oversees all residency programmes (Collide, Connect, Resonance, Guest Artist). Announced 2nd Resonance edition winner in February 2026. Served on jury for Resonance alongside Geneva cultural advisors and CERN scientists.

**Key gateway:** as Head of Arts at CERN, she is the primary point of contact for any artist seeking engagement with the programme. Email: info.arts@cern.ch
