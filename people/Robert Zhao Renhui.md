# Robert Zhao Renhui

Singapore artist who runs the [Institute of Critical Zoologists](http://www.criticalzoologists.org) — an artistic project that takes the form of a research institution studying human relationships with animals. Uses documentary aesthetics and institutional critique to probe how humans classify, narrate, and relate to non-human life.

## Why Interesting
Directly relevant to Amber's more-than-human and posthuman research threads. The Institute of Critical Zoologists is a perfect example of "weird + rigorous" — it uses the form of institutional science to ask philosophical questions about species boundaries. The name "critical zoologists" is itself a provocation: what does it mean to be critical about how we study animals? This is exactly the kind of practice-based critical computing / critical science Amber is drawn to.
