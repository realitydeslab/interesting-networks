# José Gordon

Mexican novelist, essayist, cultural journalist, and television producer. **Artistic curator of [[El Aleph Festival de Arte y Ciencia]]** since its founding in 2017. Creator and host of **[La Oveja Eléctrica](https://www.canal22.org.mx/la-oveja-electrica/)** (*The Electric Sheep*) on Canal 22 — Mexico's leading science-and-thought TV program, running since 2007. **Premio Nacional de Periodismo 2013**. Creator (with Luis Cabrera Ruiz) of **Colisionador de Ideas** — an audiovisual project bridging frontier science, poetry, and visual thinking. Author of *El inconcebible universo*, *El Libro del destino*, *Gato encerrado. Fronteras del cerebro*. Characterized by Augusto Monterroso as "dignifying of cultural television." The intellectual engine behind El Aleph's curatorial vision.
