# Christa Sommerer & Laurent Mignonneau

Pioneer duo in artificial life art. 30+ years of work creating artworks that behave as living systems — interactive installations where visitors' actions generate life-like entities. Work spans genetics, evolution, emergence, ecosystems as art. Professor at University of Art and Design Linz (Interface Cultures department).

Major 2022 retrospective at [[ZKM Center for Art and Media]]: [*The Artwork as a Living System*](https://zkm.de/en/publications/christa-sommerer-laurent-mignonneau). ZKM collection holds multiple works.

## Why Interesting
The most important artistic practitioners in the overlap of artificial life + interactive art + posthuman ecology. Their work — artworks that evolve and change in response to users — is the 30-year precedent for Amber's computational creativity + alife interests. Essential lineage to know. Austrian, based in Linz = [[Ars Electronica]] circuit + ZKM circuit.
