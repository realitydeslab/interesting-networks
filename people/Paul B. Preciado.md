# Paul B. Preciado

Philosopher, writer, and queer theorist. Author of *Testo Junkie* and *Countersexual Manifesto*. Curated [[Shu Lea Cheang]]'s 3x3x6 at the Taiwan Pavilion, Venice Biennale 2019. One of the most influential living thinkers on gender, technology, and the body.

## Why Interesting
Major queer/trans philosopher working at intersection of body politics, pharmacology, and technology. Curated one of the most radical national pavilions in Venice Biennale history. Bridges critical theory and contemporary art practice.
