# Hatis Noit

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Lucia Pietroiusti deep research. Participated in Fish Part 5 (Porto, 2022).

Japanese-British experimental vocal artist and performer. Participated in **The Shape of a Circle in the Mind of a Fish, Part 5** (Galeria Municipal do Porto, November 2022) — the most prestigious edition of Pietroiusti's interspecies symposia series. Her work explores the voice as connection between body, nature, and cosmos — making her a natural fit for the Fish series' exploration of sound, consciousness, and non-human communication. Known for layered, multi-octave vocal techniques drawing on classical music, folk traditions, and experimental composition. Signed to Erased Tapes Records.

## For Amber
- **Sound and consciousness crossover.** Her voice work is explicitly about consciousness, body, and transmission — the same territory as the Fish series. If Amber's work ever touches sound, extended voice practice, or non-human acoustic communication, Hatis Noit is a natural collaborator.

## Why Interesting
The voice is genuinely her instrument in the technical sense: she uses extended techniques (undertones, overtones, layering) that treat the human body as an ecological system for sound production. Her placement in a more-than-human consciousness symposium (Fish Part 5) wasn't gratuitous — it made serious intellectual sense.
