# Christopher Langton

Founder of the field of **Artificial Life**. Organized the first ALife conference (Los Alamos, 1987), coined the term "artificial life," and edited the founding anthology. His "edge of chaos" framework (from cellular automata studies) established the idea that computation and life both emerge near the phase transition between order and chaos. Creator of **Langton's Ant** (simple rules → complex emergent behavior). Key figure at Santa Fe Institute.
