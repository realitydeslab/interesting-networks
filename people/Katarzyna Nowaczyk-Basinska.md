# Katarzyna Nowaczyk-Basinska

Assistant Research Professor at CFI, Cambridge. Anthropologist and cultural studies scholar specializing in how AI technologies reshape our relationships with death, grief, and the afterlife. Schmidt Sciences AI2050 Fellow (2024). Author of *Nieśmiertelność* (Immortality: Contemporary Technocultural Strategies, Universitas, 2026).
