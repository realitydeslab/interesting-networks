# Ruba Katrib

American curator of contemporary art. Curator and Director of Curatorial Affairs at MoMA PS1 (since 2017; promoted to Director role 2021). CCS Bard alumna. Graduate committee member at CCS Bard (since 2017). First US-born child of Syrian immigrant parents, born Baltimore. Known for exhibitions highlighting women artists, diasporic and marginalized discourses. Curated *Greater New York* (2021) and *Theater of Operations: The Gulf Wars 1991-2011* (2019) at MoMA PS1.

## Background
Grew up in Charleston, West Virginia. Degrees from SAIC and CCS Bard. Co-founded ThreeWalls (Chicago) while a student. Worked at MOCA North Miami (2007-2012), then SculptureCenter (2012-2017), then MoMA PS1. Her curatorial work focuses on diasporic artists, global issues, political conflict, and underrepresented voices.
