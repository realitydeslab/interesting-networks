# Monika Bielskyte

Designer and futurist, founder of [Protopia Futures](https://protopiafutures.com/). Known for her critique of dystopian speculative design — she argues that SD defaults to dark, alienating aesthetics and instead advocates for **protopian** futures: plural, hopeful, non-Western, climate-positive visions. Key counter-voice to the Dunne & Raby tradition's pessimistic aesthetics.

Her work challenges: the whiteness of futures imagination, the Western-centrism of SD canon, and the culture of "doom porn" in speculative futures.
