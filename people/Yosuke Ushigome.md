# Yosuke Ushigome

Japanese designer based in London. [[Design Interactions (RCA)]] alumnus. Works at [Takram](https://www.takram.com/) — the Japanese design innovation studio with offices in London and Tokyo. Bridging Japanese design culture and British critical design tradition. Projects include speculative work on smart home systems (*Poimo*, soft robotic furniture) and the societal implications of autonomous technologies.
