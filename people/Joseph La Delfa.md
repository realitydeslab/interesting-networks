# Joseph La Delfa

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Soma Design Research Community deep research.

Researcher in the extended [[Soma Design Research Community]] network. Key recent work: "Articulating Mechanical Sympathy for Somaesthetic Human-Machine Relations" (DIS 2024, with Höök, Garrett, and Lampinen). The concept of "mechanical sympathy" — borrowed from motorsport (drivers developing somatic attunement to their vehicle) — extends soma design into body-machine coupling: what does it mean for the human body to develop a felt sense of a machine?

## Why Interesting
The body-machine extension of soma design is directly relevant to human-AI and human-robot interaction. If humans can develop "mechanical sympathy" for machines through somatic channels, this reframes how we design AI companions, embodied robots, and wearable computing. Sits at the intersection of soma design and more-than-human computing.
