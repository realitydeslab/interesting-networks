## Key Works
- **[Contagious Architecture: Computation, Aesthetics and Space](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262518659/)** (MIT Press, 2013) — major work exploring algorithms in architecture and interaction design as symptom of global cultural transformation. Argues algorithmic computation represents mode of thought challenging dominant human cognition models.
- **Abstract Sex: Philosophy, Biotechnology and the Mutations of Desire** (Continuum Press, 2004) — investigates capitalist molecular experimentations in nature with non-linear endosymbiosis theories to argue against biocentric sexual reproduction models. Develops concepts of biodigital replication and non-filiative bacterial sex.
- **"Recursive apocalypse"** in *Communication Culture and Critique* (September 2025) — analyzes recursive algorithm of apocalypse and fascism as part of recursive colonialism logic
- **"Media Ontology and Transcendental Instrumentality"** (2019) — recent work on philosophical possibilities of instrumentality
- **"Xenopatterning: Predictive Intuition and Automated Imagination"** (2019) — develops concepts of automated cognition
- **Automating Philosophy** (forthcoming) — current project exploring radical thought and critique starting with inhuman intelligence and cosmocomputations
