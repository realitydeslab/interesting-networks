# Motoi Ishibashi 石橋素

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research.

CTO and principal of [Rhizomatiks](https://rhizomatiks.com/) / Abstract Engine (Tokyo). Met [[Daito Manabe]] at IAMAS (Institute of Advanced Media Arts and Sciences), Gifu. Studied control systems engineering at Tokyo Institute of Technology, then mechanical and image processing engineering at IAMAS. Co-directed Rhizomatiks Research with Manabe since 2015. Their shared 2011 Prix Ars Electronica Award of Distinction (particles) was a landmark early recognition.

Known for engineering solutions in projection systems, interactive public spaces, and drone choreography infrastructure. Currently working on development of new artistic methods bridging visual environments and engineering solutions.
