# iLiana Fokianaki

Curator, writer, and institution builder. Founded [State of Concept Athens](https://stateofconcept.org/), an independent non-profit art space in Athens focused on the political and social dimensions of contemporary art. One of the most active curators working at the intersection of justice, decoloniality, and contemporary art practice in Southern Europe. Curated *The Trials of Justice* at [[La Colonie]], Paris (May–June 2019).

## Key Projects
- **[State of Concept Athens](https://stateofconcept.org/)** — independent non-profit art space in Athens she founded. A sister project in spirit to [[La Colonie]]: small, independent, politically explicit, operating outside institutional channels.
- **Statecraft Institute** — her curatorial and research initiative.
- ***The Trials of Justice*** — curated exhibition at [[La Colonie]], Paris (May–June 2019). An exhibition-as-tribunal investigating justice systems and their colonial legacies.

## Why Interesting
Fokianaki is a connector — she operates across Athens, Paris, Berlin, and international art circuits while maintaining a commitment to independent, non-commercial art spaces. State of Concept Athens is exactly the kind of small, rigorous, politically engaged institution that La Colonie represented in Paris. Worth tracking as part of the broader ecosystem of artist-run decolonial infrastructure in Europe.
