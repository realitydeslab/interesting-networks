# Tom Eccles

Executive Director of the Center for Curatorial Studies (CCS), Bard College (since 2005). Named to ArtReview Power 100. Previously directed the Public Art Fund, New York. Built the Hessel Museum of Art and organized its inaugural exhibition *Wrestle* (2006). Institutional power at CCS — the person who makes things happen at the administrative and strategic level.
