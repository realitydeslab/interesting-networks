# Lucy Suchman

Professor Emerita of the Anthropology of Science and Technology, Lancaster University. Former Principal Scientist at Xerox PARC. Author of *Plans and Situated Actions* (1987) — one of the foundational texts of HCI and human-computer interaction. 2010 ACM SIGCHI Lifetime Research Award.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created — discovered as keynote speaker at Aarhus 2025.

## Why Interesting
Foundational critical HCI scholar. *Plans and Situated Actions* (1987) challenged the entire computational planning paradigm by showing that human action is situated, not planned in advance — directly relevant to agent ethology and situated AI. Current work on AI and militarism (drone warfare, autonomous weapons) is politically urgent. Lancaster = geographically accessible.
