# Dominic Boyer

Anthropologist at Rice University working on energy systems, media, politics, and ecological futures. Co-founder (with Cymene Howe) of **Cultures of Energy** program at Rice. Co-authored *Hyposubjects: On Becoming Human* (2021, Open Humanities Press) with Timothy Morton — a philosophical manifesto on what it means to become (rather than simply be) human in the Anthropocene. Energy humanities pioneer.
