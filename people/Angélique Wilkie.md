# Angélique Wilkie

Dance/performance researcher at Concordia University. Head of the **Dramaturgical Ecologies** research-creation group. Her work focuses on "relating agency to concepts such as blackness, dramaturgy, gesture, ecology, and embodiment." A key Black feminist research-creation presence in the [[3 Ecologies Institute]] / [[SenseLab]] network.
