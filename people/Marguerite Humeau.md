# Marguerite Humeau

French artist working at the intersection of speculative design, paleontology, nonhuman life, and cosmic imagination. Known for reconstructing extinct organisms and imagining prehistoric/future sensory worlds. Royal College of Art trained (Design Interactions). Major institutional presence.

## Why Interesting
Directly relevant to Amber's more-than-human design interests. Humeau doesn't just make art about animals — she reconstructs their phenomenology, their communication, their interiority. This is speculative design as empathic species extension. Connects to agent ethology (what does nonhuman experience feel like?) and ALife (creating life as it could be).
