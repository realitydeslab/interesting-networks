# K Allado-McDowell

Writer, artist, and musician who co-wrote ***[Pharmako-AI](https://ignota.org/products/pharmako-ai)*** (Ignota Books, 2020) with GPT-3 — a pioneering experiment in human-AI literary co-authorship produced in an extended state of collaboration with the AI during the early pandemic. Also wrote **Amor Cringe** (Ignota, 2022). Former head of the **Artist + Machine Intelligence** program at **Google** (Google Brain). Member of the **[[Future Art Ecosystems]]** / Serpentine Arts Technologies contributor network.

Their work frames AI collaboration as a pharmacological act: AI as both poison and cure, tool and subject, other and mirror. The "pharmako-" prefix (from Derrida's *Plato's Pharmacy*) grounds the work in a tradition that refuses simple instrumentalism.

## Key Works / Projects
- ***Pharmako-AI*** (Ignota Books, 2020) — book co-written with GPT-3. Extended meditation on AI consciousness, ayahuasca, and what happens when a human writer enters genuinely open-ended dialogue with a language model. One of the earliest and most philosophically serious AI co-authorship experiments.
- ***Amor Cringe*** (Ignota Books, 2022) — follow-up, continuing the AI-human literary collaboration
- **Google Artist + Machine Intelligence** — ran the programme connecting artists with Google's AI research teams. Understanding how they left this role (quietly) matters for understanding their current independent stance.
