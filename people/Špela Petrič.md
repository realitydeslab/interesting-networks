# Špela Petrič

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created. Discovered via HeK Basel "Other Intelligences" exhibition (2025).

Slovenian artist based in Amsterdam/Ljubljana. Works at the intersection of bioart, biopolitics, and plant intelligence. Key figure in more-than-human and posthuman art practice. Shown at [[HeK (House of Electronic Arts Basel)]] "Other Intelligences" 2025, Ars Electronica, transmediale, Kapelica Gallery.

## Domains
- Bioart
- Plant intelligence / plant communication
- More-than-human / posthuman
- Feminist science studies
- Performance + installation

## Why Interesting
Špela creates work that puts plants and non-human organisms as central protagonists, not just subjects. Rigorous scientific engagement combined with speculative/critical practice. Directly in Amber's more-than-human zone. Her work on plant communication, interspecies choreography, and biopolitics is both aesthetically rigorous and philosophically interesting.

## Key Works (Stub)
- *Skotopoiesis* — plant growth performance
- *Phytoteratology* — plant speculative biology
- *Strange Encounters* — human-plant interaction
