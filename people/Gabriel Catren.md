# Gabriel Catren

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-12:** Stub created during Urbanomic deep research.

Argentine-French philosopher working at CNRS/Laboratoire SPHERE, Paris. Post-Grothendieckian speculative philosophy. Author of *Pleromatica, or Elsinore's Trance* ([[Urbanomic]] × [[Sequence Press]]). Contributor to *Collapse* V and VII. Infuses continental philosophy with Latin American thought (Viveiros de Castro, Oswald de Andrade) and advanced mathematics (Grothendieck).
