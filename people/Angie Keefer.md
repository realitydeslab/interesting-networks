# Angie Keefer

Artist, writer, and editor working at the intersection of art, AI, and critical theory. Series editor and driving force behind **Version Space** (Library Stack / Sandberg Instituut, 2020–ongoing) — a series of pamphlets transcribing AI conversations between artists and graduate students. Also a contributor to *Proof of Personhood* (End of Medium / Singapore Art Museum, 2025), the key critical AI anthology edited by William Wiebe and Duncan Bass.

## Key Work
- **[Version Space](https://www.librarystack.org/version-space/)** (2020–) — series editor. Conversations with: [[Holly Herndon]], [[K Allado-McDowell]], [[Kite (Suzanne Kite)]], [[Jenna Sutela]], Lynn Hershman Leeson, Auriea Harvey, Liz Magic Laser. Funded by Library Stack + Sandberg Instituut Artistic Research program. Research fellow at Sandberg Instituut (Arnhem) 2018–2020.
- *Proof of Personhood* (2025) — contributor, alongside Maya Indira Ganesh, Alex Quicho, Noura Tafeche

## Why Interesting
Keefer occupies the editorial/curatorial node in the Library Stack ecosystem for AI + art conversations. The Version Space pamphlets are the closest model to what Amber's agent ethology conversations could become — recorded dialogues between artists and researchers, published as small-edition texts.
