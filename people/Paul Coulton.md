# Paul Coulton

Design fiction researcher at Lancaster University. Co-authored the most rigorous academic definition of design fiction with Joseph Lindley. Works on speculative game design, IoT critique, more-than-human design.

## Domains
- Design fiction (methodology and theory)
- Speculative game design
- Internet of Things critique
- More-than-human design

## Background
Based at ImaginationLancaster. One of the key figures bridging design fiction practice with academic HCI/design research. Frequent contributor to RTD (Research Through Design) conferences. Co-authored with Joseph Lindley the Lindley-Coulton definition: design fiction = story-world + diegetic prototype + discursive space.

## Key Works
- [What is Design Fiction?](https://doi.org/10.17158/1543-7663.15.1.1) (Lindley & Coulton, *The Design Journal*, 2015) — rigorous definition paper
- RTD 2015, 2017, 2019 contributions — speculative game design, IoT futures
