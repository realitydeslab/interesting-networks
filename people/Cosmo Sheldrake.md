# Cosmo Sheldrake

British musician, composer, and naturalist whose work weaves ecology, field recording, and experimental music. Brother of Merlin Sheldrake (author of *Entangled Life: How Fungi Make Our Worlds*). Deeply embedded in the more-than-human/ecology cultural ecosystem.

Created the soundscape for **[[Superflux]]**'s *Invocation for Hope* (Vienna Biennale / MAK, 2021) — a forest of 400 fire-blackened pine trees with a living resurgent oasis at its centre.

His work is interesting to Amber because he inhabits the same more-than-human cultural space as Superflux, Donna Haraway, and Robin Wall Kimmerer — but through sound rather than design or academia. The field recording + ecological intelligence angle overlaps with Superflux's *Nobody Told Me Rivers Dream* (2025) sensing approach.

*Merlin Sheldrake's* book (*Entangled Life*) is also curated in [[Anab Jain]]'s Serpentine "shop" intervention (2022) — showing shared intellectual community.
