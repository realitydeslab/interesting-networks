# Ionat Zurr

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub enriched during Oron Catts deep research. Needs own full deep research.

Artist, researcher, and curator. Co-founder of the **[Tissue Culture & Art Project (TC&A)](https://tcaproject.net/)** with [[Oron Catts]] since 1996. Co-director of [[SymbioticA]] for over 20 years. Pioneer of art made with living, engineered tissue. Now **Chair of Fine Arts, UWA School of Design**, where she instigated the **Master of Science (Biological Arts)** — an interdisciplinary degree joining art and science. Visiting tutor in Design Interactions at [Royal College of Art](https://www.rca.ac.uk/), London. PhD: *Growing Semi-Living Art* (UWA, 2008).

## Background
- Australian artist, researcher, curator.
- Co-founded TC&A and [[SymbioticA]] with [[Oron Catts]] (launched 2000 at UWA School of Anatomy and Human Biology).
- **PhD: *"Growing Semi-Living Art"* (UWA, 2008)** — [PDF](https://tcaproject.net/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/ionat_zurr-phd-final.pdf).
- **Chair of Fine Arts, UWA School of Design** — institutional authority to reshape bio-art education.
- Instigated the **[Master of Science (Biological Arts)](https://symbiotica.uwa.edu.au/)** at UWA — new interdisciplinary degree.
- Visiting tutor in Design Interactions, [Royal College of Art](https://www.rca.ac.uk/).
- Guest artist at [Arts at CERN](https://arts.cern/) (2019) with [[Oron Catts]].

## Why Interesting
Half of one of the most important bio-art collaborations in contemporary practice. As Chair of Fine Arts at UWA and instigator of the MBiolArts degree, Zurr is doing the institutional work that makes the next generation of biological artists possible. The PhD thesis is a rare document: an artist's internal account of what it means to "grow" art. Deserves separate deep research from Catts.
