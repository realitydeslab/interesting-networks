# Gilles Retsin

London-based architect and designer. Studied in Belgium, Chile, and UK (Architectural Association graduate). Known for design practice and critical discourse on **discrete/robotic architecture**.

- **AUAR Labs** (UCL) — co-founded, led advanced research on robotic fabrication and design
- **AUAR ltd** — spin-out startup providing automated housing supply platform using robotics
- **MAEID** (Büro für Architektur und Transmediale Kunst) — multidisciplinary practice exploring computation, living systems, performance. Work at Venice Biennale, Seoul Biennale, Ars Electronica, Melbourne Triennial
- Edited *Architectural Design (AD)* "The Discrete" issue
- Co-edited *Robotic Building: Architecture in the Age of Automation* (Detail Verlag, 2019)

Awards/exhibits: Royal Academy London, Museum of Arts and Design NYC, Centre Pompidou Paris.

## Why Interesting

Operates at intersection of ALife-adjacent ideas (automated, distributed, open-ended architecture), computational fabrication, and speculative futurism. MAEID's work in "living systems + performance" overlaps with Amber's interests. Lectured at [[KYOTO Design Lab]] Dec 2025.
