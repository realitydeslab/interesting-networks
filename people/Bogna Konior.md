# Bogna Konior

Scholar and writer. Philosophy of technology, digital culture, AI and theology/mysticism/erotics. Eastern European and Chinese tech-philosophical traditions. Assistant Professor of Media Theory at [[NYU Shanghai IMA]], working at the [AI & Culture Research Center (AIC)](https://aic.shanghai.nyu.edu).

## Background
- PhD in Humanities, Hong Kong Baptist University
- RMa in Media Studies, University of Amsterdam
- BA in Film Studies, Queen Mary, University of London
- Based in Shanghai

## Why Interesting
Deeply philosophical but grounded in media arts practice. Bridges Eastern European and Chinese intellectual traditions with tech theory — rare perspective. Works with both academic and art institutions. AI × mysticism × theology is an unusual and fascinating intersection that few others occupy. Dark Forest Theory of the Internet is a major new book. *Machine Decision is Not Final* positions her at the centre of the China × AI × philosophy discourse alongside Bratton and Greenspan. Teaches courses that directly address posthumanism and AI writing. **Amber's closest intellectual inspiration.**
