# Bogna Konior

Scholar and writer. Philosophy of technology, digital culture, AI and theology/mysticism/erotics. Eastern European and Chinese tech-philosophical traditions.

## Key Works
- *Dark Forest Theory of the Internet* (Polity, 2025)
- *Machine Decision is Not Final: China and the History and Future of AI* (Urbanomic/MIT Press, 2025) — co-edited with [[Benjamin Bratton]] and Anna Greenspan
- Multimedia project on AI × erotics × theology × mysticism

## Domains
- Philosophy of digital culture, techno-politics, speculative computation
- Post-Cold War technopolitical diversity, technological determinism
- China's AI history and futures
- AI and mysticism/theology — unusual and fascinating intersection

## Why Interesting
Deeply philosophical but grounded in media arts practice. Bridges Eastern European and Chinese intellectual traditions with tech theory — rare perspective. Works with both academic and art institutions (ZKM, Ljubljana Biennale, Singapore Art Museum, MoMA Warsaw, e-flux). Affiliated with [[Antikythera]] think tank for speculative computation.
