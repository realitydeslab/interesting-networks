# Simon Schaffer

Professor of History of Science at Cambridge University. Author of landmark essays "Babbage's Intelligence" (1994) and "OK Computer" (2001) — tracing computing's social and economic roots. Core intellectual inspiration for [[Matteo Pasquinelli]]'s labor theory of AI. Endorsed *The Eye of the Master*.

## Domains
- History of science and technology
- Victorian computing and political economy (Babbage)
- Science studies / STS

## Key Works
- "Babbage's Intelligence" (1994) — the foundational essay showing how Babbage's difference engine was shaped by the organization of industrial labor
- "OK Computer" (2001) — computing and social history

## Why Interesting
Cambridge-based ⭐ proximity.
