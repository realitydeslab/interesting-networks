# Stuart Candy

Australian futurist and designer who coined **Experiential Futures (XF)** — the practice of making abstract futures physically present and sensory at 1:1 scale. First Research Fellow of the Long Now Foundation. Fellow of the World Futures Studies Federation. Collaborates with Brian Eno on music + futures (Sing Wild Seeds initiative). Co-created "The Thing from the Future" card game with Jeff Watson (Situation Lab).

PhD in Political Science and Alternative Futures, University of Hawaii (under Jim Dator). Key bridge between futures studies and speculative design.
