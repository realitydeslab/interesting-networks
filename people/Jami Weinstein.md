# Jami Weinstein

Associate Professor of Gender Studies at **Linköping University** (Sweden). Key collaborator with [[Claire Colebrook]]: co-edited *Deleuze and Gender* (Edinburgh UP, 2009) and *Posthumous Life: Theorizing Beyond the Posthuman* (Columbia UP, 2017). Co-editor (with Colebrook and Myra Hird) of the **Critical Life Studies** series at Columbia University Press.

Also a member of the advisory board for the Critical Climate Change series at [[Open Humanities Press]] (with Colebrook & [[Tom Cohen]]).

## Why Interesting
Weinstein's work at the intersection of Deleuzian feminism and critical life studies is building the infrastructure for posthumanism that interrogates "life" as a normative concept — a project closely aligned with Colebrook's. The Sweden/Linköping connection also links to the Seed Box Collaboratory (Linköping) that funds OHP's SeedBooks series, suggesting a broader Scandinavian-Australian-US triangle in critical posthumanism.
