# Stephan Harding

Ecologist, resident scientist at Schumacher College (Dartington, Devon), and coordinator of the MSc in Holistic Science (partnership with University of Plymouth). Holds a DPhil in ecology from the University of Oxford. Co-founder of the [[Alliance for Wild Ethics]] with [[David Abram]].

Author of *Animate Earth: Science, Intuition and Gaia* (2007, Chelsea Green). Worked closely with James Lovelock (Gaia hypothesis), Lynn Margulis (endosymbiosis), and Brian Goodwin (theoretical biology). Taught alongside Arne Naess, Fritjof Capra, and Abram at Schumacher. Also collaborates with philosopher of science Henri Bortoft and — recently — Martin Shaw (mythopoetic storytelling), producing fresh translations of Lorca's poetry into English.

**AMBER PROXIMITY:** Based at Schumacher College, Devon, UK — near Oxford. Gateway into the UK ecological phenomenology teaching circuit.
