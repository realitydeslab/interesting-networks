# Giuseppe Cocco

Italian-Brazilian political theorist. Bridge between Latin American social movements and European post-operaismo tradition. Professor at UFRJ (Rio de Janeiro). Long-time contributor to [[Multitudes (journal)]]. Wrote "Résister, reconstituer le sens" (Multitudes #98, 2025) on Ukraine war and the left's difficulties with war politics. Key figure in the autonomist tradition's adaptation to Latin American contexts.

## Why Interesting
Rare bridge between European autonomist theory (Negri, Lazzarato, Multitudes circle) and Latin American political movements. His work on how post-workerism adapts to Global South contexts (Brazil, Venezuela) is relevant for thinking beyond European-centric frameworks.
