# Itsuo Sakane (坂根 厳夫)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via IAMAS deep research. Died 2024.

Japanese critic and pioneer of media art. First President of IAMAS (1996). Organized landmark exhibitions in Japan including the "Interactive Art" section of early Ars Electronica festivals. Historical figure in the formation of Japanese media art discourse and institutional infrastructure. Died 2024 — his full legacy is only now becoming clear as IAMAS marks its 30th anniversary.

## Why Interesting
Foundational figure for Japanese media art institutionalization. Understanding him means understanding why IAMAS exists where it does and what intellectual tradition it carries. His Ars Electronica connections brought the European media art framework into Japan early.
