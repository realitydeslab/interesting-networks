# Akseli Virtanen

Finnish political economist and co-founder of [[Economic Space Agency (ECSA)]]; the primary theorist and organizer of ECSA's post-capitalist economic media project. D.Sc.(Econ.), Associate Professor at Aalto University (Finland). Previously co-founded [[Robin Hood Minor Asset Management]] (2012), a decentralized hedge fund/art project that parasitically mimicked Wall Street to fund alternative culture. Relocated to California 2014, Stanford School of Engineering 2018.

## Domains
Post-capitalist political economy, cryptoeconomics, protocol design, alter-economy organization, post-Fordist labor theory.
