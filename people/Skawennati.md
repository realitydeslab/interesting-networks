# Skawennati

Mohawk (Kahnawakeronon) multimedia artist. Concordia University. Co-founded **Aboriginal Territories in Cyberspace (AbTeC)** and **Initiative for Indigenous Futures (IIF)** with Jason Edward Lewis.

Key works:
- **CyberPowWow** (1997–2004): First Indigenous online art space — a chat room functioning as interactive digital art gallery; created Indigenous territory in cyberspace
- **TimeTraveller™**: 9-episode machinima series — 22nd-century Mohawk protagonist travels through key moments of Indigenous history (Oka Crisis, Dakota Uprising, Kateri Tekakwitha). Combines sci-fi with First Nations historical memory.
- **She Falls for Ages**: Haudenosaunee creation story remixed with sci-fi aesthetic in Second Life

**Significance**: Pioneer of Indigenous presence in digital/virtual spaces. Shows how cyberspace can be *territory* — not neutral infrastructure but a space that can be claimed, designed, and governed according to Indigenous protocols.

**Awards**: ImagineNATIVE Best New Media (2009, 2013). Eiteljorg Contemporary Art Fellow (2011). Smithsonian Artist Research Fellowship (2020).
