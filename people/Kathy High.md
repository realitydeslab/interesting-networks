# Kathy High

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during SymbioticA deep research.

American interdisciplinary artist, curator, and scholar (b. 1954, Bryn Mawr, PA). Known for bioart, video art, and performance art. Professor at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute since 2002. Former SymbioticA resident — her time in Perth became foundational to her bioart practice.

**Key works:** *Blood Wars* (ongoing — tournament-style human white blood cell battles; Guggenheim Fellowship 2010); *Animal Attraction* (first exhibited at Guggenheim); *Embracing Animal* (transgenic mouse project). Work addresses animal sentience, gender and technology, empathy. Shows at Guggenheim, MASS MoCA, MoMA.

Co-editor: *The Emergence of Video Processing Tools: Television Becoming Unglued*. Founded FELIX: A Journal of Media Arts and Communication (1991). Studied with Tony Conrad, Hollis Frampton, Steina Vasulka at SUNY Buffalo.

## Why Interesting
SymbioticA alumna with major feminist bio-art practice. Blood Wars is a genuinely weird and rigorous work — the scientific protocol IS the artistic gesture. Guggenheim Fellowship. Important voice in the art + animal rights + bioethics intersection.
