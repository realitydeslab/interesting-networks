# Zakiyyah Iman Jackson

Associate Professor at USC. Works at the intersection of Black studies, feminist theory, and posthumanism. *Becoming Human: Matter and Meaning in an Antiblack World* (2020, NYU Press) argues that anti-Blackness is foundational to humanism — and thus any posthumanism that doesn't grapple with race simply replaces one exclusion with another.
