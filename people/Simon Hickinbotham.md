# Simon Hickinbotham

Computer scientist at the University of York, working with Susan Stepney. Artificial life researcher specializing in artificial chemistries, open-ended evolution, and self-reproducing systems.

## Why Interesting
Wrote "Oblique Strategies for Artificial Life" (2019) — the key paper directly applying Eno & Schmidt's Oblique Strategies to ALife research methodology. This is the critical bridge between Oblique Strategies and Amber's ALife/agent work.
