# Chris Milk

**Filmmaker, VR artist, and co-founder of Within (formerly Vrse.works)** — one of the first companies dedicated to VR storytelling. Known for the "empathy machine" framing of VR (his 2015 TED Talk). A frequent exhibitor at [[Sundance New Frontier]].

Key works:
- **"The Evolution of Verse"** (New Frontier 2015) — photo-realistic CGI VR; showed on Google Cardboard; [[Shari Frilot]] demoed it with cardboard in the parking lot before the festival.
- **"Life of Us"** with Aaron Koblin (New Frontier 2017) — social VR; "a huge quantum leap" of collaborative VR (Frilot's words).
- **"Stonemilker VR"** — with Björk + Andrew Thomas Huang (2016); used Vrse.works platform.
- **"Wild: The Experience"** (New Frontier 2015) — with Felix & Paul; Reese Witherspoon and Laura Dern; Samsung Gear VR.

Within (formerly Vrse) became a major platform for VR narrative film distribution.

Connects to: [[Sundance New Frontier]], [[Shari Frilot]], [[Nonny de la Peña]]
