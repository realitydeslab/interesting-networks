# Wendi Yan 闫文迪

Artist, technologist, writer. Constructs speculative epistemologies through research-driven worldbuilding and metafictional simulation.

## Domains
- CGI, game engines, documentary practices
- Collaborates with scientists (synthetic biology, astrophysics)
- Science history writing (Asimov Press — "Best of Journalism" by The Syllabus)

## Why Interesting
Builds fictional epistemic systems to probe what counts as knowledge. Grand Prix VH Award. Exactly at art × science × speculation intersection.
