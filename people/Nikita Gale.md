# Nikita Gale

Los Angeles-based research-based artist working with installation, performance, and sculpture. Known for systematic investigations of infrastructure, embodiment, and spectacle — particularly the architecture of entertainment venues and the social physics of crowd experience. Represented by Petzel Gallery (NYC).

## Why Interesting
Her *NOSEBLEED* (2024, Petzel) explored the spatial and embodied politics of stadium seating — taking the "worst seat in the house" as a vantage point for examining who counts as a spectator. Research-based, systematic, and interested in how infrastructure shapes collective experience. Close to Amber's interests in embodied interaction, spectatorship as a site of power, and the politics of participation.
