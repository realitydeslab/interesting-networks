# Curtis Yarvin

American software engineer and political theorist. Writing under the pen name **Mencius Moldbug** (2007-2013), Yarvin constructed the conceptual basis for what would become the **Dark Enlightenment / NRx (neoreactionary) movement**. His key concept: the "Cathedral" — the idea that liberal democratic institutions (universities, media, NGOs, government) function as a secular religion suppressing dissent. He advocated replacing democracy with CEO-run competitive city-states.

[[Nick Land]] read Yarvin's work and elaborated it, coining the term "Dark Enlightenment" in 2012 to name the broader movement. Together they are the intellectual co-founders of NRx.
