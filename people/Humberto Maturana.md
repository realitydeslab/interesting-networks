# Humberto Maturana

Chilean biologist and philosopher (1928–2021). Co-creator with [[Francisco Varela]] of **autopoiesis** — the theory that living systems are self-producing, self-maintaining networks. Also developed concepts of structural determinism and structural coupling. Often grouped with second-order cybernetics theorists alongside Heinz von Foerster and Gordon Pask. Received the Chilean National Prize for Natural Sciences.

## Why Interesting
The intellectual origin point of autopoiesis. Maturana's position was stricter than Varela's — he insisted autopoiesis applied only to molecular biology, not to social systems or AI. The Maturana-Varela divergence is itself interesting: two thinkers who shared a foundational insight and then developed it in very different directions.
