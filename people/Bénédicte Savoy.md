# Bénédicte Savoy

French art historian, Professor at TU Berlin. Co-author of the [Sarr-Savoy Report](https://web.archive.org/web/20210815041636/http://restitutionreport2018.com/) (2018) with [[Felwine Sarr]]. 2021 TIME 100 Most Influential People. Leading European scholar on African heritage restitution and the ethics of museum collections with colonial-era origins.

## Domains
- Art history, museum studies, cultural heritage
- Restitution of African and colonial-era objects
- Ethics of European collections

## Why Interesting
The Sarr-Savoy collaboration is a model for philosopher + expert co-authorship that produces policy-level impact. Savoy's European institutional positioning gave the report credibility inside France that Sarr alone could not have achieved. Key node in the European museum/heritage world.
