# Sandra Calkins

STS anthropologist at Free University Berlin; studies evidence-making, lab infrastructure, and plant-human entanglements in African scientific contexts. Part of the [[LOST Research Group]] network.

## Domains
- Science and Technology Studies (STS)
- Infrastructure studies (lab failure, toxic substances)
- More-than-human: plants as scientific collaborators in Uganda
- Postcolonial science; "southern science"
- Food, medicine, and biotechnology in Africa

## Why Interesting
Calkins' work on plants as scientific collaborators in Uganda ("better bananas", vegetal collaborators, plant affective atmospheres) is one of the more unusual more-than-human STS contributions — treating plants as active agents in knowledge production, not passive objects. Her lab infrastructure work connects to questions about how postcolonial scientific institutions fail and what gets built around that failure. Relevant to Amber's more-than-human design and interest in non-human agents.
