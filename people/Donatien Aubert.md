# Donatien Aubert

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-02:** Stub created. Discovered as Le Fresnoy visiting professor 2025-26.

Artist, researcher, author. Born 1990, Chambray-lès-Tours, France.

## Background
- Graduated with honors from École nationale supérieure d'arts de Paris-Cergy
- Post-master's at EnsadLab (École nationale supérieure des arts décoratifs) — Spatial Media programme (VR, shared 3D environments)
- PhD in comparative literature, Sorbonne — thesis on "arts of memory" in human-machine interaction (Negroponte, Lanier)
- Published by Éditions Hermann: *Vers une disparition programmatique d'Homo sapiens ?* (2017)

## Domains
Cybernetics, digital cultures, Cold War techno-imaginaries, memory and machine interaction, VR, cosmism vs transhumanism.

## Key Works
- Featured in "Machina Sapiens" exhibition, Conciergerie, Paris (2025 — AI Action Summit)
- Solo exhibitions: Galerie Odile Ouizeman (Paris, 2021), 3 bis f (Aix-en-Provence, 2022–23), Le Hublot (Nice, 2024)
- Shown at Némo, Chroniques, Elektra biennials; internationally in Kyoto, Basel, Montreal, Goa
- Cnap "Image 3.0" photography commission (2020)

## Le Fresnoy Project (2025-26)
Film exploring ideological convergence between **Russian cosmism** and **American transhumanism/technological singularity** — how the two chancelleries maintain troubled relations with the technological imaginaries born within their borders.
