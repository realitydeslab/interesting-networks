# Catherine Holloway

Professor at UCL, Academic Director of the [Global Disability Innovation Hub](https://www.disabilityinnovation.com/). World's leading researcher on assistive technology (AT) access in low/middle-income countries. Chair of GDI Hub Academic Research Centre. Director of the world's first [MSc Disability, Design & Innovation](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/prospective-students/graduate/taught-degrees/disability-design-and-innovation-msc).
