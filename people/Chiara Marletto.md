# Chiara Marletto

Oxford physicist working on **Constructor Theory** — a new framework for the foundations of physics developed with Vlatko Vedral. FQXi-funded (2018) with Vedral: "Agent-based irreversibility in quantum theory: theory and experiment" ($104K). Constructor Theory reframes physics in terms of what transformations are *possible* vs. *impossible* — a radical alternative to initial-condition-based physics, with deep implications for life, information, and consciousness.
