# Gong Yan 龚彦

Deputy Director of **[[Power Station of Art]]** (PSA), Shanghai's flagship state-run contemporary art museum. Previously editor-in-chief of *[Art World](http://www.artWorldChina.com/)* (《艺术世界》), one of China's most important bilingual contemporary art magazines. The intellectual and editorial spine of PSA's programming. Her publishing background shapes how PSA frames and communicates its exhibition programme. Low public international profile; high internal institutional authority. Key person for anyone engaging seriously with PSA's programming direction.
