# Julius von Bismarck

German artist; **2012 Collide Award** winner (Arts at CERN × Ars Electronica). Multidisciplinary practice — photography, film, installation, sculpture, performance, landscape — exploring dialectics of nature/civilisation, knowledge/cultural imagination, individual/social behaviour. Uses physics, technology, and social sciences as research tools. Visual vocabulary centres on elemental forces that exceed human perception. Former apprentice at **Berlin Institute for Spatial Experiments** directed by **Olafur Eliasson**. Collaborated with Benjamin Maus on several works. His CERN-inspired work intersects perception, gravity, and reality/nature. Featured in *In the Spaces Between* (2025, dpr-barcelona). Work shown at major institutions internationally.
