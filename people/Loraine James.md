# Loraine James

British electronic music producer from working-class North London (Enfield tower block). IDM/experimental. Queer. Signed to Hyperdub 2019. Also releases as Whatever the Weather (ambient). University of Westminster (commercial music). Collaborated with Dominican performance artist Isabel Lewis at CTM Festival. Julius Eastman tribute album (Phantom Limb, 2022). Tops Quietus/DJ Mag end-of-year lists.
