# Nora Bateson

Filmmaker, educator, and systems thinker. Daughter of [[Gregory Bateson]]. President of the [International Bateson Institute](https://batesoninstitute.org/). Director of *An Ecology of Mind* (2011), documentary about her father's life and thought. Developed the concept of **"warm data"** — qualitative, contextual, relational data that preserves complexity that quantitative methods strip away.
