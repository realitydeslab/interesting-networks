# Geng Jianyi 耿建翌

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Zhang Peili deep research.

Chinese conceptual artist (1962–2017). Co-founder of the **[[Pond Society]]** (池社) with [[Zhang Peili]] in 1986. Known for deadpan, analytical work that separated art from emotion. His painting *The Second State* (1987) — showing faces caught between laughing and grimacing — is one of the most iconic works of the '85 New Wave. Later taught at [[China Academy of Art]].

## Key Relationships
- **[[Zhang Peili]]** ⭐⭐⭐ — Pond Society co-founder, lifelong collaborator and colleague at CAA.
- **Song Ling** — Pond Society co-founder.
