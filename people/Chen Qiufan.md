# Chen Qiufan (陈楸帆)

Chinese science fiction writer. Also known as Stanley Chan.

## Key Works
- *Waste Tide* (2013/2019 English) — cli-fi set in e-waste processing town
- *AI 2041: Ten Visions for Our Future* (2021) — co-authored with Kai-Fu Lee
- *The Algorithm of Life* and other short fiction
