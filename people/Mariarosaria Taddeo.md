## Why Interesting for Amber
Taddeo's ethics work extends beyond the typical "bias and fairness" framing into questions of **agency, autonomy, and moral responsibility** in AI systems — which connects to Amber's machine behavior and sovereign agents work.

Her defence/military context means she's grappling with AI agents that have genuinely high stakes — life and death consequences. This is a more philosophically demanding version of AI ethics than most OII work. The questions she asks about autonomous systems in warfare have structural similarities to questions Amber asks about machine agency and mortality.
