# Libby Heaney

London-based artist and former quantum physicist who builds immersive installations at the intersection of **quantum computing** and **AI/machine learning**. Her practice foregrounds the weirdness of quantum mechanics — superposition, entanglement, collapse — as both technical reality and aesthetic lens. Part of the **[[Serpentine Galleries]]** Arts Technologies ecosystem; played **Hivemind** (2022), the Serpentine R&D Platform's knowledge-game for artists, where she created a "quantum Hivemind" that she describes as a "non-linear labyrinth that weaves together multiplicities, entanglements and collapses to let us peer into the futures of quantum computing through art."

Former researcher: has a background in quantum computing research (post-PhD academic career before pivoting fully to art). This makes her one of the few artists who can build genuine quantum computing works rather than just using the aesthetic vocabulary.
