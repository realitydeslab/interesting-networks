# Giles Round

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Lucia Pietroiusti deep research. Created visual identity for all editions of the Fish series; designed themind.fish website; participated as artist in Fish Part 5 (Porto, 2022).

Artist whose practice spans sculpture, design, and visual identity. Created the **visual identity for all editions of *The Shape of a Circle in the Mind of a Fish*** (2018–2022) and designed the dedicated website **[themind.fish](https://themind.fish)**. Participated as an artist in Fish Part 5 (Porto, 2022). Also appeared in the **Back to Earth** exhibition (2022) with toy-like models inspired by climate-monitoring satellites orbiting the Earth.

The satellite models are interesting: small, almost playful objects that refer to the vast global infrastructure of environmental monitoring — a move that scales the cosmic down to the human-holdable.

## For Amber
- **Visual identity for research series.** Giles Round's visual work for the Fish series is an excellent study in how to give a multi-year, interdisciplinary research programme a consistent visual language that is strange and precise at the same time. Relevant for Amber if she designs her own event series.
- The satellite models are a good example of *scale translation* as an artistic strategy — making the invisible (global climate monitoring infrastructure) visible at a human scale.

## Why Interesting
The combination of visual identity design + artistic practice + ecological content is rare. He's not a "graphic designer for arts organisations" but an artist who also does design — and the *Fish* series identity is one of the most distinctive in recent UK art programming.
