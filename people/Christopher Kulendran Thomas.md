# Christopher Kulendran Thomas

Sri Lankan-British artist based in Berlin working with machine learning, AI-generated images, and post-national identity. Developed the concept of *New Eelam* — a speculative new state delivered by subscription, bypassing nationality through platform capitalism. Works often collaborate with writer Annika Kuhlmann. Known for using AI image generation as artistic medium before it became mainstream.
