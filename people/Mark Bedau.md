# Mark Bedau

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created. Discovered via ISAL deep research.
- **2025:** Received ISAL Lifetime Achievement Award at ALIFE 2025, Kyoto.

Philosopher of artificial life. Reed College, Portland. Long-time editor of the *Artificial Life* journal (MIT Press). Seminal work on proto-cells, weak emergence, and the philosophical foundations of artificial life. 2025 ISAL Lifetime Achievement Award.

## Why Interesting
As long-time editor of the *Artificial Life* journal and a major philosophical voice in ALife, Bedau bridges the philosophy of life with computational models. His work on "weak emergence" (distinguishing emergent phenomena from their substrates) is foundational. If Amber wants to publish in *Artificial Life*, knowing Bedau and his framing is essential.
