# Yunchul Kim 김윤철

Korean artist working with complex material phenomena — magnetic fluids, particle physics, acoustic fields, fluidics — as artistic media. Develops original theoretical frameworks: **Mattereality**, **Crossmorphing**, **Transmattering**. Views matter as active agent with inherent agency, co-directing the creative process. Keynote speaker ISEA 2025 (Seoul).

## Domains
- **Material-based art** — magnetic fluids, ferrofluids, particle physics, acoustic phenomena
- **Complex systems** — scientific phenomena as artistic medium
- **Art-science collaboration** — works at physics/engineering/art intersection
- **Process philosophy** — matter as "reality in process," quantum relational ontology

## Key Concepts
- **Mattereality** — matter as active agent with inherent agency; artist + material in mutual dialogue; matter expresses through physical, chemical, temporal properties; matter as "reality in process"
- **Crossmorphing** — bidirectional transformation between artistic activities and other disciplines (science, technology, philosophy); beyond simple collaboration
- **Transmattering** — process by which materials transform from natural states into cultural artifacts; moving beyond physical transformation to evolving meaning and concept
