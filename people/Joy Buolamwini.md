# Joy Buolamwini

Researcher, poet, and activist who exposed racial and gender bias in facial recognition systems. MIT Media Lab PhD. Founded the [Algorithmic Justice League](https://www.ajl.org/). Known for "Gender Shades" research (2018) showing that commercial facial recognition failed dramatically on darker-skinned women. Testified before US Congress on AI bias. Author of *Unmasking AI: My Mission to Protect What Is Human in a World of Machines* (2023). Close intellectual neighbor to [[Ruha Benjamin]] in the critical AI space.
