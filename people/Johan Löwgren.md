# Johan Löwgren

Professor at Linköping University, Sweden. Co-author with [[Kristina Höök]] of ["Strong Concepts: Intermediate-Level Knowledge in Interaction Design Research"](https://doi.org/10.1145/2362364.2362371) (ACM TOCHI, 2012) — 793 citations. This paper established "strong concepts" as a knowledge contribution mode in design research: intermediate between concrete examples and high-level theory. The methodological backbone of soma design and much of design research in HCI.

Also known for his work on interaction design theory, particularly the notion of "thoughtful interaction design."

## Key Works

- *Thoughtful Interaction Design: A Design Perspective on Information Technology* (with Stolterman, MIT Press, 2004)
