# Leif Weatherby

Associate Professor of German and Director of the **Digital Theory Lab** at NYU. Co-author of *Digital Theory* (Minnesota/meson, 2026) with M. Beatrice Fazi, Alexander Galloway, and Matthew Handelman. Author of *Language Machines: Cultural AI and the End of Remainder Humanism*. Works on the philosophical and cultural dimensions of digital media, AI, and German intellectual history.
