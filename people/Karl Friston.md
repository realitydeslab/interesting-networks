# Karl Friston

British neuroscientist. Originator of the Free Energy Principle and Active Inference framework. One of the most cited neuroscientists in history.

- "Active Inference: A Process Theory" (Neural Computation, 2017)

## Why Interesting
The Free Energy Principle is the most ambitious theory in neuroscience — claims all living things minimize surprise. If correct, it unifies perception, action, learning, and life itself. Active Inference extends this to AI. Directly connects to machine behavior, ALife, and more-than-human thinking. Controversial but generative — edge of chaos.
