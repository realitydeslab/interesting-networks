# Hans Ulrich Obrist

Artistic Director of [Serpentine Galleries](https://www.serpentinegalleries.org/), London. One of the world's most influential curators and connectors of ideas across art, science, philosophy, and politics. Co-founder (with Bernard Stiegler) of [[Collectif Internation]] at the Serpentine Work Marathon, September 2018.

## Domains
Curation, art × ideas programmes, philosophy of culture, ecological thinking, art × technology, political aesthetics.

## Background
Swiss curator, born 1968. Has conducted thousands of interviews with artists, scientists, philosophers. Edited numerous anthologies. Known for "Interview Project" (ongoing). Artistic Director of Serpentine since 2006. Runs annual ideas marathons at Serpentine.

## Key Works
- Co-signed letter to UN Secretary-General António Guterres (Nov 2019) on behalf of [[Collectif Internation]]
- Numerous anthologies and curatorial projects at Serpentine
- Ecology-focused Serpentine programme (ecologies initiative)

## Key Communities
- [[Collectif Internation]] — co-founder
- [[Serpentine Galleries]] — Artistic Director

## Why Interesting
Key node connecting art, philosophy, and political action in London. Through Serpentine, has built infrastructure for ideas-marathons and transdisciplinary collaboration. The Internation project shows his willingness to take philosophy seriously as political instrument. London-based — geographically proximate to Amber.
