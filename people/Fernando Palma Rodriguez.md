# Fernando Palma Rodríguez

Mexican artist working at the intersection of **robotics, cybernetics, and pre-Columbian cosmologies**. Builds kinetic/robotic sculptures that embed Nahuatl/indigenous worldviews into mechanical systems — questioning what "intelligence" and "agency" mean through non-Western frameworks. Part of MUAC's permanent collection.

## Why Interesting
Rare artist who seriously combines machine systems with indigenous knowledge frameworks. Relevant to Amber's more-than-human interests and cosmotechnics thread. The question of what machines "are" from a Nahuatl cosmological standpoint is genuinely different from Western AI discourse.
