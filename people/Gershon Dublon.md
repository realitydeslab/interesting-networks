# Gershon Dublon

Artist and electrical/mechanical engineer. Co-founder of [slow immediate](https://slowimmediate.com/) with [[Xin Liu]]. MIT Media Lab Responsive Environments group alum. Believes taking things slow can make everything feel more immediate. Work explores planetary-scale sensing, sleep, dreams, and sound.

## Key Works
- *Wandering Mind* — sound experience that guides sleeping audiences; draws from 50,000+ planetary field recordings (Radio Aporee), reassembled by AI. Performed at MAXlive 2021: The Neuroverse, Ars Electronica 2020, and others.
- *Sleepwalk* (slow immediate)

## Key Communities
- [[slow immediate]] studio
- MIT Media Lab Responsive Environments group
- New York Live Arts / ONX Studio / Invisible Dog Art Center (performance venues)
- Ars Electronica

## Why Interesting
The slow immediate studio is a serious art-engineering collaborative unit. The focus on sleep, planetary sensing, and dreams bridges somaesthetics (Amber-relevant) and computational creativity. The *Wandering Mind* system demonstrates exactly the kind of AI-as-artistic-material that Amber finds interesting. Gershon's engineering credentials parallel Xin Liu's — they form a credible artist-engineer duo rare in the art world.
