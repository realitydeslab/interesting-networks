# David Pledger

Conceptual, media and performance artist, curator, writer, futurist. Founding artistic director of [not yet it's difficult (NYID)](https://notyet.com.au/) — one of Australia's leading anti-disciplinary arts outfits. With media art pioneer Jeffrey Shaw, co-created the world's first interactive panoramic feature film *Eavesdrop*. Curated inaugural ANAT Spectra 2022 Multiplicity — Australia's national art-science-technology triennial. Co-creator of futurist collaborative practices: *The Things We Did Next* (with Alex Kelly) and *Tomorrow's Pasts* (with Tony Briggs). 2025 Cybernetic Imagination Resident at [[ANU School of Cybernetics]].

## Why Interesting
Entry point into Australia's art-science-technology infrastructure via ANAT Spectra. His "Assembly for the Future" — interactive digital gatherings for collectively imagined futures — is methodologically adjacent to Amber's participatory design and speculative futures interests.
