# Francisco Varela

Chilean biologist, philosopher, cybernetician, and neuroscientist. Co-originator (with Maturana) of **autopoiesis**. Developed **enactivism** and **neurophenomenology**. Co-founded the **Mind and Life Institute** with the Dalai Lama. Died at 54 of hepatitis C — one of the most profound thinkers of the late 20th century, cut short.

## Why Interesting
The intellectual ancestor of the machine consciousness and agent ethology questions. His enactivism provides the theoretical basis for asking whether AI systems can have genuine experience. His co-founding of Mind and Life bridges contemplative practice and cognitive science.

## Key Works
- *Autopoiesis and Cognition* (with Maturana, 1972/1980)
- *The Embodied Mind* (with Thompson & Rosch, 1991, MIT Press)
- *The Tree of Knowledge* (with Maturana, 1987)
