# Peggy Weil

Digital media artist who co-created *Gone Gitmo* (2007) with Nonny de la Peña — one of the first VR journalism experiences, built in Second Life as a virtual Guantanamo Bay detention center. Co-authored the foundational "Immersive Journalism" paper (2010) in *Presence: Teleoperators and Virtual Environments* with de la Peña and European VR presence researchers (Slater, Llobera, Spanlang, Sanchez-Vives). USC connection.

## Why Interesting
Bridging digital art and journalism ethics through virtual environments. One of the earliest practitioners of VR for social justice. Part of the founding moment of immersive journalism.
