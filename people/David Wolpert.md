# David Wolpert

Santa Fe Institute researcher working at the intersection of thermodynamics, information theory, and intelligence. Multiple FQXi grants (2013, 2016, 2019): "A Semantic Information-Theory Model of Reality," "Observers as self-maintaining non-equilibrium systems," "The role of constraints in the thermodynamics of intelligence." One of the most interesting figures bridging physics-of-information with theories of intelligence and life.
