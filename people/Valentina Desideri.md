# Valentina Desideri

Italian artist and therapist. Co-created [[Sensing Salon]] and *Poethical Readings* with [[Denise Ferreira da Silva]]. Developer of *Political Therapy* — a practice that works at the intersection of therapeutic techniques and political thought. Key practitioner in the overlap of healing arts, feminist philosophy, and collaborative art.

## Key Works
- **[Sensing Salon](https://www.thesensingsalon.org/)** (ongoing, with Denise Ferreira da Silva) — Studio practice expanding art beyond objects to healing arts. Formats: study groups, public symposia, private sessions. Uses tarot, astrology, reiki, Political Therapy. Venues: Vancouver, Lisbon, Hamburg, Paris, London, Saint Erme.
- **Poethical Readings** (with Denise Ferreira da Silva) — Performance/lecture format drawing from Joan Retallack's poethics.
- **Political Therapy** — Desideri's signature practice; uses the structure of therapy for political thinking and collective care.
- **[DESACTA](https://savvy-contemporary.com/en/events/2025/desacta/)** (Savvy Contemporary, Berlin, 2025) — Counter-spells against the Berlin Conference; rituals + exhibition format with da Silva and others.
- **Fake Therapy** — Earlier performative practice treating philosophy as therapy and therapy as fake.

## Why Interesting
Desideri represents a distinctive mode of feminist collaborative practice that treats healing arts (tarot, therapy, reiki) as philosophically serious — not as alternative wellness but as rigorous epistemological tools. Her Political Therapy practice is a genuine innovation in how collective thinking can be structured. The Sensing Salon model (invite-only study groups + public symposium) is one of the best current models for building intellectual community outside the conference form. Relevant to Amber's interest in collaborative practice formats and feminist art-science.
