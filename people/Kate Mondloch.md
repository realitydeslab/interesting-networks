# Kate Mondloch

Scholar of new media art and digital culture at the University of Oregon. On the advisory board of [[Joanna Zylinska]]'s MEDIA:ART:WRITE:NOW series ([[Open Humanities Press]]). Wrote the blurb for Zylinska's *AI Art* (2020): "inscribes AI art into a vibrant media ecology, while deftly engaging pressing aesthetic and socio-political concerns."
