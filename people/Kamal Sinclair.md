# Kamal Sinclair

**Director of the New Frontier Story Lab and World Building Residency at Sundance Institute.** Close collaborator of [[Shari Frilot]] — they co-select projects for the residency, which incubates immersive and interactive media works that often become New Frontier exhibition pieces.

The New Frontier Story Lab / World Building Residency was a multi-year development program for immersive storytellers. Frilot describes attending it "like going to Venice or IDFA DocLab" — as a scouting opportunity, though she also has a hand in selecting the projects.
