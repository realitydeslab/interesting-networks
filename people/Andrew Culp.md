# Andrew Culp

Political theorist and Deleuzian scholar. Author of *Dark Deleuze* (Minnesota, 2016) — a reading of Deleuze against the grain, emphasizing destruction, negativity, and anti-state politics rather than the affirmative/celebratory Deleuze. Co-hosted [The Being and Event Podcast](https://cultureandcommunication.org/BeingAndEvent/) with Alexander Galloway — nine-episode close reading of Badiou's major treatise.
