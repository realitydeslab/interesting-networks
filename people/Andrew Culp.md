# Andrew Culp

Political theorist and Deleuzian scholar. Author of *[Dark Deleuze](https://www.upress.umn.edu/book-division/books/dark-deleuze)* (University of Minnesota Press, 2016) — a contrarian reading of Deleuze that emphasizes destruction, negativity, and anti-state politics rather than the affirmative/"happy" Deleuzians. Faculty at Claremont Graduate University. Co-hosted [The Being and Event Podcast](http://cultureandcommunication.org/BeingAndEvent/) with [[Alexander Galloway]] — nine-episode close reading of Alain Badiou's *Being and Event* (1988), with guest interviews including Knox Peden, Nick Nesbitt, Calvin Warren, and Anna Kornbluh.
