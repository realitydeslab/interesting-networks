# Taeyoon Choi

## ⚡ Recent Updates

Korean-American artist, educator, and disability activist. Co-founded SFPC in 2013 with Zachary Lieberman, Amit Pitaru, and Jen Lowe. Known for work at the intersection of art, technology, and disability justice. Pioneer Works residency alumnus. Wrote open letter about SFPC's 2021 structural transformation. His practice engages with handmade computing, poetic code, and care ethics.
