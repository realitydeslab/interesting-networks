# Pietro Omodeo

Political epistemology scholar at Ca' Foscari University Venice. Author of *Political Epistemology: The Problem of Ideology in Science Studies* (Springer, 2019). Close collaborator with [[Matteo Pasquinelli]] — cited in AIMODELS project bibliography, co-presented at Ca' Foscari Jan 2024. Also published in *Monthly Review* (Nov 2024) a review of *The Eye of the Master* titled "The Social Dialectics of AI."
