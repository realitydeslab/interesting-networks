# Laura Tripaldi

Materials scientist and writer. Author of [*Parallel Minds: Discovering the Intelligence of Materials*](https://www.urbanomic.com/product/parallel-minds/) (Urbanomic, 2022). Proposes that materials — polymers, ceramics, textiles — have their own forms of intelligence and agency. A materialist, posthumanist account of non-human cognition grounded in actual material science.
