# Tomasz Hollanek

Researcher at the Leverhulme Centre for the Future of Intelligence (LCFI), Cambridge. Formerly Research Assistant on the Global AI Narratives (GAIN) project led by [[Kanta Dihal]] and [[Stephen Cave]]. Now works on AI and digital death, including the "deadbots" paper (alongside [[Katarzyna Nowaczyk-Basinska]]). Also contributing to the HEAT EU AI Act toolkit project ([[Eleanor Drage]]).
