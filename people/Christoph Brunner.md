# Christoph Brunner

Media theorist and researcher-creator at Concordia University, Montreal. Long-time collaborator with [[SenseLab]]. Bridges affect theory, media theory, and collective practice.

**Key role in SenseLab:** Editor of *Inflexions* (issues 3, 4, 10). Issue 4 (2010): "Transversal Fields of Experience"; Issue 10 (2017): "Modes of Exhaustion."

Specializes in **collective individuation** (drawing from Simondon), the politics of perception, and art as research. His work bridges the SenseLab's process-philosophy orientation and European media theory traditions.
