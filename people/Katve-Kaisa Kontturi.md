# Katve-Kaisa Kontturi

Finnish art researcher. Writes on the agency of materials in artistic practice, collaboration, and materialist art theory. Part of the [[SenseLab]] network.

**Key publication:** [*Ways of Following: Art, Materiality, Collaboration*](http://www.openhumanitiespress.org/books/titles/ways-of-following) (Open Humanities Press / Immediations series, 2018). One of the Immediations series monographs.

Her work sits at the intersection of new materialism and process philosophy — asking how materials participate in the making of art, not merely as passive media but as active agents.
