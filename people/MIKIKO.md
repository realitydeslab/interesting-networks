# MIKIKO (Mizuno Mikiko) MIKIKOさん

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note via Daito Manabe deep research.

Choreographer and director. Founder of [ELEVENPLAY](https://www.elevenplay.net/) — the female-only dance company that has been [[Daito Manabe]] / Rhizomatiks' primary dance collaborator since c. 2010. She met Manabe through the Dumb Type artist collective. It was MIKIKO who initiated the Perfume technology collaboration by asking Rhizomatiks: "For Perfume's next gig, I want to do something that uses technology." That single request became one of the most significant art-technology partnerships in contemporary performance.

She is the human movement and choreography expert in the Rhizomatiks ecosystem; Manabe provides the technology. Together they created drone+dancer works, ML+dancer works (*discrete figures*), and the landmark Olympic ceremonies presentation.

## Key Works (with Rhizomatiks)
- **Perfume** — ongoing collaboration (from c. 2010)
- **24drones** (2015) — drones dancing alongside ELEVENPLAY dancers
- **YASKAWA × Rhizomatiks × ELEVENPLAY** (2015) — industrial robot arm + dance
- **Nosaj Thing "Cold Stares"** (2016) — AR + dancers + drones
- **[discrete figures](https://research.rhizomatiks.com/s/works/discrete_figures/en/)** (2018) — with Kyle McDonald + ELEVENPLAY; ML-generated dance touring internationally
- **Rio Olympics 2016 AR ceremony** — dancers in the performance
- **Rhizomatiks × ELEVENPLAY "multiplex"** (2023) — Ars Electronica Honorary Mention
- **+1+1+1+** (2025) — Shanghai International Arts Festival

## Why Interesting
MIKIKO is the often-unacknowledged creative driver in the Rhizomatiks aesthetic. In most coverage, Manabe gets the technological credit; MIKIKO and ELEVENPLAY are the human embodiment that makes the technology meaningful. The question "what happens when dancers encounter machines?" requires a choreographer to explore — she's the one making that exploration.
