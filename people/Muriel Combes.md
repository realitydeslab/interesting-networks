# Muriel Combes

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during [[Gilbert Simondon]] deep research.

French philosopher at Université Aix-Marseille. Leading Simondon scholar. Author of *Gilbert Simondon and the Philosophy of the Transindividual* ([MIT Press, 2013](https://mitpress.mit.edu/); originally *Simondon, Individu et collectivité*, PUF, 1999, translated by Thomas Lamarre). Also works on biopolitics and feminist theory (*La vie inséparée. Vie et sujet au temps de la biopolitique*, Dittmar, 2011).

## Domains
- Simondon studies and transindividuality
- Biopolitics and life
- Feminist philosophy
- Continental philosophy

## Key Works
- *[Gilbert Simondon and the Philosophy of the Transindividual](https://mitpress.mit.edu/books/gilbert-simondon-and-philosophy-transindividual)* (MIT Press, 2013) — standard English-language introduction to the transindividual dimension of Simondon's thought
- *La vie inséparée. Vie et sujet au temps de la biopolitique* (Dittmar, 2011)

## Why Interesting
The most accessible entry point into Simondon's transindividual concept. Bridges Simondon to biopolitics and feminist theory — exactly the intersection that connects philosophy-of-technology to more-than-human and posthuman theory. MIT Press publication gives her work wide reach.
