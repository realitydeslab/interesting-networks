# Stephanie Dinkins

Transmedia artist and professor at Stony Brook University. Creates AI systems rooted in Black community oral histories and explorations of belonging, aging, and futurity. Visited SAIC Visiting Artists Program 2023–24. Work bridges AI ethics, community-centered design, and speculative art practice from a Black feminist standpoint.

## Key Work
- **[Not The Only One (N'TOO)](https://www.stephaniedinkins.com/ntoo.html)** — AI entity trained on a Black American family's oral histories. A conversational AI that holds intergenerational memory.
- **Bina48** — ongoing conversation/collaboration with Bina48, a social robot.
- [*Conversations with Bina48*](https://www.stephaniedinkins.com/conversations-with-bina48.html) — video series.

## Why Interesting
Critical AI from artistic practice perspective. Not academic ethics — actually builds AI systems that embody alternative values. Black feminist standpoint epistemology applied to machine learning. Machine behavior as community practice. Directly relevant to Amber's agent ethology + more-than-human design interests.
