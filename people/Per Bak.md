# Per Bak

Danish theoretical physicist who invented **self-organized criticality (SOC)** — the theory that many complex systems in nature spontaneously evolve to a critical state between order and chaos, producing power-law distributions (the "1/f noise" pattern). Author of *How Nature Works: The Science of Self-Organized Criticality* (1996). Key intellectual parallel to Stuart Kauffman: where Kauffman called it "edge of chaos," Bak called it SOC. The two frameworks are related but distinct.
