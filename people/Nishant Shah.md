# Nishant Shah

Digital cultures scholar, now based at the [Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/). Previously co-founder and director of [Centre for Internet and Society](https://cis-india.org/) (CIS), Bangalore. Pioneer of internet governance and digital rights research in India. ORCID: [0000-0002-3451-2368](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-3451-2368). Close collaborator with Maya Indira Ganesh: doctoral referee for her Leuphana dissertation; co-authored "Between memory and storage" in *Forces of Art* (Valiz, 2020); co-investigator on ArCH: AI for Cultural Heritage project (with Yale-MacMillan Grant, 2025–2026).
