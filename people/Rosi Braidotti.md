# Rosi Braidotti

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Karen Barad deep research.

Feminist philosopher of posthumanism and new materialism. Author of *Nomadic Subjects* (1994), *Metamorphoses* (2002), and *The Posthuman* (2013). Founding director of the [Centre for the Humanities at Utrecht](https://www.uu.nl/en/humanities/centre-for-the-humanities). Foucault-trained; develops a Deleuzian feminist posthumanism that is more affirmative than Barad's quantum-inflected version. One of the two major architects of European feminist posthumanism (alongside [[Karen Barad]]). Co-founded the European network for feminist philosophy. New book *Posthuman Convergences* (2025) extends the project.

## Why Interesting
Major node in the European feminist posthumanism network. Her Deleuzian approach to posthumanism complements Barad's quantum approach. The Utrecht Centre for the Humanities is an important institutional node. Her *The Posthuman* (2013) is the most cited entry point into posthuman studies for most readers — more accessible than Barad's dense quantum engagements. For Amber: provides a different angle on posthumanism that is less physics-heavy and more politically explicit.
