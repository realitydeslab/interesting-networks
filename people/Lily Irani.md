# Lily Irani

Associate Professor of Communication and Science Studies at UC San Diego. Critical tech scholar focused on labor, political economy of AI, and the politics of design. Author of *Chasing Innovation: Making Entrepreneurial Citizens in Modern India* (Princeton UP, 2019). Co-creator of [Turkopticon](https://turkopticon.net/) — a tool that lets Amazon Mechanical Turk workers rate employers, turning the surveillance gaze back around.

## ⚡ Recent Updates

## Why Interesting
One of the sharpest critics of "entrepreneurialism" as an ideology. *Chasing Innovation* examines how tech startup culture becomes colonial — Indian engineers interpellated into Western entrepreneurial citizenship. Also a serious design researcher who makes actual tools (Turkopticon). The Aarhus 2025 "Tech Lash to Tech Fash" workshop she co-organized named something important: the shift from liberal tech criticism to organizing against techno-fascism.
