# Yvonne Rogers

Director of [[UCL Interaction Centre]] and Professor of Interaction Design. Co-author of *Interaction Design: Beyond Human-Computer Interaction* (Wiley, 6th ed. 2023) — the most widely used HCI textbook in the world. Coined "research in the wild" methodology, which transformed how HCI studies technology deployment. CHI Academy member (HCI's highest honour). Most cited HCI researcher in UK.
