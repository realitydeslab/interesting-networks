# Cuauhtémoc Medina

Mexican curator, art critic, and historian. Senior researcher at UNAM (Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México). Chief Curator of **MUAC** (Museo Universitario Arte Contemporáneo), one of Mexico's most significant contemporary art museums. One of the most intellectually powerful voices in global contemporary curating, known for his rigorously political and postcolonial approach. Chief curator of the **12th Shanghai Biennale** "Proregress — Art in an Age of Historical Ambivalence" (2018–2019) — his title invoking e.e. cummings' portmanteau to name modernity's dialectical structure (neither pure progress nor pure regression). A key figure in thinking about how contemporary art navigates the contradictions of capitalist development in the Global South. Co-curated the Mexican Pavilion at Venice Biennale (2001). His Shanghai Biennale was notable for its political sharpness within the constraints of the Chinese institutional context.
