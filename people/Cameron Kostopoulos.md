# Cameron Kostopoulos

Immersive creator specializing in haptic VR and deeply transformative bodily experiences. Founder & CEO of Kost (storytelling collective). Won **Agog Immersive Impact Award** at SXSW 2025 for "In the Current of Being" (haptic VR about conversion therapy survivor Carolyn Mercer). Earlier *Body of Mine* won Producers Guild of America Innovation Award + SXSW Special Jury Award + BAFTA Student Award. ONX member.

## Domains
- Haptic VR and somatic storytelling
- LGBTQ+ and social justice through immersive media
- Embodied empathy experiences

## Key Works
- *In the Current of Being* (2025) — haptic VR experience, survivor of electroshock conversion therapy. Wearable haptic vests/gloves transmit heartbeat, breath, tremors. **Won Agog Immersive Impact Award, SXSW 2025**. World Premiere at SXSW XR Experience Competition.
- *Body of Mine* — VR experience about body dysmorphia/gender identity. PGA Innovation Award, SXSW Special Jury Award, BAFTA Student Award, Best XR at Games for Change.
