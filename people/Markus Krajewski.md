# Markus Krajewski

German media historian and cultural techniques theorist. Professor at Universität Hamburg. Develops cultural techniques of service, bureaucracy, lists, and paper. Author of *Paper Machines* (MIT Press, 2011) on the file card as a medium. Close to the [[IKKM Weimar]] and [[Bernhard Siegert]].

## Key Works
- [*Zettelwirtschaft: Die Geburt der Kartei aus dem Geiste der Bibliothek*](https://www.kadmos.de/) (Kadmos, 2002)
- [*Paper Machines: About Cards and Catalogs, 1548–1929*](https://mitpress.mit.edu/) (MIT Press, 2011), trans. Peter Krapp
- [*The Plumber: A Poetics of Technical Services*](https://mitpress.mit.edu/) (MIT Press, 2019)
- "The Power of Small Gestures: On the Cultural Technique of Service," *Theory, Culture & Society* 30:6 (2013)
- Co-editor, *Das Recht und seine Mittel* (posthumous collected writings of [[Cornelia Vismann]], 2012)

## Key Relationships

## Why Interesting
*Paper Machines* is essential reading for anyone thinking about how knowledge management infrastructure shapes thought — directly relevant to AI knowledge graphs, RAG systems, and the material history of information. His work on servitude and "the plumber" as cultural technique offers a non-heroic history of infrastructure.
