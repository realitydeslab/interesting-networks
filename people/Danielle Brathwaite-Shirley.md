# Danielle Brathwaite-Shirley

British-Jamaican artist working primarily in game-based digital installations, sound, performance, and video. Creates immersive game environments exploring and archiving Black trans lives. Her work requires audience participation in decisions about trans survival and preservation. Known for large-scale installation works at major institutions.
