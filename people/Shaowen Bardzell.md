# Shaowen Bardzell

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created. Core panelist at [[Halfway to the Future]] 2024 (reflecting on "Feminist HCI: Taking Stock and Outlining an Agenda for Design", CHI 2010).

Professor at University of Michigan School of Information. Co-founder (with Jeffrey Bardzell) of feminist HCI as an explicit research agenda within the ACM CHI community. Her 2010 CHI paper "Feminist HCI" is the landmark text that legitimized feminist theory as a design methodology in computing.

## Why Interesting
Feminist HCI methodology is directly relevant to Amber's intersectional, critical approach to design research. Bardzell's work on making feminist theory actionable for designers (not just critical) is a practical resource. She represents the critical design strand within mainstream HCI — the insider doing outsider work.

## Key Works
- ["Feminist HCI: Taking Stock and Outlining an Agenda for Design"](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/1753326.1753521) (CHI 2010) — the foundational paper
- *Humanistic HCI* (2016, with Jeffrey Bardzell)
- *Crazy Mad Love* — research on design and intimacy
