# Joshua M. Epstein

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-11:** Stub created during CSH deep research.

**Pioneer of agent-based modeling.** Professor of Epidemiology, NYU. Affiliated faculty at Courant Institute and Wolff Department of Politics. Founding Director of NYU Agent-Based Modeling Laboratory. External Faculty Fellow at Santa Fe Institute. CSH External Faculty since 2025.

## Background
PhD MIT. Earlier at Brookings Institution and Johns Hopkins. Developed the method of agent-based modeling for which he received NIH Director's Pioneer Award and Honorary Doctorate from Amherst College. His *Growing Artificial Societies* (with Axtell, 1996) is a foundational text.

## Key Works
- *Growing Artificial Societies: Social Science from the Bottom Up* (MIT Press, 1996, with Robert Axtell) — foundational ABM text
- *Generative Social Science* (Princeton University Press)
- *Agent_Zero: Toward Neurocognitive Foundations for Generative Social Science* (Princeton University Press) — combines affective, deliberative, and social modules
- *Nonlinear Dynamics, Mathematical Biology, and Social Science* (Wiley)
