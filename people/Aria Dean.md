# Aria Dean

American artist, critic, and curator. Former Curator and Editor at [[Rhizome]] (Sept 2016–Jan 2021). Now independent artist represented by Greene Naftali Gallery, NYC. Critical writer on the intersection of Blackness, technology, and media culture — her essay "Notes on Blacceleration" (e-flux, 2017) is widely cited. Published *Bad Infinity* (MIT Press/Sternberg, 2023), collecting her critical writings.

## Background

## Why Interesting
Dean represents the intersection of rigorous critical theory, Black studies, and digital culture — a combination that's rare and important. Her writing on "Blacceleration" and post-internet culture frames technology not as neutral infrastructure but as racially encoded. At Rhizome she brought this critical perspective to net art curation. *Bad Infinity* is the collected evidence of what this looks like as sustained intellectual work. Directly relevant to Amber's interest in whose behaviors AI systems model, what cultural logics are embedded in digital systems, and who is or isn't centered in tech discourse.
