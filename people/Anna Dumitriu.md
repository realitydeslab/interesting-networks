# Anna Dumitriu

UK-based bioartist (born Brighton, 1969) working at the intersection of microbiology, infectious disease, robotics, and artificial life. Artist in Residence at Oxford (Modernising Medical Microbiology group). One of the most established UK practitioners of bio art.

## Domains
- Bio Art (bacteria, living organisms, biological processes as artistic medium)
- Microbiology + infectious disease art
- Robotics + artificial life (ArchaeaBot project)
- Art-science ethics

## Key Works
- *The Romantic Disease* — tuberculosis materials, historical and contemporary microbiology
- *Make Do and Mend* — CRISPR-edited textiles
- *ArchaeaBot: A Post Singularity and Post Climate Change Life-form* — robotics + extremophile bacteria
- *Pneumothorax Machine* — TB treatment history made material
