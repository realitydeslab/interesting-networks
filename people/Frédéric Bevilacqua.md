# Frédéric Bevilacqua

Head of the [Sound Music Movement Interaction (IRCAM)](http://ismm.ircam.fr) team at IRCAM, Paris (CNRS UMR 9912 / Sorbonne Université). Co-founder and long-standing steering committee member of **[[MOCO (Movement and Computing)]]**.

Research: gesture control of digital media, sound/music/movement interaction, gesture modeling and recognition, interactive machine learning, sensorimotor learning via auditory feedback.

Background: Started in biomedical optics at EPFL and UC Irvine before pivoting to music + movement at IRCAM. Unusual trajectory — brings quantitative rigor to embodied interaction.

## Key Works / Projects
- [ELEMENT project](https://element-project.ircam.fr/) — Enabling Learnability in Movement Interaction (ANR-funded)
- [LEGOS project](http://legos.ircam.fr/) — Sensorimotor learning in gesture-sound interactive systems
- [Interlude project](http://interlude.ircam.fr/) — won Prix ANR du Numérique 2013
