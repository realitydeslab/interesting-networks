# Bernard Stiegler

French philosopher of technology. One of the most influential European philosophers of the 21st century. Doctoral student of Derrida. Founded IRI at Centre Pompidou.

## Key Works
- *Technics and Time, 1: The Fault of Epimetheus* (1994/1998)
- *Technics and Time* series (3 volumes)
- Founded Ars Industrialis (2005) — political/cultural group on technology
- Founded pharmakon.fr philosophy school

## Key Concepts
- **Pharmakon** — technology is simultaneously poison and cure
- **Tertiary retention** — technology as externalized memory
- Technics as constitutive of the human (not merely instrumental)
- Symbolic misery — mass exclusion from cultural production

## Connections
- [[Yuk Hui]] — doctoral student (key intellectual heir)
- Jacques Derrida — doctoral advisor
- [[Gilbert Simondon]] — major influence
- Centre Pompidou / IRI — institutional base

## Why Interesting
The Technics and Time series is essential reading for anyone thinking about technology philosophically. Stiegler → Yuk Hui is a direct intellectual lineage. His concept of pharmakon (technology as simultaneously poison and cure) is foundational for critical tech thinking. Tragically died 2020 but his influence is growing.
