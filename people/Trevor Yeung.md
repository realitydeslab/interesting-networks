# Trevor Yeung

Hong Kong artist working with aquatic ecosystems, botany, and ecological installation. Represented Hong Kong at the **60th Venice Biennale** (2024) with *Courtyard of Attachments* (curated by Olivia Chow). The presentation — fishless aquariums exploring water agency, attachment, and care — was one of the most discussed national pavilions of the year. Return exhibition at M+: *Courtyard of Detachments*. Grows up in Yuen Long; research through pet shops and aquatic ecology. Practice explores attachment, absence, memory, and the relational ecology of living organisms. Strong alignment with post-human ecological aesthetics.
