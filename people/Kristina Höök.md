# Kristina Höök

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Lian Loke deep research.

Professor at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. The central figure in **soma design** — the field of designing for and from bodily experience. Her book *[Designing with the Body: Somaesthetic Interaction Design](https://mitpress.mit.edu/books/designing-body)* (MIT Press, 2018) is the canonical text. Somaesthetic interaction design is deeply influenced by philosopher Richard Shusterman's somaesthetics and philosopher Maurice Merleau-Ponty. Her research includes designing affective wearables, movement-based interaction, and practices for training somatic awareness in designers. Built a large, internationally distributed community around soma design.

## Key Works
- *[Designing with the Body: Somaesthetic Interaction Design](https://mitpress.mit.edu/books/designing-body)* (MIT Press, 2018) — the canonical soma design book.
- [**Embracing First-Person Perspectives in Soma-Based Design**](https://doi.org/10.3390/informatics5010008) (*Informatics*, 2018) — 21-author community manifesto.
- "Move to be Moved" — CHI 2016 SIG, soma design community formation.
