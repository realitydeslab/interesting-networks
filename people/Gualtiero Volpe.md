# Gualtiero Volpe

Senior researcher at [Casa Paganini – InfoMus](http://www.infomus.org/), University of Genova. Steering committee member of **[[MOCO (Movement and Computing)]]**. Hosted **MOCO'18** in Genoa.

Research: expressive movement in music and dance, affective computing, multimodal interaction, movement analysis in performance contexts. Casa Paganini is one of Europe's leading labs for music, movement, and computing.

## Key Communities
- [[MOCO (Movement and Computing)]] — steering committee, hosted MOCO'18
- [Casa Paganini – InfoMus](http://www.infomus.org/) — flagship music/movement/computing lab in Italy
- University of Genova

## Why Interesting
Casa Paganini is an internationally recognized center for research on expressive gesture in music. The lab has deep expertise in mapping movement data to affective meaning — a technically rigorous but humanistically sensitive approach.
