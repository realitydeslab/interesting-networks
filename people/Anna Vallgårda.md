# Anna Vallgårda

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created — DIS 2024 General Chair.

Associate Professor at IT University Copenhagen. Leads the Interaction Design group. Works on computational composites — artifacts that are both computational AND material, where the computational and material are inseparably entangled. DIS 2024 General Chair ("Why Design?").

## Why Interesting
Vallgårda's concept of "computational composites" and "tempos of change" as design material is a philosophically sophisticated approach to the materiality of computing. Her work asks: what *is* computation as a design material? This is adjacent to Amber's interest in computation as a cultural/material/political force.
