# Alex Poots CBE

Founding Artistic Director of [[The Shed]] (New York, 2015–). Previously: founding AD/CEO of Manchester International Festival (2005–2015) and founding AD of Park Avenue Armory (2012–2015). Builder of major new cultural institutions from scratch; known for artist-led, radically interdisciplinary commissions that refuse genre categories.

## Background
Born Edinburgh, Scottish/Irish/French parentage. Music background (trumpet, composition; City University London). Began career at Barbican Centre programming 'Inventing America' season including James Brown, Alice Walker, La Monte Young, Karlheinz Stockhausen. Commissioned interdisciplinary work for Tate Modern/Britain.

## What He Does
Commissions new works that couldn't exist without institutional infrastructure. At MIF: Gorillaz × Monkey (Damon Albarn + Jamie Hewlett + Chen Shi-Zheng), *Massive Attack v Adam Curtis*, Marina Abramović works. At The Shed: Björk's Cornucopia, *Reich Richter Pärt*, Steve McQueen's Soundtrack of America, The Looking Glass (AR).
