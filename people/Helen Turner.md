# Helen Turner

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Stub created during E-WERK Luckenwalde deep research.

British curator and Co-Artistic Director of [[E-WERK Luckenwalde]], the regenerative power station and contemporary art centre in Brandenburg. Co-founder of the E-WERK project alongside artist [[Pablo Wendel]]. Curated the inaugural *Kunststrom* exhibition (2019). Has given public lectures on "Experimenting with new prototypes for ecosystems" (medienwerk.nrw conference) — framing E-WERK as an institutional prototype for the post-growth cultural economy.

She is the programmatic intelligence behind E-WERK — Wendel brings the energy infrastructure concept, Turner brings the curatorial vision that makes the programme relevant to the international ecology-art circuit. The combination has attracted Lucia Pietroiusti, Arthur Jafa, Agnes Denes, Karrabing Film Collective, Revital Cohen & Tuur Van Balen.
