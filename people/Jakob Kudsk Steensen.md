# Jakob Kudsk Steensen

Danish artist working at the intersection of ecology, VR, and immersive installation. Known for *Berl-Berl* (2021, Halle am Berghain / LAS Art Foundation) — a 3D photogrammetry VR journey into Berlin's ancient wetland origins. Collaborated with sound artist Matt McCorkle and musician Arca. Featured in *Interspecies Future: A Primer* (2024, LAS).
