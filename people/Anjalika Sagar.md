# Anjalika Sagar

Co-founder of [[The Otolith Group]] (2002) with [[Kodwo Eshun]]. Born 1968, London. Studied Anthropology and Hindi at SOAS (1994-7). Creates film essays, installations, and curatorial projects exploring temporal anomalies, posthuman conditions, and environmental crisis.

## Key Works
- [[The Otolith Group]] works (2002-present) — Turner Prize nominated 2010

## Why Interesting
Essential creative partner in one of the most important art collectives working at intersection of moving image, post-colonial theory, and speculative thought. Two-decade collaboration with [[Kodwo Eshun]] demonstrates sustained research-based artistic practice.
