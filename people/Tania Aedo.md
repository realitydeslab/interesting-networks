# Tania Aedo

**Coordinator** of [[Cátedra Max Aub]], Transdisciplina en Arte y Tecnología at UNAM — Mexico's principal institutional node for critical art-technology research and the producing organization behind **El Aleph Festival de Arte y Ciencia**.

Tania Aedo is the key figure at the nexus of art, technology, and critical/decolonial thinking in Mexican academic culture. She co-organized (with [[MUAC]] and Fundación Telefónica Movistar México) the 2024 **"Museo digital. Inteligencias y artificios"** forum, which centered **data colonialism** as a frame for thinking about AI, digital infrastructure, and museum futures in the Global South.
