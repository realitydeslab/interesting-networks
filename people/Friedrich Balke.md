# Friedrich Balke

German media theorist and literary scholar at Ruhr University Bochum. Works on sovereignty, media aesthetics, and literary theory. Part of the same German post-structuralist theory generation as [[Joseph Vogl]] and [[Bernhard Siegert]]. Co-edited the major German Deleuze anthology *Gilles Deleuze: Fluchtlinien der Philosophie* (Fink, 1996) with Joseph Vogl — a foundational text for bringing Deleuzian thought into German theoretical discourse.

## Domains
- Sovereignty and political philosophy (Schmitt, Foucault, Agamben)
- Literary theory and media aesthetics
- Deleuze and post-structuralism in German context
- Media of the political

## Key Works
- (Ed. with Joseph Vogl): *Gilles Deleuze: Fluchtlinien der Philosophie* (Fink, 1996)
- *Der Staat nach seinem Ende: Die Versuchung Carl Schmitts* (Fink, 1996)
- *Figuren der Souveränität* (Fink, 2009)

## Key Relationships

- **[[Bernhard Siegert]]** — same German media theory generation
