# Sarah Schulman

Novelist, playwright, screenwriter, activist, and co-founder of [[MIX NYC]] (1987) and the [ACT UP Oral History Project](http://www.actuporalhistory.org/). Key figure in lesbian feminist writing and AIDS activism. Author of *The Gentrification of the Mind* (2012) and *Let the Record Show: A Political History of ACT UP New York* (2021).
