# Eleanor Rosch

Cognitive psychologist at UC Berkeley (b. 1938). Revolutionized psychology with **prototype theory** of categorization — showing that categories are organized around best examples ("prototypes"), not necessary/sufficient conditions. Later co-authored *The Embodied Mind* (1991) with Varela and Thompson, bringing Buddhist philosophy into cognitive science. Her empirical psychology grounded the more philosophical enactivist claims.
