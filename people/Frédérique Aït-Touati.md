# Frédérique Aït-Touati

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Bruno Latour deep research.

French theater director, historian of science, and researcher at CNRS / Centre Alexandre Koyré. Director at the [Centre Dramatique National de Thionville](https://www.centredramatique.org/). Long-time collaborator of Bruno Latour at Sciences Po SPEAP (School of Arts and Politics). Co-created theatrical "lecture-performances" and pieces with Latour exploring the earthly condition — including *Inside* (2016) and others. A key figure in the translation of Latour's philosophical ideas into performance and artistic practice.

Key connection: Frédérique is the transmitter of Latourian thought into art/theater — she bridges the academic and artistic worlds in a way that is directly relevant to Amber's practice.
