# Mary Maggic

Bioartist working on hormones, estrogen, bodies, and environmental endocrine disruption as art medium. Chapter "We're all living in an Estroworld" in *Routledge Handbook of Art, Science, and Technology Studies* (Routledge, 2022). Works at intersection of bioart, feminist science studies, and environmental politics of hormonal chemicals.

## Domains
- Hormonal bioart (estrogen, environmental estrogens)
- Feminist science studies through practice
- DIY biology
- Environmental endocrine disruption

## Why Interesting
Works on biochemical / more-than-human entanglement at a level Amber hasn't explored. Estrogen-as-medium opens weird and rigorous territory about bodies, chemicals, and non-human relations. The "Estroworld" framing suggests a speculative/environmental approach.
