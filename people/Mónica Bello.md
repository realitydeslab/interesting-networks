# Mónica Bello

**Former Head of Arts at CERN** (Curator and Head) — served as the programme's founding/building director for many years, departing ~2024. Spanish curator. Under her leadership, Arts at CERN scaled from a small residency to an internationally recognised platform with multiple programmes (Collide, Connect, Simetría, Accelerate, Guest Artist). Editor of ***In the Spaces Between*** (dpr-barcelona, 2025) — the first major book dedicated to Arts at CERN. The book's contributors include Fabiola Gianotti (CERN DG), Ryoji Ikeda, Yunchul Kim, Joan Heemskerk, Raqs Media Collective, Rasheedah Phillips, and 40+ others.

Key architect of the Collide partnership model (Ars Electronica, FACT Liverpool, Barcelona, Copenhagen) and the Connect/Simetría cross-continental science partnerships.
