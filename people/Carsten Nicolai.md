# Carsten Nicolai (Alva Noto)

German artist and musician (b. 1965). As **Alva Noto** (music alias): one of the defining voices of minimalist electronic music; co-founder of **Raster-Noton** label (with Olaf Bender); long-running collaboration with Ryuichi Sakamoto (numerous albums, soundtracks). As **Carsten Nicolai** (visual art alias): works with scientific phenomena — light, sound, time, physics — as visual medium. Two works *ENDO EXO* and *PHOSPHENES* currently at M+ Hong Kong (2026), paired with Sakamoto's final album *12*. Also exhibited his work within the Ryuichi Sakamoto retrospective. Key figure in the art-music intersection (EAI / electroacoustic improvisation / visual music). Circuit: Raster-Noton, ZKM, Sonar, CTM, and major art museums.
