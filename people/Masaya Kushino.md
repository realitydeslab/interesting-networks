# Masaya Kushino

Japanese fashion designer/artist. Co-founded **AnotherFarm** (2018) with [[Sputniko!]] and Kensho Tambara — art collective exploring genetic modification, luminescent silk from GMO silkworms, and traditional Japanese craft techniques. Works at the intersection of haute couture and synthetic biology. Shown at Ars Electronica, Cooper Hewitt, Triennale di Milano.
