# Michael Anastassiades

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Anthony Dunne deep research.

Cypriot-British designer, now internationally renowned for minimalist lighting design (especially for Flos). Early career deeply intertwined with [[Dunne & Raby]] — co-created *Weeds, Aliens and Other Stories* (1994–98), *Designs for Fragile Personalities in Anxious Times* (2004/05), and *Do You Want to Replace the Existing Normal?* (2007/08). His "Huggable Atomic Mushroom" is in MoMA's permanent collection. Represents the path from critical design to commercial design success.

## Why Interesting
The Dunne & Raby collaborator who became commercially successful while maintaining a conceptual edge. His trajectory from critical/speculative design to Flos lighting is an interesting case study in how experimental design practice translates to industry.
