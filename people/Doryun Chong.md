# Doryun Chong

Chief Curator at M+ Museum, Hong Kong. Former curator at MoMA New York. Expert in post-war and contemporary East Asian art. Leads M+'s curatorial direction across all four disciplines (visual art, design & architecture, moving image, HK visual culture). Central figure in positioning M+ within global contemporary art discourse. Key institutional figure for the field.
