# Sydney Skybetter

Choreographer, researcher, and futurist at Brown University. Artist-in-residence chair at **MOCO'22** (Chicago). Works at the intersection of dance, technology, surveillance, and futures.

Known for creating work about surveillance and choreographic AI. Organizes [Conference for Research on Choreographic Interfaces](https://choreographicinterfaces.org/) (CRCI). Editor of *Dance Futures* (Intellect Books).

## Key Communities
- [[MOCO (Movement and Computing)]] — artist-in-residence chair (MOCO'22)
- Brown University
- Conference for Research on Choreographic Interfaces (CRCI)

## Why Interesting
Rare practitioner-theorist who makes dance that is explicitly *about* the conditions of movement computation. Surveillance + choreography intersection is politically sharp and underexplored. Worth following as a critical voice at the dance-tech intersection.
