# Justine Emard

French artist working with AI, robotics, and neural networks. Collaborates with AIST (Japan's National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology), particularly with humanoid robot Alter3. Key figure in French art+AI+robotics scene. Exhibited at Le Fresnoy Panorama 26 (2024). Has shown at major international institutions.

## Why Interesting
Direct peer to Amber: AI + embodied systems + artistic practice. The AIST/Alter3 collaboration is one of the most serious art-robotics partnerships internationally. Her work explores the boundary between human and machine agency/performance — directly adjacent to Amber's agent ethology and more-than-human interests.
