# Ryoji Ikeda 池田亮司

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via NTT ICC deep research.

Japanese artist (born 1966) working with sound, data, and audiovisual installation at extreme scale. Original member of [[Dumb Type]] (joined as composer 1994). Has been intensively active in sound art through concerts, installations, recordings since 1995. Collaboration with Carsten Nicolai (Berlin) as duo "cyclo." **Golden Nica, Ars Electronica 2001** (Digital Musics). Known for data-driven monochrome visual installations (*test pattern*, *spectra*, *datamatics* series) that treat data as aesthetic material. Major solo exhibitions globally. Now based in Paris.

## Key Works
- *db* (2002, NTT ICC) — solo sound installation
- *test pattern* series — barcode data rendered as audiovisual
- *datamatics* series — data materialization
- *spectra* series — light installations
- cyclo. (collaboration with Carsten Nicolai)

## Key Communities
- [[NTT ICC]] — *db* solo 2002, Sound Art exhibition 2000
- [[Ars Electronica]] — Golden Nica 2001
- Dumb Type — founding composer member

## Why Interesting
One of Japan's most internationally recognized media artists. His work rigorously explores data as aesthetic material — pure, abstract, mathematical. Directly relevant to computational creativity and the relationship between information and perception. The Dumb Type connection (Kyoto collective) links him to the foundational layer of Japanese media art.
