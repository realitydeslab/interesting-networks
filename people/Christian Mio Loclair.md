# Christian Mio Loclair

Berlin-based media artist and computer scientist working with AI, architecture, and digital space. For [[NEWNOW Festival]] (2021), built "Zeche" — a virtual digital twin of the Zollverein Mischanlage as interactive exhibition and event space, making the physical festival accessible globally online.

## Why Interesting
His "Zeche" virtual platform for NEWNOW took an industrial heritage building and made a parallel digital version — an early example of MR/XR approaches to institutional space that's conceptually adjacent to Amber's HoloKit work. The idea of a virtual version of a physical site (a UNESCO World Heritage mine) for democratic access is interesting as a model for hybrid cultural participation.
