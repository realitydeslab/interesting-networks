# Thandika Mkandawire

Malawian economist and public intellectual (1940–2020). Executive Secretary of [[CODESRIA]] 1985–1996 — the longest tenure, during which he built the institution into a globally recognized center for African social science. Subsequently Director of the [UN Research Institute for Social Development (UNRISD)](https://www.unrisd.org/) and Chair of African Development at the London School of Economics. On the board of the Social Science Research Council (SSRC). Honorary degrees from University of Helsinki, University of Ghana, and York University. Olof Palme Professor at the Swedish Institute for Future Studies.

Research focused on development theory, social policy, and African political economy. Key text: *Social Policy in a Development Context* (ed., 2004, Palgrave). Major voice arguing that social policy in Africa should be universal rather than targeted. Bridged Marxist political economy with pragmatic development economics.
