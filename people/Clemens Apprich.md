# Clemens Apprich

Media theorist and historian at [University of Applied Arts Vienna](https://www.dieangewandte.at/). Affiliated external faculty at [[Digital Democracies Institute]] (SFU). Co-author of *[Pattern Discrimination](https://meson.press/books/pattern-discrimination/)* (meson press / Univ of Minnesota Press, 2019) with [[Wendy Chun]], [[Hito Steyerl]], and Florian Cramer. Author of *[Technotopia: A Media Genealogy of Net Cultures](https://rowman.com/ISBN/9781783485055/Technotopia-A-Media-Genealogy-of-Net-Cultures)* (Rowman & Littlefield International, 2017). Co-founding editor of *[spheres — Journal for Digital Cultures](http://www.spheres-journal.org/)*. Background: philosophy, political science, cultural history/theory (Berlin, Bordeaux, Vienna). Previous positions: Centre for Digital Cultures, Leuphana (research associate + guest professor); visiting fellow at Mel Hoppenheim School of Cinema, Concordia; assistant professor at U Groningen. Research: filter algorithms, ML methods, data analysis.
