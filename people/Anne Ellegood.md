# Anne Ellegood

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Stub created during ICA LA deep research.

American curator and museum director. Executive Director of [[ICA LA]] since 2019. Previously Senior Curator at [[Hammer Museum]] (2009–2019), where she co-organized the *Made in L.A.* biennials (2012, 2018) and curated the Jimmie Durham retrospective (2017). Career also includes New Museum (curator), Hirshhorn Museum (curator 2005–2009). Education: Women's Studies (University of Colorado Boulder), Curatorial Studies (Bard College CCS, 1998). Mentor: Marcia Tucker (New Museum founder). Politically engaged curatorial vision — feminist, decolonial, artist-first.

## Why Interesting
Serious curator with track record of championing underrepresented artists at the LA level. ICA LA under her leadership has sharpened its identity as a politically engaged non-collecting institution. Low direct relevance to Amber's tech/speculative domain.
