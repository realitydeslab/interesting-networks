# Nica Ross

Artist, cultural producer, and educator. Director of the [[Frank-Ratchye STUDIO for Creative Inquiry]] (2023–present) and Associate Professor of Video and Media Design at CMU's School of Drama. Work challenges normative ideologies reinforced by technology, performance, and play — via video installation, performance, "gayming," and sport. Based in Pittsburgh; from Brooklyn/SF/Tempe.

## Background
- B.A. Cinema, San Francisco State University
- M.F.A. Advanced Photographic Study, ICP/Bard College program
- 15+ years in video, theater, and event production

## Why Interesting
As current director of the STUDIO, Nica is the key contact for visiting artist residencies and institutional collaboration. Their practice is explicitly invested in technology critique and queer/BIPOC representation — different in flavor from the Levin-era computational art focus, signaling the STUDIO's evolution toward broader social/political practice.
