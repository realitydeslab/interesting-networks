# Karen Barad

Physicist-philosopher. Developed "agential realism" extending Donna Haraway's situated knowledges. Key figure in new materialism and posthuman theory.

## Key Works
- *Meeting the Universe Halfway: Quantum Physics and the Entanglement of Matter and Meaning* (2007) — foundational text in new materialism
- "Posthumanist Performativity: Toward an Understanding of How Matter Comes to Matter" (1999)

## Key Concepts
- Agential realism — matter and discourse co-emerge
- Intra-action — relationships within phenomena, not between separate entities
- Onto-epistem-ology — being and knowing as inseparable

## Connections
- [[Donna Haraway]] — UC Santa Cruz colleague, intellectual kinship
- UC Santa Cruz History of Consciousness program
- New materialism network

## Why Interesting
Major theorist bridging quantum physics and feminist theory. Her "agential realism" provides philosophical foundation for much posthuman design thinking. Regular reference in more-than-human design literature.
