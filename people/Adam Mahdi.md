## Background

Adam has an eclectic research trajectory: started in mathematical modelling of biological systems (cerebral autoregulation, epidemiology), moved through biomedical engineering, and transitioned to AI evaluation science and LLM research after joining OII in 2021.

**Education & career arc:**
- PhD in mathematical modelling (Oxford Engineering)
- Research Fellow, Institute of Biomedical Engineering, Oxford
- Visiting Fellow, Isaac Newton Institute for Mathematical Sciences, Cambridge
- Research Assistant Professor, North Carolina State University
- Joined OII as Departmental Research Lecturer (Oct 2021) → Associate Professor (Apr 2022)
- Director of Graduate Studies at OII (Nov 2022 - Jan 2025)
- Associate member of St Antony's College, Oxford

## Key Works

- [Clinical knowledge in LLMs does not translate to human interactions](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=clinical+knowledge+LLMs+human+interactions+Bean+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Bean, Parsons, Kirk, Mahdi et al., Apr 2025) — landmark 1,300-participant study. Nature family.
- [Measuring what Matters: Construct Validity in LLM Benchmarks](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=Measuring+Matters+Construct+Validity+LLM+Bean+Kearns+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Bean, Kearns, Mahdi et al., Nov 2025) — 29-expert systematic review of benchmark validity
- [It's a TRAP!](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=TRAP+Task+Redirecting+Agent+Persuasion+Korgul+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Korgul, Yang, Mahdi et al., Dec 2025) — adversarial web agent benchmark
- [Agentic Reinforcement Learning for Search is Unsafe](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=Agentic+Reinforcement+Learning+Search+Unsafe+Yang+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Yang, Padarha, Mahdi, Oct 2025)
- [How Does DPO Reduce Toxicity?](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=DPO+Reduce+Toxicity+Mechanistic+Yang+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Yang, Mayne, Mahdi, Nov 2024) — mechanistic neuron-level analysis
- [Sensitivity analysis methods in the biomedical sciences](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=sensitivity+analysis+biomedical+Qian+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Qian, Mahdi, 2020) — methodological foundation paper
