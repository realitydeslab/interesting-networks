# Oskar Negt

German philosopher and critical social theorist. Professor of sociology at University of Hanover (1972–2002). Studied with Theodor Adorno in Frankfurt; assistant to Jürgen Habermas. Student movement mentor (APO). Most significant intellectual collaboration: with [[Alexander Kluge]] on *Public Sphere and Experience* (1972) and *History and Obstinacy* (1981) — the most systematic extension of Frankfurt School critical theory into a Marxist account of media and proletarian experience. Died 2 February 2024, aged 89.
