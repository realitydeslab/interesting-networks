# Thomas Laurien

Artist/designer/curator, educator, researcher. Co-founder of [[Design+Posthumanism Network]] (2018). Based at HDK-Valand, University of Gothenburg. Chair of The Natureculture Association Shimmer and Entanglements. Co-founder of Species Embassy Viskan-Borås. Multispecies justice + Rights of Nature activist-researcher.

## Why Interesting
Thomas Laurien is the archetype of a designer-activist working at the intersection of posthumanism and political action. His work making water bodies, watersheds, and species politically legible (Species Embassy, Rights of Nature) is a model for how design can function as advocacy for non-human entities. The Tools & Methods session on "making absent more-than-human bodies present" is directly methodologically relevant to anyone working on how to represent non-human interests.
