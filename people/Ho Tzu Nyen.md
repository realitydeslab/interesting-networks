# Ho Tzu Nyen

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Stub created — appointed Artistic Director of 16th Gwangju Biennale (September 2026). Flagged for deep research.

Singaporean artist, filmmaker, and curator (born 1976). Appointed Artistic Director of the **16th Gwangju Biennale (September 2026)**. Works with myth, identity, history, and Southeast Asian narrative through film, video installation, and performance. Strongly influenced by Félix Guattari's schizoanalysis and ecosophy. One of the most important Asian artists working at the intersection of speculative practice, historical archive, and non-Western epistemology.

## Background
Born Singapore 1976. Has been a key figure in Southeast Asian contemporary art for two decades. Strong connections to the global biennial circuit (Venice, Shanghai, Aichi, Sharjah, Gwangju). Now stepping into a curatorial role at one of Asia's most important biennials.

## Key Works
- *HERE* (2009) — film about experimental "videocure" at psychiatric facility, inspired by Félix Guattari's anti-psychiatry work at La Borde
- *[The Critical Dictionary of Southeast Asia](https://www.criticaldictofsea.com/)* (2012–) — ongoing mapping project centering contemporary historical narrative of Southeast Asia; hundreds of entries organized by letters
- Represented Singapore at Venice Biennale 2011
- Contributed commissioned work to 13th Gwangju Biennale (2021)
