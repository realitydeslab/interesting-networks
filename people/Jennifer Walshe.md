# Jennifer Walshe

Radical Irish experimental composer and performance artist. Professor of Composition at Oxford. Works at the intersection of music, text, performance, and technology. Coined the term "Newfoundland" to describe a generation of experimental musicians using internet culture, pop, electronics, and body-based performance together. Commissioned Timothy Morton to write the libretto for her opera *Time, Time, Time* (2019). Works with Laurie Anderson, Meredith Monk, and numerous philosophers/writers.
