# Françoise Vergès

French political theorist, decolonial feminist, Frantz Fanon scholar. Member of [[Practicing Refusal Collective]]. Grew up in Réunion; father was a leading figure in the Réunion Communist Party. Her work connects Fanon's anticolonial psychiatry to contemporary racial capitalism, climate crisis, and feminist internationalism.

## Key Works
- *[A Decolonial Feminism](https://plutopressbooks.com/9780745341620/)* (Pluto Press, 2021) — critiques white Eurocentric feminism; proposes a feminism articulated from the global South and racialized communities
- *Abolir l'inhumanité* — on abolition and colonial violence
- *Un féminisme décolonial* (La Fabrique, 2019) — original French version
- *[Monsters and Revolutionaries: Colonial Family Romance and Métissage](https://www.dukeupress.edu/monsters-and-revolutionaries)* (Duke UP, 1999) — psychoanalysis + colonialism in Réunion
