# Scott Alexander

Psychiatrist and writer. Runs [Astral Codex Ten](https://www.astralcodexten.com/) (formerly Slate Star Codex), the "soft rationalism" blog that split from LessWrong in 2013 and became perhaps the most intellectually diverse part of the rationalist ecosystem. Writes about psychiatry, AI, EA, forecasting, culture, and book reviews with unusual depth.

## Domains
Psychiatry, AI, effective altruism, forecasting, cultural analysis, book reviews, political epistemology.

## Why Interesting
Scott Alexander is the rationalist writer most accessible to non-technical audiences and most open to interdisciplinary thinking. His writing bridges LW-style epistemics with broader cultural questions. A potential entry point for engaging with rationalist culture without the most techno-utopian elements. More likely than other LW figures to appreciate Amber's more-than-human framing.
