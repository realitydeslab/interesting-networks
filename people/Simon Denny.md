# Simon Denny

New Zealand-born Berlin-based artist. One of the most important figures in art addressing blockchain, platform capitalism, and Silicon Valley ideology. Co-founder of [[BPA// Berlin Program for Artists]] (with Angela Bulloch and Willem de Rooij). MFA supervisor of [[Paul Kolling]] at HFBK Hamburg. Curated *Proof of Work* at Schinkel Pavillon (2018) — the pivotal exhibition introducing blockchain art to the European contemporary art mainstream. Major exhibitions at Venice Biennale (German Pavilion 2015), Serpentine, MoMA, Petzel Gallery New York.
