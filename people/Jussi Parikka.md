# Jussi Parikka

Finnish media theorist. Pioneer of media archaeology and geology of media.

## Key Works
- *A Geology of Media* (2015) — materiality of media technologies
- *Insect Media* (2010) — nonhuman media, swarm intelligence
- *Digital Contagions* (2007) — computer viruses as cultural phenomena

## Connections
- [[Antikythera]] — affiliate researcher (2023)
- [[transmediale]] — frequent speaker/contributor
- [[Ars Electronica]] — connected
- Aarhus University — professor (connects to Amber's Aarhus interest)

## Why Interesting
"Geology of Media" is foundational for thinking about technology's material/environmental dimensions. "Insect Media" is pure more-than-human thinking applied to computation. At Aarhus — direct institutional connection to critical computing tradition Amber values.
