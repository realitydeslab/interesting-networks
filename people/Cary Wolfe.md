# Cary Wolfe

Bruce and Elizabeth Dunlevie Professor of English at Rice University. Key posthumanist thinker bridging Luhmann's systems theory, animal studies, and posthumanism. *What is Posthumanism?* (2010, Minnesota UP) is a canonical text. Edits the "Posthumanities" series at University of Minnesota Press.
