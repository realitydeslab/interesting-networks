# Primavera De Filippi

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Summer of Protocols deep research.

Legal scholar and artist at Harvard University. Research at intersection of art, law, and technology — specifically blockchain's legal and political implications, and AI governance. Artistic practice: creates blockchain-based lifeforms that evolve and reproduce as people feed them cryptocurrencies. CNRS researcher in Paris. SoP24 alum and SoP25 Teaching Fellow.

## Why Interesting
Rare person who bridges legal scholarship and artistic practice through blockchain. Blockchain-based lifeforms as art objects are genuinely weird and interesting. Her "Plantoid" project (blockchain creatures that commission their own reproduction) is a landmark work in protocol art. Amber's work on protocols in XR/art contexts would resonate with De Filippi's practice.
