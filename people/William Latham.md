# William Latham

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created from Generative Art deep research.

Pioneer of evolutionary generative art. Developed MUTATOR system at IBM UK Scientific Research Centre in the late 1980s–90s — used genetic algorithms to evolve 3D organic forms with aesthetic fitness functions. One of the earliest examples of evolutionary art at the intersection of ALife and generative practice. Now Professor at Goldsmiths, University of London (Computer Science).
