# Joke Brouwer

## ⚡ Recent Updates

Co-founder and longtime director of [[V2_Lab for the Unstable Media]], Rotterdam. Co-architect (with [[Arjen Mulder]]) of the DEAF publication series — key anthologies in media art theory including *The Art of the Accident* (1998), *Machine Times* (2000), and *Interact or Die!* (2007).

## Domains
- Media art curating and theory
- Critical philosophy of technology

## Why Interesting
Intellectual architect of DEAF's thematic agenda. Her curatorial framing of "unstable media" — embracing instability, emergence, failure, and process as artistic substance — maps directly onto Amber's territory (agents as unstable behavioral systems, ALife as productive instability). If DEAF revives, she would be a key figure. Her book series remain foundational reading for V2_ community membership.
