# Matteo Pasquinelli

Italian philosopher and media theorist. Author of ***The Eye of the Master: A Social History of Artificial Intelligence*** (Verso, 2023) — arguably the most rigorous Marxist-materialist account of machine intelligence currently available. Former HfG Karlsruhe professor of Media Philosophy. ERC Consolidator Grant 2022.

## Why Interesting
Working on AI from a labor theory / political economy angle — very different from mainstream ML criticism. *The Eye of the Master* traces how AI emerged from industrial measurement and labor abstraction. Essential reading for anyone doing AI-critical research. His HfG work seminared on "The Evolution of Machine Intelligence" (documented in HfG archives).
