# Memo Akten

Artist and researcher working at the intersection of AI, machine learning, embodied cognition, and consciousness. Known for large-scale immersive installations using real-time ML, deep learning, and live video synthesis. 2019 artist-in-residence at the [[Frank-Ratchye STUDIO for Creative Inquiry]] (CharRNNs, ml5.js). Professor at UC San Diego.

## Domains
- Real-time machine learning in performance/installation
- Embodied cognition and consciousness through computational lens
- AI-generated imagery and interactive systems
- Large-scale immersive video/sound installations

## Why Interesting
Akten is one of the most technically sophisticated artists working with ML as a medium. His work on embodiment and consciousness through AI resonates with Amber's interests in machine behavior and more-than-human design. His STUDIO residency produced ml5.js contributions.
