# Kabage Karanja ⭐⭐

Co-founder and director of **[[Cave_bureau]]**, a Nairobi-based architecture practice that excavates colonial geological histories and Indigenous African spatial knowledge. Co-curator (with Kathryn Yusoff, Stella Mutegi, Owen Hopkins) of **GBR: Geology of Britannic Repair**, the British Pavilion at the 19th Venice Architecture Biennale 2025, which won a Special Mention for National Participation.

Cave_bureau's work interrogates the relationship between geological extraction, colonial architecture, and African futures. Based in Nairobi, Kenya.
