# Stelarc

Cypriot-Australian performance artist exploring the body's relationship with technology. Third Hand, Extra Ear, exoskeletons, internet-actuated body. Pioneer of posthuman art practice since the 1970s.

## ⚡ Recent Updates

## Why Interesting
One of the original posthuman artists — has been augmenting and extending the body with technology for 50+ years. Third Hand (1980), Stomach Sculpture, Extra Ear (surgical implant), Prosthetic Head (AI avatar). Direct lineage to more-than-human design, embodied interaction, and art-science practice.
