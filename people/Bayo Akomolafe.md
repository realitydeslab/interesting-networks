# Bayo Akomolafe

Nigerian philosopher, author, and academic. Known for a post-anthropocentric, "slow" approach to political thought that draws on Yoruba cosmology, new materialism, and Black studies. Author of *These Wilds Beyond Our Fences: Letters to My Daughter on Humanity's Search for Home* (North Atlantic Books, 2017). Founder of the **Emergence Network** — a collective engaged in post-activist thinking and slowing down. 

Visited [[3 Ecologies Institute]] for the Ground Provisions Residency (December 2022), in conversation with [[Erin Manning]] on "para-ontological" conceptions of Blackness. Engaged [[Angélique Wilkie]]'s Dramaturgical Ecologies group.
