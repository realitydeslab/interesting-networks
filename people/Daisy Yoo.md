# Daisy Yoo

Assistant professor at TU Eindhoven's Industrial Design department. Research focuses on designing interfaces and technology to increase public awareness and participation in political and social issues. PhD work focused on designing information systems for the UN International Criminal Tribunal for Rwanda. Postdoc at Aarhus University. Co-author on [[Ron Wakkary]]'s "The Future of More-Than-Human Design" coalition paper.
