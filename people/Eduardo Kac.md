# Eduardo Kac

Brazilian-American artist, pioneer of bio art and transgenic art. Created GFP Bunny (Alba, the fluorescent rabbit), "Natural History of the Enigma" (plantimal), telepresence art. One of the founding figures of bio art as a field.
