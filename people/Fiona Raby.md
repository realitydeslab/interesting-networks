# Fiona Raby

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during RCA deep research. Returned from Parsons/The New School (2016-2025) to Distinguished Visiting Professor at China Academy of Art (2025-). New book *Not Here, Not Now* (2025).

RCA-trained (Architecture + MPhil in Computer Related Design). Founding member of CRD Research Studio at RCA. Reader in Design Interactions (2005-2015). Co-creator (with Anthony Dunne) of speculative design / critical design. Work in permanent collections at MoMA, V&A, MAK Vienna. MIT Media Lab Award (2015, inaugural). Royal Designer for Industry and Life Fellow of Royal Society (2021).

## Why Interesting
Fiona's specific background — architecture + computation + design research — is a fascinating trajectory. The CRD (Computer Related Design) research studio she co-founded at RCA was the institutional predecessor to the whole critical computing + design research field. Her return to Europe / Asia after decade in New York worth tracking.
