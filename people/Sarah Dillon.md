# Sarah Dillon

Professor of Literature and Media at the University of Cambridge. Co-editor (with [[Stephen Cave]] and [[Kanta Dihal]]) of *[AI Narratives: A History of Imaginative Thinking about Intelligent Machines](https://global.oup.com/academic/product/ai-narratives-9780198846666)* (OUP, 2020) — the foundational anthology of the AI narratives field. Brings literary studies rigor to AI ethics discourse.
