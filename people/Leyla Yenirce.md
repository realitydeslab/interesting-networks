# Leyla Yenirce

Sound and installation artist. Co-recipient of **ars viva prize 2023** alongside [[Paul Kolling]] and Shaun Motsi. Known for *Holy Water* (2023) — a spatial sound installation about Yazidi women who survived the 2014 IS genocide and the ritual of re-baptism at the holy Lalisch temple in Northern Iraq. Works at the intersection of trauma, ritual, and sonic perception.
