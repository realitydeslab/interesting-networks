# Lukáš Likavčan

Philosopher. Core researcher at [[Antikythera]]. Works on planetary politics, comparative planetology, environmental humanities.

## Key Works (prolific!)
- *Introduction to Comparative Planetology* (2019) — free book
- "Is the Solar System a Wilderness or a Construction Site?" (2025)
- "The Planetary and Its Uses" (2025)
- "Another Earth: An astronomical concept of the planet for the environmental humanities" (2024)
- "Can Artificial Neural Networks Be Normative Models of Reason?" (2023)
- Exhibitions, lectures, preprints — very active

## Why Interesting
Prolific young philosopher doing extraordinary work on "the planetary" — how to think about Earth from multiple scales simultaneously (astronomical, ecological, political). His question about the solar system as wilderness vs. construction site captures the speculative ambition of this network. Free book and preprints make his work accessible.
