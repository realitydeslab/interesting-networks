# Simone Wierød

Danish choreographer. Collaborated with [[Kimchi and Chips]] on *Collective Behaviour* (2019) — a 40-minute contemporary dance performance exploring individual vs. collective identity through mirrors, haze, and light manipulation. Premiered at KPH Volume, Copenhagen. The work asks: "Is my behaviour caused by my will? Can a collective of people exist without shared norms?"
