# Orian Barki

Israeli filmmaker and artist based in New York. Co-creator with [[Meriem Bennani]] of *2 Lizards* (2020) — the pandemic-era viral animated web series — and *Bouchra* (2024), their debut animated feature film. Uses hybrid live-action/3D animation technique with anthropomorphic animal characters to explore queer identity, diaspora, and intimacy.

## Domains

- Animated documentary / hybrid film
- Queer storytelling
- Collaboration-based practice

## Key Works

- **[*2 Lizards*](https://www.moma.org/magazine/articles/505)** (2020, with Meriem Bennani) — 8-episode Instagram/Adult Swim animated series. Viral pandemic hit. Acquired by MoMA and Whitney Museum.
- **[*Bouchra*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bouchra)** (2024, with Meriem Bennani) — Feature film. TIFF Platform 2025, NYFF Currents 2025. North American distribution: Film Movement.

## Key Relationships

- [[Meriem Bennani]] — primary collaborator

## Why Interesting

Part of the Bennani collaboration ecosystem. The *2 Lizards* → *Bouchra* trajectory shows how an Instagram experiment evolves into arthouse cinema. Her specific contribution to the Bennani partnership worth understanding — co-director credit means genuine creative parity.
