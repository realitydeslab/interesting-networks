# George Poonkhin Khut

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Lian Loke deep research.

Australian interactive artist working at the intersection of biofeedback, body awareness, and healing art. Known for participatory art installations designed for hospital/clinical environments (cardiac patients, children with anxiety). Uses heart rate, breath, and other physiological signals as interactive materials. Collaborated with Lian Loke on early work combining Feldenkrais movement reeducation with interactive art (2013), and on *Intimate Aesthetics and Facilitated Interaction* (book chapter, 2014).

## Key Works
- [**Re-sensitising the body: interactive art and the Feldenkrais method**](https://doi.org/10.1504/IJART.2013.058283) (*Int. J. Arts and Technology*, 2013) — with Lian Loke et al.
- **Intimate Aesthetics and Facilitated Interaction** — book chapter with Lian Loke, in *Interactive Experience in the Digital Age* (Springer, 2014).
- Clinical biofeedback art works at hospitals — long-term practice.

## Key Relationships
- **[[Lian Loke]]** — early collaborator on body + interactive art

## Why Interesting
Australian node of body × interactive art. His clinical/healing art practice (hospitals, cardiac patients) is a distinctive application of soma-informed design — more-than-human design in the healthcare space. Not a mainstream HCI figure but an interesting practitioner.
