# Brett Kagan

Chief Scientific Officer and co-founder of Cortical Labs. Lead researcher on the DishBrain experiment (2022), in which cultured human neurons were embodied in a simulated game environment and learned to play Pong.

## Domains
- In vitro neuronal computation
- Biological intelligence and sentience
- Embodied learning in biological systems
- Brain-computer interfaces

## Key Works
- [In vitro neurons learn and exhibit sentience when embodied in a simulated game-world](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2022.09.001) (Kagan et al., *Neuron*, 2022) — DishBrain paper; hugely influential

## Why Interesting
The DishBrain result is philosophically disruptive: it demonstrates that minimal biological systems exhibit learning when given embodied feedback — raising questions about sentience, moral status, and what "machine behavior" means when the machine is alive. Directly relevant to Amber's mortality/machine consciousness thread and ALife interests. Cortical Labs is building commercial products from this, so it's not merely speculative.
