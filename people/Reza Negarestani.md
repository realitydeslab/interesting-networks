# Reza Negarestani

Iranian philosopher. Key figure in speculative realism and accelerationism.

## Key Works
- *Cyclonopedia: Complicity with Anonymous Materials* (2008) — theory-fiction, oil and geopolitics
- *Intelligence and Spirit* (2018) — philosophy of mind, AI, rationalism
- Contributor to Collapse journal ([[Urbanomic]])
