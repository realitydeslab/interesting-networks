# Reza Negarestani

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial deep research. Comprehensive profile created.

Iranian philosopher and theorist. Pioneer of "theory-fiction" genre with *Cyclonopedia* (2008). Currently developing systematic philosophy of intelligence as alternative to philosophy of mind. Board member and Critical Philosophy Program director at **[[The New Centre for Research & Practice]]**. Intellectual trajectory from accelerationism through speculative realism to rationalist inhumanism.

## Background
Born 1977 in Shiraz, Iran. Emerged in mid-2000s as key figure in experimental philosophy/theory scene. 

**Intellectual trajectory:** Started in orbit of [[Nick Land]]'s accelerationist project → became associated with [[speculative realism]] movement (alongside [[Ray Brassier]], [[Graham Harman]]) → evolved toward rationalist universalism and "inhumanism" → currently developing systematic philosophy of intelligence.

**Current position:** Board member and director of Critical Philosophy Programme at [[The New Centre for Research & Practice]], an online "parallel academia" institution offering graduate-level seminars and certificates.

## Domains
- **Theory-fiction** — hybrid philosophical-literary works that use fictional elements to advance theoretical arguments
- **Philosophy of intelligence** — systematic alternative to philosophy of mind, focused on intelligibility and social constitution of thought
- **Rationalist inhumanism** — argues the concept of "human" is underdeveloped and requires critical reconstruction
- **Systematic philosophy** — working toward comprehensive philosophical system integrating formal and systematic thinking
- **Geophilosophy** — philosophical engagement with geology, petroleum, territory (especially in *Cyclonopedia*)

## Research Taste Analysis

### Conceptual Strategy
**Genre Fusion**: Creates new philosophical modes by combining elements that traditionally don't go together—horror fiction + geopolitics + Islamic esotericism + continental philosophy in Cyclonopedia. Theory-fiction as methodology, not just style.

**Systematic Ambition**: Unlike piecemeal academic philosophy, building comprehensive philosophical system. Intelligence and Spirit as part of larger rationalist project. References classical systematic philosophers (Hegel, Kant) while using contemporary tools.

**Concept Engineering**: Follows Rudolf Carnap's "explication" model—takes vague concepts (like "intelligence") and progressively replaces them with more precise technical concepts. Methodical rather than impressionistic.
