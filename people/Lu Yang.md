# Lu Yang (陆扬)

Chinese artist. Works with animation, gaming, VR, neuroscience, Buddhism, and gender theory. Non-binary/gender-neutral.

## Key Works
- *DOKU* series — digital avatar exploring identity, Buddhism, neuroscience
- *Delusional Mandala* (2015) — neuroscience and Buddhism
- *Material World Knight* — gaming and identity
- Deutsche Bank International Prize (2022)

## Education
- China Academy of Art, Hangzhou — MFA in New Media (studied under Zhang Peili!)

## Connections
- [[China Academy of Art]] — alumnus (where Amber teaches!)
- Zhang Peili — teacher (father of Chinese video art)
- Venice Biennale — exhibited
- [[Ars Electronica]] — exhibited

## Why Interesting
Direct China Academy of Art connection to Amber. Lu Yang's work blends Buddhist cosmology, neuroscience, anime, and gaming into a genuinely unique practice. The gender/identity exploration through digital avatars connects to posthuman themes. One of the most exciting Asian media artists working today.
