# Ai Hasegawa

Design researcher and artist working at the intersection of speculative biology, more-than-human reproduction, and technology futures. Notable alumna of [[Design Interactions (RCA)]] under [[Dunne & Raby]] (2005–2015). Works on radical speculative scenarios involving non-human reproduction (famous for "(Im)possible Baby" project and shark-assisted human reproduction proposals). Based in Japan.

## Why Interesting
One of the most provocative DI alumni working with speculative biology and more-than-human reproduction. "A Utility for Human Fertilization by Shark" — a scenario in which humans reproduce via shark intermediary — is a landmark in posthuman speculative design. The work sits directly at the intersection of Amber's more-than-human thread. Based in Japan — important node in the East Asian speculative design circuit.
