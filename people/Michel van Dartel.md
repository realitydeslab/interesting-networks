# Michel van Dartel

## ⚡ Recent Updates

Research Professor at CARADT (Avans Centre of Applied Research for Art, Design and Technology) and Treasurer of ISEA International. Formerly Director of Events at V2_Lab for the Unstable Media (2005-2024). MSc in cognitive psychology, PhD in artificial intelligence. Key figure bridging media art practice, theory, and academic research in the Dutch/European scene.

- Aesthetics after the "situated turn"
