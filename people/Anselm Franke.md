# Anselm Franke

German curator and critic. Head of Visual Art and Film at **HKW** (Haus der Kulturen der Welt / House of World Cultures), Berlin — one of the most intellectually rigorous contemporary art and culture institutions in the world, known for its politically engaged, long-form research programmes. Chief curator of the **10th Shanghai Biennale** "Social Factory" (2014–2015), which examined social production, collective labour, and contemporary forms of factory/cultural production. Franke has curated major thematic exhibitions including *Animism* (2010–2014, touring to Brussels, Vienna, New York, Shanghai) — an examination of the animist imagination as a counter to modern Western nature/culture divides — and *Hauntology* and *The Whole Earth* projects. His intellectual framework draws on anthropology, critical theory, and postcolonial thought. Key connector between the HKW's Berlin critical theory ecosystem and the Shanghai Biennale.
