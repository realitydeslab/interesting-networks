# Li Zhenhua (李振华)

Independent curator and media artist, based between Switzerland and China. Co-founder of [[Chronus Art Center]] (2013). Major figure in the international Chinese media art scene — regularly bridges Swiss/European institutions and China.

## Key Role
- Co-founded CAC with Dillion Zhang and Hu Jieming
- Curated multiple international shows involving Chinese media artists
- Active in Swiss-China cultural exchange (Pro Helvetia, Art Basel, Swiss art institutions)

## Why Note-Worthy
His Switzerland-China position makes him a key figure for international-Chinese media art exchange. Useful contact for anyone navigating both worlds.
