# Ron Wakkary

Professor of Interaction Design at TU Eindhoven. Pioneer of **more-than-human design** — an approach that takes seriously the agency of non-human entities (tools, environments, materials, animals) in design processes. One of the key theoretical voices pushing HCI and interaction design beyond anthropocentrism.
