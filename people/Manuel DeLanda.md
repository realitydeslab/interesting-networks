# Manuel DeLanda

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Graham Harman deep research.

Mexican-American philosopher. Known for **assemblage theory** — a neo-Deleuzian framework applying Deleuze's ideas rigorously to social and material reality. Major figure in the intersection of continental philosophy, science, and architecture. EGS faculty. His "flat ontology" (all assemblages equally real, regardless of scale) is adjacent to but distinct from Harman's OOO.

## Domains
- **Assemblage theory** — neo-Deleuzian social ontology; heterogeneous assemblages at every scale (atoms, organisms, cities, states)
- **Philosophy of science** — nonlinear dynamics, emergence, self-organization; bridging continental philosophy and complexity science
- **Philosophy of history** — *A Thousand Years of Nonlinear History* applies complexity theory to historical dynamics
- **Architecture theory** — influential at Columbia GSAPP and the AA; influenced a generation of architects toward parametric and computational design

## Key Works
- *[War in the Age of Intelligent Machines](https://www.zonebooks.org/books/68-war-in-the-age-of-intelligent-machines)* (Zone Books, 1991) — military technology and emergence
- *[A Thousand Years of Nonlinear History](https://www.zonebooks.org/books/67-a-thousand-years-of-nonlinear-history)* (Zone Books/Swerve, 1997) — complexity theory applied to history; bones, flesh, genes, words
- *[Intensive Science and Virtual Philosophy](https://www.bloomsbury.com/uk/intensive-science-and-virtual-philosophy-9781847063304/)* (Continuum/Bloomsbury, 2002) — rigorous reading of Deleuze
- *[A New Philosophy of Society: Assemblage Theory and Social Complexity](https://www.bloomsbury.com/uk/new-philosophy-of-society-9780826479587/)* (Continuum, 2006) — the assemblage theory textbook
- *[Assemblage Theory](https://edinburghuniversitypress.com/book-assemblage-theory.html)* (Edinburgh UP, 2016)
- *[The Rise of Realism](https://www.politybooks.com/bookdetail?book_slug=the-rise-of-realism--9781509519736)* (Polity, 2017, with [[Graham Harman]]) — dialogue between assemblage theory and OOO

## Key Communities

## Key Relationships
- **[[Graham Harman]]** — co-authored *The Rise of Realism*; productive intellectual dialogue between OOO and assemblage theory
- **Gilles Deleuze** (deceased) — primary philosophical source; DeLanda is the leading systematizer of Deleuzian ontology

## Why Interesting
DeLanda bridges continental philosophy and complexity/systems science better than almost anyone. His influence on architecture and design is profound. The comparison with Harman (both anti-correlationist, different accounts of objects/assemblages) is productive. *A Thousand Years of Nonlinear History* is one of the most cited books in architecture schools.
