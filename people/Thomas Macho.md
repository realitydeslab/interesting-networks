# Thomas Macho

Austrian cultural historian and philosopher. Formerly Humboldt University Berlin, now International Research Center for Cultural Studies Vienna. A central figure in the development of the cultural techniques concept — his formulation (writing precedes the concept of writing; counting precedes the concept of number) became the canonical definition of Kulturtechnik. Works on cultural techniques of time, death, animals, and second-order identity.
