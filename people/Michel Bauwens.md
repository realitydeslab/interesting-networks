# Michel Bauwens

Belgian theorist and activist. Founder of the P2P Foundation (2005), the most comprehensive global network researching commons-based peer production and transition to a commons economy. Coined "Peer-to-Peer theory." Maintains the P2P Foundation wiki — largest knowledge commons on commons theory.

## Domains
P2P theory, digital commons, Commons Transition, post-capitalist economics, open source, cosmo-localism.

## Key Works
- P2P Foundation Wiki: <https://wiki.p2pfoundation.net> — massive curated resource
- ["The Political Economy of Peer Production"](http://www.ctheory.net/articles.aspx?id=499) (2005, CTheory)
- *P2P: The Coming Transformation* (various essays)

## Why Interesting
Most likely person to immediately understand and champion Agentive Commoning. P2P Foundation is the best community entry point for Amber's Digital Commons work. Bauwens actively theorizes what comes after CBPP — agents as next phase of peer production.
