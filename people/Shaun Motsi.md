# Shaun Motsi

Video and film artist. Co-recipient of **ars viva prize 2023** alongside [[Paul Kolling]] and Leyla Yenirce. Known for *MASTERS* (2023) — a video work examining contemporary visual culture, the distribution of moving images in a post-pandemic world, online edutainment platforms, and Black independent cinema. Works in a hybrid register of comedy, thriller, horror, drama. Critically examines how knowledge is produced and distributed, and who gets access to education.
