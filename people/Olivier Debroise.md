# Olivier Debroise

(1952–2008) French-born Mexican curator, art historian, and critic. One of the key intellectual figures in Mexican contemporary art from the 1970s to the 2000s. Longtime researcher at IIE-UNAM. Co-edited *La era de la discrepancia / The Age of Discrepancies* (with [[Cuauhtémoc Medina]]) — the canonical survey of Mexican art 1968–1997. Author of important studies on Mexican photography and modern art.
