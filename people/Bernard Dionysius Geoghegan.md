# Bernard Dionysius Geoghegan

American media theorist and historian at King's College London. Works on cybernetics, information theory, cultural techniques, and the history of computing. Key interpreter of German media theory for Anglo-American audiences. Wrote "After Kittler: On the Cultural Techniques of Recent German Media Theory" (*Theory, Culture & Society* 30:6, 2013) — one of the sharpest analyses of what cultural techniques theory actually does.
