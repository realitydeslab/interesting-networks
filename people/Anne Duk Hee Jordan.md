# Anne Duk Hee Jordan

Korean-German artist, currently Professor for Digital Arts at [[HfG Karlsruhe]]. Explores "verwobene Beziehungen zwischen Menschen und Nicht-Menschen" (entangled relations between humans and non-humans). Fascinated by marine organisms, technology, sexuality, food, ecological systems. Creates installations merging organic materials and robotic beings.

Known for the **"artificial stupidity"** series — an ongoing series of failing robotic machines that demand their right to exist. Their failure to "save the world" is the point.

## Why Interesting
Direct alignment with Amber's more-than-human design research. Jordan approaches machine agency from an embodied, ecological, non-anthropocentric stance. "Artificial stupidity" as a concept inverts AI optimization — directly relevant to Amber's work on machine behavior, ALife, agent ethology.
