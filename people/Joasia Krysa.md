# Joasia Krysa

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-04:** Stub created. Discovered as Head Curator of Helsinki Biennial 2023 ("New Directions May Emerge"), where she invited an **A.I. Entity** as an official curatorial collaborator.

Professor at Loughborough University London. Works at the intersection of computational culture, networked art, and curatorial practice. In 2023, curated Helsinki Biennial with the landmark move of inviting an AI Entity as one of five curatorial collaborators alongside arts collectives, research institutions, and ocean institutes.

## Why Interesting
Krysa is one of the few curators who takes AI agency seriously as a *curatorial* question — not just as a subject for art, but as an *agent* in the curatorial process. This directly connects to Amber's questions about machine behavior and agent ethology. She is also geographically close (London/Loughborough) and in Amber's broader network (Aarhus/Aalto/Parikka connections).
