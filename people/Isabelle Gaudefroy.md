# Isabelle Gaudefroy

New director of [[Le Fresnoy]] since February 2026, succeeding Alain Fleischer. Previously at [Fondation Cartier pour l'art contemporain](https://www.fondationcartier.com/) for 12 years (1999–2022) as director of programming then artistic director. Earlier: produced contemporary opera/musical theatre with composer Georges Aperghis at Théâtre des Amandiers, Nanterre. Curated *America Latina* (Cartier, 2013), *Junya Ishigami: Freeing Architecture* (Cartier, 2017), *Nous les Arbres* (Cartier, 2019). President of Compagnie RB (Jérôme Bel), 2000–2020. Currently president of Compagnie Echelle 1:1 (Joris Lacoste).

Vision for Le Fresnoy: "La relation" as through-line — between disciplines, territory, international creation, science, and the living.
