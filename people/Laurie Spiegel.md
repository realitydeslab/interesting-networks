# Laurie Spiegel

Electronic musician and software engineer at Bell Labs. Developed [Music Mouse](https://lauriespiegel.net/ls/writings/musmouse.html) — an "intelligent instrument" algorithmic composition software (1986). A pioneering figure at the intersection of algorithmic systems, computational creativity, and human-machine collaboration. Her composition *Harmonices Mundi* was included on the Voyager Golden Record (1977). A rare engineer-artist hybrid who built creative tools that empowered human musical expression through computational means.
