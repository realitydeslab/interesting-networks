# Nina Lykke

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Karen Barad deep research.

Feminist scholar and Professor Emerita of Gender Studies at Linköping University, Sweden. One of the central nodes in European feminist science studies and new materialism. Co-authored a chapter with [[Karen Barad]] in *Bits of Life: Feminism at the Intersections of Media, Bioscience, and Technology* (ed. Smelik and Lykke, University of Washington Press, 2008). Developed feminist materialist seminars that brought together European scholars working on matter, technology, and feminism. Directs the European graduate school in feminist theory (cofem). Ran multi-year feminist research seminar series influential in European STS.

## Why Interesting
The key node connecting Barad's work to European feminist studies infrastructure. Her seminars at Linköping were formative for a generation of European feminist STS scholars. Useful for understanding how Barad's ideas circulate and develop in European feminist contexts — different from the US feminist theory tradition.
