# Eva Jäger

Works in **Arts Technologies** at **[Serpentine Galleries](https://www.serpentinegalleries.org/)**, London. Co-investigator on the **[[Creative AI Lab]]** (the Serpentine side of the KCL–Serpentine partnership). Co-authored the 2023 "Towards a C5 model in Creative AI" position paper alongside [[Mercedes Bunz]], [[Daniel Chávez Heras]], Alice Milne, and [[Joanna Zylinska]].

Represents the curatorial/institutional end of the Creative AI Lab — the bridge between academic research at KCL and the programmatic/exhibition work at Serpentine.

## Why Interesting
The Arts Technologies team at Serpentine is one of the most influential galleries-as-AI-research-lab operations in the world. Eva sits at the interface between gallery programming and AI research — a role with few parallels elsewhere.
