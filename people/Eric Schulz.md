# Eric Schulz

Director, Institute for Human-Centered AI at Helmholtz Munich. Cognitive scientist who uses foundation models to understand AI behavior through a psychological lens — "building blocks of intelligence" via computational, cognitive, and neuroscientific methods. Speaker at Machine+Behavior 2026.

## Background
PhD UCL 2017 (supervisor: Maarten Speekenbrink) on generalization as function learning. Postdoc at Harvard with Sam Gershman and Josh Tenenbaum (2017-2019). Now Director of HCAI at Helmholtz Munich. His framing: use foundation models as instruments to understand human cognition, and vice versa.
