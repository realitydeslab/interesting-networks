# Gill Eatherley

British structural/expanded cinema filmmaker; member of [[Filmaktion]] and [[London Film-Makers Co-operative (LFMC)]]. Identified by Tate as a proponent of expanded cinema alongside [[Annabel Nicolson]], [[Malcolm Le Grice]], and [[William Raban]]. Participated in Tate Modern's Tanks Live Programme (2012) Filmaktion event alongside Le Grice and Raban.

## Domains
Structural/materialist film; expanded cinema performance; British avant-garde.

## Background
Active in the early 1970s through the LFMC. Later co-edited *Like a Fox* (1988) with [[Malcolm Le Grice]].

## Key Communities
- [[London Film-Makers Co-operative (LFMC)]] — member
- [[Filmaktion]] — member (with Nicolson, Le Grice, Raban)

## Key Relationships
- [[Malcolm Le Grice]] — co-edited *Like a Fox* (1988); fellow Filmaktion member
- [[Annabel Nicolson]] — fellow Filmaktion member
- [[William Raban]] — fellow Filmaktion member

## Why Interesting
Confirmed participant in the Filmaktion group that represents the British expanded cinema moment at its most radically participatory. Tate staged a Filmaktion retrospective at the Tanks in 2012. Needs further research on specific works.
