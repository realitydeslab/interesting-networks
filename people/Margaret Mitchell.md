# Margaret Mitchell

AI ethics researcher. Now Chief Ethics Scientist at [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/). Previously co-led Google's Ethical AI Team with [[Timnit Gebru]] (2018–2021). Fired from Google in February 2021 after she used automated scripts to gather evidence of Gebru's mistreatment. Co-author of:
- [Stochastic Parrots](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3442188.3445922) (FAccT 2021) with Gebru, Bender, McMillan-Major
- [Model Cards for Model Reporting](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.03993) (FAT* 2019)
