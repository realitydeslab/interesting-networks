# Mabel O. Wilson

Professor of Architecture at Columbia GSAPP. Member of [[Practicing Refusal Collective]]. Architect and scholar whose work explores Black spatial imagination, racial capitalism and built environments, and the contested history of African American architecture. Co-director of [Global Africa Lab](https://globalafricalab.com/) at Columbia.
