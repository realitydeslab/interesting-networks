Critical computing scholar, design researcher. Bridges tech industry critique, design labor studies, and feminist STS. Known for Turkopticon (worker visibility tool for Mechanical Turk), book *Chasing Innovation* on India's tech development imaginaries. UCSD, Dept of Communication + Design Lab.
