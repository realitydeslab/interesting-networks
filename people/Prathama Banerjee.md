# Prathama Banerjee

Historian at CSDS (Centre for the Study of Developing Societies), Delhi — the same institution that houses [[Sarai]]. Works on time, temporality, and political thought in colonial India. Author of *Politics of Time: "Primitives" and History-Writing in a Colonial Society* (Oxford 2006) and *Elementary Aspects of the Political: Histories from the Global South* (Duke 2020).

**Raqs connection**: participated in the artist talk for [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s *Cavalcade* at the Neubauer Collegium, University of Chicago (May 8, 2025), alongside [[Dipesh Chakravarty]], moderated by [[Dieter Roelstraete]]. Her work on colonial temporal politics directly resonates with Raqs's sustained interest in time as a political instrument.
