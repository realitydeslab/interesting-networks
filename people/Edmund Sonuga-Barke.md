# Edmund Sonuga-Barke

Professor of Developmental Psychology, Psychiatry and Neuroscience at King's College London. Co-lead of the [[Centre for Attention Studies]] (ASK). Specialist in ADHD and attention disorders — the clinical/scientific anchor of a centre that also has a strong humanities wing. Bridge between neuroscience of attention and humanistic/philosophical attention studies.

## Why Interesting
Rare figure who sits at the exact interface of clinical neuroscience and humanities-inflected media philosophy — by virtue of co-leading ASK with [[Joanna Zylinska]]. If Amber's research on HoloKit/XR attention ever needs an empirical/clinical dimension, Sonuga-Barke is the gateway.
