Film and media theorist. Faculty at Yale University in Film and Media Studies. Organized the **Serpentine Gallery** "day of study" in London connected to Arthur Jafa's exhibition, in which **[[Saidiya Hartman]]** participated. Writes at the intersection of film theory, Black aesthetics, and performance.
