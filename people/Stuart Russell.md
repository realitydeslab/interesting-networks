# Stuart Russell

Professor of Computer Science at UC Berkeley and co-founder of the [Center for Human-Compatible AI (CHAI)](https://humancompatible.ai/). Co-author of [*Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach*](http://aima.cs.berkeley.edu/) — the most widely used AI textbook in the world. Author of [*Human Compatible: Artificial Intelligence and the Problem of Control*](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/566677/human-compatible-by-stuart-russell/) (2019). Pioneer of inverse reinforcement learning (IRL) as an alignment approach. Signed CAIS's AI Risk Statement.
