# Grisha Coleman

Performance artist, choreographer, and scholar at Arizona State University's Herberger Institute. Steering committee member of **[[MOCO (Movement and Computing)]]**. Organized **MOCO'19** (Tempe, AZ — theme: "Movement Imaginaries").

Research: dance and technology, cultural politics of movement, movement and race, ecological art practices. Brings critical race theory and feminist perspectives to movement computing.

## Key Communities
- [[MOCO (Movement and Computing)]] — steering committee, hosted MOCO'19
- Arizona State University — Herberger Institute

## Why Interesting
Critical angle on movement + computing rare at technical conferences. Coleman brings Black feminist performance theory into the MOCO space. "Movement Imaginaries" as a conference theme signals the political-imaginative possibilities she sees in the field.
