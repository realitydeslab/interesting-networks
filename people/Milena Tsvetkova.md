## Background
Cornell PhD → Oxford Internet Institute postdoc → LSE faculty. Research trajectory from social network analysis to human-machine collective dynamics. The HUMANET lab is funded to study how human-machine, machine-machine, and human-human interactions differ and how they affect collective outcomes (coordination, cooperation, social contagion).
