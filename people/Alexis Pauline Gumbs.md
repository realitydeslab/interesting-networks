# Alexis Pauline Gumbs

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created via American Artist deep research. Essay contributor to *Shaper of God* monograph (Pioneer Works Press, 2025).

Black feminist scholar, activist, author, and oracle. Known for *Undrowned: Black Feminist Lessons from Marine Mammals* (AK Press, 2020) — a remarkable work using marine biology to develop Black feminist theory. Also: *Dub: Finding Ceremony* and *M Archive: After the End of the World*. Runs Brilliance Remastered, an online learning community. Connects Black feminist thought to more-than-human and ecological frameworks — directly relevant to Amber's more-than-human design interest.
