# Bonaventure Soh Bejeng Ndikung

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-01:** Stub created during HKW deep research. ⭐⭐ — biotech PhD + decolonial curator, director of HKW since Jan 2023.

Cameroonian-born curator, writer, and institutional director. Born 1977 in Yaoundé, Cameroon. Unique figure: holds a doctorate in **medical biotechnology** and postdoctorate in **biophysics**, then pivoted to become one of Germany's most important art curators. Director of [[Haus der Kulturen der Welt]] since January 2023. Founder of [SAVVY Contemporary](https://savvy-contemporary.com/), an independent art space in Berlin.

## Background
- Doctorate in medical biotechnology, Heinrich-Heine-Universität Düsseldorf / TU Berlin
- Postdoctorate in biophysics, University of Montpellier
- Founder, SAVVY Contemporary (Berlin independent project space and discursive platform)
- Curator at Galerie Wedding, Berlin (2015-2018, with Solvej Helweg Ovesen)
- Curatorial team, **documenta 14** (2017, under Adam Szymczyk)
- Curated Finnish pavilion, 58th Venice Biennale (2019)
- Artistic director, 12th Bamako Encounters (2019) — African photography biennial, Mali
- Artistic director, Sonsbeek 2020-2024 (Arnhem, Netherlands)
- Guest professor, Städelschule Frankfurt (2017-2019, curatorial studies + sound art)
- Professor, Spatial Strategies MA, Weißensee Academy of Art Berlin
- **Director, Haus der Kulturen der Welt** (from Jan 2023)
- **Chief Curator, 2025 São Paulo Art Biennial**

## Why Interesting
Extremely rare career: hard scientist (biotech, biophysics) who became a leading decolonial art curator. Under his HKW directorship, the institutional mission now explicitly includes "non-living beings" and uses ontological language of "to world, unworld, reworld." His scientific background gives depth to his posthuman and biopolitical thinking — this isn't decorative theory but an informed position. Connected to major international biennial circuit (Venice, São Paulo, Bamako, documenta) through SAVVY Contemporary's network. For Amber: model of how scientific training transforms into critical cultural practice.
