# Thomas Pesquet

French astronaut with European Space Agency (ESA). Collaborated with [[Eduardo Kac]] to create the first artwork made in space aboard the International Space Station - "Inner Telescope" (2017). Represents new model of artist-astronaut collaboration bridging space exploration and contemporary art.
