# Auryn Macmillan

Co-creator of [[Gnosis Guild]] and co-author of ERC-5005 (Zodiac Modular Accounts standard). Lead developer of the Zodiac composable governance ecosystem and the Enclave FHE/MPC protocol.

## Domains
- Composable governance infrastructure for DAOs
- Ethereum protocol design (ERC standards)
- Privacy-preserving computation (FHE, ZKP, MPC)
- Smart contract architecture

## Background
Built [[Gnosis Guild]] alongside [[Kei Kreutler]] at Gnosis (Berlin). Primary technical architect of the Zodiac ecosystem — designed the Avatar/Module/Modifier/Guard architecture. Co-authored [ERC-5005](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-5005) with Kei Kreutler (April 2022). Currently leading development of Enclave, a FHE + ZKP + MPC protocol for Encrypted Execution Environments.
