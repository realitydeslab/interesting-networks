# aaajiao (Xu Wenkai, 徐文恺)

Shanghai-based net artist, programmer, blogger, and critical technologist. One of the most significant figures in Chinese new media art. Works at the intersection of internet infrastructure, open-source culture, and Chinese digital society.

## Key Works
- *We=Link: Ten Easy Pieces* (2020, [[Chronus Art Center]] + Rhizome) — WeChat as platform for net art during COVID
- *Tumblr* series — social media platform aesthetics
- Various net.art works exploring censorship, platform capitalism, and Chinese internet culture
- Blog/writing practice: prolific commentator on Chinese technology and internet culture

## Why Note-Worthy
Uniquely combines technical depth (programmer), cultural criticism (prolific writing), and art practice. His position in both Shanghai and Berlin makes him a key bridge in global-Chinese net culture discourse. Rhizome-adjacent.
