# Steve Goodman (Kode9)

Scottish electronic music artist, DJ, founder of Hyperdub record label. PhD in philosophy from University of Warwick. CCRU member who coined the term "sinofuturism."

## Key Works
- *Sonic Warfare: Sound, Affect, and the Ecology of Fear* (2009) — MIT Press
- Hyperdub label — signed Burial, DJ Rashad, Fatima Al Qadiri
- Multiple albums as Kode9

## Connections
- [[CCRU]] — member, coined "sinofuturism"
- [[Nick Land]] — CCRU colleague
- [[Mark Fisher]] — CCRU colleague
- Hyperdub — cultural institution bridging theory and music

## Why Interesting
Rare figure who bridges academic philosophy (Warwick PhD), experimental music, and label management. Sonic Warfare is a key text on affect theory and sound. Coined sinofuturism — connects to Yuk Hui's cosmotechnics and Chinese tech-philosophy.
