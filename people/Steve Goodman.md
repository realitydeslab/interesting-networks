# Steve Goodman (Kode9)

Scottish electronic music artist, DJ, philosopher. PhD in philosophy from University of Warwick under [[Nick Land]]. [[CCRU]] member who coined the term "sinofuturism." Founder of **[[Hyperdub Records]]** — one of the most influential UK electronic music labels of the 2000s-2010s. Bridging CCRU theory and electronic music production. Performs and records as **Kode9**.

## Background
- Came to Warwick as a PhD student, studying under Nick Land. His dissertation "Turbulence: A Cartography of Postmodern Violence" (1999) developed CCRU ideas in sonic-theoretical direction.
- Land's writing and jungle music converged for Goodman: "The thing that always got me about the resonance between Nick's writing and jungle was this theme of turbulence... his interest in the productivity of systems on the edge of chaos, for me, was basically a direct and very literal description of what was going on rhythmically in jungle with breakbeat science."
- Founded Hyperdub in 2004. Originally a website before becoming a record label. Signed Burial in 2005 — transforming the label's reach.
- Coined "sinofuturism" — the concept of China as ideal accelerationist society — which became a major aesthetic and theoretical movement.

## Key Works
- *[Sonic Warfare: Sound, Affect, and the Ecology of Fear](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262514019/)* (MIT Press, 2009) — Theoretical culmination of CCRU sonic theory. How sound operates as a weapon and political/social force.
- Multiple albums as Kode9 (with The Spaceape): *Memories of the Future* (2006), *Black Sun* (2012), *Nothing* (2015)
- *[Sonic Faction](https://www.urbanomic.com/product/sonic-faction/)* — Audio essay format developed with Angus Carlyle (Urbanomic)

## Key Communities

## Why Interesting
Unique bridge between rigorous theoretical philosophy (Land, CCRU) and electronic music production and culture. Hyperdub is one of the great taste-making labels of its era — signing Burial (2005) before anyone knew who he was is a canonical example of scouting. The sinofuturism concept he coined is directly relevant to Amber's interests in Chinese tech culture. *Sonic Warfare* is a serious MIT Press book connecting sound, affect theory, and social control — genuine scholarship.
