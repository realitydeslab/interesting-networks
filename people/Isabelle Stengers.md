# Isabelle Stengers

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Donna Haraway deep research. Close intellectual companion and collaborator.

Belgian philosopher of science. One of the most important European thinkers of science, cosmopolitics, and speculative philosophy. Co-author with Ilya Prigogine (*Order out of Chaos*, 1984). Her *Cosmopolitics* series (7 volumes, 1997–2003) proposes a radical pluralism for how different knowledge practices can coexist. Deeply connected to Donna Haraway's circle. Whitehead scholar.

## Key Works
- **[*Order Out of Chaos: Man's New Dialogue with Nature*](https://www.amazon.com/Order-Out-Chaos-Dialogue-Nature/dp/0553343637)** (with Ilya Prigogine, 1984) — Classic of non-equilibrium thermodynamics and complexity theory. Co-authored with Nobel laureate. Made her internationally known.
- **[*Cosmopolitics I & II*](https://www.upress.umn.edu/book-division/books/cosmopolitics-i)** (University of Minnesota Press, 2010/2011; original French 1997–2003) — Her major philosophical project. Proposes "cosmopolitics" as a practice of taking seriously all that exists, including non-humans, in political deliberation. Companion to Haraway and Latour's projects.
- **[*In Catastrophic Times: Resisting the Coming Barbarism*](https://www.openhumanitiespress.org/books/titles/in-catastrophic-times/)** (Open Humanities Press, 2015) — On Gaia, capitalism, and political resistance.
- **[*Another Science is Possible: A Manifesto for Slow Science*](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Another+Science+is+Possible%3A+A+Manifesto+for+Slow+Science-p-9781509521814)** (Polity, 2018) — Critique of fast, competitive academic science.
