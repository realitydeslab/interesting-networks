# Tania Bruguera

Cuban-born performance and social practice artist. One of the most important political artists of her generation. Uses art to intervene in immigration policy, civic participation, and oppressive political systems. SAIC MFA alumna. Known for durational performance, institutional critique, and creating art that produces real political change — not just representation.

## Key Work
- **[Immigrant Movement International](https://www.immigrantmovementinternational.org/)** — long-term political art project in Queens, NY; a community space and political movement.
- **10,143,765** — durational performance about immigration deaths (Tate Modern)
- **Arte de Conducta (Behavior Art)** — pedagogy as artwork; ran a school for socially engaged art in Havana

## Why Interesting
Social practice that creates actual political infrastructure, not just representations. Model for art that is both aesthetic and functional/civic. SAIC alumna who built a major career through institutional critique and participatory design.
