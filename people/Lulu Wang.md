# Lulu Wang

London-based Chinese interdisciplinary artist. Co-founder of **Diasporas Now**, currently in residence at ICA as part of the Speaking Futures × Diasporas Now programme (2025-26). Her practice spans sculpture, installation, and immersive choreography. Draws from Chinese heritage and **digital subcultures** to explore hybrid identities and social relationships. Works at intersection of visual art, **new technologies**, fashion, and experimental music.
