# José-Carlos Mariátegui

Peruvian writer, curator, and professor based in Europe. Specialist in **Latin American digital art** and media theory. Affiliated with the London School of Economics and LUISS (Rome).

**Note**: This is José-Carlos Mariátegui the critic/curator, not to be confused with his namesake José Carlos Mariátegui (1894–1930), the foundational Peruvian Marxist thinker — though the naming is surely not accidental.

## Known Role
- Presented at [[MUAC]]'s 2024 "Museo digital. Inteligencias y artificios" forum, organized with [[Cátedra Max Aub]] and Fundación Telefónica Movistar México. Positioned as a specialist bringing Latin American digital art and media theory perspective to questions of AI and museum futures.
