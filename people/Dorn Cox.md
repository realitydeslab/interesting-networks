## Background
Research Director at Wolfe's Neck Center for Agriculture and the Environment. Founder of FarmOS — an open-source, Drupal-based farm management platform used by farmers, researchers, and NGOs worldwide. FarmOS is licensed under GPL, meaning it is freely available and community-maintained. Speaks nationally and internationally on participatory science, soil health, and regenerative agriculture. Board member at Regen Foundation, bridging the open agriculture data world with the ecological governance world.

## Why Interesting
Dorn represents the intersection of open-source digital infrastructure and agricultural commons — the practical implementation layer of what Regen Foundation theorizes at institutional level. FarmOS is an example of digital public infrastructure for land-based communities that predates the blockchain era and provides a more grassroots, less financialized model. The connection between FarmOS (participatory agricultural data) and Regen Network (ecological credits and governance) is a rich lineage.
