# Allison Parrish

Computer programmer, poet, educator, and game designer. Assistant Arts Professor at NYU's ITP. Works at the intersection of AI and language — treating computational text generation as an artistic medium and philosophical inquiry. Named "Best Maker of Poetry Bots" by Village Voice (2016). AMI grant recipient (2019–20).

## Why Interesting
- Serious computational poet — not just "AI generates text" but deep engagement with what language *is* computationally
- NYU ITP connection — central node of creative tech education
- Work on sound-shape of language (not just semantics) is genuinely novel

## Key Works
- *[The Nonsense Laboratory](https://artsexperiments.withgoogle.com/nonsense-laboratory/)* (2020, AMI) — NLP tools for playing with sound/spelling of language
- *[Articulations](https://counterpathpress.org/articulations-allison-parrish)* (2018, Counterpath) — computer-generated poetry book
- *@Everyword: The Book* (2015, Instar) — output of her bot tweeting every English word
