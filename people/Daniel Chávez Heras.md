# Daniel Chávez Heras

Researcher at the Department of Digital Humanities, King's College London. Core member of the **[[Creative AI Lab]]** (King's + Serpentine Galleries). Works at the intersection of **computational cinema** — applying machine learning to film analysis and generation — and critical digital humanities.

Explores how ML models see and "understand" moving images, and what this means aesthetically, politically, and epistemologically. Brings a film studies background to computational questions about vision and representation.

## Research Interests
- Computational approaches to film and moving images
- Machine vision and aesthetics
- Critical digital humanities
- AI art and the politics of image generation
