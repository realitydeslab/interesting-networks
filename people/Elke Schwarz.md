# Elke Schwarz

Professor of Political Theory at Queen Mary University London. Author of *Death Machines: The Ethics of Violent Technologies* (Manchester University Press). Vice-Chair of [[International Committee for Robot Arms Control (ICRAC)]]. Works on the nexus of ethics, technology and politics with focus on military AI, autonomous weapons, drones. Recent work on venture capital dynamics and military norms (blitzscaling → blitzkrieg). RSA Fellow, Leverhulme Fellow 2024, CAPAS Fellow 2022/23.
