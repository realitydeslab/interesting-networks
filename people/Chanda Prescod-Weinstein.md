# Chanda Prescod-Weinstein

Particle astrophysicist and feminist science studies scholar. One of the rare figures bridging hard physics and critical theory. FQXi-funded (2016): "Epistemological Schemata of Astro|Physics: A Reconstruction of Observers" ($100K) — an explicitly feminist-epistemological intervention into the physics of observation. Author of *The Disordered Cosmos*.

## Domains
- Dark matter (axion dark matter)
- Cosmology / particle astrophysics
- Feminist science studies
- Black feminist epistemology
- Physics of observation / the observer problem

## Key Works
- [*The Disordered Cosmos: A Journey into Dark Matter, Spacetime, and Dreams Deferred*](https://www.hachettebookgroup.com/titles/chanda-prescod-weinstein/the-disordered-cosmos/9781541724501/) (2021) — landmark: physics + Black feminist thought + dreams for a different science
- [Epistemological Schemata of Astro|Physics](https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.04502) — FQXi-funded; feminist reconstruction of observer concept

## Why Interesting
Prescod-Weinstein bridges Amber's two worlds more directly than almost anyone: rigorous physics AND critical theory of observation, knowing, and justice. Her FQXi work on "Epistemological Schemata" is about what it means to be an observer in physics — directly relevant to machine consciousness / agent ethology angles. Also a writer and public intellectual.
