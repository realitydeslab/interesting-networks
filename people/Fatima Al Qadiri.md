# Fatima Al Qadiri

Kuwaiti conceptual artist and electronic musician. Born in Dakar (diplomat family). NYU linguistics. Music on Hyperdub explores Gulf identity, Orientalism, police brutality, medieval Islamic culture. Member of GCC collective (MoMA PS1, Whitney, Fridericianum). Sister of visual artist Monira Al Qadiri. Music used in Beyoncé's Renaissance World Tour.
