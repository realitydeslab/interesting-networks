# Peter Wolfendale

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Discovered/stub created during Urbanomic deep research.

British philosopher. Key Urbanomic author associated with the "rationalist" wing of post-speculative-realism discourse. Close to the [[Urbanomic]] network; [[Ray Brassier]] wrote the preface to his latest book.

## Key Work
- *Object-Oriented Philosophy: The Noumenon's New Clothes* (Urbanomic, 2014) — the most comprehensive and critical examination of Graham Harman's OOO (Object-Oriented Ontology). Postscript by Brassier.
- *The Revenge of Reason* (Urbanomic, 2025) — major new work. Tagline: "unbinding our rationality from every petty naturalism and every fixed image of thought." Preface by Brassier.

## Key Concepts
- Critique of OOO (Object-Oriented Ontology) — detailed philosophical dismantling of Harman's framework
- Rationalist universalism — arguing for rationality as emancipatory force beyond naturalism

## Connected People
- [[Ray Brassier]] — preface to *The Revenge of Reason*, postscript to *Object-Oriented Philosophy*; close intellectual ally
- [[Robin Mackay]] — publisher

## Connected Communities
- [[Urbanomic]] — primary publisher

## Why Interesting
Represents the rationalist-Prometheanism strand of post-speculative-realism — the tendency that emphasizes reason, abstraction, and emancipation over OOO's anti-rationalism. His work is the intellectual backbone for Brassier-adjacent thought.
