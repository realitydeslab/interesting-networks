# Heather Dewey-Hagborg

Information artist and bio-hacker. Works on DNA, surveillance, and biotechnology.

## Key Books/Papers
- *Stranger Visions* (2012-2013) — 3D-printed faces from DNA found in public (gum, hair, cigarettes)
- *Invisible* — DNA spoofing spray
- *Probably Chelsea* (2017) — 30 portraits of Chelsea Manning from DNA

## Why Interesting
*Stranger Visions* is iconic bio-art — reveals how much information we shed in public. DNA art connects to surveillance, privacy, and the politics of biological data. More-than-human because it engages with DNA as a medium.
