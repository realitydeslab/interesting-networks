# Mónica Amieva Montañez

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-01:** Stub created during deep research on [[IIE-UNAM]].

Investigadora Asociada C at [[IIE-UNAM]] (since 2020). Art historian, curator, educator. Research: contemporary art, education/pedagogy, Mexican art. Trained at UAB (two master's degrees + PhD in philosophy), ICI curatorial intensive, São Paulo Biennial curatorial toolbox. Had research residency at Centre Pompidou studying Isidore Isou and Guy Debord archives. Edited *Museo digital: Futuros y posibilidades* (MUAC, 2019), a key text on digital museum futures.
