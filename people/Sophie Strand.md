# Sophie Strand

Writer, poet, and mythopoetic thinker working at the intersection of ecology, embodied mythology, and more-than-human intelligence. Known for "mycelial" writing practice — essays and stories that spread and network rather than argue linearly. Author of *The Flowering Wand: Rewilding the Sacred Masculine* (2022) and the mythopoetic novel *The Madonna Secret*. A rising voice in the more-than-human ecology space.

Co-led "Ecology and Magic: A Daylong Workshop on Animism and Nature" with [[David Abram]] (March 2022). This collaboration signals intellectual alignment between her mythopoetic feminism and Abram's ecological phenomenology.
