# Evan Roth

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created via [[F.A.T. Lab]] deep research.

American artist/hacker. Co-founded [[Graffiti Research Lab]] (with James Powderly, 2005) and [[F.A.T. Lab]] (2007). MFA Parsons School of Design (valedictorian). Work in MoMA permanent collection. 2012 Smithsonian Cooper-Hewitt National Design Award. Eyebeam OpenLab Fellow. Now based in Berlin, makes internet archaeology and landscape video works documenting the physical infrastructure of the internet.

## Why Interesting
Pioneer of tactical media through open-source art tools. Post-FAT work on internet infrastructure as landscape is interesting — treating fiber optic cables and data centers as landscape photography subjects.
