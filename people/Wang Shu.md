# Wang Shu 王澍

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during China Academy of Art deep research.

Chinese architect and Dean of the School of Architecture at [[China Academy of Art]]. In 2012, became the **first Chinese citizen to win the [Pritzker Architecture Prize](https://www.pritzkerprize.com/laureates/2012)** — the world's top architecture award. Co-founder (with wife/partner Lu Wenyu) of [Amateur Architecture Studio](https://www.amateurarchitecturestudio.com/). Known for critical regionalism: using recycled traditional materials (salvaged tiles) to build contemporary structures that question China's rampant development. Designed CAA's Xiangshan Campus (2007) — a masterwork integrating millions of recycled tiles from demolished traditional Chinese buildings into contemporary form.

## Domains
- Architecture and critical regionalism
- Traditional material practice (recycled tiles, traditional building techniques)
- Pedagogy: hands-on building before design

## Key Works
- **[Xiangshan Campus, China Academy of Art](https://www.pritzkerprize.com/laureates/2012)** (2007) — CAA's main campus. Uses millions of recycled tiles. Integrates with natural landscape.
- **[Ningbo Museum](https://www.pritzkerprize.com/laureates/2012)** (2008) — Won 2009 Lu Ban Prize (top Chinese architecture award). Facade of recycled bricks.
- **Ningbo Museum of Art** (2005)
- **Library of Wenzheng College, Soochow University** (2000) — Won inaugural Architecture Art Award of China (2004).

## Awards & Fellowships
- **[Pritzker Architecture Prize](https://www.pritzkerprize.com/laureates/2012)** (2012) — First Chinese citizen. World's top architecture award.
- **[Global Award for Sustainable Architecture](https://www.citedelarchitecture.fr/en/events/global-award-sustainable-architecture)** (2007) — First winner, with Balkrishna Doshi.
- **Schelling Architecture Prize** (2010) — with Lu Wenyu.
- **Gold Medal, French Academy of Architecture** (2011).
- **Lu Ban Prize** (2009) — Top Chinese architecture award for Ningbo Museum.
- **Architecture Art Award of China** (2004) — inaugural winner, for Wenzheng College Library.

## Key Communities
- **[[China Academy of Art]]** — Dean of School of Architecture. Joined faculty 2000, became Dean of School of Architecture 2007.
- **[Amateur Architecture Studio](https://www.amateurarchitecturestudio.com/)** — his practice with Lu Wenyu. Founded 1997.

## Why Interesting
Wang Shu demonstrates what critical regionalism looks like at its best — not nostalgia, but rigorous engagement with local material traditions to critique globalist development. His approach (1 year of manual building work before design) is a pedagogical philosophy that connects practice to theory in a way that resonates with Amber's interest in embodied design.
