# Marc Andreessen

Co-founder of Netscape; co-founder and GP of Andreessen Horowitz (a16z); author of the *Techno-Optimist Manifesto* (2023); primary institutional amplifier of e/acc ideology. Net worth ~$1.9B. Trump advisor (2024).
