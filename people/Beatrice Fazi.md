# M. Beatrice Fazi

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial deep research completed. Major philosopher of computational aesthetics working at intersection of continental philosophy and computer science.

Philosopher of computation who develops rigorous theoretical frameworks for understanding computational processes philosophically. Leading voice in computational aesthetics who goes beyond art-with-computers to examine the ontology of computation itself. Bridges continental philosophy (Deleuze, Whitehead) with formal computational theory (Gödel, Turing) to argue that indeterminacy is central to—not a bug in—computational systems.
