# Victoria Sin

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Lucia Pietroiusti deep research. Co-hosted Serpentine's Back to Earth podcast (2020–2022) with Pietroiusti.

Non-binary artist and filmmaker working with drag, mythology, storytelling, and queer ecology. Co-hosted the **Back to Earth podcast** (2020–2022) at Serpentine Galleries alongside [[Lucia Pietroiusti]], presenting artist campaigns and environmental work as part of the General Ecology programme. Their practice explores cosmology, embodiment, and alternative forms of kinship — bridging queer theory with ecological thinking.
