# Doenja Oogjes

Design researcher at TU Eindhoven's Future Everyday cluster. Develops more-than-human thinking in design, bringing together digital textile fabrication, ecological approaches, and interaction design. [[Ron Wakkary]]'s most frequent collaborator — multiple best paper awards together.

## Why Interesting
Core contributor to the more-than-human design movement. Her textile and domestic design work gives materiality to Wakkary's theoretical framework — she makes the theory tangible through Jacquard looms, speculative homes, and backyard ecologies. The combination of craft practice and posthuman theory is exactly the kind of art-science bridging Amber values.
