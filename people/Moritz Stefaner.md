# Moritz Stefaner

Independent data visualization designer based in Germany. One of the leading practitioners in the intersection of data visualization and art/culture. Long-term collaborator of [[Cultural Analytics Lab]] / [[Lev Manovich]] — co-created Selfiecity, SelfieSãoPaulo, and On Broadway (NYPL commission, Silver Award Best Visualization 2015). His studio [Truth & Beauty](https://truth-and-beauty.net/) works on data visualization projects across science, culture, and journalism.

## Domains
- Data visualization as art and practice
- Cultural data
- Scientific visualization

## Key Collaborations
- [[Cultural Analytics Lab]] / [[Lev Manovich]] — Selfiecity (2014), SelfieSãoPaulo (2014), On Broadway (2015)

## Why Interesting
Occupies the rare space of being both rigorously technical and aesthetically sophisticated in data visualization. His projects with Manovich's lab show how large-scale cultural data can be made perceptually and aesthetically meaningful — relevant to Amber's interest in making agent behavior legible and beautiful.
