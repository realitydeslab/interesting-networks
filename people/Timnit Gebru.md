# Timnit Gebru

Ethiopian-American computer scientist and AI ethics researcher. Co-authored Gender Shades (2018) with [[Joy Buolamwini]] — one of the most cited papers in AI bias. Founded [DAIR Institute](https://www.dair-institute.org/) (Distributed AI Research Institute) after being fired from Google Brain in 2020 in a high-profile controversy over a paper on large language models. One of the most important figures in critical AI globally.

## Why Interesting
Founded DAIR after being fired from Google — an act that made her a symbol of the structural barriers facing Black women in AI. DAIR is an independent research institute doing critical AI research outside industry control. Hugely influential in the critical computing community.

## Key Works
