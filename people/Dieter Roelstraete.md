# Dieter Roelstraete

Curator at the Neubauer Collegium for Culture and Society, University of Chicago. Previously at MCA Chicago and documenta 14. Works on art and history, archive fever, the Marxist tradition in contemporary art.

**Raqs connection**: Moderated the artist talk and discussion for [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s *Cavalcade* at Neubauer Collegium, Chicago (May 8, 2025). Ongoing curatorial relationship with Raqs — the Neubauer Collegium hosted the *Cavalcade* solo exhibition in 2025.
