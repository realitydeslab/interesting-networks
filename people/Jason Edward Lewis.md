# Jason Edward Lewis

Kanien'kehá:ka (Mohawk) poet, artist, and scholar. Professor at Concordia University, Montreal. **Founder of the Initiative for Indigenous Futures** — a partnership of Canadian universities developing visions for Indigenous futures. Director of the Concordia computation+AI research lab **PoeticsLab / AbTeC (Aboriginal Territories in Cyberspace)**.

Key figure in Indigenous AI: edited and co-authored the landmark *Indigenous Protocols and Artificial Intelligence Position Paper* (2020, CIFAR/Initiative for Indigenous Futures). Co-authored **"Making Kin with Machines"** (award-winning, MIT Press JoDS) with [[Kite (Suzanne Kite)]], Noelani Arista, and Archer Pechawis.

Likely [[Kite (Suzanne Kite)]]'s PhD supervisor at Concordia.
