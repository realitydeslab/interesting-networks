# Hank Willis Thomas

Conceptual artist working primarily with photography. Explores race, identity, mass media, popular culture, advertising. Co-founder of [For Freedoms](https://forfreedoms.org/), a nonpartisan platform for civic engagement. Works directly in the tradition of [[Kerry James Marshall]], who Thomas has cited as a major influence. Represented by Jack Shainman Gallery. Known for *B®anded* series (branded Black skin).
