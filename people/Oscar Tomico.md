# Oscar Tomico

Associate professor at TU Eindhoven on Design Research Methodologies for Posthuman Sustainability. Research expands human-centered design to position designers as part of complex ecological systems. Focuses on multispecies design, plant cohabitation, and 1st-person research methodologies. Key collaborator of [[Ron Wakkary]] and [[Doenja Oogjes]].

## Background
- MSc Industrial Engineering, Polytechnic University of Catalonia, Spain
- PhD (cum laude, 2007) from same institution on subjective experience-gathering techniques
- Previously head of Industrial Design Bachelor's at ELISAVA Barcelona (2017–2023)
- Co-director of Design for Emerging Futures Master Program (IAAC × ELISAVA)
- Co-editor-in-chief of Temes de Disseny Journal
- Guest positions at AUT Creative Technologies (NZ), TaiwanTech, Swedish School of Textiles, IAAC, University of Tsukuba (Japan)

## Why Interesting
The Catalonia → Netherlands trajectory and the "posthuman sustainability" framing bridge Southern European design practice with Northern European design theory. His focus on plant studios and multispecies collaboration as design methodology is genuinely novel.
