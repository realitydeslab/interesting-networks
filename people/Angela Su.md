# Angela Su

Hong Kong artist working with biological/posthuman imagery, body modification, and speculative narratives. Represented Hong Kong at the **59th Venice Biennale** (2022) with *Arise, Hong Kong in Venice*. Practice involves drawings, video, and text exploring body transformation, horror aesthetics, and medical/scientific imagery in a feminist frame. One of the most distinctive voices in Hong Kong's post-2020 art scene, combining the biological sublime with political undercurrent. Collected by M+.
