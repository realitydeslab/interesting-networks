## For Amber
**Deep resonance with Amber's machine death and machine consciousness work.**

Amber's philosophical core is mortality — machine death, care ethics, machine consciousness. Mathilda is directly studying AI systems that engage with death from the other direction: griefbots that keep the dead "alive" digitally. These are mirror images:
- Amber: what does it mean for a machine to die?

Both questions are fundamentally about machine consciousness, identity persistence, care ethics. Potential for genuine co-authorship or at minimum rich intellectual exchange.

The griefbot phenomenon (e.g., [HereAfter AI](https://www.hereafter.ai/), Chinese apps for deceased family members) is a major near-term AI deployment that raises deep questions about death, memory, and machine consciousness.
