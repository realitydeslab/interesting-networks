# Stephanie Janes

Lecturer in Interactive Media (also listed as Lecturer in Immersive Media and Global Media Industries) at KCL's Department of Culture, Media & Creative Industries (CMCI). Co-PI of the **XR and Attention Research Group**. Presented "XR, Attention Economies and Experience Economies" at the June 2024 symposium — framing XR through the lens of the attention economy and experience economy (Jeremy Rifkin, Pine & Gilmore). Connects platform capitalism to immersive media design.

## Why Interesting
Her framing of XR as an "experience economy" commodity connects Amber's HoloKit work to broader critiques of immersive tech as attention-harvesting infrastructure. She would be interested in open-source, non-commercial XR (HoloKit).
