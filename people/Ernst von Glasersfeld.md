# Ernst von Glasersfeld (1917–2010)

German-Austrian philosopher and psychologist. The principal developer of **radical constructivism** — the epistemological position that reality is *constructed* by the observer, not discovered. BCL (Biological Computer Laboratory) visitor under [[Heinz von Foerster]]. Board member of the American Society for Cybernetics.

## Why Interesting
His radical constructivism is the philosophical partner to second-order cybernetics — together they form the epistemological foundation for constructivist science. Relevant to any critical AI research that takes seriously the observer's role in constructing knowledge.
