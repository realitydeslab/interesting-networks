# Evan Thompson

Philosopher of mind at UBC. Extended Varela's enactivism into the most comprehensive theory of life and mind continuity. Author of *Mind in Life* (2007) and *Waking, Dreaming, Being* (2015). Argues consciousness requires autopoietic organization as substrate — which directly challenges claims about AI consciousness.

## Why Interesting
His argument that "mind" is continuous with "life" (both requiring autopoietic organization) is the strongest theoretical challenge to AI consciousness claims. *Mind in Life* is essential reading for Amber's machine consciousness work.
