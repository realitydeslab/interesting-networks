# Joanie Lemercier

French visual artist and activist working with light, projection, and environmental data. Known for spectacular large-scale installations at sites of industrial and ecological significance. Strong practice at the intersection of technology and environmental critique.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created from NEWNOW Festival deep research.

## Domains
- Large-scale light and laser installations
- Environmental data visualization
- Industrial heritage and ecological critique
- Anti-consumerist digital art practice (notably renounced NFTs after calculating their energy footprint)

## Key Works
- *Untitled* (2021) — NEWNOW Festival, Zeche Zollverein, Essen DE. Work on industrial heritage and impact of strip mining on the Hambacher Forst (one of Europe's oldest forests)
- Multiple large-scale projection works at industrial and heritage sites

## Why Interesting
His Hambacher Forst work at NEWNOW resonates with cosmotechnics and the technosphere — using art to make visible the damage of industrial extraction on ancient ecosystems. His decision to leave the NFT market over energy concerns (documented publicly) makes him a rare practitioner who explicitly politicizes the energy ethics of digital art. Connects to [[Kimchi and Chips]]' energy-ethics approach with *Another Moon*.
