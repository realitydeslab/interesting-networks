# Toni Pape

Film theorist and researcher at Concordia University. Co-director of the [[3 Ecologies Institute]] book series (Immediations/3Ecologies at punctum books). Part of the [[SenseLab]] network. Co-authored *Nocturnal Fabulations* — a collective exploration of ecological thinking in cinema through Whitehead's process philosophy.
