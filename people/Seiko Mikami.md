# Seiko Mikami

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via YCAM deep research. Retroactive retrospective at ICC 2025–2026.

Japanese media artist working with large-scale interactive installations on themes of information society, the human body, surveillance, and perception. A foundational figure in Japanese media art. Professor at Tama Art University.

## Key Works
- *molecular Informatics – Morphogenic substance via eye tracking* (1996, Canon ARTLAB)
- *gravicells – gravity and resistance* (2004, YCAM) — 6th sense/gravity themed installation
- *[Desire of Codes](https://www.ycam.jp/en/archive/works/desire-of-codes/)* (2010, YCAM) — Grand Prize, Japan Media Arts Festival 2012. 90 robotic tentacle devices, 6 search arms, compound-eye screen; surveillance art with global database of CCTV footage. Toured 5 countries/8 venues.
- Retrospective exhibition "Toward a Mausoleum of Perception" at NTT ICC (December 2025 – March 2026)

## Exhibitions
DEAF Rotterdam (1995, 1996, 2004), transmediale Berlin (2002, 2005, 2007), Ars Electronica (2005), Dortmunder U, Vienna Künstlerhaus, NTT ICC, Ars Electronica 2012, La Alhóndiga (Bilbao), National Taiwan Museum (2024), NTT ICC (2025–2026 retrospective).

## Key Communities

## Why Interesting
Pioneer of Japanese media art with deep engagement with surveillance, body, perception, and information society — themes directly relevant to Amber's critical computing interests. *Desire of Codes* is a landmark work in surveillance art. Still active with major retrospective 2025–2026.
