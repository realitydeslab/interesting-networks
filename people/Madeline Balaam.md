# Madeline Balaam

Professor at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, within the Interaction Design (IxD) group. Close collaborator in the soma design community around [[Kristina Höök]]. Known for feminist HCI, bodily experience, and health technologies — particularly around reproductive health, menstruation, and intimate body technologies.

Her work sits at the intersection of soma design and feminist HCI — asking whose bodies are designed for and how bodily experience in marginalized or medicalized contexts (menstrual health, birth, reproductive care) can be designed with, not just about.
