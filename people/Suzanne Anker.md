# Suzanne Anker

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created from SLSA deep research. SLSA Lifetime Achievement Award 2024.

Pioneer of **bioart** — art that uses biological processes, DNA, and living systems as medium. Chair of Fine Arts at SVA New York. Co-author of *The Molecular Gaze: Art in the Genetic Age* (Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory Press, 2004) with Dorothy Nelkin. One of the founding figures of the art-science intersection in the posthuman era. SLSA Lifetime Achievement Award 2024.
