# Mariame Kaba

Prison abolitionist organizer, educator, and writer. One of the most important figures in the US abolition movement. Her work is deeply informed by **[[Saidiya Hartman]]**'s "afterlife of slavery" — she draws on Hartman's framework to ground abolitionism in long-arc historical analysis rather than reform politics.

- **[*Let This Radicalize You: Organizing and the Revolution of Reciprocal Care*](https://www.haymarketbooks.org/books/2145-let-this-radicalize-you)** (Haymarket Books, 2023, with Kelly Hayes) — practical guide to radical organizing
