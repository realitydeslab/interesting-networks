# Dan Hendrycks

Founder and Director of the [Center for AI Safety (CAIS)](https://safe.ai/). Former PhD student of [[Jacob Steinhardt]] at UC Berkeley (co-advised with [[Dawn Song]]). Creator of MMLU, MATH, APPS — the dominant benchmarks for measuring AI language understanding and reasoning. Co-authored "Unsolved Problems in ML Safety" with Steinhardt, Carlini, and Schulman. One of the most impactful AI safety researchers in terms of building institutional infrastructure (CAIS) and measurement tools.
