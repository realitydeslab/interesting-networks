# Anton Vidokle

Co-founder and director of e-flux, one of the most influential platforms for contemporary art theory and criticism. Artist working with film, video, and installation.

## Background
Russian-born artist and curator based in New York and Berlin. Co-founded e-flux in 1998, transforming it from announcement platform into major art theory publication.

- "Immortality and Decay" film series
- Various curatorial projects

Central figure in contemporary art theory publishing. E-flux Journal has become essential platform for critical discourse around art, technology, and politics. Connected to network of major theorists and artists.
