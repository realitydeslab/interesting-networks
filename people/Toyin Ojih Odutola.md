# Toyin Ojih Odutola

Nigerian-American artist creating speculative narrative drawings of fictional aristocratic African families using graphite, charcoal, pastel, and pigment. Builds entire alternative worlds — speculative fiction through visual art. First NY show at Jack Shainman Gallery. Included in Whitney Biennial, major museum collections. One of the most interesting speculative worldbuilders in contemporary art.
