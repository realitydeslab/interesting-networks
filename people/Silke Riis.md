# Silke Riis

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-02:** Stub created during Mediamatic deep research.

Sculptor and installation artist (b. 1998, Copenhagen). Works with transformation and **mortality** at the core of her practice. Currently in residency at Mediamatic (Jan–Mar 2026), running *Aquatic Speculations* — a speculative evolution workshop imagining future species in a flooded Netherlands. Connects speculative evolution (imagining new species through fantasy + science) to climate anxiety, artistic practice, and world-making.
