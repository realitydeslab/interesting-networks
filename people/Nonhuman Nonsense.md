## Key Works
- **["Haunted Waters: The Chemical Cocktail Bar"](https://designandposthumanism.org/2024/11/20/haunted-waters-the-chemical-cocktail-bar/)** — a growing collection of contaminated waters. Menu of water samples from around the world. Visitors encounter pollution, contamination, forever chemicals through the register of a cocktail bar. Conceptually clever critical design.

## Why Interesting
"Haunted Waters" is a great example of critical posthuman design — it makes invisible pollution visceral and experiential through the unexpected register of a cocktail bar. This is exactly the kind of practice-based conceptual work that Amber values: weird, rigorous, takes a position. The studio is getting mainstream (ACM Interactions) visibility for explicitly posthuman design work.

## For Amber
- Good case study of how to make posthuman design *concrete* and *communicable* to mainstream design/HCI communities
- The "Haunted Waters" format (contaminated waters from global sites) could be a model for how to make abstract environmental issues tangible through design
- Stockholm-based — if Amber visits Scandinavia, worth connecting
