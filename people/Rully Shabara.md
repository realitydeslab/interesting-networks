# Rully Shabara

Vocalist and co-founder of [Senyawa](https://senyawa.bandcamp.com/) — the Indonesian experimental duo (with Wukir Suryadi) who made their album *Alkisah* (2020) available to be released by any label worldwide simultaneously, spawning a transnational decentralized distribution network. Guest on [[Interdependence Podcast]] (2021) — episode titled "The Album That Spawned a Transnational Decentralized Network."

## Domains
- Experimental music with roots in Javanese/Indonesian tradition
- Decentralized music distribution
- Non-Western experimental music practice
- Music as network infrastructure

## Why Interesting
Senyawa's radical distribution experiment — releasing one album through hundreds of labels across every continent simultaneously — is a real-world example of decentralized cultural infrastructure that bypasses Western industry structures. The model predates and conceptually exceeds most Web3 music experiments. Non-Western origins make it significant for Amber's interest in non-Western tech/cultural traditions.
