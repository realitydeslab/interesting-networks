# Manuela Naveau

Head of the [[Interface Cultures Lab]] at the University of Arts Linz (Kunstuniversität Linz). Appointed Professor for Interface Cultures / Critical Data on December 1, 2025 — the first new head since Christa Sommerer and Laurent Mignonneau founded the department in 2004.

Researcher and curator. Works at the intersection of critical data studies, feminist AI, sound art, and interactive art. Leads the Critical Data Research Group at Interface Cultures, which examines how artistic practice can decode, critique, and reimagine data systems.

**Collaborators:** Alexander Wöran (co-author), Vladan Joler (Critical Data On Tour 2024), Julia Kloiber

**Publications:**
- Naveau, M., Wöran, A. (2024). "Feminist AI at Interface Cultures Linz." *Journal Netzwerk NRW*, Vol. 55, pp. 53–60. DOI: [10.17185/duepublico/82754](https://doi.org/10.17185/duepublico/82754)
- Naveau, M., Wöran, A. (forthcoming). "Sounding out AI." *AIREA*, University of Edinburgh.
