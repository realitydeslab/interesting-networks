# Karen Yeung

Legal scholar and Interdisciplinary Professorial Fellow at University of Birmingham. Known for theorizing "algorithmic regulation" as a distinct regulatory modality — she distinguishes it from traditional law and argues it requires new accountability frameworks. Her 2018 paper "Algorithmic regulation: A critical interrogation" (*Regulation & Governance*) is a key text in the field.
