# Arjen Mulder

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during DEAF deep research.

Writer, theorist, and long-term V2_ associate. Co-edited the DEAF anthology series with [[Joke Brouwer]], including *The Art of the Accident* (1998), *Machine Times* (2000), and *Interact or Die!* (2007, V2_/NAi Publishers). Known for drawing on biology, evolutionary theory, and perception science to frame media art questions. Author of *Understanding Media Theory* and essays on posthumanism, network culture, and unstable systems.

## Domains
- Media theory
- Biology/evolution and media (ALife adjacent)
- Philosophy of technology
- Posthumanism

## Why Interesting
His cross-domain approach — biology + technology + art — resonates with Amber's ALife work. His framing of media systems through biological evolution and behavior anticipates later machine behavior discourse. The DEAF anthologies he co-edited are key texts for entering the V2_ intellectual community.
