# Patricia Domínguez

Chilean artist and educator; practice spans watercolours, ceramics, sculptural assemblage, video installation. Founder of **Studio Vegetalista** — experimental platform for ethnobotanical research in Chile. Work draws on plant intelligence, Andean cosmologies, quantum non-locality, corporate wellness critique, and shamanic healing traditions. MFA Hunter College NY; Botanical Illustration Certificate, New York Botanical Garden.

**2019 Simetría Award** (Arts at CERN / Connect): dual residency at CERN + ESO/ALMA observatories in the Atacama Desert (Chile). At CERN, researched quantum physics, non-locality, and entanglement — then connected this to plant healer knowledge in Peru.

Represented Chile at **56th Venice Biennale** (2015). Exhibited widely internationally.

**For Amber:** Strong resonance — more-than-human thinking, non-Western knowledge systems, quantum physics/ecology crossover. Her path from quantum physics to shamanic plant knowledge to public art is exactly the kind of unexpected intellectual trajectory Amber is interested in.
