# Ye Funa (叶甫纳)

Shanghai-based artist working with social media platforms, beauty culture, consumer aesthetics, and digital feminism. One of the most distinctive voices in Chinese post-internet art.

## Key Works
- *Nail to Go* (2018, solo at [[Chronus Art Center]]) — nails as social media performance objects
- *We=Link: Ten Easy Pieces* (2020, CAC + Rhizome) — social media + COVID response
- Work frequently involves platform-specific aesthetics (WeChat, Instagram, Taobao), beauty standards, and female labor

## Why Note-Worthy
Brings a feminist, consumer-culture-critical lens to Chinese internet art that is distinct from the more macho tech-hacker aesthetics of some peers. Her use of nail art and beauty culture as artistic medium is surprisingly rigorous.
