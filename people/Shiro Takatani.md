# Shiro Takatani

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-28:** Stub created via Dumb Type deep research.

Co-founder and (since 1995) artistic director of [[Dumb Type]]. Solo visual/installation artist working with video, photography, fog, light, and sound. Born 1963, Nara. Graduated Kyoto City University of Arts. 40 years shaping Japanese media art.

Extensive solo collaborations with Ryuichi Sakamoto (LIFE, LIFE-WELL, water state 1, async, TIME), Fujiko Nakaya (fog sculptor), Mansai Nomura (Noh actor). Awarded Japan's 65th Prize of Fine Arts (Media Art, 2015), Kyoto Prefecture Cultural Award (2019).

## Why Interesting
The surviving artistic director of Dumb Type — key figure if Amber wants to engage with the collective or with Japanese media art infrastructure. His solo work explores: time, memory, nature, technology, climate. Connected to the broadest network of Japanese and international media art institutions.
