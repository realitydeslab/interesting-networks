# Alice Bucknell

British artist working at the intersection of speculative architecture, AI, game engines, and ecological thinking. Practice spans film, installation, and immersive environments. **2024 Collide Copenhagen winner** (Arts at CERN × Copenhagen Contemporary). Exhibited at HEK Basel (House of Electronic Arts), among other major institutions.

A rising figure in the digital/AI art space engaging with architecture and speculative futures — relevant to current discourse on AI worldbuilding, non-human architectures, and post-anthropocentric space-making.
