# Kodwo Eshun

British-Ghanaian writer, theorist, filmmaker. Co-founded The Otolith Group (2002) with Anjalika Sagar.

## Key Works
- *More Brilliant than the Sun: Adventures in Sonic Fiction* (1998) — Afrofuturist music theory
- The Otolith Group — art collective, films, installations
- "Further Considerations on Afrofuturism" (2003)

## Domains

- Film and moving image art
- Post-colonial technology narratives

## Why Interesting
Bridges music theory, Afrofuturism, and speculative thought. The Otolith Group does extraordinary art-theory hybrid work. His concept of "sonic fiction" prefigures many current art-AI discussions. Key voice on non-Western futurisms.
