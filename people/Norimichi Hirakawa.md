# Norimichi Hirakawa 平川紀道

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via NTT ICC deep research.

Japanese audiovisual artist born 1982. Creates computer program-based real-time processing installations for museums and media art festivals. **Ars Electronica Award of Distinction, Interactive Art (2008).** Wide-ranging activities: production work for Ryoji Ikeda, Otomo Yoshihide, and [[Seiko Mikami]]; participated in Salone del Mobile Milano; live performances as Typingmonkeys; artistic direction for the ARTSAT project (satellite art).

## Key Communities
- [[NTT ICC]] — frequent exhibitor and production collaborator
- [[Ars Electronica]] — Award of Distinction 2008

## Why Interesting
Japanese technical artist who bridges real-time computation, audiovisual installation, and collaboration with landmark media artists (Ikeda, Mikami). Part of the NTT ICC core circle.
