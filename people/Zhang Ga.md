# Zhang Ga (张嘎)

Media art curator, writer, and professor. **Artistic Director of [[Chronus Art Center]]** since 2015, and the single most strategically connected individual in the China–global media art circuit. Professor of media art at NYU Shanghai (ITP-style Interactive Media Arts program), where he teaches alongside [[Gottfried Haider]].

## Key Role
- Founded/directs CAC's intellectual and curatorial programme
- Central node connecting China to ZKM, Ars Electronica, Leonardo/ISAST, Rhizome, Art Center Nabi, HeK Basel
- Curated major international exhibitions including *Entangled: bio/media*, *AI Delivered*, *We=Link: Ten Easy Pieces*
- Co-organized *We=Link* online platform with Rhizome + Art Center Nabi
- Led CAC's theoretical framework: posthumanism, new materialism, entanglement ontology

## Why Note-Worthy
Rare dual position: serious academic (NYU) + serious institutional director (CAC). Operates in both Chinese and English-language discourse. Deeply invested in posthumanism/new materialism as critical frameworks, not just aesthetic preferences.
