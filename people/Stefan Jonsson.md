# Stefan Jonsson

Swedish cultural theorist and historian at REMESO Institute, Linköping University, Sweden. Published in conversation with [[Premesh Lalu]], moderated by Tracy Murinik (2015). Works on race, representation, European crowds, colonial history. Author of *A Brief History of the Masses: Three Revolutions* (Columbia UP) and *Crowds and Democracy: The Idea and Image of the Masses from Revolution to Fascism* (Columbia UP, 2013).

## Why Interesting
Connects European (especially Scandinavian) cultural studies to African postcolonial thought via the Lalu conversation. Works on the politics of representation and mass politics — relevant to machine behavior at scale and crowd dynamics.
