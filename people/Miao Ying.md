# Miao Ying (苗颖)

Post-internet and platform artist based between Shanghai and New York. Known for work engaging Chinese internet culture, censorship infrastructure (the "Great Firewall"), and the aesthetics of social media platforms. One of the most internationally recognized Chinese net artists.

## Key Works
- *Holding a Kitchen Knife to Cut the Internet Cable* (2018, solo at [[Chronus Art Center]]) — explores the GFW as aesthetic and political material
- *Chinternet Plus* series — Chinese internet aesthetics and nationalist discourse
- Featured in Wuhan Biennale 2024 ("Future Coding: Signals of the Sentient")

## Why Note-Worthy
Bridges Chinese internet culture studies (censorship, platform capitalism, nationalist memes) with international contemporary art discourse. Her work is funny, sharp, and irreplaceable as a lens into Chinese platform culture.
