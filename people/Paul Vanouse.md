# Paul Vanouse

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during SymbioticA deep research.

American artist and professor at University at Buffalo (SUNY). Works with DNA as artistic material to critique genetic determinism, race science, and biological essentialism. Former SymbioticA resident.

**Key works:** *Labor* (live DNA gel electrophoresis that generates images in real time — the scientific measurement IS the drawing); *Relative Velocity Inscription Device* (DNA of different "races" in gel; genetic similarity reveals race as social construct); *Spit* (public saliva/genetic profiling critiques); *Latent Figure Protocol* series.

His approach: use actual biotech techniques (gel electrophoresis, PCR) as artistic medium, making the scientific process visible and making its hidden assumptions legible. Critical approach to molecular biology, biometrics, surveillance.

## Why Interesting
DNA as critical medium. The scientific apparatus is the artistic gesture. Critiques race science from inside the lab — methodologically similar to Catts/Zurr's "inside infiltration" approach. SymbioticA alumnus connecting bio-art to political critique.
