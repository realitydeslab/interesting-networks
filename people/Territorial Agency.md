# Territorial Agency

Architectural collective and curatorial practice by **John Palmesino** and **Ann-Sofi Rönnskog**. Works at the intersection of architecture, geopolitics, and environmental theory — mapping and analyzing territorial transformations at planetary scale.

Curated the 7th Lisbon Architecture Triennial "How Heavy Is a City?" (2025), for which they commissioned [[Raqs Media Collective]]'s essay "Biting Tongue, Biding Time" (published via e-flux "Intensification" series).

Known for projects like *Oceans in Transformation* and UN collaboration on visualizing Arctic transformations.
