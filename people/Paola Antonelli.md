# Paola Antonelli

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Anthony Dunne deep research.

Senior Curator of Architecture and Design and Director of R&D at [[MoMA]], New York. One of the most influential curators in contemporary design. Described [[Dunne & Raby]]'s tenure at the RCA as having "changed the course of design." Co-curator of the inaugural [[MIT Media Lab]] Award (2015) alongside [[Neri Oxman]] and [[Kevin Slavin]]. Has been instrumental in bringing speculative design, critical design, and design-as-inquiry into the museum context. Key exhibitions include *Design and the Elastic Mind* (2008), *Talk to Me* (2011), *Broken Nature* (XXII Milan Triennale, 2019).

## Why Interesting
The single most powerful institutional advocate for speculative/critical design in the museum world. Any designer working at the intersection of technology, society, and critical inquiry needs to understand Antonelli's curatorial vision and influence. She is a potential connection point for Amber.
