# Matt McCorkle

Sound designer, music composer, and worldbuilder. Collaborator with [[Jiabao Li]] / [[Ecocentric Future Lab]] on **Nocturnal Fugue** (2024–2025) — immersive bat vocalization soundscape. Responsible for "worldbuilding & music composition" — transforming bat social calls (isolation, sleeping, kissing, mating, feeding, grooming, fighting) into spatial sound environments synchronized with projection mapping. Co-author on "Becoming Bat" at HttF Symposium ([https://doi.org/10.1145/3686169.3686212](https://doi.org/10.1145/3686169.3686212)).

## Why Interesting
Specialist in immersive worldbuilding through sound — one of the few practitioners working at the intersection of scientific data (AI-discovered bat vocalizations) and experiential composition. Connection to Amber through EchoVision/Nocturnal Fugue collaboration chain.
