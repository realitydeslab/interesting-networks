# Rafael Lozano-Hemmer

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via YCAM deep research.

Mexican-Canadian electronic artist working with large-scale interactive installations that use robotics, sensors, networks, and databases. Known for "relational architecture" series — transforming public buildings and spaces. Commissions from Ars Electronica, MoMA, Tate Modern, Guggenheim. Created YCAM's very first commissioned work (*Amodal Suspension*, 2003).

## Key Works
- *[Amodal Suspension / relational architecture 8](https://www.ycam.jp/en/archive/works/amodal-suspension/)* (2003, YCAM opening commission) — 20 searchlights controlled by SMS messages from 28 art centers in 15 countries. YCAM's inaugural commission.
- Multiple "relational architecture" pieces transforming public buildings globally
- Work in MoMA, Tate Modern, Guggenheim collections

## Why Interesting
Seminal figure in participatory interactive art at architectural scale. The *Amodal Suspension* commission established YCAM's international ambitions on day one. Deep engagement with participation, surveillance, and networks as artistic material — connects to Amber's critical computing interests.
