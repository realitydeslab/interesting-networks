# Charlotte Eifler

Artist and filmmaker. Currently Professor for Mixed Realities at [[HfG Karlsruhe]] (Media Art department). Works at intersection of Extended Reality, moving image, performance, installation. Themes: representation politics, queerness, archives, speculative futures.

Uses "experimental narrative techniques to question existing power structures and imagine alternative forms of history-making and social coexistence."

Shown at: Sapporo International Art Japan, ACM SIGGRAPH Digital Art (Los Angeles), Staatliche Kunsthalle Baden-Baden, Art Basel, Haus der Kulturen der Welt Berlin, IMPAKT Utrecht, Kurzfilmtage Oberhausen, Rencontres Internationales Paris.

Member of: unlearning canon, Digital Critique, feat.fem, FACES (gender/art/technology), G-Edit.

Residencies in Moskau, New York, Gent, Mexico City, Paris.

Currently mentoring for Kunstbüro Baden-Württemberg.

## Why Interesting
New XR professorship at HfG — directly adjacent to HoloKit territory. Her queer/archival/speculative approach to mixed reality is different from typical XR art (less spectacle, more politics). Connection point for Amber's interest in XR as a medium for more-than-human/somaesthetic practice.
