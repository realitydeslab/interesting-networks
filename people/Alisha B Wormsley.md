# Alisha B Wormsley

Black feminist artist, performer, and cultural producer based in Pittsburgh. Co-creator (with [[Kite (Suzanne Kite)]]) of *Cosmologyscape* (Creative Time commission, Brooklyn, 2024) — a participatory public art project using community dreams to generate ML-based mosaic sculptures. For 5+ years, Wormsley and Kite co-hosted the **Black and Indigenous Dreaming Workshop series** building a community of dreamers rooted in Black quilting traditions and Lakota visual language.

Known for public art, participatory projects, and afrofuturist practice. Her phrase "THERE ARE BLACK PEOPLE IN THE FUTURE" appeared on a Pittsburgh billboard in 2018 and became a widely circulated cultural touchstone.
