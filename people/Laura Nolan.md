# Laura Nolan

Former Staff Site Reliability Engineer at Google; left in 2018 in protest against Project Maven (DoD AI drone surveillance). Key signatory of the "cancel Maven" open letter. Founded **TechWontBuildIt Dublin** (2018). Now an independent advocate and member of [[International Committee for Robot Arms Control (ICRAC)]]. One of the most high-profile examples of a tech worker who translated inside knowledge into public advocacy on autonomous weapons.
