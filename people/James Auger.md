# James Auger

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during Anthony Dunne deep research.

RCA [[Design Interactions]] graduate and key figure in speculative/critical design. Co-created Auger-Loizeau with Jimmy Loizeau — one of the most important speculative design duos after [[Dunne & Raby]] themselves. Credited on the Robots project (2007). Now based in Paris at ENS Paris-Saclay. Published extensively on speculative design methodology.

## Why Interesting
A "second generation" critical designer who studied directly under [[Anthony Dunne]] and has developed his own significant practice and theoretical contributions. Represents how DI alumni have spread speculative design globally.
