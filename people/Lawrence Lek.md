# Lawrence Lek

Malaysian-born, London-based artist. Works with simulation, gaming, CGI, and AI to create speculative worldbuilding.

## Key Works
- *Sinofuturism (1839–2046 AD)* (2016) — influential video essay
- *Geomancer* (2017) — CGI film about an AI that wants to be an artist
- *AIDOL* (2019) — CGI feature about AI pop star
- *NOX* (2022) — game/film about automated nightclub
- Unreal Engine as artistic medium

## Domains
- Sinofuturism, AI creativity, simulated worlds
- Game engines as art medium
- Questions of AI authorship and creativity

## Why Interesting
Geomancer asks "can an AI be an artist?" — the most direct artistic exploration of computational creativity. His use of game engines to build entire speculative worlds connects to Amber's interest in mixed reality and speculative design. The sinofuturism essay is a landmark.
