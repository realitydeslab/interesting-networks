# Alexandra Daisy Ginsberg

London-based designer and artist working at the intersection of design, synthetic biology, and more-than-human futures. Edited the landmark *Synthetic Aesthetics* book (MIT Press, 2014). Known for *Machine Kingdom*, *Designing for the Sixth Extinction*, and speculative SynBio design.
