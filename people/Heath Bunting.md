# Heath Bunting

British net artist and activist based in Bristol; co-founder of irational.org (1996); pioneered tactical media net art; *StatusProject* maps personal identity through bureaucratic documentation networks — an infrastructure-as-art practice.

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Net Art deep research.

## Domains
Net art, tactical media, identity infrastructure, culture jamming, activist art

## Key Works
- *King's Cross Phone-In* (1994) — used website to coordinate strangers calling public phones at King's Cross; social network as art
- *Own, Be Owned or Remain Invisible* (1998) — article about Bunting with every word hyperlinked to [word].com; critique of internet commodity
- *Visitors Guide to London* (1997) — included in documenta X
- [*StatusProject*](http://status.irational.org/) (ongoing) — mapping personal identity through bureaucratic documents (passport, bank account, etc.); identity as network of institutions

## Why Interesting
*StatusProject* directly connects to questions of machine behavior and agent identity — it maps how human identity is constituted through institutional networks, anticipating contemporary questions about AI agent identity and sovereignty. His Bristol base makes him geographically relevant to Amber (UK). His tactical media work is a direct predecessor to contemporary activist HCI. irational.org is one of the few surviving early net art platforms.
