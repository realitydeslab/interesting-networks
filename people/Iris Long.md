# Iris Long 龍星如

Independent curator and writer based in China. Works at the intersection of art, technology, infrastructure, and astronomy/astrophysics.

## Background
Curator with a distinctive practice bridging art, infrastructure studies, and cosmic/astronomical perspectives. Connected to Tsinghua University's Department of Astronomy through "Touch the Universe" project.

## Key Curatorial Projects
- *Earth Heat Flow: the Visitor Who Returns to Solar Time* (大地热流)
- *Cosmos Archaeology* (宇宙考古)
- *Temporal Stack: the Deep Sensor* (时满时空，深层感应)
- *Fictions of Inter-domain Routing* (山海运算，域间路由) — infrastructure/routing as art
- *Blue Cables in Venetian Watercourse* — Venice
- *PORT: UNDER THE CLOUD* (端口：云下贵州) — infrastructure in China, see [temporalstack.com](https://temporalstack.com)
- *When Gaia Holds Her Breath* (当盖娅屏住呼吸)
- *Cosmic Arclight* (寰宇弧光)
- *Lying Sophia & Mocking Alexa* — AI/robotics critique
- *DE JA VU* (机器人间)
- *Mind the Deep* (脑洞)

## Key Communities
- Tsinghua University, Department of Astronomy
- Chinese art-technology scene

## Why Interesting
Rare curator working at the intersection of infrastructure studies, astronomy/astrophysics, and contemporary art — with a distinctly Chinese perspective. The infrastructure focus (routing, cloud computing, data centers in Guizhou) connects to critical computing. The cosmic/astronomical framing connects to speculative design and more-than-human thinking. Added directly by Amber.
