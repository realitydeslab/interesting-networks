# Soh Yeong Roh 노소영

Founder and Director of [[Art Center Nabi]] — Korea's first and leading digital/media art institution. Built from scratch in 2000 with SK Group backing. General Chair of ISEA 2025. Daughter of former South Korean President Roh Tae-woo; ex-spouse of SK Group chairman Chey Tae-won (divorced 2024).

## Background
Born 1961, Daegu, South Korea. Education: College of William & Mary (BA), University of Chicago (Economics grad), Stanford University (MEd), Yonsei University (Media Communication). Founded Nabi in 2000, transforming the Walkerhill private contemporary art museum into Korea's first new media art museum. Her political-family background and chaebol connections gave Nabi institutional legitimacy and resources; her genuine intellectual mission (Nam June Paik's "Humanizing Technology" philosophy) gave it critical credibility. Published *Digital Art* (2014). Directed SK Telecom Pavilion at Expo 2012 (Yeosu).
