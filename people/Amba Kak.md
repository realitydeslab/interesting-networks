# Amba Kak

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during AI Now Institute deep research. Co-Executive Director of AI Now.

Co-Executive Director of the [AI Now Institute](https://ainowinstitute.org/). Indian-born technology policy expert and Rhodes Scholar. One of the leading voices in AI governance globally. TIME100 AI 2024. Served as Senior Advisor on AI to FTC Chair Lina Khan (2021). Former Mozilla Technology Policy Fellow and Google Policy Fellow. Board member of Signal Foundation.

## Background
Born and raised in New Delhi. Studied law at National University of Juridical Sciences, Kolkata. As a Rhodes Scholar, obtained MA in Law and MSc in Social Science of the Internet at Oxford University (research focused on net neutrality). Former Google Policy Fellow. Mozilla inaugural Technology Policy Fellow (2016). Mozilla global policy advisor (2017–2019). Joined AI Now 2019 as Director of Global Policy; became Executive Director 2022. Currently Co-Executive Director alongside Sarah Myers West.
