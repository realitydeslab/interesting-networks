# Patrick Jagoda

Media theorist and game designer at the University of Chicago. Author of *Network Aesthetics* (Chicago, 2016) and *Experimental Games* (Chicago, 2020). Co-editor of *Critical Inquiry*. Sits at the intersection of game studies, network theory, and experimental narrative. Makes games and writes theory about them.
