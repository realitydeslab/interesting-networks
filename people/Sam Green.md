# Sam Green

**Documentary filmmaker and live cinema artist.** Pioneer of the "live documentary" format — combining documentary film with live performance, guest speakers, and spatial audio. A frequent collaborator with [[Shari Frilot]] and [[Sundance New Frontier]].

Has shown at New Frontier since 2010, including with a live performance with the Kronos Quartet. Later works:
- **"7 Sounds"** (New Frontier 2021) — binaural spatial audio experience; live-streamed performance
- **"32 Sounds"** (New Frontier 2022) — immersive cinematic audio with live performance; binaural recording

Frilot: *"He's done so many of them in such a successful way that we wondered, 'Is it New Frontier anymore? Is it something that's established now?'"*
