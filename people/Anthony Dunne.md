# Anthony Dunne

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during RCA deep research. Dunne & Raby returned from Parsons/The New School (2016-2025) to become Distinguished Visiting Professors at China Academy of Art (2025-). New book *Not Here, Not Now* (2025).

Co-creator (with Fiona Raby) of speculative design / critical design as an academic discipline. Professor and Head of Design Interactions at RCA (2005–2015) — the programme that defined a generation of critical designers. Author of *Hertzian Tales* (1999), co-author of *Speculative Everything* (2013).

## Why Interesting
The intellectual godfather of the field Amber works in. Understanding Dunne's trajectory from CRD at RCA → Design Interactions → Parsons → China Academy of Art is understanding the arc of critical design as a discipline. [[Dunne & Raby]]'s *Speculative Everything* is the canonical text. They received the inaugural MIT Media Lab Award (2015) and were named Royal Designers for Industry (2021).

## Key Works
- [*Hertzian Tales*](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262541992/) (1999/2005) — Critical design bible. Electronic products, aesthetic experience, critical design.
- [*Design Noir: The Secret Life of Electronic Objects*](https://birkhaeuser.com/products/9783764365660) (Dunne & Raby, 2001/2021)
- [*Speculative Everything*](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262019842/) (Dunne & Raby, 2013 MIT Press) — The canonical speculative design text
- *Not Here, Not Now* (Dunne & Raby, 2025) — Latest book
