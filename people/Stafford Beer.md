# Stafford Beer (1926–2002)

British theorist in operational research and management cybernetics. Creator of the **Viable System Model** (VSM) — a model of organizational structure based on cybernetic principles. BCL visitor under [[Heinz von Foerster]]. Led **Project Cybersyn** (1971–1973) under Salvador Allende's government in Chile — the most famous attempt to apply cybernetics to national-scale governance. Destroyed by Pinochet's coup.

## Why Interesting
Project Cybersyn is one of the most remarkable (and tragic) experiments in cybernetic governance — a real-time decentralized economic management system for a whole country. Fascinating for anyone thinking about distributed AI systems and governance. His VSM remains influential in organizational theory.
