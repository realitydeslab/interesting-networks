# Max Wilson

Professor of Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Nottingham. Specializes in cognitive personal informatics — how people track, interpret, and act on data about their own cognitive states (stress, focus, mental load). Key collaborator with [[Anna Cox]] on the CHI 2024 and CHI 2026 workshops on the Future of Cognitive Personal Informatics. Also works on brain-computer interfaces as personal informatics and how wearable neuroimaging integrates into everyday life.
