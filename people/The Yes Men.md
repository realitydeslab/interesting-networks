# The Yes Men (Jacques Servin & Igor Vamos)

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Tactical Media deep research.

Culture-jamming activist duo — Jacques Servin (aka Andy Bichlbaum) and Igor Vamos (aka Mike Bonanno) — formed 1996. Famous for impersonating WTO, Dow Chemical (Bhopal BBC hoax 2004), Shell Oil, US Chamber of Commerce. Three documentary films. Run the [Yes Lab](https://yeslab.org/) which teaches tactical media/activist impersonation techniques to social movements. Vamos is Associate Professor at RPI.

## Why Interesting
Canonical example of tactical media in action. Their Yes Lab workshops are a live transmission vector for the tradition.
