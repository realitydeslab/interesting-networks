# Andrew Bean

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-09:** Stub created during OxRML deep research.

DPhil student in Social Data Science at the [Oxford Internet Institute (OII)](https://www.oii.ox.ac.uk/). Member of the [Reasoning with Machines Lab (OxRML)](https://oxrml.com/). Research focus: **cooperative AI** — studying types of human-AI cooperation, the nature of those interactions, and technical developments (RL, NLP) to enable them.

## Key Works

- [Clinical knowledge in LLMs does not translate to human interactions](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=clinical+knowledge+LLMs+human+interactions+Bean+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Bean, Payne, Parsons, Mahdi et al., Apr 2025) — landmark 1,300-participant study. Lead author.
- [Measuring what Matters: Construct Validity in LLM Benchmarks](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=Measuring+Matters+Construct+Validity+LLM+Bean+Kearns+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Bean, Kearns, Mahdi et al., Nov 2025) — lead author on 29-expert benchmark validation study
- [LINGOLY-TOO: Disentangling Reasoning from Knowledge](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=LINGOLY-TOO+Bean+Kearns+Mahdi&searchtype=all) (Khouja, Korgul, Mayne, Kearns, Bean, Mahdi, Mar 2025)
