# Donna Haraway

American scholar. Foundational figure in posthumanism, feminist technoscience, multispecies thinking.

## Key Works
- *A Cyborg Manifesto* (1985) — landmark text on technology, gender, hybridity
- *Staying with the Trouble: Making Kin in the Chthulucene* (2016)
- *When Species Meet* (2008) — companion species, multispecies relations
- *Primate Visions* (1989)

## Key Concepts
- Cyborg — hybrid of machine and organism
- Companion species — interspecies entanglement
- Chthulucene — alternative to Anthropocene
- "Make kin, not babies"
- Situated knowledges
