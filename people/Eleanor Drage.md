Senior Research Fellow and Co-Director of the AI: Narratives and Justice Programme at CFI, Cambridge. Feminist and anti-racist AI ethics scholar. Co-editor of *Feminist AI* (OUP, 2023). Works at intersection of feminist theory, participatory methods, and AI governance.
