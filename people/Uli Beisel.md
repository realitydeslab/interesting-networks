# Uli Beisel

Human geographer and STS scholar at Free University Berlin; works on multispecies relations, mosquitoes, GMO organisms in global health, and ANT (Actor-Network Theory) in African healthcare contexts. Part of [[LOST Research Group]] network.

## Domains
- Multispecies STS: mosquitoes, malaria, Ebola, genetically modified insects
- ANT (Actor-Network Theory) applied to Global South healthcare
- Bioeconomy of science and public trust
- Migration control and borderlands

## Why Interesting
Beisel's work on mosquitoes-as-agents, GMO insect release in Burkina Faso, and ANT in African healthcare is genuinely more-than-human STS in practice — not just theoretical posthumanism but empirical study of multispecies entanglements in life-and-death situations. Her chapter in *Mosquitopia* (2022) on moving "from mosquito eradication to coexistence" is conceptually close to more-than-human design thinking Amber engages with.
