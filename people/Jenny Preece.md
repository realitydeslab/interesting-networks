# Jenny Preece

Professor at University of Maryland iSchool. Co-author of *Interaction Design: Beyond Human-Computer Interaction* (6th ed. 2023, Wiley, with [[Yvonne Rogers]] and [[Helen Sharp]]) — the world's most widely used HCI textbook. Pioneer in online communities, participatory design, and citizen science. Decades-long collaboration with Yvonne Rogers as textbook co-author and intellectual partner.

## Key Works
- [*Interaction Design: Beyond Human-Computer Interaction*](https://www.wiley.com/en-gb/Interaction+Design%3A+Beyond+Human-Computer+Interaction-p-9781119901143) (with Rogers, Sharp, Wiley, 6th ed. 2023)
- *Online Communities: Designing Usability and Supporting Sociality* (2000) — influential early work on designing digital communities
- *Interaction Design* (1994, Pearson, with Rogers, Sharp, Benyon et al.)

## Why Interesting
Foundational figure in participatory and community-focused HCI. The Rogers-Preece-Sharp tripartite is the defining collaborative formation of HCI education globally.
