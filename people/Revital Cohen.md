# Revital Cohen

Designer and artist, one half of [Cohen & Van Balen](https://cohenvanbalen.com/) with Till Vorsatz (formerly Cohen Van Balen). Design Interactions (RCA) alumna under [[Anthony Dunne]] and [[Fiona Raby]]. Known for work at the intersection of biotechnology, labor, and speculative design. Work featured in MoMA Design and Violence series (Republic of Salivation project with Michael Burton). Winner of multiple design awards.

## Why Interesting
Key second-generation critical designer from [[Design Interactions (RCA)]]. Demonstrates how the DI programme produced practitioners who expanded critical design beyond its academic frame into commercial galleries and museums. Her work on bio-labor (Life Support, 2008) and human-animal hybridity is directly relevant to more-than-human design.

- [[Fiona Raby]] — mentor at DI
