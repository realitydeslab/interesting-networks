# Iohanna Nicenboim

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Initial deep research. Created comprehensive note with full profile.

Designer and researcher specializing in more-than-human design and speculative approaches to IoT and connected technologies. Works at TU Delft exploring how design can account for the agency of non-human entities, particularly in the context of AI and IoT systems.

## Background
Based at TU Delft Industrial Design Engineering faculty. Research focuses on challenging anthropocentric design approaches by developing frameworks and methods that acknowledge the agency and participation of more-than-human entities in designed systems. Particularly interested in how IoT devices and AI systems can be understood as participants rather than just tools.

## Domains

- **IoT and connected things** — investigating the agency and social roles of smart devices
- **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)** — critical approaches to interaction design
