# Max Tegmark

MIT physicist, cosmologist, and AI safety advocate. Co-founder of both [[Foundational Questions Institute (FQXi)]] (2006) and the [[Future of Life Institute]] (2014). Known for the Mathematical Universe Hypothesis (MUH) — the idea that mathematical structures are the ultimate reality. Bridges foundational physics, consciousness research, and AI existential risk.

## Domains
- Foundational cosmology: multiverse, many-worlds interpretation
- Mathematical Universe Hypothesis: reality is mathematics
- Consciousness: integrated information theory adjacent, "consciousness as a state of matter"
- AI safety: existential risk from advanced AI

## Key Works
- [*Our Mathematical Universe*](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/311461/our-mathematical-universe-by-max-tegmark/) (2014) — MUH: the universe is a mathematical structure
- [*Life 3.0*](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/530584/life-30-by-max-tegmark/) (2017) — AI and the future of life; FLI-adjacent
- [Consciousness as a State of Matter](https://arxiv.org/abs/1401.1219) (2014) — mathematical framework for consciousness
