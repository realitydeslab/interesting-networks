# Edgar Reitz

German filmmaker. Co-signatory of the [Oberhausen Manifesto](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oberhausen_Manifesto) (1962) with [[Alexander Kluge]]; co-founded the Ulm Institut für Filmgestaltung at [HfG Ulm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hochschule_f%C3%BCr_Gestaltung_Ulm); co-directed *In Gefahr und größter Not bringt der Mittelweg den Tod* (1974) with Kluge. Best known for *Heimat* (1984) and sequels — 11+ hour chronicle of German village life across the 20th century, one of the most significant works of German cinema. Foundational figure of New German Cinema alongside Fassbinder, Herzog, Wenders.
