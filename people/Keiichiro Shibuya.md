# Keiichiro Shibuya

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created via YCAM deep research.

Japanese composer and musician. Born 1973. Graduated from Tokyo National University of Fine Arts and Music (composition). Founded ATAK music label in 2002 — releasing cutting-edge electro-acoustic works. Created *THE END* (2012), the world's first vocaloid opera. Collaborates with complex systems researcher Takashi Ikegami (University of Tokyo) — an interesting bridge between music and ALIFE/complexity science.

## Key Works
- *filmachine* (2006, YCAM) — 3D sound + LED installation; Honorary Mention Ars Electronica Digital Music 2007
- *[THE END](https://www.ycam.jp/en/archive/works/the-end/)* (2012, YCAM) — World's first vocaloid opera (no human performers). Toured: Bunkamura Orchard Hall, Théâtre du Châtelet (Paris), Holland Festival, Georgian National Opera, Matadero Madrid
- Collaborated with Takashi Ikegami (complex systems) at NTT ICC
- ATAK label: Ryoji Ikeda, Yasunao Tone, Pansonic on ATAK NIGHT4 Tour

## Key Relationships
- **Takashi Ikegami** (University of Tokyo, complex systems/ALIFE) — ongoing interdisciplinary collaboration
- **[[YCAM]]** — major productions
- **[[NTT InterCommunication Center (ICC)]]** — Ikegami collaboration shown here

## Why Interesting
Bridge between experimental electronic music and computational art/science. His collaboration with Takashi Ikegami (ALIFE, complex systems, consciousness) is particularly interesting — a different entry point into the art-ALIFE intersection. THE END as a cultural artifact of post-human performance is a landmark.
