# Lisa Nakamura

Gwendolyn Calvert Baker Collegiate Professor of American Culture at [University of Michigan](https://www.umich.edu/). Inaugural Director of the [Digital Studies Institute at Michigan](https://lsa.umich.edu/digitalstudies/). Founding member of [Precarity Lab](https://precaritylab.org/) collective. Author of four books on **race, gender, and digital media/gaming**, including work on identity in virtual environments, indigenous electronics manufacture in post-war America, and women of color's labor in building the internet. Affiliated external faculty at [[Digital Democracies Institute]] (SFU).
