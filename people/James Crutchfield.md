# James Crutchfield

UC Davis complexity theorist and director of the Complexity Sciences Center. Pioneer of **computational mechanics** — a framework for extracting structure and complexity from data using minimal models (epsilon-machines). Multiple FQXi grants: information thermodynamics of observers (2016), intelligence in thermodynamic agents (2019), signatures of cetacean consciousness (2020). Runs the Art and Science Laboratory.

- Information thermodynamics

- Non-human consciousness / intelligence
- Statistical physics of computation
