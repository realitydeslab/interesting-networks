# Cecilia Alemani

Italian curator based in New York. Donald R. Mullen Jr. Director & Chief Curator of High Line Art (since 2011). Artistic Director of the 59th Venice Biennale (2022, *The Milk of Dreams*) — first Italian woman to hold this position. CCS Bard alumna. Married to curator Massimiliano Gioni.

## Background
Born 1977 in Italy. MA in Curatorial Studies from CCS Bard; BA in Philosophy from University of Milan. Built an expertise in commissioning ambitious artworks for public and unusual spaces through the High Line. The Venice Biennale appointment in 2022 positioned her as one of the most powerful curators in the world.

## Domains
- Public art (High Line commissioned works)
- Large-scale biennial curation (Venice Biennale, Art Basel Cities)
- Contemporary art across media
- Italian pavilion at Venice (curated 2017, *Il Mondo Magico*)

## Key Works
- **59th Venice Biennale** (2022, *The Milk of Dreams*) — Artistic Director: <https://www.labiennale.org/en/art/2022>
- **High Line Art Program** (2011–present) — commissioned work by El Anatsui, Barbara Kruger, Ed Ruscha, Adrián Villar Rojas, and many others
- **Italian Pavilion, Venice Biennale** (2017, *Il Mondo Magico*) — featured Adelita Husni-Bey, Roberto Cuoghi, Giorgio Andreotta Calò
- **Art Basel Cities: Buenos Aires** (2018, Artistic Director) — *Hopscotch (Rayuela)*
- **X Initiative, New York** (2009–2010) — Curatorial Director of experimental nonprofit
- **High Line Plinth** (program launched 2019) — monumental public art commissions; first work by Simone Leigh, *Brick House*
