# Elena Vogman

Film scholar, media theorist, and artist associated with the KIM Critical AI Studies group at HfG Karlsruhe. Collaborator with [[Matteo Pasquinelli]] on "Catastrophe and Schizophrenia: Artaud, Tosquelles, Goldstein, and Fanon at the Crossroad of Europe's Wars" (forthcoming in *Fragments of Repair*, MIT Press). Chaired the Mental Ecologies of Images series 2023.

## Domains
- Film theory, cinema and psychopathology
- Media philosophy
- Artaud, Tosquelles — intersection of avant-garde arts and psychiatry
- Critical AI studies (adjacent, through KIM)

## Why Interesting
The Artaud/Tosquelles/Fanon combination suggests deep interest in madness, colonialism, and the body — unusual theoretical intersection. The MIT Press chapter with Pasquinelli on "catastrophe and schizophrenia" touches on themes relevant to machine consciousness and AI pathology.
