# Mimi Onuoha

Artist and researcher working on missing data, data ethics, and the politics of information. Known for *The Library of Missing Datasets* — an artwork highlighting data that is not collected. Faculty at NYU ITP. Conversation partner with [[Mindy Seu]] for United States Artists ("[The Digital is Physical](https://newsuns.net/mimi-onuoha-and-mindy-seu-the-digital-is-physical/)").

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-01:** Updated during [[Ida B. Wells Just Data Lab]] deep research — she was the inaugural Artist-in-Residence (2021).
- **2025–26:** Solo exhibition "Soft Zeros" at Secession Vienna; MoMA R&D Salon 56; Ohio State University Visiting Artist; Wexner Center presentation.
- **2025:** "Ground Truths" — single-channel 4K film using ML to predict hidden mass graves of convict leasing victims in Texas.
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created during [[Mindy Seu]] deep research.

## Why Interesting
Missing datasets as artistic/political concept. Bridges data science, art, and social justice. Connected to critical computing and data ethics communities. Now using ML to investigate historical injustice (hidden mass graves from convict leasing) — methodologically interesting: using the tool to reveal what the tool normally obscures. Inaugural Artist-in-Residence at [[Ida B. Wells Just Data Lab]] (Princeton, 2021) makes her a key node in the critical data art network around [[Ruha Benjamin]].
