# Lucia Pietroiusti

Italian curator at **[[Serpentine Galleries]]**, London. Founder and lead of the **General Ecology** project — Serpentine's multi-year programme exploring ecological thinking, more-than-human relations, and artistic responses to the climate crisis. Co-curator of the **13th Shanghai Biennale** "Wetware" (2021) alongside Andrés Jaque and Marina Otero Verzier. Her involvement in the Shanghai Biennale brought the Serpentine's ecological discourse into PSA, signalling a growing alignment between Western ecology-art institutions and China's contemporary art scene. Key figure in the global ecology-art circuit: natural connection for Amber's work (somaesthetics + more-than-human) and for understanding how climate/ecology discourse flows between the Serpentine and [[Power Station of Art]].
