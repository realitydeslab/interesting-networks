# Dilda Ramazan

Kazakh curator and artist. Member of [[DAVRA Collective]], the pan-Central Asian contemporary art research collective founded by [[Saodat Ismailova]]. Curated three programmes of Central Asian cinema for the [EYE Film Player](https://www.eyefilm.nl/) (Amsterdam, 2023) alongside the *18,000 Worlds* retrospective — presenting three generations of Central Asian artists. Performed readings at Tate Modern (2023) as part of the DAVRA delegation.

## Why Interesting

Emerging curatorial voice for Central Asian contemporary art in European institutional contexts. The EYE Film Player curation is significant — it's the first institutional programming of Central Asian cinema across generations at a major European film museum. Worth tracking as an independent figure beyond DAVRA.
