# Tariq Goddard

British novelist and publisher. Co-founded [[Zero Books]] (2007) with [[Mark Fisher]] and, after parting ways with John Hunt Publishing, co-founded [[Repeater Books]] (2014) with Fisher and others under [[Watkins Media]]. Ran both imprints for 17 years before departing in July 2024.

- **2024-07:** Goddard departed Repeater Books after 17 years. Published leaving statement.

## Background
Born 1975. Studied philosophy at King's College London and Continental Philosophy at University of Warwick and University of Surrey — where he encountered the intellectual milieu of the CCRU period. Lives on a farm in Wiltshire.

Both a novelist and an editor — an unusual combination that shaped the Repeater/Zero editorial identity. His fiction is psychedelic, baroque, politically charged, rooted in British settings.
