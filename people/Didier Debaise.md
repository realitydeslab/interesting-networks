# Didier Debaise

Belgian philosopher of speculative naturalism and process philosophy. Research Professor at [Free University of Brussels (ULB)](https://www.ulb.be/). Leading interpreter of Alfred North Whitehead in the contemporary continental tradition. Director of the [Process Research Group](https://processresearch.org/) at ULB — a major hub connecting process philosophy with ecology, contemporary art, and speculative realism. Close intellectual peer of Stengers, Latour, and Hörl.

## Background
Belgian philosopher trained in continental philosophy. Has become the leading continental European interpreter of Whitehead's speculative philosophy, connecting process thought to ecology, aesthetics, and politics. Works at the intersection of Whitehead, Deleuze, Stengers, and ecology.

## Key Works
- *L'appât des possibles: Reprise de Whitehead* (2015) — on the lure of possibilities in Whitehead
- *Speculative Empiricism: Revisiting Whitehead* (Edinburgh UP, 2017) — English translation
- *Nature as Event: The Lure of the Possible* (Duke UP, 2017) — ecology and Whitehead
- *Un empirisme spéculatif: lecture de Procès et réalité de Whitehead* (Vrin, 2006) — foundational reading
