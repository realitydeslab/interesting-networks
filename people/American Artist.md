# American Artist

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created ⭐⭐⭐ — via [[School for Poetic Computation]] board member discovery. Flagged for deep research.

Conceptual artist whose multidisciplinary practice examines the persistence of anti-Black racism in technological systems, surveillance, incarceration, and historical memory. Changed their legal name to "American Artist" in 2013 as a conceptual gesture. Received widespread institutional recognition for critiques of Silicon Valley mythology, algorithmic policing, and digital labor.

## Domains

- Net art and browser-based work
