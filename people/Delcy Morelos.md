# Delcy Morelos

Colombian artist known for large-scale immersive installations with earth, soil, cacao, and organic materials — building total sensory environments that collapse distinctions between body, material, and place. Currently showing at [[MUAC-UNAM]] (2025-2026).

## Why Interesting
Her work engages land, body, and indigenous/Afro-Colombian traditions through installation. Connects to more-than-human + embodied + ecological threads. Resonates with the IIE-UNAM's "Hibridaciones: estudio del suelo" (soil studies) programme.
