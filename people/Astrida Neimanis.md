# Astrida Neimanis

Canadian-Australian feminist theorist of the body and more-than-human relations. Best known for *Bodies of Water: Posthuman Feminist Phenomenology* (Bloomsbury, 2017), which develops "hydrofeminism" — thinking through water as a medium that connects all bodies across species, times, and places. Professor at the University of Sydney. Works on posthumanism, environmental humanities, feminist theory, and bodies as more-than-human assemblages.
