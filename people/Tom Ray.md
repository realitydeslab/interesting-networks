# Tom Ray

Creator of **Tierra** (1991), the first fully digital ecosystem with evolvable organisms. Where Langton studied *possible* life through cellular automata, Ray *synthesized* it — Tierra's programs evolved, parasitized each other, and produced genuine Darwinian dynamics in silico. ALife II (1991) attendee and contributor. Declared: "The intent of this work is to synthesize rather than simulate life."

## Key Works
- **Tierra** (1991) — digital evolvable organism system. Programs compete for CPU time and memory ("RAM soup"). Resulted in parasites, hyperparasites, immune strategies emerging spontaneously. URL: <http://life.ou.edu/>

## Why Interesting
Classic ALife lineage. Tierra was one of the most influential demonstrations that open-ended evolution could occur in silico. Directly relevant to Amber's OEE / ALIFE interests.
