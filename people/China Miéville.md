British speculative fiction author. Significant crossover between genre fiction and continental philosophy/critical theory.

## Background
Author of *Perdido Street Station*, *The City & The City*, and other "New Weird" fiction. Strong philosophical engagement, particularly with Marx, critical theory, and speculative philosophy.

## Key Works
- *Perdido Street Station* (2000)
- *The City & The City* (2009)
- *Embassytown* (2011)
- Various critical theory essays
