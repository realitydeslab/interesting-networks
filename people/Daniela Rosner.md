# Daniela Rosner

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Stub created — DIS prominent feminist technology studies voice.

Associate Professor at University of Washington, Human Centered Design & Engineering. Works at the intersection of feminist technology studies, repair, craft, and interaction design. Her work treats making and unmaking as political acts.

## Why Interesting
Rosner's "Resistive Threads" (DIS 2024) — on electronic streetwear as social movement material — is exactly the kind of feminist, materially-engaged, politically-motivated design research that connects to Amber's interests. Her lab (Design Fixation Lab) treats repair and craft as ethical-political practices.
