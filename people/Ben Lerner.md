# Ben Lerner

American poet, novelist, essayist, and critic. Recipient of Fulbright, Guggenheim, and MacArthur Fellowships. Finalist for National Book Award, National Book Critics Circle Award, and Pulitzer Prize for Fiction. Distinguished Professor of English at Brooklyn College (2016). Key works: *Leaving the Atocha Station* (2011), *10:04* (2014), *The Topeka School* (2019). Notable: collaborated with [[Alexander Kluge]] beginning 2016; collected in *The Snows of Venice* ([Spector Books](https://www.spectorbooks.com/), 2018). One of the most significant American literary voices of his generation.
