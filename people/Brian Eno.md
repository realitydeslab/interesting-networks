# Brian Eno

Musician, producer, visual artist. Co-inventor of ambient music, pioneer of generative music, one of the most influential thinkers at the intersection of art and technology. Produced U2, Talking Heads, David Bowie (Low, Heroes, Lodger), Coldplay. Founding member of Roxy Music. But the origin story is crucial: **studied under [[Roy Ascott]] in the Groundcourse at Ipswich Civic College (1964–66)** — the cybernetic feedback-loop pedagogy that shaped his entire career. Eno himself has cited Ascott's teaching as formative: the Groundcourse's identity/behavior/environment triad, the idea of the artist as systems designer rather than craftsman, underpins all his generative music work.

Key concepts: **Oblique Strategies** (with Peter Schmidt, 1975); **ambient music** as environment/system; **generative music** (software compositions that evolve); **scenius** (genius of a scene/community, not individual).
