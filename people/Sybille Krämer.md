# Sybille Krämer

German philosopher and media theorist. Professor emerita (formerly Freie Universität Berlin, then Leuphana). Key developer of cultural techniques theory, parallel to [[Bernhard Siegert]] but with a distinct focus on writing, notation, the diagram, and the figure of the messenger. Co-edited the foundational cultural techniques volume *Bild, Schrift, Zahl* (Fink, 2003) with Horst Bredekamp.

## Why Interesting
