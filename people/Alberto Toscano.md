# Alberto Toscano

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Speculative Realism deep research.

Political philosopher and theorist. Moderated the founding 2007 Goldsmiths Speculative Realism conference. Long-term based at Goldsmiths; moved to UC San Diego around 2022. Works on Marxist political philosophy, philosophy of abstraction, fanaticism, and the politics of temporality. Translated several key French philosophical texts into English. His connection to SR is primarily as the moderator and Goldsmiths institutional link — his own work is in the Marxist/political philosophy tradition rather than SR proper.

## Why Interesting
Mainly of historical significance as the conference organizer/moderator. His work on abstraction and political philosophy is interesting in its own right but not directly relevant to Amber's core threads. ⭐ — watch but no urgent deep research.
