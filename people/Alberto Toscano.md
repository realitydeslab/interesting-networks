# Alberto Toscano

Marxist philosopher at [SFU School of Communication](https://www.sfu.ca/communication/) and Faculty Fellow at the [Digital Democracies Institute](https://digitaldemocracies.org/). Translator of major continental philosophers (Badiou, Negri, Agamben, Fortini). Author of *[Late Fascism: Race, Capitalism and the Politics of Crisis](https://www.versobooks.com/products/2971-late-fascism)* (2023), *[Fanaticism: On the Uses of an Idea](https://www.versobooks.com/products/3036-fanaticism)* (2017, 2nd ed.), and *[Cartographies of the Absolute](https://zeroonebooks.com/products/cartographies-of-the-absolute)* (with Jeff Kinkle, 2015). Co-editor of three-volume *Georges Bataille's Critical Essays* (2023–). Columnist for *In These Times*. Edits Seagull Essays and The Italian List for [Seagull Books](https://www.seagullbooks.org/).
