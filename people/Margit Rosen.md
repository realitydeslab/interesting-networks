# Margit Rosen

Head of Collections, Archives & Research at ZKM since 2016. Art history trained (LMU Munich, HfG Karlsruhe, Paris I Panthéon-Sorbonne). Expert on history and aesthetics of electronic arts. Teaches at HfG Karlsruhe, CAFA Beijing (China connection!), and MediaArtHistories Master's (Danube University Krems). UNESCO Expert Committee on Culture.

## Why Interesting
Key person for archive/research access at ZKM. CAFA Beijing connection — bridges European and Chinese media art scenes. UNESCO role gives her access to global cultural heritage discourse. Most accessible ZKM contact for academic/research collaboration.
