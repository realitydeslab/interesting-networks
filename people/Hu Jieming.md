# Hu Jieming (胡介鸣)

Pioneer Shanghai video and new media artist since the 1990s. Co-founder of [[Chronus Art Center]] (2013). One of the foundational figures of Chinese media art alongside Zhang Peili.

## Key Works
- *Tai Chi* series (interactive video)
- *T_Visionarium* (with Jeffrey Shaw, iCinema UNSW) — immersive panoramic cinema
- *Twofold* solo exhibitions at CAC (multiple) with Jeffrey Shaw
- *Media Accumulation: Archiving* — durational video work

## Why Note-Worthy
Bridges Chinese media art from the early 1990s (analogue video, early digital) through to the present. His partnership with Jeffrey Shaw (iCinema, UNSW Sydney) connects Chinese media art to the Australian/European new media tradition.
