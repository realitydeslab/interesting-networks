# Morgan Ames

Associate Professor at UC Berkeley I School. Postdoc with [[Paul Dourish]] at UCI (2013-2015). Author of *[The Charisma Machine: The Myth of Technology in Education](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262538985/the-charisma-machine/)* (MIT Press, 2019) — a critical ethnography of the One Laptop Per Child (OLPC) project as a failure of techno-utopianism. Studies "charismatic technology" — tech products and programs that attract outsized belief despite thin evidence. Feminist STS approach.

## Why Interesting
The "charismatic technology" concept Ames developed is broadly applicable to AI products and promises — directly relevant to Amber's machine behavior and AI ethics work. *The Charisma Machine* is a model for how to write a critical tech book that's both rigorous and readable.
