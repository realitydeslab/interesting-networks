# Jacques Derrida

Founder of deconstruction. One of the most influential philosophers of the 20th century. Doctoral supervisor of [[Bernard Stiegler]] at EHESS. Co-authored *Echographies of Television* with Stiegler. His concept of the *supplement* and *pharmakon* directly informed Stiegler's philosophy of technics. Key figure in the genealogy that leads to [[cosmotechnics]] through Stiegler → [[Yuk Hui]].
