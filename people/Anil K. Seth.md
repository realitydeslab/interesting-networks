## Background
Over 200 academic publications. Pioneer of the "controlled hallucination" / "predictive processing" theory of consciousness — the idea that conscious experience is the brain's "best guess" about the causes of sensory signals. Research spans neuroscience, philosophy, and increasingly AI consciousness.
