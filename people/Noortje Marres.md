# Noortje Marres

Professor of Science, Technology and Society at the [Centre for Interdisciplinary Methodologies](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/cross_fac/cim/), University of Warwick. Major figure in STS, digital sociology, and public engagement with AI. PI on [AI in the Street](https://www.careful.industries/ai-in-the-street/) (BRAID/UKRI), a multi-site field research project observing how everyday publics encounter AI in urban environments. Author of *Digital Sociology* (Polity, 2017) and *Material Participation* (Palgrave, 2012).
