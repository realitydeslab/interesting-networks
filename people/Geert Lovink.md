# Geert Lovink

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-03:** Stub created during Tactical Media deep research.

Dutch media theorist and internet critic (b. 1959). Co-coined "tactical media" with David Garcia (1997). Founding director of the [Institute of Network Cultures](https://networkcultures.org/) at Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. Most recent book: *Platform Brutality* (Valiz, 2025). Still very active.
