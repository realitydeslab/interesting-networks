# Peter Weibel

(1944 Odessa – 2023) Artist, media theorist, curator, and museum director. Founder of ZKM's intellectual framework. Studied literature, medicine, logic, philosophy, and film in Paris and Vienna. Directed Ars Electronica 1986–1995. Founded Institute of New Media at Städelschule Frankfurt 1989. Professor, University of Applied Arts Vienna 1984–2017. ZKM director 1999–2023 — the defining 24 years. 

Coined the concept of **"BioMedia"** — media that exhibit life-like behavior (not Bio Art but biomimetic media). Co-edited landmark volumes with Bruno Latour: *Iconoclash* (MIT, 2002) and *Making Things Public* (MIT, 2005). Produced 70+ landmark exhibitions. Awards: Oskar-Kokoschka-Preis, Austrian Cross of Honor for Science and Art, French Officier Arts et Lettres.

## Why Interesting
The theoretical architect of media art as a discipline. His *BioMedia* concept — 21st century media will exhibit life-like behavior — is the most productive theoretical frame for understanding the intersection of alife, computational creativity, and media art. *Iconoclash* (with Latour) is one of the great exhibition-books connecting science, religion, and art — exactly Amber's zone.
