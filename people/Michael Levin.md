# Michael Levin

Developmental biologist at Tufts University. Studies bioelectricity, regeneration, and biological intelligence. Online keynote at ALIFE 2025.

## Domains
Bioelectric signaling, morphogenesis, regenerative biology, basal cognition, collective intelligence of cells, xenobots.

## Key Works
- **Xenobots** — living robots made from frog cells that self-assemble and self-replicate
- Research on bioelectric patterns guiding cellular decision-making
- "Technological Approach to Mind Everywhere" (2022) — framework for understanding intelligence at all scales
- Work on how cells communicate and make collective decisions about body form

## Key Communities

## Why Interesting
Levin's work is paradigm-shifting: he treats cells and tissues as agents with goals, memory, and problem-solving abilities. This is "more-than-human intelligence" in the most literal sense — intelligence that exists at scales below the organism. His xenobots are the most dramatic ALife result in recent years. His ideas connect directly to ALife, active inference, and multi-agent systems. YouTube talks have made his work viral (millions of views). Extremely relevant to Amber's more-than-human and agent behavior interests.
