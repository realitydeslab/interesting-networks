# Nontsikelelo Mutiti

Zimbabwean-born graphic designer, educator, co-director of [Encyclopedia of Diversity in Education](https://ede.yale.edu/) project, faculty at Yale School of Art (Graphic Design). Led the visual/web design of the [[Practicing Refusal Collective]]'s [[The Sojourner Project]] South Africa online platform (2020–2021). Works at the intersection of Black typography, decolonial design, and community knowledge-making.
