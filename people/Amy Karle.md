# Amy Karle

American bioartist working at the intersection of regenerative medicine, 3D bio-printing, and speculative biology. Known for *Regenerative Reliquary* (2016) — stem cells grown into bone over a 3D-printed hand scaffold — and *Echoes from the Valley of Existence* (2024), which sends DNA converted to powder into space.
