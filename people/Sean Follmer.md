# Sean Follmer

Associate Professor at Stanford HCI Group. Co-created inFORM and TRANSFORM with Daniel Leithinger under [[Hiroshi Ishii]] at MIT Tangible Media Group (Ph.D. 2015). inFORM is the most viral tangible computing project of the 2010s. Also created Jamming User Interfaces (UIST 2012 Best Paper).

## Domains
Shape-changing interfaces, physical telepresence, actuated surfaces, assistive technology, robotics.

## Key Works
- **[inFORM](https://tangible.media.mit.edu/project/inform/)** (2013) — dynamic shape display rendering 3D content physically. Remote participants displayed physically. Viral (10M+ YouTube views).
- **[TRANSFORM](https://tangible.media.mit.edu/project/transform/)** (2014) — kinetic furniture-scale pin display. A'Design Platinum 2015.
- **Jamming User Interfaces** (2012) — UIST 2012 Best Paper.
