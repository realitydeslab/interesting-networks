# Florian Hecker

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub during Reza Negarestani research. Requires deep research.

German sound artist and experimental musician. Known for conceptually rigorous electroacoustic works and philosophy-based sound art.

## Background
Based in Vienna/London. Works with advanced synthesis techniques, psychoacoustics, and philosophical concepts. Regular presentations at major art institutions and festivals.

## Key Works
- **"Chimerization"** (2012) — collaboration with [[Reza Negarestani]] exhibited at documenta (13)
- Series of full-length albums based on "chimerization" concept with Negarestani librettos
- Various electroacoustic compositions and installations

## Exhibitions / Events
- **documenta (13)**, Kassel (2012) — "Chimerization" collaboration
- Major art institutions and experimental music venues internationally

## Connections
- **[[Reza Negarestani]]** — Major collaboration partner. Created "Chimerization" for documenta (13) and subsequent album series with Negarestani's librettos
- documenta network
- Experimental music/sound art communities
