# Andrés Jaque

Spanish architect, educator, and director of the [Office for Political Innovation](https://www.officeforpoliticalinnovation.com/). Director of the Advanced Architectural Design Programme at Columbia GSAPP. His practice investigates how architecture, technology, and ecology intersect with political power. Co-curated the **13th Shanghai Biennale "Wetware"** (2021) at [[Power Station of Art]] with [[Lucia Pietroiusti]] and Marina Otero Verzier. Won the Silver Lion at Venice Architecture Biennale 2016 for Superpowers of Ten. Known for work that treats buildings as political actors and infrastructure as sites of social negotiation.
