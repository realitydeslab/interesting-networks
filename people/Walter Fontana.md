# Walter Fontana

Theoretical chemist and systems biologist who developed **"Alchemy"** — a formal model of self-organizing chemistry using lambda calculus, where molecules are functions that act on other functions to produce new functions. An extension and formalization of Kauffman's autocatalytic sets framework. Now at Harvard Medical School in Systems Biology. Former Santa Fe Institute researcher.
