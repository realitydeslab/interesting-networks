# Joan Heemskerk

Dutch artist; half of the legendary net art duo **JODI** (with Dirk Paesmans). JODI are pioneers of net art from the early 1990s: subversive, disruptive web art, HTML deconstruction, software glitch aesthetics, video game hacking. Work shown at MoMA, Stedelijk Museum, ZKM Karlsruhe, Guggenheim, Centre Pompidou.

**2023 Inaugural Collide Copenhagen winner** (Arts at CERN × Copenhagen Contemporary). At CERN, Heemskerk engaged with quantum computing and cryptography research — extending JODI's long practice of exposing and subverting computational systems into the frontier of quantum computation.

A canonical figure in the history of internet art. At CERN, the pairing of digital subversion with quantum physics research is conceptually electric.
