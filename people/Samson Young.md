# Samson Young

Hong Kong artist working in sound, moving image, and conceptual art. Won the inaugural **Sigg Prize** (2019) — the most significant prize for contemporary Chinese/Greater China art. Represented Hong Kong at the 2018 Venice Biennale with *Songs for Disaster Relief World Tour* — a project deconstructing the history and politics of charity music singles. Works at the intersection of sonic production, political economy, and media archaeology. One of M+'s most important collected artists and the defining figure of M+'s engagement with sound/moving image from Hong Kong.
