## Background
Co-directed with Bratton at Strelka Institute (Moscow) over six years — two flagship programmes: The New Normal (2017-2019, speculative urbanism) and The Terraforming. Both programmes hosted hundreds of faculty and researchers from 25 countries on original projects modeling city-planet-culture-computation relationships. Co-edited with Bratton: *The New Normal* (Park Books, 2020). Now Design Director at Antikythera — the institutional successor to the Strelka programmes.
