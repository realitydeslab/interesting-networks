# Emilija Škarnulytė

Lithuanian artist and filmmaker working with geology, archaeology, deep time, cosmology, and ecological entanglement. Creates immersive films and installations that explore what it feels like to exist across planetary and cosmic timescales. Films frequently feature non-human protagonists — mountains, rivers, ancient structures, radioactive sites — that embody deep time in ways that make the human timescale feel brief and strange.
