# Ian Cheng

Artist creating "virtual ecosystems" — live-simulated digital artworks with autonomous agents. Combines cognitive science, ALife, and contemporary art.

## Key Books/Papers
- *Emissaries Guide to Worlding* (2018) — his theoretical framework
- Emissaries Trilogy (2015-2017) — live simulations exploring evolution and agency
- BOB (Bag of Beliefs) — AI creature that learns from audience interaction
- *Life After BOB* (2021) — animated film/simulation about AI and consciousness

## Key Communities
- Contemporary art world (gallery/museum circuit)

## Key Venues

## Key Relationships

## Why Interesting
The most compelling artist working with ALife simulations. His work is genuinely autonomous — not pre-rendered, but live-simulated ecosystems that evolve. BOB is a proto artificial life form exhibited in museums. Bridges cognitive science, game engines, and contemporary art. "Worlding" connects to speculative design.
