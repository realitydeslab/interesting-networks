# Eliezer Yudkowsky

Founder of the LessWrong rationalist community, co-founder of MIRI/SIAI, author of The Sequences. The prophetic figure of the AI safety movement whose writings seeded both LessWrong (2009) and, indirectly, the AI safety culture at Anthropic and OpenAI.

## Domains
AI safety, decision theory, Bayesian epistemics, philosophy of mind, rationalism.

## Key Works
- [*Rationality: A-Z / The Sequences*](https://www.readthesequences.com/) (2006-2009) — ~1.5M words on Bayesian reasoning, cognitive biases, AI risk. Foundational LW text.
- [*Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality*](https://www.hpmor.com/) (2010-2015) — Fan fiction applying LW rationalism. Surprisingly influential as gateway text.
- [TIME op-ed calling to shut down AI labs](https://time.com/6266923/ai-eliezer-yudkowsky-open-letter-not-enough/) (2023)

## Why Interesting
Yudkowsky is the intellectual origin point of the discourse ecosystem that produced Anthropic, shaped OpenAI's safety culture, and set the terms for AI risk debate. Understanding him is required background for understanding AI safety as a field. However, his deductive/apocalyptic approach is in direct tension with Amber's empirical machine behavior science framing.
