# Johan Redström

Major design theorist. Professor, Umeå Institute of Design. One of the key architects of the design research field's post-humanist turn. Critiques user-centeredness as fundamentally anthropocentric; develops frameworks for post-anthropocentric design. Connected to [[Design+Posthumanism Network]].

## Why Interesting
Redström is one of the few design theorists who has been systematically dismantling the philosophical foundations of user-centered design from *within* the mainstream (not from art or philosophy). His critique of the "world as matter of use" worldview and the move toward design that acknowledges non-human agencies is the philosophical foundation for the D+PH Network's entire project. If Amber wants to understand where more-than-human design comes from intellectually, Redström is essential.
