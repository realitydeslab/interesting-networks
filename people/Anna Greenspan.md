# Anna Greenspan

Associate Professor of Contemporary Global Media at [[NYU Shanghai IMA]], Global Network Associate Professor at NYU Steinhardt (Media, Culture, and Communication). Director of the [Center for AI and Culture (AIC)](https://aic.shanghai.nyu.edu). PhD in Continental Philosophy from Warwick University. **Founding member of [[CCRU]]** (Cybernetic Culture Research Unit).

## Background
- PhD in Philosophy, University of Warwick (dissertation: *Capitalism's Transcendental Time Machine*)
- MA in Continental Philosophy, University of Warwick
- Founding member of [[CCRU]] alongside [[Nick Land]], Sadie Plant, Mark Fisher, Steve Goodman, and others
- Based in Shanghai since ~2010s

## Why Interesting
**The institutional keystone of IMA/AIC.** She runs the Center for AI and Culture and is the most senior theory faculty. The CCRU founding membership is historically significant — she carries the accelerationist/speculative realism genealogy into Shanghai. Her work on Chinese modernity (*Shanghai Future*) and media philosophy (*China and the Wireless Undertow*) positions her as the key theorist bridging Western philosophy and Chinese technoculture. As AIC Director, she's the gatekeeper for events, affiliates, and collaborations. **Amber should know her well — she's the person who can open doors at NYU Shanghai.**

Upgraded from ⭐⭐ to ⭐⭐⭐ after deep research: her CCRU connection, AIC directorship, and role in *Machine Decision is Not Final* make her a central node, not just a peripheral one.
