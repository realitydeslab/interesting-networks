# Anna Greenspan

Philosopher based at NYU Shanghai. Her PhD thesis *Capitalism's Transcendental Time Machine* (Warwick, 2000) — on Kantian transcendental philosophy, capitalist time, and cyberspace — was the intellectual anchor for Paul Seidler and Max Hampshire's *Temporal Secessionism* research. Seidler interviewed her in 2019. Connected to the CCRU (Cybernetic Culture Research Unit at Warwick) and accelerationist philosophical circles (Nick Land), but has since developed her own independent trajectory focused on Shanghai and Chinese urbanism/technology. Author of *Shanghai Future: Modernity Remade* (Hurst, 2014).

## Key Texts
- *Capitalism's Transcendental Time Machine* — PhD thesis, Warwick (2000). On capitalist time as synthetic temporal regime, from Kant through digital cyberspace. 
- *Shanghai Future: Modernity Remade* (Hurst, 2014) — on Shanghai urbanism and Chinese modernity
