## Who She Is
**Associate Professor, School of Architecture, Design and Planning, University of Sydney.** Artist, designer, educator and researcher with the body as the central focus of her interdisciplinary practice. Architect of "the somatic turn" naming in HCI — co-authored the landmark paper with Thecla Schiphorst (ACM Interactions 2018).

Her practice sits at the intersection of **dance, interaction design, HCI, robotics, and performance art**. She combines movement analysis, somatic methodologies, and creative technology to explore how bodies relate to machines, spaces, and each other.

**Trained in:** Japanese Bodyweather dance methodology (with de Quincey Co), Body-Mind Centering, Qigong, and earlier somatic/shamanic practices with American choreographer Annetta Luce.

**Was:** Director, Master of Interaction Design and Electronic Arts, University of Sydney (2013–2020); Head of Design (2021).
