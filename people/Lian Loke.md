# Lian Loke

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during TEI Community deep research.

Associate Professor at University of Sydney's Design Lab. TEI steering committee member. Research focus: somatic interaction design, movement practices, dance as design method, embodied computing. One of the closest academic neighbors to Kia Höök's soma design tradition — bringing movement and dance into HCI research. Pioneer in using dance/movement practices as a methodology for interaction design.

Directly aligned with Amber's somaesthetic interests. If Amber's work moves toward soma × XR, Loke is exactly the kind of researcher to know. Her body-based design methods could inform how HoloKit experiences are designed and evaluated. Australia-based but active at TEI.
