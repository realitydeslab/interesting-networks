# Paul Seidler

Artist, researcher, and programmer. Co-founder of [[terra0]] (2015). Works with blockchain-based technologies to investigate value, ownership, and encryption — building smart contracts, zero-knowledge circuits, and token-based protocols. Situates his practice within cypherpunk history, institutional critique, systems aesthetics, and software art.

## Background
- Graduated Berlin University of the Arts (class of Prof. Joachim Sauter, 2020)
- Part of terra0 since 2015

## Exhibitions (selected)

- Schinkel Pavillon, Berlin
- Transmediale, Berlin
- 58th Carnegie International, Pittsburgh
- KW Institute for Contemporary Art, Berlin

## Key Communities

## Why Interesting
One of the most rigorous artist-programmers working on autonomous non-human systems and blockchain governance. His writing on smart contracts as artistic medium is essential reading for understanding the intersection of cryptography, art, and machine agency.
