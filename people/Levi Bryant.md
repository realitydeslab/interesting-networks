# Levi Bryant

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-27:** Stub created during Graham Harman deep research.

American philosopher. Coined the term **"object-oriented ontology"** (OOO) in 2009 to describe the field Harman founded. Professor of philosophy at [Collin College](https://www.collin.edu/), Dallas–Fort Worth area. Also practiced as a Lacanian psychoanalyst (unlicensed, permitted in Texas).

## Domains
- **Object-Oriented Ontology / Onticology** — his version of OOO called "onticology"; objects are split between virtual (potentialities) and actual (manifested qualities)
- **Lacan, Deleuze, Žižek** — earlier scholarly career
- **Machine-oriented ontology** — later departure from OOO; objects as machines/processes
- **Flat ontology** — following Manuel DeLanda's Deleuzian framework
- **Political philosophy** — ecology and wilderness ontology

## Key Works
- *[Difference and Givenness: Deleuze's Transcendental Empiricism and the Ontology of Immanence](https://www.nupress.northwestern.edu/9780810123847/difference-and-givenness/)* (Northwestern UP, 2008)
- *[The Democracy of Objects](http://www.openhumanitiespress.org/books/titles/the-democracy-of-objects/)* (Open Humanities Press, 2011) — free download; OOO in Bryant's "onticology" framework
- *[Onto-Cartography: An Ontology of Machines and Media](https://edinburghuniversitypress.com/book-onto-cartography.html)* (Edinburgh UP, 2014) — machine-oriented ontology
- (Co-editor) *[The Speculative Turn](http://re-press.org/book-files/OA_Version_Speculative_Turn_9780980668346.pdf)* (re.press, 2011, with [[Graham Harman]] and [[Nick Srnicek]])

## Key Communities
- **[Larval Subjects blog](https://larvalsubjects.wordpress.com/)** — over 2 million hits as of 2011; one of OOO's founding dissemination channels
- **[[The New Centre for Research & Practice]]** — board member

## Key Relationships
- **[[Graham Harman]]** — co-edited *The Speculative Turn*; coined the term OOO for Harman's movement; later diverged toward machine ontology
- **[[Nick Srnicek]]** — co-edited *The Speculative Turn*; Srnicek later moved toward platform capitalism theory
- **[[Timothy Morton]]** — OOO movement participant

## Why Interesting
Bryant's *Democracy of Objects* is the second most influential OOO text after Harman's books. His blog built OOO's early community. The divergence (Bryant → machines/processes, Harman → withdrawal) illustrates OOO's internal tensions productively.
