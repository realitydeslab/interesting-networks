# Jer Thorp

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-02-26:** Created stub note. Discovered as [[Eyeo Festival]] co-founder during Eyeo deep research.

Canadian data artist, writer, teacher. **Co-founder of [[Eyeo Festival]]** alongside Dave Schroeder, Wes Grubbs, Julian Dolce. NYT's first Data Artist in Residence. National Geographic Explorer. Library of Congress Innovator in Residence (2017–2018). Author of *[Living in Data](https://us.macmillan.com/books/9780374189426/livingindata)* (Farrar, Straus and Giroux, 2021) — a book about the human experience of data.

Known for making data feel personal, political, and ethical — not just visual. His piece *[Here & There: Satellite](http://www.jerthorp.com/hereandthere)* maps the sky. His NYT work includes the famous *September 11 Memorial* data project.
