# Federica Martini

## ⚡ Recent Updates
- **2026-03-01:** Stub created during HEAD Geneva deep research.

PhD curator, art historian, and professor heading the **CCC (Critical Curatorial Cybermedia) Research Master** at [[HEAD Geneva]]. Research focuses on exhibition history and geopolitics, intersections between aesthetic gestures and industrial histories, and (in)visibility in contemporary art practices. Feminist curatorial theory and archive practice.

## Domains

- Feminist curatorial practice and archives
- Art-science research methodology (PhD-Making as Art Practice)
